## 📝 Summary

The expectedparrot/edsl project is an open-source tool for designing, conducting, and analyzing AI-powered surveys and experiments. It allows users to simulate social science and market research scenarios with large numbers of AI agents and large language models (LLMs), enabling data-driven insights and analysis. This project facilitates the creation of complex, scalable simulations for studying human behavior and decision-making processes.
