## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the ClashRule project:

ClashRule is an open-source rule set designed for the Clash proxy tool, providing coverage for popular apps and maintained in the long term. This collection of rules is designed to optimize performance and security for users who rely on Clash for their proxy needs. By contributing to this project, users can help ensure its continued maintenance and improvement.
