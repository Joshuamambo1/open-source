## 📝 Summary

Emanote is an open-source project that provides a structured view of plain-text notes, allowing users to easily organize and explore their knowledge. Developed with AI/ML at its core, emanote utilizes natural language processing techniques to create a hierarchical and interconnected network of notes. This enables users to visually navigate and link related concepts, enhancing their note-taking experience and improving knowledge retention.
