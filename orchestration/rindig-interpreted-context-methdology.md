## 📝 Summary

RinDig/Interpreted-Context-Methdology is an open-source project that utilizes a novel approach to orchestration, leveraging the filesystem structure to replace framework-level orchestration. This agent architecture-based methodology, known as ICM, provides a unique and efficient way to manage complex workflows. By decoupling orchestration from frameworks, ICM enables flexible and scalable AI/ML development.
