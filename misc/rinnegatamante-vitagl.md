## 📝 Summary

vitaGL is an open-source, openGL wrapper for the PlayStation Vita, allowing developers to access and utilize openGL functionality on the handheld console. This project enables the creation of more complex graphics and applications on the Vita, giving developers greater flexibility and power in their projects. By providing an openGL interface, vitaGL facilitates the porting of games and applications from other platforms to the Vita.
