## 📝 Summary

This open-source project, DarklightGames/io_scene_psk_psa, provides a Blender extension for seamless importing and exporting of Unreal Engine PSK (physics skeleton) and PSA (physics animation) files. This extension simplifies the process of working with PSK and PSA files within Blender, a popular 3D modeling and animation software. It facilitates collaboration between Blender artists and Unreal Engine developers.
