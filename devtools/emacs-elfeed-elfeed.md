## 📝 Summary

Elfeed is an open-source, Emacs-based client for managing and reading web feeds, providing an efficient and customizable way to stay up-to-date with your favorite blogs and news sources. It allows users to subscribe to feeds, mark articles as read, and search through their feed history. With its flexibility and power, Elfeed is an ideal choice for Emacs users looking to enhance their feed reading experience.
