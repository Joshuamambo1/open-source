## 📝 Summary

GooseRelayVPN is an open-source Socks5 VPN solution that leverages Google Apps Script to securely tunnel raw TCP traffic to a VPS exit server. This project utilizes AES-256-GCM encryption and domain-fronting for enhanced security. By utilizing Google's infrastructure, GooseRelayVPN aims to provide a unique and secure way to bypass geo-restrictions and access blocked content.
