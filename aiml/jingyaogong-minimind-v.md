## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary of the project:

The Minimind-V project is an open-source initiative that enables the rapid training of a 65M-parameter Visual Language Model (VLM) from scratch within just two hours. This achievement pushes the boundaries of model training efficiency, making it a valuable resource for researchers and developers in the field of AI and machine learning. By providing a scalable and efficient VLM solution, Minimind-V aims to accelerate the development of advanced visual AI applications.
