## 📝 Summary

Petrichor is an open-source, offline music player designed specifically for macOS devices. This user-friendly application allows users to stream and play their music collections without the need for an internet connection, providing a seamless listening experience. By offering a simple and intuitive interface, Petrichor aims to provide a unique music listening experience for macOS users.
