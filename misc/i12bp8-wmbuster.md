## 📝 Summary

This open-source project utilizes a Flipper Zero device to sniff data from European Union smart meters, specifically focusing on WM-Bus and 868MHz frequency communication protocols. By leveraging the Flipper Zero's capabilities, users can potentially intercept and analyze data transmitted by these smart meters. This project may be of interest to individuals researching smart grid security or looking to explore the inner workings of smart meter communication systems.
