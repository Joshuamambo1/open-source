## 📝 Summary

Here's a 2-3 sentence summary for the omniget project:

Omniget is an open-source desktop application designed to streamline online course learning. It enables users to download and study courses from popular platforms like Hotmart, Udemy, Kiwify, and YouTube, with advanced features such as a video player with timestamped notes, spaced repetition flashcards, and focus timers. Additionally, the app includes a built-in PDF and EPUB reader with annotation tools and a comprehensive search function.
