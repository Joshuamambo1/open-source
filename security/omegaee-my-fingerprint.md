## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the open-source project:

"My-Fingerprint is an open-source browser extension that helps protect your online identity by minimizing your browser's unique fingerprint. Compatible with Chrome, Edge, and Firefox, this extension aims to reduce the risk of being tracked and targeted by online advertisers and malicious actors. By masking your browser's identifying characteristics, My-Fingerprint provides an additional layer of security and anonymity."
