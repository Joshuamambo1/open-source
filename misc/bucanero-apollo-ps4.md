## 📝 Summary

Apollo Save Tool is an open-source project that provides a solution for PlayStation 4 users to extract and save game data. This tool allows users to back up their game saves, enabling them to restore their progress in case of a console malfunction or other issues. With Apollo Save Tool, users can easily manage their game saves and ensure their gaming experience remains uninterrupted.
