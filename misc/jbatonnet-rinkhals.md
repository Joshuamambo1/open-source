## 📝 Summary

The Rinkhals project is an open-source custom firmware designed for the Anycubic Kobra series of 3D printers. It aims to enhance the capabilities and user experience of these printers by providing customizable settings, improved performance, and advanced features. This firmware is a collaborative effort to unlock the full potential of the Anycubic Kobra series.
