## 📝 Summary

The kubewarden/adm-controller is an open-source project that simplifies the management of admission policies in Kubernetes clusters, providing a more streamlined and efficient experience for DevOps and Security teams. This project helps automate the enforcement of security and compliance rules at the cluster's admission level, enhancing overall security and reducing administrative burdens. By leveraging the kubewarden/adm-controller, users can more easily manage and enforce complex admission policies in their Kubernetes environments.
