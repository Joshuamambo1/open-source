## 📝 Summary

NegPy is an open-source tool designed to process film negatives, providing a convenient and accessible solution for photographers and film enthusiasts. It offers a range of capabilities to enhance and restore images, helping users to achieve high-quality results from their film negatives. With NegPy, users can automate and streamline the processing of their negatives, saving time and effort.
