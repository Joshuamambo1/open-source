## 📝 Summary

Cypht is a lightweight, open-source webmail aggregator that simplifies email management across multiple accounts. It supports various protocols, including IMAP/SMTP, JMAP, and EWS (Exchange Web Services), enabling users to consolidate their emails into a single, user-friendly interface. This versatile tool is suitable for professionals, developers, and anyone seeking to streamline their email experience.
