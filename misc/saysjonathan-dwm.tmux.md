## 📝 Summary

The saysjonathan/dwm.tmux project brings a dwm-inspired tiling pane management system to tmux, allowing users to easily manage and arrange their terminal sessions with a familiar and efficient interface. This open-source project combines the strengths of dwm and tmux to create a seamless and customizable experience for power users. By leveraging the power of tmux and the simplicity of dwm, users can optimize their productivity and workflow.
