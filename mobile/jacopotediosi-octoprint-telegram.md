## 📝 Summary

This open-source project, OctoPrint-Telegram, is a plugin for controlling and monitoring 3D printers through the OctoPrint platform. It enables seamless integration with Telegram Messenger, allowing users to receive notification messages about their printer's status and control their printer remotely. This plugin provides users with a convenient and mobile-friendly way to stay connected to their printer on-the-go.
