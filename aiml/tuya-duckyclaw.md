## 📝 Summary

### English

DuckyClaw is an open‑source edge‑hardware framework designed for SoC and MCU platforms, providing a modular “claw” architecture that streamlines the deployment of AI/ML models directly on resource‑constrained devices. It offers ready‑to‑use drivers, inference pipelines, and integration tools that simplify building intelligent, low‑latency applications at the edge.
