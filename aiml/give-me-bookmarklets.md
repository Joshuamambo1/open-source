## 📝 Summary

The **give‑me/bookmarklets** project provides a lightweight bookmarklet that lets users export chatbot conversations—such as Google AI Mode, Claude, ChatGPT, Grok, and Gemini—to PDF or plain‑text with a single click. It streamlines saving AI interactions for later reference, documentation, or analysis, and is packaged as an open‑source developer tool for easy integration into any browser.
