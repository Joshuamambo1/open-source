## 📝 Summary

Drafft-ink is an open-source, self-hosted digital whiteboard that enables high-performance live collaboration across various platforms. Built using Rust and WebGPU, this tool offers a seamless user experience without any subscription fees. It is licensed under the AGPLv3 and is suitable for individuals and organizations seeking a flexible, zero-cost solution for collaborative whiteboarding.
