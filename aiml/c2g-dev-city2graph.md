## 📝 Summary

The city2graph project is an open-source initiative that enables the conversion of geospatial relationships into graph structures, facilitating their application in Graph Neural Networks (GNNs) and network analysis. This transformation empowers users to leverage the strengths of graph-based methods for spatial data analysis and machine learning tasks. By bridging the gap between geospatial and graph data, city2graph empowers researchers and developers to unlock new insights and applications in fields such as urban planning and geography.
