## 📝 Summary

Mbin is an open-source, decentralized platform that enables users to aggregate, share, and discuss content in a secure and community-driven environment. It combines elements of microblogging, voting, and discussion forums to foster a collaborative and open exchange of ideas. By leveraging a federated architecture, Mbin allows users to connect with others across different servers, promoting a more diverse and decentralized online community.
