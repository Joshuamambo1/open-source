## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the open-source project:

This open-source project, 'ojosama', is a DevTool that converts text into a elegant, refined style reminiscent of the character "Hundred Million Star Original Salome Miss" from the Japanese light novel series. It applies a distinct, aristocratic tone to input text. The result is a unique transformation that adds a touch of sophistication to any written content.
