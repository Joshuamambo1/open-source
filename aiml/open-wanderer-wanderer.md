## 📝 Summary

Wanderer is an open‑source, self‑hosted platform for storing and managing trail data, letting users log their hikes, routes, and outdoor adventures in a personal database. Built with modern AI/ML tools, it offers searchable, searchable trail metadata, maps, and analytics so explorers can easily record, share, and analyze their journeys.
