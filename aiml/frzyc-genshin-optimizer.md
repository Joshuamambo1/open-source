The genshin-optimizer project is an open-source tool designed to optimize Artifact sets for Genshin Impact and other gacha games, utilizing artificial intelligence and machine learning techniques. This project aims to provide users with the most effective Artifact combinations for their characters, enhancing gameplay experience and maximizing their in-game potential. By leveraging AI-driven algorithms, genshin-optimizer helps players make informed decisions and improve their overall performance in the game.
