## 📝 Summary

Misconfig Mapper is an open-source tool designed to quickly identify potential security misconfigurations in third-party services used by companies or bug bounty targets. It facilitates the discovery of vulnerabilities by systematically scanning and mapping misconfigured services, helping organizations to strengthen their security posture. By utilizing Misconfig Mapper, users can efficiently uncover and address potential security risks.
