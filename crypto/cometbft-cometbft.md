## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the CometBFT project:

CometBFT is an open-source, distributed Byzantine fault-tolerant state machine replication engine that ensures the integrity and reliability of networks. As a fork and successor to Tendermint Core, CometBFT provides a deterministic and secure foundation for blockchain and distributed systems applications. Developed with the needs of high-stakes systems in mind, CometBFT offers a robust and dependable solution for complex network architectures.
