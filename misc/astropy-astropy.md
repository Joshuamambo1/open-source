## 📝 Summary

Astropy is an open-source, community-developed library that serves as a foundation for astronomy and astrophysics research. It provides a comprehensive set of tools and routines for tasks such as data analysis, calibration, and visualization of astronomical data. By offering a unified and consistent interface, Astropy enables researchers to efficiently and accurately process and interpret large datasets from a wide range of astronomical sources.
