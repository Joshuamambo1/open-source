## 📝 Summary

The MCP-appstore project is an open-source server that enables Large Language Models (LLMs) to search and analyze applications from both the Google Play Store and Apple App Store. This tool is specifically designed to support App Store Optimization (ASO) efforts by providing LLMs with access to a wealth of app data. By leveraging this project, developers can improve their app's visibility and rankings in the app stores.
