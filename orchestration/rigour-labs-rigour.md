## 📝 Summary

The Rigour project is an open-source platform designed to safeguard AI coding agents from security threats and vulnerabilities, much like the human immune system protects the body. It provides an orchestration layer for monitoring, detecting, and responding to potential attacks or anomalies in AI systems. This innovative tool helps ensure the reliability, integrity, and trustworthiness of AI-powered applications and services.
