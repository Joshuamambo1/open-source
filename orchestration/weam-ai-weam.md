## 📝 Summary

Here's a 2-3 sentence summary of the weam project:

Weam is an open-source web application designed for teams of 20+ members, offering seamless connections to leading Large Language Models (LLMs) via APIs. The platform enables teams to share, organize, and deploy AI-powered workflows, agents, and apps through its modern, fully responsive stack. Weam's flexible architecture allows teams to customize their AI experiences or leverage pre-built solutions from its extensive library.
