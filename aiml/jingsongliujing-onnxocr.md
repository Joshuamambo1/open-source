## 📝 Summary

Jingsongliujing/OnnxOCR is a lightweight optical character recognition (OCR) solution that re‑implements PaddleOCR using the ONNX runtime, removing the dependency on the PaddlePaddle training framework. By converting the original models to ONNX, it delivers ultra‑fast inference while retaining high accuracy, making it well suited for real‑time or resource‑constrained applications.
