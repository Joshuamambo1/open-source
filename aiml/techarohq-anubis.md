## 📝 Summary

The Anubis project is an open-source solution designed to detect and prevent AI-powered web crawlers from overwhelming HTTP servers. It achieves this by analyzing incoming requests and weighing their "soul" to determine their legitimacy, thereby reducing the risk of Denial of Service (DoS) attacks. This initiative aims to enhance web security by leveraging AI to fight AI-driven threats.
