## 📝 Summary

The ai-workspace is an open-source project that enables AI agents to retain memory across various projects, providing a unified and persistent knowledge base for machine learning applications. This innovative workspace allows AI agents to learn from previous experiences and leverage previously acquired knowledge to inform decision-making in new contexts. By storing and retrieving contextual information, ai-workspace enhances the overall efficiency and effectiveness of AI models.
