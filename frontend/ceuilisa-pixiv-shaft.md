## 📝 Summary

CeuiLiSA/Pixiv-Shaft is an open-source, third-party Android client for Pixiv, a popular online platform for Japanese manga and artwork. This project allows users to access and browse Pixiv content on their mobile devices, providing an alternative to the official app. Developed by the community, CeuiLiSA/Pixiv-Shaft offers a customizable and user-friendly experience for Pixiv enthusiasts.
