## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary of the norrietaylor/distillery project:

Distillery is an open-source project that aims to capture and connect team knowledge, allowing it to evolve into a living graph that can be surfaced conversationally. It integrates with various feeds, tracks key project information, and sends alerts when necessary, effectively acting as a team brain that learns over time. This project leverages AI/ML and orchestration capabilities to streamline team collaboration and knowledge management.
