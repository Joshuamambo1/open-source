## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the project:

"Rectg is an open-source project that provides a curated navigation station for high-quality Chinese channels and groups on Telegram. With a database of over 1,000 nodes, it filters out spam groups, malicious content, and advertisements, offering a pure and reliable collection of Chinese resources on Telegram. This project aims to streamline navigation and discovery for users seeking authentic Chinese content on the platform."
