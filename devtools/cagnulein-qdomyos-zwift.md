## 📝 Summary

The cagnulein/qdomyos-zwift project provides a bridge for Zwift users to integrate their smart home fitness equipment, including treadmills and bikes/cyclettes, with the Zwift platform. This open-source project enables seamless connectivity and synchronization between the equipment and Zwift, enhancing the user experience for those who want to take their workouts to the next level. By utilizing this bridge, users can unlock new features and capabilities on Zwift.
