## 📝 Summary

The Splitting Conic Solver (SCS) is an open-source project that provides a high-performance solver for conic optimization problems. It uses a splitting method to efficiently solve large-scale problems with a wide range of applications, including signal processing, machine learning, and cryptography. Developed by the cvxgrp team, SCS is designed to be modular and flexible, making it suitable for a variety of use cases.
