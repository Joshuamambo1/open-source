## 📝 Summary

The aws/eks-anywhere project enables users to deploy and manage Amazon EKS (Elastic Container Service for Kubernetes) clusters on their own infrastructure, providing a flexible and on-premises alternative to cloud-based solutions. This open-source project allows organizations to maintain control over their IT infrastructure while still leveraging the scalability and reliability of EKS. By running EKS anywhere, users can optimize their workloads for performance and security.
