## 📝 Summary

HandBrake is an open-source video transcoder that allows users to convert video files from various formats into different sizes and formats for easier playback on various devices. This project enables users to easily rip and convert DVD, Blu-ray, and other video content to formats suitable for hand-held media players, mobile devices, and other digital platforms. It supports a wide range of input and output formats.
