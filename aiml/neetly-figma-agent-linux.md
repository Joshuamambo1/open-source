## 📝 Summary

The neetly/figma-agent-linux project is an open-source solution that enables seamless font syncing between your local system and Figma, allowing you to access your installed fonts directly within the design platform. This lightweight service streamlines the design process by eliminating the need to upload or manually manage fonts in Figma. As a result, designers can focus on their creative work without interruptions.
