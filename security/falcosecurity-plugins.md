## 📝 Summary

The falcosecurity/plugins project serves as a registry for Falco plugins, providing a centralized platform for users to discover, share, and manage custom security-related extensions for the Falco runtime. These plugins enhance the capabilities of Falco, enabling users to monitor and respond to security threats in a more effective and tailored manner. By facilitating community-driven plugin development, falcosecurity/plugins promotes collaboration and innovation in security monitoring.
