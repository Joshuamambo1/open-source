Mailpit is an open-source email and SMTP testing tool designed to facilitate developers in testing and validating their email sending functionality. This comprehensive tool comes equipped with a user-friendly API, allowing developers to effortlessly simulate email sending scenarios and troubleshoot issues with their email workflows. By leveraging Mailpit, developers can streamline their testing processes and ensure seamless email integration in their applications.
