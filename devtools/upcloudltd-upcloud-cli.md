UpCloudLtd/upcloud-cli is an open-source command line client, also known as 'upctl', designed to manage UpCloud services through a terminal interface. This tool allows users to easily automate and manage their cloud resources, including servers, storage, and more, using simple and intuitive commands. By leveraging the upctl client, users can streamline their cloud management tasks and improve overall efficiency.
