## 📝 Summary

Golembot is a versatile, open‑source framework that lets you plug any LLM (Claude, Codex, OpenCode, etc.) into any communication platform—Slack, Telegram, Discord, Feishu, DingTalk, WeCom, WeChat, and more. By abstracting both agents and providers, it enables developers to build and deploy custom AI assistants that work seamlessly across diverse messaging services.
