## 📝 Summary

max-ng/datamoat is an open‑source dev‑tool that automatically records every interaction you have with AI assistants such as Claude, Codex, Cursor, and OpenClaw, turning those sessions into a searchable, personal knowledge base. By capturing prompts, code snippets, outputs, and context, it lets you curate your own private data asset that can be leveraged for future projects, debugging, and learning. The project combines AI/ML techniques with a lightweight frontend to organize and retrieve this information efficiently.
