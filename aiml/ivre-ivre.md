## 📝 Summary

Ivre is an open-source network recon framework that empowers users to create self-hosted and fully-controlled alternatives to popular network scanning and intelligence services. Built on top of powerful tools such as Nmap, Masscan, and Zeek, Ivre enables users to collect, analyze, and visualize network intelligence from their sensors, facilitating advanced threat hunting and security operations. With Ivre, users can build custom EASM tools, passive DNS services, and more, enhancing their ability to monitor and secure their networks.
