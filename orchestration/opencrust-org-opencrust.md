## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary of the open-source project:

Opencrust is an open-source, multi-agent AI assistant platform written in Rust. It enables self-learning skills, real-world applications, and compatibility with RAG, MCP, and agentskills.io, allowing users to create personalized AI experiences. This platform empowers users to orchestrate and manage multiple AI agents to achieve complex tasks and improve overall efficiency.
