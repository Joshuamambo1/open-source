## 📝 Summary

The manim-slides project is an open-source tool designed to enhance live presentations by utilizing the Manim library. It enables users to create interactive slides with animations and visualizations, making presentations more engaging and effective. This tool is ideal for educators, researchers, and professionals seeking to convey complex ideas in a captivating and memorable manner.
