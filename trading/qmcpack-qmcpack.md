## 📝 Summary

QMCPACK is an open-source, production-level many-body ab initio Quantum Monte Carlo (QMC) code that computes the electronic structure of systems ranging from atoms and molecules to solids. This code is optimized for performance on high-performance computing architectures, including GPUs, and supports full portability across various systems. By leveraging QMC methods, QMCPACK provides a powerful tool for simulating complex electronic structures in materials science and condensed matter physics research.
