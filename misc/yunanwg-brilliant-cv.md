## 📝 Summary

The brilliant-CV project is an open-source, customizable resume template designed for job applications, powered by the Typst library and other tools. This template offers a unique and visually appealing layout for presenting your skills and experience in a professional manner. By utilizing Typst and other technologies, brilliant-CV provides a flexible and user-friendly solution for creating a compelling CV.
