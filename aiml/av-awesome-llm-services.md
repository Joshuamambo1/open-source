## 📝 Summary

The "awesome-llm-services" project is an open-source collection of self-hostable Large Language Model (LLM) services, providing users with a comprehensive list of options to deploy and manage their own LLM solutions. This project caters to developers and researchers looking to leverage the capabilities of LLMs in a decentralized and customizable manner. It serves as a valuable resource for those seeking to integrate LLMs into their applications and workflows.
