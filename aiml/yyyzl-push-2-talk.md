> PushToTalk 是一个高性能的桌面语音输入工具。它不仅仅是一个语音转文字工具，更集成了大语言模型（LLM）能力。你可以按住 Ctrl+Win 说话，松开后应用会自动将你的语音转为文字，并根据你的设定进行润色、翻译或整理成邮件，最后自动粘贴到当前光标位置。支持豆包/千问

## 📝 Summary

### English

PushToTalk is a high‑performance desktop voice‑input utility that transcribes spoken words into text and then leverages integrated large language models (LLM) to polish, translate, or format the output—e.g., turning it into an email draft. Activated by holding Ctrl + Win, the app automatically pastes the processed text at the current cursor location, and it supports Chinese LLM services such as Doubao and Qianwen. It is positioned as an AI‑enhanced speech‑to‑text tool for fast, context‑aware writing.
