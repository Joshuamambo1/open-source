## 📝 Summary

Wenyan-cli is a command-line tool that simplifies the process of formatting markdown content and publishing it to a WeChat public account. This open-source project is designed to streamline the workflow for users who frequently need to share content on WeChat. With wenyan-cli, users can easily convert and upload markdown files to their WeChat public account.
