## 📝 Summary

Voxy is an open-source, level of detail (LoD) rendering mod for Minecraft that enhances the game's performance and graphics by dynamically adjusting the level of detail in-game objects. This mod allows for smoother gameplay and improved visuals, especially in densely populated areas or complex environments. By implementing LoD rendering, Voxy aims to provide a more immersive and efficient Minecraft experience.
