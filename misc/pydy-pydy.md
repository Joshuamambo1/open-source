## 📝 Summary

The pydy/pydy project is an open-source, Python-based tool kit designed for simulating and analyzing multibody dynamics systems. This library provides a flexible and efficient framework for modeling complex mechanical systems, allowing users to easily define and solve equations of motion. By leveraging the pydy framework, researchers and engineers can efficiently simulate and optimize the behavior of multibody systems, facilitating the development of innovative solutions in fields such as robotics, mechanical engineering, and biomechanics.
