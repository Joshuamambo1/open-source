## 📝 Summary

Zenii is an open-source, Rust-powered AI brain for machines that integrates a comprehensive suite of tools, scripts, and cron jobs into a single, shared AI memory. With just a 20MB binary, developers can access 133 API routes across various interfaces, including desktop apps, command-line interfaces, and Telegram integrations. This multifaceted platform streamlines AI-powered workflows and enables seamless automation across different environments.
