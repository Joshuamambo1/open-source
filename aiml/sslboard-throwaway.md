## 📝 Summary

Throwaway is an open-source, disposable email checker and API that allows users to verify the existence of an email address without actually sending an email. This project utilizes AI/ML capabilities to streamline the email verification process, making it efficient and scalable. By providing a disposable email checker, Throwaway aims to simplify email validation and authentication for developers and users alike.
