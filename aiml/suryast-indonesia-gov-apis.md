## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary of the project:

The indonesia-gov-apis project provides a comprehensive collection of over 50 Indonesian government APIs and data sources, including BPS, OJK, BPJPH, BPOM, Bank Indonesia, and more. This open-source repository offers Python examples, scraping patterns, and practical tips to help developers navigate the Indonesian government's digital resources. It is designed to be a valuable resource for those working with Indonesian government data, but users are advised to rigorously test and verify the APIs due to their frequently changing nature.
