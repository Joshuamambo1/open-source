## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary of the project:

AK-Threads-booster is an open-source, data-driven writing advisor for Threads, utilizing historical posting data, algorithms, and social media psychology to assist in topic selection, content drafting, diagnosis, performance prediction, and post review. This tool leverages machine learning to provide users with informed decisions before publishing, ultimately enhancing their content creation experience. By harnessing the power of data, AK-Threads-booster aims to optimize users' writing strategies for better engagement and visibility.
