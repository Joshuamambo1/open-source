## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the MSD project:

MSD (Mass Storage Device) is an open-source Android app that enables the emulation of mass storage devices over USB, allowing users to access files and transfer data between devices. This project provides a flexible and innovative solution for Android developers and users who need to work with USB storage on their mobile devices. By utilizing MSD, users can easily access mass storage devices from their Android smartphones or tablets.
