## 📝 Summary

Lantern is a free and open-source Virtual Private Network (VPN) that prioritizes speed, privacy, and the ability to bypass censorship. Available across multiple platforms including Android, iOS, Windows, macOS, and Linux, Lantern provides users with a secure and unrestricted internet experience. By utilizing a peer-to-peer network, Lantern offers a decentralized approach to online security and access.
