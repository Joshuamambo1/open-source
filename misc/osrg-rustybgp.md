## 📝 Summary

RustyBGP is an open-source implementation of the Border Gateway Protocol (BGP) in Rust, designed to provide a flexible and efficient platform for exploring and experimenting with BGP-related concepts. This project aims to make BGP more accessible and easily understandable by providing a Rust-based toolkit for researchers and developers. By leveraging Rust's performance and safety features, RustyBGP enables the creation of robust and reliable BGP applications.
