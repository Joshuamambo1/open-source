## 📝 Summary

The ingero project is an open-source, eBPF-based agent that provides GPU causal observability, enabling deep insights into the complex relationships between AI/ML workloads and their underlying infrastructure. By leveraging eBPF, ingero provides a lightweight and efficient way to monitor and troubleshoot GPU-related issues, ultimately improving the performance and reliability of AI/ML systems. This agent is designed to help DevOps teams and AI/ML engineers identify and resolve bottlenecks in their GPU-based workloads.
