## 📝 Summary

Alexandria-Audiobook is an open-source project that utilizes AI technology to generate high-quality, multi-voice audiobooks. This tool, powered by Qwen3-TTS, offers advanced features such as voice cloning, style control, and export options to various audio formats. It is designed to streamline the audiobook creation process, making it accessible to creators and enthusiasts alike.
