## 📝 Summary

PrismLauncher is a free, open-source launcher for Minecraft that simplifies the management of multiple game installations. Built as a fork of the popular MultiMC project, it provides users with a user-friendly interface for organizing and launching their Minecraft setups. With PrismLauncher, gamers can easily switch between different Minecraft versions and configurations.
