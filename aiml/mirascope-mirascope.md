## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the Mirascope project:

Mirascope is an open-source, anti-framework for Large Language Models (LLMs) that challenges traditional approaches to LLM development. By providing a framework that intentionally subverts conventional expectations, Mirascope allows developers to explore new ways of working with LLMs and pushes the boundaries of what is possible in the field of AI/ML. This experimental project offers a unique opportunity for innovators to rethink their approach to LLM development and creation.
