## 📝 Summary

### English

opengeos/aws-open-data-geo is an open‑source curated collection of publicly available geospatial datasets hosted on Amazon Web Services. It provides easy-to‑browse links, metadata, and usage notes for satellite imagery, terrain models, climate records, and other spatial data, helping AI/ML and data professionals quickly discover and integrate high‑quality geodata into their workflows. The repository is continuously updated and encourages community contributions to expand the catalog.
