## 📝 Summary

VLMEvalKit is an open-source evaluation toolkit designed to assess the performance of large multi-modality models (LMMs) across various applications. This comprehensive tool offers support for over 220 LMMs and 80+ benchmarks, providing developers with a unified platform to evaluate and compare the capabilities of these complex models. By leveraging VLMEvalKit, researchers and practitioners can streamline their evaluation workflows and make more informed decisions when developing and deploying LMMs.
