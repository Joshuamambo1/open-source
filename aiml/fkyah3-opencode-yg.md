## 📝 Summary

The open-source project "opencode-yg" is a research fork of opencode that focuses on Language Anchoring, a technique to train Large Language Models (LLMs) to consistently think in a specific language. This project has achieved a high level of success in implementing Language Anchoring, with over 95% compliance in Chinese language thinking. By leveraging this technology, developers can create more effective LLMs that accurately reflect and generate language-specific content.
