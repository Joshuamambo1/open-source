## 📝 Summary

The Muzza project is an open-source, Material 3-themed YouTube Music client designed specifically for Android devices. This project offers a customizable and feature-rich alternative to the official YouTube Music app, providing users with a unique and visually appealing experience. By utilizing the Material 3 design language, Muzza aims to deliver a seamless and user-friendly interface for music enthusiasts.
