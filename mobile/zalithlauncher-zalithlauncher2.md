## 📝 Summary

ZalithLauncher and ZalithLauncher2 are open-source, Android-based Minecraft Java Edition launchers that enable players to access and play Minecraft on their mobile devices. These launchers provide an alternative to the official Minecraft launcher and offer features and customization options tailored for mobile players. They are free to use and modify, making them a great option for Minecraft enthusiasts who want to explore their mobile gaming experience.
