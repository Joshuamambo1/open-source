## 📝 Summary

Safeclaw is an open-source alternative to OpenClaw, offering a conversational AI experience through both text and voice interfaces. By default, it leverages natural language understanding to pick up on intent and semantics, providing a robust and secure interaction without the need for language models or prompt injection. This project offers a comprehensive solution with features such as text-to-speech (TTS) and voice-to-text functionality.
