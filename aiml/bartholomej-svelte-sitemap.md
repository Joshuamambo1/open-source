## 📝 Summary

Bartholomej’s **svelte‑sitemap** is a lightweight utility for SvelteKit projects that automatically scans your file‑based routes and produces a static `sitemap.xml` for SEO and indexing. By integrating it into your build process, you get an up‑to‑date sitemap without manual maintenance, making it a handy dev‑tool for SvelteKit applications.
