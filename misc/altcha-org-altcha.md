## 📝 Summary

This project offers a self-hosted, open-source alternative to Google reCAPTCHA, allowing users to manage their own CAPTCHA services. It aims to provide a secure and customizable solution for website owners seeking to protect their platforms from spam and abuse without relying on external services. By hosting it locally, users can maintain control over their user data and reduce their dependence on third-party providers.
