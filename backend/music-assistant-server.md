## 📝 Summary

Music Assistant is a free, open-source media library manager that seamlessly integrates with popular streaming services and a variety of connected speakers. This project's server component, a core component, runs on a dedicated device such as a Raspberry Pi or NAS to provide a stable and always-on connection. By managing your media library and controlling connected devices, Music Assistant simplifies music playback and discovery.
