## 📝 Summary

This open-source project discusses the limitations of retrieval-augmented generation, a technique used in natural language processing to improve text generation by incorporating relevant external information. The project investigates why these models often fail to accurately capture the intended meaning of the context, leading to incorrect or misleading outputs. By exploring the challenges of retrieval-augmented generation, this project aims to advance our understanding of language models and their potential applications.
