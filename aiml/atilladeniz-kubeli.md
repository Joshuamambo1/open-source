It offers a comprehensive set of features, including multi-cluster support, real-time monitoring, an AI-powered assistant, and terminal access, making it an ideal tool for Kubernetes administrators and developers. With Kubeli, users can efficiently manage and monitor their Kubernetes clusters from a user-friendly interface.
