## 📝 Summary

nucleus-ffm/foss_warn is an unofficial, open‑source mobile application that aggregates and displays emergency alerts from official warning systems, providing users with timely notifications about natural disasters, severe weather, and other public safety events. Designed for community use, the app offers a lightweight, privacy‑focused alternative to proprietary alert services, supporting multiple regions and customizable alert preferences.
