## 📝 Summary

Tapmap is an open-source project that visualizes a live world map of your computer's connections, providing real-time insight into network interactions. It stands out as a valuable tool for DevOps and security professionals, offering a unique perspective on system connectivity and potential security vulnerabilities. With Tapmap, users can gain a deeper understanding of their computer's network behavior.
