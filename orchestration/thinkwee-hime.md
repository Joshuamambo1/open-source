## 📝 Summary

The HiMe project, developed by thinkwee, is an open-source personal health AI agent that aims to streamline users' health management through a unified, AI-driven platform. This one-stop solution enables users to track and analyze their health data, receive personalized recommendations, and make informed decisions about their well-being. By leveraging AI and machine learning capabilities, HiMe strives to empower individuals to take control of their health and achieve a healthier lifestyle.
