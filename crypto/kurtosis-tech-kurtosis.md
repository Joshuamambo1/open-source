## 📝 Summary

Kurtosis is an open-source platform designed to streamline the deployment of blockchain infrastructure. It aims to simplify the process of packaging and launching blockchain environments, much like Docker Compose, but specifically tailored for blockchain applications. This platform facilitates the development and testing of blockchain projects by providing a standardized and efficient way to set up and manage complex blockchain ecosystems.
