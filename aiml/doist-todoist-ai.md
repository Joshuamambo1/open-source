## 📝 Summary

The Doist/todoist-ai project is an open-source initiative that enables integration with AI agents to automate tasks on Todoist, a popular task management platform. This toolset provides support for MCP (Multi-Core Processing), allowing for efficient and multi-threaded interactions with AI agents. By leveraging AI capabilities, users can streamline their Todoist experience and optimize their productivity.
