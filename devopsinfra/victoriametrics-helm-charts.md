## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the VictoriaMetrics/helm-charts project:

VictoriaMetrics/helm-charts is an open-source collection of Helm charts that simplify the deployment of VictoriaMetrics and its ecosystem on Kubernetes. These charts provide a standardized and efficient way to manage and monitor VictoriaMetrics, as well as its companion logging solution VictoriaLogs, in a Kubernetes environment. By using these charts, users can quickly and easily integrate VictoriaMetrics into their DevOps workflows.
