## 📝 Summary

The "hands-on-modern-rl" project is an open-source curriculum designed to bridge the gap between basic Reinforcement Learning (RL) concepts and more advanced topics such as Large Language Model (LLM) alignment, Reinforcement Learning with Value-based Reward (RLVR), and Agentic systems. This hands-on approach allows learners to gain practical experience and in-depth knowledge of RL and its applications. By exploring these topics, learners can develop a comprehensive understanding of modern RL and its role in AI/ML research.
