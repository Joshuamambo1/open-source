## 📝 Summary

The Voice-Recorder project is an open-source mobile application that provides a simple and ad-free method for recording discussions or sounds without requiring internet access. This user-friendly tool is designed to facilitate smooth audio capture and management, making it ideal for various applications such as marketing, interviews, or personal note-taking. With its offline functionality, users can freely record and save their audio files without worrying about internet connectivity or data limitations.
