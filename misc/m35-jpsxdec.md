## 📝 Summary

jPSXdec is an open‑source, cross‑platform utility that extracts and converts audio and video assets from PlayStation 1 game discs into modern formats. It supports a wide range of PS‑1 codecs, providing high‑fidelity decoding for both music tracks and full‑motion video, and runs on Windows, macOS, and Linux. The project is licensed under an open‑source license and aims to help preservationists, developers, and hobbyists work with legacy PlayStation media.
