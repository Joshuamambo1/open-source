## 📝 Summary

Here is a brief summary for the Kunena Forum project:

Kunena Forum is an open-source, Joomla component that enables users to create a powerful and customizable bulletin board for discussions and forums. This project is specifically designed for Joomla 6.x and 5.x versions and offers a flexible platform for users to engage in online conversations. With Kunena, users can create, manage, and moderate discussions, fostering a rich and interactive community experience.
