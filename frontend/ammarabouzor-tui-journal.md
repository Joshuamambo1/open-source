## 📝 Summary

Tui-Journal is a user-friendly, open-source journaling application designed for the terminal. This project provides a simple and intuitive interface for users to record and manage their thoughts, ideas, and daily reflections in a text-based environment. With Tui-Journal, users can create, edit, and organize their journal entries from the comfort of their terminal.
