## 📝 Summary

znapzend is an open-source project that provides a robust and scalable solution for ZFS backups with remote capabilities and mbuffer integration. It enables users to automate and manage backups of their ZFS datasets across multiple locations, ensuring data integrity and redundancy. With its ability to efficiently transfer and store data, znapzend helps users protect their critical data against loss and corruption.
