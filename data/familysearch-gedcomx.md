## 📝 Summary

FamilySearch/gedcomx is an open-source project that aims to standardize genealogical data exchange by providing a universal data model and serialization format. This initiative allows for seamless interoperability between various genealogical tools, platforms, and repositories. By promoting open data exchange, gedcomx facilitates the sharing and collaboration of family history information across the globe.
