## 📝 Summary

Evalmonkey is an open-source command-line interface (CLI) designed to help developers evaluate and stress-test their artificial intelligence (AI) agents. This tool enables coding agents to benchmark and simulate chaos testing scenarios, providing valuable insights into AI performance and resilience. By utilizing evalmonkey, developers can refine and optimize their AI solutions for real-world applications.
