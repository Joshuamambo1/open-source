## 📝 Summary

OTRv4 is an open-source, post-quantum IRC client designed for secure and private communication. This innovative project enables users to connect via I2P ( Invisible Internet Project), Tor, and TLS, providing a high level of anonymity and encryption on a phone. By utilizing advanced cryptographic techniques, OTRv4 aims to safeguard users' conversations from potential quantum computer threats.
