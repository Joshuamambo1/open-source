## 📝 Summary

Serial-Studio is an open-source, versatile telemetry dashboard that provides real-time monitoring and data visualization capabilities for various communication protocols, including UART, BLE, MQTT, Modbus, and CAN Bus. This frontend-focused tool allows users to connect to a wide range of devices and systems, making it an ideal solution for IoT applications and industrial monitoring. By leveraging its open-source nature, users can customize and extend its functionality to suit their specific needs.
