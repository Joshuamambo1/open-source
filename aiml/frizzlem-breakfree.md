## 📝 Summary

### English

FrizzleM/BreakFree is an open‑source iOS shortcut that enables users to sideload an unlimited number of apps on iPhone and iPad devices. It works by routing traffic through custom DNS servers that block Apple’s app‑verification checks and by employing publicly available certificates to bypass the App Store restrictions. The project targets mobile users and leverages networking tricks rather than AI/ML techniques.
