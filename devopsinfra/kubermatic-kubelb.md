## 📝 Summary

Kubelb is an open-source project that leverages Kubernetes to provide a scalable and highly available load balancing solution. It enables users to manage and configure load balancers within their Kubernetes clusters, simplifying the deployment and management of network resources. By integrating load balancing with Kubernetes, Kubelb facilitates efficient traffic management and improves application reliability.
