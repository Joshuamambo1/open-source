## 📝 Summary

Project64 is an open-source, free Nintendo 64 (N64) emulator designed to provide an accurate and reliable gaming experience. This project aims to emulate the N64 hardware, allowing users to play their favorite N64 games on a computer. With a growing community and continuous development, Project64 strives to become the most feature-rich and stable N64 emulator available.
