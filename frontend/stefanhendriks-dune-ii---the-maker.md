## 📝 Summary

A modern open‑source remake of Westwood Studios’ classic real‑time strategy game **Dune II – The Building of a Dynasty**, rebuilt with enhanced features such as high‑resolution graphics, zoom support, unit multiselection, and skirmish multiplayer modes. The project modernizes the original gameplay while preserving its iconic feel, making it accessible on both desktop and mobile platforms.
