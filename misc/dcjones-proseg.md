## 📝 Summary

The proseg project is an open-source tool that utilizes probabilistic methods for cell segmentation in in situ spatial transcriptomics. This tool enables researchers to accurately identify and separate cells based on their gene expression profiles, providing valuable insights into tissue structure and function. By applying probabilistic models to spatial transcriptomics data, proseg facilitates the analysis of complex biological systems at the single-cell level.
