## 📝 Summary

The TMA1 project is an open-source, local-first observability platform designed for AI agents. It provides a built-in dashboard to monitor and analyze key metrics, including cost, sessions, anomalies, and conversation replay, all on your local machine. This allows for seamless and secure AI development and testing without relying on cloud services.
