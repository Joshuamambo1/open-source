## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary of the project:

Salesforce Inspector Reloaded is an open-source Chrome extension designed to enhance the Salesforce user experience by adding a metadata layout on top of the standard Salesforce UI. This extension aims to boost productivity and joy for Salesforce developers, configurators, and integrators by providing a more efficient and organized interface. By leveraging its metadata layout, users can streamline their workflow and optimize their Salesforce experience.
