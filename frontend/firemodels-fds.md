## 📝 Summary

The firemodels/fds project is an open-source implementation of the Fire Dynamics Simulator (FDS), a computational tool used to model and analyze fire behaviors in various environments. This software allows users to simulate fire scenarios, predict fire spread, and analyze the effects of fire on structures and occupants. By providing a free and customizable alternative, the project aims to advance fire safety research and education.
