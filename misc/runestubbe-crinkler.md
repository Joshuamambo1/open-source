### English

Crinkler is a specialized compressing linker that dramatically reduces the size of 32‑bit Windows executables, making it ideal for the demoscene’s ultra‑small 1 KB, 4 KB, and 8 KB intros. By performing aggressive code‑size optimizations and on‑the‑fly compression, it produces highly compact binaries while preserving full functionality. As of 2026 it remains the de‑facto standard tool for creating the smallest possible Windows demos.
