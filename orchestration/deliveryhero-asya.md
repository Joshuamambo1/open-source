## 📝 Summary

The Asya project is an open-source, distributed actor mesh designed for Kubernetes (K8s) environments to support the deployment and orchestration of AI agents. This framework enables the creation of scalable and fault-tolerant AI systems by providing a robust architecture for actor-based AI agent interaction. By leveraging Asya, developers can streamline the development and deployment of AI applications on Kubernetes.
