## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary:

AutoTeam is an open-source project that automates account rotation management and Codex credit monitoring for ChatGPT Team accounts. It features automatic number swapping, email registration, and synchronization of CPA/Sub2API certifications. This tool aims to streamline and simplify the process of managing multiple accounts for ChatGPT Team administrators.
