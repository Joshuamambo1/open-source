### English

Markifact‑MCP is an open‑source backend server that centralizes ad‑campaign management across major platforms—including Google Ads, Meta Ads, GA4, TikTok Ads, and LinkedIn Ads—while exposing a simple API for AI assistants such as Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini, Cursor, or any custom AI client.
