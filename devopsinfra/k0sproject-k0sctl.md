k0sctl is an open-source tool designed to simplify the bootstrapping and management of k0s clusters. It provides a convenient interface for creating and configuring k0s clusters, allowing users to easily deploy and manage their Kubernetes environments. With k0sctl, developers and DevOps teams can streamline their cluster setup and management processes.
