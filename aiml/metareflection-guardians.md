## 📝 Summary

Guardians is an open-source project that utilizes static verification to ensure the reliability and integrity of AI agent workflows. By employing static verification, Guardians helps identify and mitigate potential errors or vulnerabilities in AI systems, thereby enhancing their trustworthiness and overall performance. This project aims to contribute to the advancement of AI development by promoting more secure and robust AI agent workflows.
