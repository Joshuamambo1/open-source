## 📝 Summary

The Caracal project is an open-source initiative that enables pre-execution authority enforcement for AI agents, allowing for delegated actions to be controlled and revoked in real-time. This system also provides immutable proof, ensuring the integrity and transparency of AI decision-making processes. By implementing Caracal, developers can enhance the security and accountability of their AI-powered applications.
