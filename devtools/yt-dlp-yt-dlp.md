## 📝 Summary

yt-dlp is an open-source command-line tool for downloading audio and video content from various platforms, offering an array of features and flexibility. This powerful downloader supports multiple video streaming services and allows users to customize their downloads with ease. It serves as an alternative to the popular YouTube-DL project.
