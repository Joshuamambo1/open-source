## 📝 Summary

This project is an open-source, unofficial reimplementation of Sid Meier's Alpha Centauri and its expansion, Alien Crossfire. It utilizes OpenGL and SDL2 to recreate the classic game's graphics and gameplay, offering a modern alternative to the original. This reimplementation aims to preserve the original game's essence while providing a potentially more stable and customizable experience.
