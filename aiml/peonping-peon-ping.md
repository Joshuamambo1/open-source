## 📝 Summary

The PeonPing project is an open-source initiative that aims to provide voice notifications inspired by Warcraft III's Peon voices, enabling users to stay informed about their AI agents, such as Claude, Codex, and other IDEs, without constantly monitoring their terminal. This project seeks to alleviate the need for constant terminal checking, allowing users to focus on more critical tasks. By employing a Peon, users can receive timely and engaging audio updates.
