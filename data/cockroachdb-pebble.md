## 📝 Summary

Pebble is an open-source, RocksDB/LevelDB inspired key-value database written in Go. It provides a high-performance and reliable storage engine for storing and retrieving data. Developed as a lightweight alternative to RocksDB, Pebble is suitable for use in a wide range of applications.
