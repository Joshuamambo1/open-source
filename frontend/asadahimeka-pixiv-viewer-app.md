## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary of the open-source project:

The asadahimeka/pixiv-viewer-app is an open-source, third-party app that allows users to browse pixiv's illustrations, animations, comics, and novels without needing to log in. This app supports multiple layouts, proxy-free direct connections, customizable APIs and image hosting, and various login methods including RefreshToken, OAuth, and Cookie-based authentication.
