## 📝 Summary

This open-source project involves porting a custom-built 500M large language model (LLM) training pipeline to utilize AMD's ROCm (Radeon Open Compute) platform on the Strix Halo system. The goal is to optimize performance and efficiency for this complex AI/ML workload. By leveraging ROCm, the project aims to unlock the full potential of the Strix Halo hardware for large-scale LLM training.
