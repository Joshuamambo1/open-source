## 📝 Summary

### English

evcc is an open‑source software platform that integrates electric vehicle (EV) charging with solar power generation, allowing users to intelligently manage and optimize charging based on real‑time photovoltaic output and grid conditions. It supports a wide range of chargers, inverters, and energy meters, providing a customizable web UI, API, and automation features for home energy management. The project aims to maximize the use of renewable energy for EVs while reducing reliance on the grid.
