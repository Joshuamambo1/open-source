## 📝 Summary

VGMTrans is an open-source tool that facilitates the conversion of proprietary videogame music into widely accepted formats, allowing for easier playback and manipulation. This conversion utility is specifically designed for sequenced videogame music, providing a means to liberate and share game soundtracks with a broader audience. By promoting music accessibility, VGMTrans supports the preservation and appreciation of classic videogame soundtracks.
