## 📝 Summary

WorkProof aims to standardize the representation of skill evidence graphs through a JSON schema, enabling efficient and interoperable exchange of information. This open-source project seeks to provide a structured format for documenting and verifying skills, expertise, and qualifications. Its goal is to facilitate seamless integration and validation of skill evidence across various platforms and applications.
