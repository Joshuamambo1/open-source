## 📝 Summary

Friday Studio is an open‑source runtime for AI agents that provides shareable workspaces equipped with a modular component platform (MCP), reusable skills, persistent memory, and built‑in cron/webhook automation. Designed for self‑hosting, it lets developers orchestrate and extend AI agents transparently while integrating with other tools and services.
