## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the mensfeld/pocketrb project:

Pocketrb is an open-source, multi-LLM (Large Language Model) capable Ruby framework that enables the creation of pocket-sized AI agents. This project provides an orchestration tool for leveraging the power of various LLMs, facilitating the development of efficient and versatile AI assistants. By supporting multiple LLMs, pocketrb allows users to experiment with different models and find the best fit for their specific needs.
