## 📝 Summary

The OnePlus_KernelSU_SUSFS project offers open-source OnePlus kernels that have been enhanced with KernelSU and SUSFS, two features that provide kernel-level customizations and performance optimizations. With these kernels, users can unlock additional functionality and improve system performance on their OnePlus devices. This project aims to provide a community-driven platform for OnePlus kernel enthusiasts to explore and customize their devices.
