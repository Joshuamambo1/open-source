## 📝 Summary

SkillsCat is an open‑source platform that lets developers discover, share, and install reusable AI agent “skills”—modular capabilities that can be orchestrated to build more complex intelligent systems. By providing a searchable catalog, versioned packages, and simple installation workflows, SkillsCat streamlines the reuse and composition of AI/ML functionalities across projects.
