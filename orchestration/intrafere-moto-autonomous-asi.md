## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary of the Intrafere/MOTO-Autonomous-ASI project:

MOTO is an open-source, autonomous research harness for S.T.E.M. users that enables continuous proof generation with minimal interaction required. Utilizing simultaneous agents working in parallel from local hosts or OpenRouter, MOTO can run for extended periods without internet connectivity. This innovative tool is built with Lean 4 and is designed for users seeking to optimize their research workflows with AI/ML orchestration.
