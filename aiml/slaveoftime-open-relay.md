## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the open-source project:

This open-source project allows users to keep their processes alive even after closing the terminal, receive notifications when input is required, and easily resume their sessions from anywhere. By providing a reliable and flexible workflow management system, open-relay streamlines collaborative work between humans and AI agents."
