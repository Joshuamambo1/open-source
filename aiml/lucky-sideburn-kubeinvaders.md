Kubeinvaders is an open-source, gamified Chaos Engineering tool designed for Kubernetes environments. By simulating failures and disruptions, users can proactively test their applications' resilience and identify potential weaknesses. This tool empowers developers and DevOps teams to improve system reliability and robustness through a fun and engaging approach.
