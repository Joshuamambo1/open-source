WolvenKit is an open-source, community-driven editor and creator for REDengine games, allowing users to create and customize mods for these titles. This versatile tool empowers fans to breathe new life into their favorite games by modifying assets, scripts, and other game components. With WolvenKit, users can unleash their creativity and share their custom content with the gaming community.
