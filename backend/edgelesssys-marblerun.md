## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the MarbleRun project:

MarbleRun is an open-source control plane for confidential computing, designed to deploy, scale, and verify confidential microservices on vanilla Kubernetes. Built entirely in Go and optimized for cloud-native environments, MarbleRun provides a secure foundation for confidential computing applications. By leveraging Kubernetes, developers can easily manage and orchestrate their confidential workloads.
