## 📝 Summary

Backrest is an open-source, web-based user interface (UI) designed to streamline restic backups. It serves as an orchestrator, offering a user-friendly interface to manage and monitor restic backups, making it easier to ensure data protection and recovery. By leveraging Backrest, users can simplify their backup processes and maintain a secure, centralized backup environment.
