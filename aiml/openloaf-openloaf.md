## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary of the OpenLoaf project:

OpenLoaf is an open-source, local-first AI workspace that enables users to work with their data on their device, without it ever leaving the local machine. This AI workspace features a range of powerful tools, including multi-model chat interfaces, AI image and video generation, document management, and email and calendar integration. With its local-first approach, users can maintain complete control and security over their data.
