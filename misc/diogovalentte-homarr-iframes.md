## 📝 Summary

The open-source project "diogovalentte/homarr-iframes" provides a collection of pre-configured iFrames for various self-hosted services, offering a convenient way to easily integrate these services into any dashboard. This project caters to users who want to utilize these services without the need for extensive coding or setup. By utilizing homarr-iframes, users can quickly and efficiently enhance their dashboards with a range of self-hosted tools.
