## 📝 Summary

Microsoft's open-source Component Detection project is a tool designed to scan and analyze a project's dependencies, identifying the components and libraries being used. This helps developers to maintain transparency, ensure compatibility, and make informed decisions about their project's dependencies. By utilizing Component Detection, developers can streamline their workflow and improve the overall reliability of their applications.
