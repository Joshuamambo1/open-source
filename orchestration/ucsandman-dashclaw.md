## 📝 Summary

DashClaw is an open-source decision infrastructure designed for AI agents, providing an intercept system for actions, enforcing guard policies, and managing approvals to ensure transparent decision-making processes. This project enables the creation of audit-ready decision trails, enhancing accountability and trust in AI-driven decisions. By integrating with AI/ML systems, DashClaw facilitates more informed and controlled decision-making processes.
