## 📝 Summary

Lindera is an open-source, multilingual morphological analysis library that provides a comprehensive tool for analyzing and processing the structure of words in various languages. This library enables developers to break down words into their fundamental components, such as roots, prefixes, and suffixes, facilitating tasks like text processing, language modeling, and natural language understanding. With its multilingual capabilities, Lindera offers a versatile solution for developers working with languages beyond English.
