## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the katanemo/plano project:

Plano is an open-source, AI-native proxy and data plane that streamlines the development of agentic apps by providing built-in orchestration, safety features, and observability. It also offers smart Large Language Model (LLM) routing, allowing developers to focus on the core logic of their agents. By leveraging Plano, developers can create more efficient and maintainable AI-powered applications.
