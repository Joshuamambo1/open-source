This translates into lower engineering overhead, fewer bugs, and easier onboarding for new team members.

Before using it in production, perform due‑diligence on licensing, long‑term maintenance, and release cadence, and consider adding internal health checks and version‑pinning to mitigate the risk of unmaintained dependencies.
