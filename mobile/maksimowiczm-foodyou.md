## 📝 Summary

FoodYou is a free, open-source, and privacy-focused mobile application designed to track your daily food intake and nutrition. This personal food diary allows users to easily log meals, monitor their diet, and make informed decisions about their health. By prioritizing user privacy and security, FoodYou provides a secure platform for individuals to manage their nutrition and wellness goals.
