## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the veighna-global/vnpy_binance project:

The veighna-global/vnpy_binance project is an open-source Binance trading gateway for VeighNa Evo, designed to provide a seamless integration with the Binance cryptocurrency exchange. This gateway allows users to access and automate trading on Binance using VeighNa Evo's platform, empowering users to streamline their trading workflows and make data-driven decisions. By leveraging this gateway, users can tap into the full potential of Binance's trading capabilities within VeighNa Evo.
