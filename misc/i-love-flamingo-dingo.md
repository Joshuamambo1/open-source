## 📝 Summary

The dingo project is an open-source, Go-based dependency injection framework designed to simplify the management of dependencies within Go applications. It enables developers to decouple components and promote loose coupling, making their codebases more modular, maintainable, and scalable. By leveraging dingo, developers can focus on writing more efficient and effective code.
