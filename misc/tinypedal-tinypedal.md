## 📝 Summary

### English

TinyPedal is a free, open‑source telemetry overlay that plugs into popular racing simulators to display real‑time vehicle data, lap times, and performance metrics directly on the screen. Designed for simplicity and extensibility, it runs on Windows and offers customizable widgets, logging, and support for multiple data sources, making it a lightweight yet powerful tool for sim racers and developers alike.
