## 📝 Summary

Lychee is an open-source, server-based photo management system that offers an intuitive and visually appealing interface for organizing and sharing photos. This user-friendly platform enables users to manage their digital library with ease, making it an ideal solution for personal and professional photo collections. By hosting it on a server, users can access their photos from anywhere and share them with others securely.
