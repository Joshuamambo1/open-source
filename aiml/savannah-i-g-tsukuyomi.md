## 📝 Summary

TSUKUYOMI is an open-source project that investigates how specific language and weight combinations can influence Large Language Model (LLM) behavior without retraining. This early experiment, developed by savannah-i-g, utilizes pseudo methods to test the effects of jargon and artificial weights on LLM performance. The project's findings, although outdated, offer insights into LLM behavior and the potential for future research in this area.
