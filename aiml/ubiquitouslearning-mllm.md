## 📝 Summary

The UbiquitousLearning/mllm project is an open-source initiative that aims to bring fast and efficient multimodal large language models to mobile devices. By leveraging advanced AI/ML and frontend technologies, this project seeks to bridge the gap between cutting-edge language processing capabilities and the mobility of modern devices. This innovation has the potential to enable seamless and ubiquitous access to sophisticated language models.
