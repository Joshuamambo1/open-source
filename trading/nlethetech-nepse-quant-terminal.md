## 📝 Summary

The nepse-quant-terminal is an open-source, terminal-based trading dashboard that enables users to analyze and trade on the Nepal Stock Exchange (NEPSE) with advanced features such as paper trading, backtesting, and AI agent support. This project is designed to provide a comprehensive and user-friendly interface for traders to make informed investment decisions. By leveraging AI and machine learning capabilities, nepse-quant-terminal aims to enhance trading efficiency and accuracy.
