## 📝 Summary

OpenAL Soft is an open-source implementation of the OpenAL 3D audio API, providing a software-based solution for advanced audio processing and spatialization. This project allows developers to leverage the capabilities of OpenAL in a variety of applications, from games to multimedia software. By offering a cross-platform and highly customizable backend, OpenAL Soft enables efficient and immersive audio experiences.
