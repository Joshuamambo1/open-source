## 📝 Summary

Charon is an open-source, Proof of Stake Ethereum Distributed Validator Middleware Client. It is designed to facilitate secure and efficient interactions between Ethereum validators and the network, enabling a more scalable and decentralized Ethereum ecosystem. By utilizing a middleware approach, Charon aims to simplify the validation process and promote the adoption of staking and distributed validation.
