## 📝 Summary

SoulFire is an open-source, advanced Minecraft bot tool designed for server testing, automation, and development. It allows users to deploy automated bots with ease, streamlining the process of testing and optimizing their Minecraft servers. With SoulFire, developers can focus on creating and refining their server experiences without the manual labor of bot deployment.
