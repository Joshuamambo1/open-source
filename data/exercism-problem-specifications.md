## 📝 Summary

The **exercism/problem-specifications** repository houses the canonical metadata that defines every Exercism exercise, including its description, test suite, and configuration details. By maintaining a single source of truth for exercise specifications, the project enables consistent, language‑agnostic implementations across the entire Exercism platform and supports community contributions to keep the exercises up to date.
