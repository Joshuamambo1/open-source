## 📝 Summary

Balance of Satoshis is an open-source, frontend tool designed to help users manage and track their satoshis on the Lightning Network Daemon (LND). It provides a user-friendly interface for interacting with one's satoshi balance, allowing for streamlined management of their funds. This tool is ideal for those using LND and seeking a simple, intuitive way to monitor and balance their satoshis.
