## 📝 Summary

Developers.events is an open-source, community-driven platform that provides a comprehensive listing of developer and tech conferences worldwide. The platform offers a list, calendar, and map view to help organizers, speakers, sponsors, and attendees stay informed about upcoming events and Calls for Papers (CFPs). Its goal is to foster a connected community of developers and tech enthusiasts globally.
