## 📝 Summary

This open-source project offers a customizable GRUB theme inspired by the classic Minecraft singleplayer world selection screen, providing a unique visual experience for users. The theme can be easily applied to GRUB environments, adding a touch of nostalgia and Minecraft flair to the system boot process. It's a free and community-driven project, available for anyone to use and modify.
