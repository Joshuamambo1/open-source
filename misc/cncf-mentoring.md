## 📝 Summary

The cncf/mentoring project is an open-source initiative that combines the efforts of the Cloud Native Computing Foundation (CNCF), CommunityBridge, and the Google Summer of Code to provide mentorship opportunities. This collaborative platform aims to foster community growth and knowledge sharing by pairing individuals with experienced mentors in various areas of cloud native technology. Through this project, participants can gain valuable skills and contribute to the development of open-source projects.
