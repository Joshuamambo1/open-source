## 📝 Summary

The N64RecompLauncher is an open-source project that streamlines the process of accessing N64 recompiled games. This launcher simplifies the download and update process for users, providing them with easy access to a vast library of recompiled N64 titles. By automating the process, the launcher enhances the overall gaming experience for enthusiasts of the classic Nintendo 64 console.
