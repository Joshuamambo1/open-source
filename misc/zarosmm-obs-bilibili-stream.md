## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the open-source project:

The obs-bilibili-stream plugin for OBS Studio simplifies the live streaming process on Bilibili by automating login, updating live room information, and retrieving RTMP streaming addresses and push codes. With this plugin, users can streamline their live streaming workflow on the popular Chinese video-sharing platform. By providing a seamless integration, the plugin aims to enhance the overall user experience for Bilibili live streamers.
