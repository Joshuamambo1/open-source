## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary of the golutra project:

Golutra is an open-source, multi-agent AI orchestration platform that enables automation, workflow management, and developer tool integration. It unifies various AI models, including Codex, Claude Code, and OpenClaw, into a single, parallel-execution system for efficient task orchestration and long-running workflows. This platform provides a comprehensive AI productivity workspace for developers and organizations to streamline their operations.
