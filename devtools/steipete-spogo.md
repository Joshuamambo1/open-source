## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the steipete/spogo project:

Spogo is an open-source project that replicates the Spotify music streaming service within a terminal interface. Utilizing web cookies, it provides a command-line interface (CLI) experience, allowing users to power their music preferences from the comfort of their terminal. This innovative tool offers a unique way to interact with music in a text-based environment.
