## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the leenooks/phpLDAPadmin project:

phpLDAPadmin is a free and open-source web-based LDAP administration tool, allowing users to easily manage and maintain their LDAP directories. This intuitive interface provides a user-friendly way to perform various LDAP tasks, such as adding, editing, and deleting users, groups, and other directory objects. With phpLDAPadmin, administrators can efficiently manage their LDAP infrastructure without requiring extensive technical expertise.
