## 📝 Summary

EvoAgentX is an open‑source platform that orchestrates a network of AI agents capable of autonomously improving and expanding their own capabilities over time. By combining a modular orchestration layer, machine‑learning pipelines, and an interactive frontend, it enables developers to create, manage, and monitor self‑evolving AI ecosystems for complex, adaptive applications.
