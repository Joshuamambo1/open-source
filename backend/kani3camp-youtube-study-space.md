## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary of the open-source project:

The kani3camp/youtube-study-space project is an innovative online study space built on YouTube Live, allowing users to enter a virtual study room through a custom command in the live chat. This unique platform provides a dedicated space for students and learners to focus on their studies in a collaborative environment. By harnessing the power of YouTube Live, this project aims to create a seamless and interactive learning experience.
