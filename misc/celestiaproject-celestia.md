## 📝 Summary

Celestia is an open-source, real-time 3D astronomy simulator that enables users to visualize the night sky and explore various celestial objects, from planets and stars to galaxies and asteroids. This project allows users to travel through space and time, observing the universe in stunning detail. With its vast library of customizable models and realistic graphics, Celestia offers an immersive and educational experience for space enthusiasts and scientists alike.
