## 📝 Summary

Jellyfin is a media server and client application that allows users to manage and stream their video and audio content across various platforms. This open-source project, jellyfin/jellyfin-android, provides an Android client for Jellyfin, enabling users to access and control their media library on-the-go. The Android client offers a seamless and intuitive experience for discovering, playing, and managing media content within the Jellyfin ecosystem.
