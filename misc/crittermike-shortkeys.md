## 📝 Summary

### English

**Shortkeys** is an open‑source browser extension that lets users create and manage custom keyboard shortcuts for virtually any web‑page action, from opening URLs to executing JavaScript snippets. It offers a simple UI for defining, editing, and importing shortcut configurations, works across major browsers, and is licensed for free community contributions.
