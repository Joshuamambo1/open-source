## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the gdsfactory project:

gdsfactory is an open-source Python library designed to make hardware design accessible and intuitive. It enables users to create and design chips, printed circuit boards (PCBs), and 3D-printable objects, including photonics, analog, quantum, and MEMS devices. With gdsfactory, anyone can build and innovate in the fields of hardware design, empowering the next generation of engineers and creators.
