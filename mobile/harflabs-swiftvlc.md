## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the SwiftVLC open-source project:

SwiftVLC is a modern Swift wrapper around the popular libVLC library, allowing for seamless integration of video playback capabilities into iOS, macOS, tvOS, and Mac Catalyst applications. This open-source project provides a convenient and efficient way to add robust video playback features to your cross-platform projects. With SwiftVLC, developers can easily create high-performance, media-rich experiences for their users.
