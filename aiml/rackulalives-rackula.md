## 📝 Summary

Rackula is an open-source, drag-and-drop rack layout designer that enables users to efficiently plan and visualize their rack configurations. With a user-friendly interface, Rackula streamlines the process of designing and optimizing rack layouts for various applications, including AI/ML, backend, and DevOps/Infra environments. This project is ideal for developers, system administrators, and data center managers seeking a flexible and customizable solution for rack layout design.
