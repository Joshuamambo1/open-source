## 📝 Summary

Minimap2 is an open-source, versatile pairwise aligner designed for aligning genomic and spliced nucleotide sequences. This tool efficiently maps DNA or RNA reads to a reference genome, making it a valuable resource for various bioinformatics applications, including genome assembly, variant detection, and single-molecule sequencing analysis. By leveraging advanced algorithms, minimap2 achieves fast and accurate alignment results.
