## 📝 Summary

Craftzdog/solarized-osaka.nvim is a clean, dark Neovim colorscheme written entirely in Lua. It provides comprehensive support for built‑in features like LSP and Tree‑sitter, as well as a wide range of popular plugins, ensuring consistent highlighting across the editor. Ideal for developers who want a modern, Solarized‑inspired look with minimal configuration.
