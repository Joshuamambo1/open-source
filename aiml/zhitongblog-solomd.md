## 📝 Summary

Solomd is an open-source markdown editor that offers a local-first approach, allowing users to work offline. This editor serves as a bridge to large language models (LLMs) and includes a bundled Multi-Cloud Provider (MCP) server, enabling seamless integration with various AI providers such as Claude Code, Codex, and Cursor. Solomd supports Bring Your Own Key (BYOK) for 14 different AI providers.
