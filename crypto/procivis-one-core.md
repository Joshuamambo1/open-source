## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary of the procivis/one-core project:

One-core is an open-source platform for secure digital identity management, compliant with eIDAS 2.0 and ISO 18013-5 standards. It enables devices to issue, hold, and verify digital identities and credentials, leveraging IETF SD-JWT VC, OID4VC, and W3C VC standards for secure data exchange. This platform provides a robust foundation for secure digital identity verification across various devices and systems.
