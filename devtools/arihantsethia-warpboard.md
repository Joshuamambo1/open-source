## 📝 Summary

Warpboard is an open-source project that enables seamless integration of screenshot pasting into SSH sessions directly from iTerm. This feature allows users to easily share images with others by simply copying and pasting them into their iTerm SSH sessions. By streamlining this process, Warpboard enhances productivity for developers and IT professionals working remotely.
