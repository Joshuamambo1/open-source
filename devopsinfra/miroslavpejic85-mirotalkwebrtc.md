## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary of the project:

Mirotalkwebrtc is an open-source, self-hosted room scheduler for MiroTalk instances that enables the creation and management of scheduled video meeting rooms. This project is specifically designed to support real-time communication workflows, allowing for seamless and secure video conferencing. By hosting this solution internally, users can maintain control over their communication infrastructure while leveraging the benefits of WebRTC.
