## 📝 Summary

The Zotero translators project is an open-source initiative that provides translations for Zotero, a popular citation management tool, allowing users to work seamlessly across languages. This project contributes to the accessibility and global reach of Zotero by enabling users to easily translate the software into their native languages. By facilitating cultural and linguistic inclusivity, Zotero translators empower users worldwide to efficiently manage their research and publications.
