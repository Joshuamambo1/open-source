## 📝 Summary

SillyTavern is an open-source project designed to serve as a user-friendly frontend for Large Language Models (LLMs), catering to power users who require efficient and intuitive interactions with AI-driven systems. This project aims to simplify the process of leveraging LLM capabilities, making it more accessible for users with advanced needs. By providing a streamlined interface, SillyTavern empowers users to harness the full potential of LLMs.
