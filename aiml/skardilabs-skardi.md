Skardi is an open‑source data‑plane designed for AI agents, enabling them to fetch, store, and manage their own data without relying on external pipelines. By providing a lightweight, extensible framework for data autonomy, Skardi lets developers build agents that can securely interact with diverse data sources and retain control over their information flow. It targets AI/ML applications that require robust, self‑contained data handling.
