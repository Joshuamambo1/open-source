## 📝 Summary

KKManager is an open-source project that provides a comprehensive mod, plugin, and card management solution for games developed by Illusion that utilize BepInEx. This tool enables users to easily install, manage, and customize various mods, plugins, and cards within the games, enhancing their overall experience. By facilitating seamless mod management, KKManager empowers gamers to unlock new content, features, and gameplay mechanics.
