## 📝 Summary

The NVIDIA-NeMo/Megatron-Bridge project is an open-source training library for Megatron-based models, enabling efficient and scalable training of large language models. It offers a bidirectional conversion capability with Hugging Face, allowing for seamless integration and compatibility with various frameworks. This library facilitates the development and deployment of advanced AI models in a wide range of applications.
