## 📝 Summary

The ai-robots-txt project, also known as ai.robots.txt, is an open-source initiative that maintains a comprehensive list of AI agents and robots to block, ensuring website owners can effectively manage automated traffic and protect their online content. This project categorizes and updates the list of AI-powered crawlers, bots, and scrapers to help users maintain website integrity and prevent unwanted data extraction. By contributing to this project, users can help keep the web secure and promote responsible AI development.
