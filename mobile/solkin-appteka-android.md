## 📝 Summary

Appteka is an open-source, alternative store for Android that provides users with a unique platform to discover and install apps outside of Google Play. This project aims to offer a decentralized and community-driven experience, allowing developers to publish their apps and users to access a wide range of content. Appteka is a mobile app designed to cater to users seeking an alternative to traditional app stores.
