## 📝 Summary

This open-source project involves creating a unique AI argumentation system where multiple AI models engage in debates, allowing them to present their perspectives and counterarguments. The project then leverages the mythological figure of Hermes, known for his role as a messenger and mediator, to serve as a neutral judge, assessing the validity of each AI model's arguments. This innovative approach aims to push the boundaries of AI development and decision-making.
