## 📝 Summary

The Gradle Profiler is an open-source tool that allows users to gather valuable insights into Gradle build performance, enabling them to optimize and streamline their build processes. By providing detailed profiling and benchmarking information, this tool helps developers identify bottlenecks and areas for improvement in their Gradle builds. This results in faster, more efficient builds and enhanced overall development productivity.
