## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary of the Uncle-Gao/Live-Translator-Hub project:

The Uncle-Gao/Live-Translator-Hub is an open-source GUI desktop application that enables one-click localization for Cursor and Claude, utilizing an AI translation engine for seamless translation. This application leverages the power of Webview plugins to penetrate and provide a seamless translation experience. By harnessing the capabilities of AI/ML and Frontend technologies, it facilitates more efficient and effective translation workflows.
