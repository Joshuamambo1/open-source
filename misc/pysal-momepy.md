## 📝 Summary

The pysal/momepy project is an open-source toolkit for analyzing and measuring urban morphology. It provides a comprehensive set of algorithms and tools to quantify and understand the geometric properties of urban areas, enabling researchers and analysts to gain insights into the structure and evolution of cities. By leveraging this toolkit, users can extract meaningful metrics from geographic data, facilitating the study of urbanization, spatial patterns, and related phenomena.
