## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the Wholphin project:

Wholphin is an open-source Android TV client for Jellyfin, providing users with a seamless media experience on their Android TV devices. This project aims to offer a reliable and feature-rich alternative to traditional Jellyfin clients, catering to the growing demand for media center solutions on Android TV. By contributing to Wholphin, developers can help shape the future of Jellyfin on Android TV.
