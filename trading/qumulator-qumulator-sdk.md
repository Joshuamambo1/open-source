## 📝 Summary

Qumulator is an open-source quantum circuit simulator capable of handling 1,000 qubits without requiring a GPU. This innovative tool allows for the exploration and analysis of complex quantum systems, providing a valuable resource for researchers and developers in the field of quantum computing. By making quantum simulation accessible without the need for specialized hardware, Qumulator has the potential to accelerate the development of quantum algorithms and applications.
