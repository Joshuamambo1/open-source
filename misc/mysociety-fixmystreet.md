## 📝 Summary

FixMyStreet is an open-source, map-based reporting platform designed by mySociety, allowing citizens to easily report local issues such as potholes, graffiti, and other community concerns. The platform is highly customizable and can be easily installed in various countries and regions. By enabling citizens to report issues and track their progress, FixMyStreet aims to promote transparency and community engagement in local governance.
