## 📝 Summary

**ml‑intern** is an open‑source “ML engineer” framework from Hugging Face that automates the end‑to‑end workflow of machine‑learning projects: it can ingest research papers, generate and train the described models, and package the results for production deployment. By combining natural‑language processing, model‑training pipelines, and deployment utilities, it helps both engineers and marketers quickly prototype, evaluate, and ship state‑of‑the‑art ML solutions.
