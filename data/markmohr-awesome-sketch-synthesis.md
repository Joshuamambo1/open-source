## 📝 Summary

The Awesome-Sketch-Synthesis project is an open-source collection of academic papers and research on Sketch Synthesis, a technique used for generating and manipulating 2D sketches and drawings. This repository provides a comprehensive resource for researchers and practitioners interested in exploring the capabilities and applications of Sketch Synthesis. By aggregating relevant papers and studies, the project aims to facilitate knowledge sharing and accelerate innovation in the field of computer-aided design and graphics.
