## 📝 Summary

### English

Open Babel is an open‑source cheminformatics toolkit that enables conversion, analysis, and manipulation of chemical data across a wide variety of file formats and representation standards. It provides a comprehensive library and command‑line utilities for tasks such as molecular format interconversion, substructure searching, descriptor calculation, and 3‑D geometry generation, making it a versatile resource for researchers, developers, and educators in chemistry and related fields.
