## 📝 Summary

Continuum is an open-source recreation of the OMNI font, a classic typeface known for its distinctive and nostalgic design. This project aims to provide a precise and accurate representation of the original font, catering to designers and enthusiasts who appreciate the unique aesthetic of OMNI. By making it open-source, the project fosters collaboration and accessibility to this iconic font.
