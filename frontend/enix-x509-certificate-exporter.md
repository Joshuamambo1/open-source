## 📝 Summary

The enix/x509-certificate-exporter is an open-source Prometheus exporter that allows organizations to monitor the expiration of x509 certificates in their Kubernetes clusters or standalone environments. This tool helps ensure the timely renewal of certificates, reducing the risk of expirations that can compromise security and disrupt services. By integrating with Prometheus, users can utilize this exporter to gain visibility into their certificate expiration dates and automate monitoring and alerting processes.
