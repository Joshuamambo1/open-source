## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the open-source project:

OpenClip is an open-source, AI-powered tool for extracting highlights from long videos. It utilizes machine learning to identify the most engaging and significant moments in video content, saving users time and effort in editing and sharing their favorite clips. This tool is designed to streamline video post-production and enhance content sharing experiences.
