## 📝 Summary

The TTPForge is an open-source cybersecurity framework designed to streamline the development, automation, and execution of attacker Tactics, Techniques, and Procedures (TTPs). This framework enables security professionals to simulate real-world attacker behaviors, facilitating more effective threat modeling and incident response. By leveraging TTPForge, organizations can enhance their cybersecurity posture and improve defenses against sophisticated threats.
