## 📝 Summary

LiftShift is an open‑source analytics tool that imports workout logs from platforms such as Hevy, Strong, and Lyfta, then delivers AI‑driven insights—including plateau detection, interactive muscle heatmaps, and detailed exercise‑by‑muscle breakdowns. Users can filter data by calendar view, generate shareable progress cards, and explore comprehensive visualizations—all completely free and customizable.
