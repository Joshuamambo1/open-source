## 📝 Summary

The moov-io/watchman open-source project provides a comprehensive AI/ML-based search engine for global watchlists and sanctions, enabling organizations to conduct AML (Anti-Money Laundering), CTF (Counter-Terrorism Financing), KYC (Know Your Customer), and OFAC (Office of Foreign Assets Control) checks. This tool helps to streamline and automate the process of identifying and mitigating financial risks associated with illicit activities. By leveraging advanced AI and machine learning algorithms, watchman enhances the accuracy and efficiency of watchlist and sanctions searches.
