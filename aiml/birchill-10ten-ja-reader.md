## 📝 Summary

Here is a brief summary for the open-source project:

The 10ten-ja-reader is an innovative browser extension that utilizes AI/ML technology to provide real-time Japanese text translation. By simply hovering over Japanese words, users can access their English translations, making it easier to navigate and understand Japanese content. This extension is particularly useful for travelers, language learners, and anyone interested in exploring Japanese media and culture.
