## 📝 Summary

### English

SameeraSW’s **AirSync‑mac** is an open‑source tool that recreates macOS Continuity features—such as Handoff, Universal Clipboard, and SMS forwarding—on Android devices. By bridging the gap between Apple’s ecosystem and Android, it enables seamless cross‑platform interactions, letting users start tasks on a Mac and continue them on their Android phone. The project combines AI‑assisted synchronization logic with a lightweight frontend and mobile client to deliver a smooth, real‑time experience.
