## 📝 Summary

The lan-mouse project is an open-source initiative that enables the sharing of mouse and keyboard inputs across a local area network (LAN), allowing for seamless collaboration and remote control between devices. This DevTool is particularly useful for developers, professionals, and gamers who require simultaneous access to multiple computers. By facilitating LAN-based mouse and keyboard sharing, the project enhances productivity and flexibility in various settings.
