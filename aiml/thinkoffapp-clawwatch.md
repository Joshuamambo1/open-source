## 📝 Summary

### English

ThinkOffApp/ClawWatch is an open‑source project that brings a fully offline, AI‑powered personal assistant to Wear OS devices. By combining the NullClaw framework, Vosk speech‑to‑text, and Claude Opus 4.6, it enables real‑time voice interaction and intelligent responses directly on a Galaxy Watch without relying on cloud services. The project showcases a compact, on‑device AI/ML pipeline for mobile wearables.
