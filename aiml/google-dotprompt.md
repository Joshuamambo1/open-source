## 📝 Summary

The google/dotprompt project provides a collection of executable GenAI prompt templates, offering a starting point for users to interact with and utilize AI models in a more streamlined and efficient manner. These templates aim to simplify the process of crafting and executing AI prompts, allowing developers and users to explore the capabilities of GenAI technology. By leveraging dotprompt, users can accelerate their AI development and experimentation.
