## 📝 Summary

Skforecast is an open-source Python library that enables users to perform time series forecasting using a variety of scikit-learn compatible models, statistical methods, and foundation models. This library provides a unified interface for time series forecasting tasks, allowing users to easily integrate different techniques into their workflows. With skforecast, users can leverage a wide range of models and methods to make accurate predictions and gain valuable insights from their time series data.
