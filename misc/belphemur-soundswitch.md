Here's a brief summary for the Belphemur/SoundSwitch project:

SoundSwitch is an open-source C# application that allows users to easily switch their default playing device, making it convenient to change between audio output sources on their computer. This free tool is designed to simplify the process of selecting a new audio device, eliminating the need to navigate through complex system settings. Available for download at https://soundswitch.aaflalo.me/, SoundSwitch is a lightweight and user-friendly solution for managing audio output devices.
