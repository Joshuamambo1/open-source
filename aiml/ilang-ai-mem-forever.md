## 📝 Summary

The Mem-Forever project is an open-source initiative that enables AI tools to retain user information across sessions, eliminating the need for re-entry and reconfiguration. This project provides a zero-install, zero-configuration solution that allows users to maintain a continuous, seamless experience with their AI tools. By forking the project, users can ensure their AI interactions are preserved and accessible everywhere, forever.
