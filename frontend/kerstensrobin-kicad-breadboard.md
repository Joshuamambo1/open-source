## 📝 Summary

The KiCad Breadboard Builder is an open-source project that enables users to design and prototype electronic circuits on a virtual breadboard, making it easier for hobbyists, students, and professionals to test and iterate on their ideas. Developed as a frontend application, it integrates seamlessly with KiCad's powerful PCB design capabilities. By providing a user-friendly breadboard simulation environment, the KiCad Breadboard Builder streamlines the circuit design process and reduces the risk of costly mistakes.
