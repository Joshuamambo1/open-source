## 📝 Summary

Farben is an open-source, lightweight markup language designed for the terminal, offering faster and safer alternatives to traditional formatting options. It allows developers to enhance their command-line interfaces with customizable styling and formatting, all while maintaining a focus on security and efficiency. By using Farben, users can streamline their terminal workflows and improve overall productivity.
