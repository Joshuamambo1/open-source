## 📝 Summary

The PlasmaControl/DESC project is an open-source, stellarator-specific equilibrium and optimization suite. It provides a comprehensive set of tools for researchers and engineers to analyze and optimize the performance of stellarator devices, such as magnetic confinement fusion reactors. By harnessing the power of open-source collaboration, DESC aims to accelerate the development of next-generation fusion energy technologies.
