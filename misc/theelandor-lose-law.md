## 📝 Summary

Lose_law is an open-source, tiny command-line interface (CLI) that generates random laws of software engineering. These laws, inspired by the classic "Laws of Software Engineering" by Larry Wall, serve as humorous reminders of the complexities and idiosyncrasies of software development. The project aims to provide a lighthearted and engaging tool for developers to reflect on their craft.
