## 📝 Summary

Theseus is an open-source project that aims to reverse-engineer and reconstruct the original Xbox 360 5960 dashboard. By recreating the original interface, the project seeks to provide a nostalgic experience for gamers who remember the console's early days. The project's work is based on publicly available information and reverse-engineering efforts.
