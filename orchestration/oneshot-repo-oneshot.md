## 📝 Summary

### English

OneShot is an open‑source orchestration framework that streamlines AI/ML workflows by enabling users to define a single, comprehensive prompt that drives the entire end‑to‑end process. It abstracts the complexities of multi‑step model interactions, allowing developers to specify “one prompt, full delivery” and automatically handling data routing, model execution, and result aggregation. Ideal for rapid prototyping and production pipelines, OneShot simplifies the creation, deployment, and management of AI solutions.
