## 📝 Summary

The iCloud-backup project is an open-source tool that offers a user-friendly solution for backing up and managing iCloud data. This project aims to make it easier for users to access and restore their iCloud backups, providing a more transparent and customizable experience. By leveraging this tool, users can take control of their iCloud backups and enjoy greater flexibility and control over their data.
