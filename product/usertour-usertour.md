## 📝 Summary

Usertour is an open-source user onboarding platform that enables users to create in-app product tours, checklists, and surveys with ease and full control. As a free alternative to commercial solutions like Userflow and Appcues, Usertour allows businesses to streamline their user onboarding process without incurring additional costs. With Usertour, users can effectively guide their customers through product usage and enhance overall user experience.
