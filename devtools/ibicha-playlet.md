## 📝 Summary

The iBicha/playlet project is an open-source, unofficial YouTube client for Roku that prioritizes user privacy. This project aims to provide a secure and private alternative to the official YouTube app, catering to users who value their online data and security. By offering a free and customizable solution, iBicha/playlet empowers Roku users to take control of their digital experience.
