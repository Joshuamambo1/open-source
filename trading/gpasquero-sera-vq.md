## 📝 Summary

Sera-VQ is an open-source project that explores the application of discrete codes in dimensionality reduction tasks, particularly under extreme compression rates. According to its findings, discrete codes can outperform traditional Principal Component Analysis (PCA) in such scenarios. This project aims to provide a novel approach to dimensionality reduction under compression constraints.
