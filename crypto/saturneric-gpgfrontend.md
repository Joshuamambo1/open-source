## 📝 Summary

Saturneric’s **GpgFrontend** is a contemporary, user‑friendly interface for OpenPGP that streamlines encryption, digital signing, and key management. By presenting complex cryptographic operations in an intuitive UI, it helps users incorporate strong security practices into everyday workflows while maintaining trust and transparency. It’s an open‑source project aimed at modernizing the “Enigma” experience for both casual and advanced users.
