## 📝 Summary

### English

product‑on‑purpose/pm‑skills is an open‑source library that bundles 40 ready‑to‑use product‑management capabilities for AI agents, covering the entire Triple‑Diamond process plus foundational tools such as Lean Canvas, personas, and meeting‑lifecycle patterns, as well as utility actions for creating, validating, and iterating on product ideas. Each skill is delivered as a plug‑and‑play template, workflow, and thread‑aligned example, with CI‑enforced contracts that keep skill families consistent. The project is licensed under Apache 2.0 and fits within the Orchestration, AI/ML, and Product domains.
