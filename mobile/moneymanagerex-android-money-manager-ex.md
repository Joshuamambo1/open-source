## 📝 Summary

Money Manager Ex is an open-source, local-first personal finance app that prioritizes user data security and self-hosting. This mobile app allows users to manage their finances across multiple devices while keeping their data encrypted and under their control. By syncing data locally, users can maintain a secure and private financial record.
