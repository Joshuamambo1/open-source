## 📝 Summary

The CatColab project is an open-source, collaborative platform for formal conceptual modeling. It enables users to create, share, and integrate conceptual models in a standardized and interoperable manner. This environment facilitates the development and refinement of conceptual models among diverse stakeholders, promoting consistency and efficiency in their creation.
