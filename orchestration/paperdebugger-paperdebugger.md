## 📝 Summary

The PaperDebugger project is an open-source, plugin-based platform designed to streamline academic writing, review, and editing within a single editor interface. This innovative system utilizes multi-agent technology to facilitate collaborative and efficient workflows among writers, reviewers, and editors. By leveraging AI/ML capabilities, PaperDebugger aims to simplify the research and publication process.
