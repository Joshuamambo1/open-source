## 📝 Summary

NoctaVox is an open-source, local terminal-based user interface (TUI) music player. It allows users to browse, play, and control their music library from the command line, providing a unique alternative to traditional desktop music players. With NoctaVox, users can enjoy their music in a distraction-free and minimalistic environment.
