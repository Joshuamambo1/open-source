## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the eitchtee/WYGIWYH project:

WYGIWYH (Where You Go, I Will Go With You) is an open-source, self-hosted finance tracker that provides a simple yet powerful tool for managing personal finances. This AI-driven platform allows users to track their income, expenses, and budgets in a centralized and secure environment. By hosting WYGIWYH on their own servers, users can maintain control over their financial data and enjoy the flexibility to customize the application to their needs.
