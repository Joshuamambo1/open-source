## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the open-source project:

The openai-dotnet library is an official .NET wrapper for the OpenAI API, enabling developers to seamlessly integrate OpenAI's AI capabilities into their .NET applications. With this library, users can access OpenAI's range of APIs, including text generation, completion, and more, through a simple and intuitive .NET interface. This library simplifies the process of leveraging OpenAI's powerful AI technology in .NET-based projects.
