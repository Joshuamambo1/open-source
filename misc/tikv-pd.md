## 📝 Summary

tikv/pd is the open‑source Placement Driver that coordinates the TiKV distributed key‑value store. It manages cluster metadata, monitors health, performs load balancing, and handles region scheduling and replication to ensure high availability and optimal performance. As a core component of the TiDB ecosystem, PD provides the control plane for scaling and maintaining TiKV clusters.
