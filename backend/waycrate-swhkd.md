## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the open-source project:

"Swhkd is an open-source, lightweight key manager for Wayland, providing a similar experience to sxhkd, while also supporting both TTY and X11 environments. This project offers a flexible and customizable solution for managing keyboard shortcuts and mappings. With its cross-platform capabilities, swhkd is an ideal choice for users seeking a powerful and adaptable key manager."
