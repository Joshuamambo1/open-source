## 📝 Summary

This open-source project, taks/esp32-nimble, is a wrapper for the ESP32 NimBLE Bluetooth stack, providing a convenient interface for developers to leverage the capabilities of the ESP32's Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) functionality. By utilizing this wrapper, users can easily integrate BLE connectivity into their projects, simplifying the development process and enabling seamless wireless communication. This project offers a user-friendly and efficient solution for ESP32-based applications that require BLE support.
