## 📝 Summary

kubev2v/forklift is an open-source toolkit designed to simplify the migration of virtual machines from various environments, including VMware, OVA, oVirt, and OpenStack, to KubeVirt. This DevOps/Infra project enables seamless transfer of virtual machines to a Kubernetes-based infrastructure, streamlining the process for administrators and developers. By leveraging forklift, users can efficiently migrate their workloads to a cloud-native platform with minimal disruption.
