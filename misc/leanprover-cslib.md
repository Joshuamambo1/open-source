## 📝 Summary

The Lean Computer Science Library (CSLib) is an open-source project providing a collection of mathematical and computational tools for use in Lean, a proof assistant and programming language. CSLib offers a range of functionalities, including data structures, algorithms, and utilities, to facilitate computer science-related proofs and computations. This library is designed to support education and research in computer science within the Lean ecosystem.
