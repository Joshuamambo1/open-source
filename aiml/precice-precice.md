## 📝 Summary

The Precice project is an open-source coupling library that enables partitioned multi-physics and multi-scale simulations, facilitating the integration of diverse physical models and scales within a single framework. It supports both surface and volume coupling, allowing researchers and developers to efficiently combine simulations from different domains. This library serves as a foundation for the Precice ecosystem, providing a versatile tool for advancing simulation-based research and applications.
