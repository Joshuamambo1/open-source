Cordys CRM is an open-source, next-generation AI-powered customer relationship management (CRM) system that seamlessly integrates informationization, digitalization, and intelligence. This innovative platform supports private deployment and features the "Skills" skill set, enabling comprehensive driving of the OpenClaw AI assistant. By harnessing the power of AI and ML, Cordys CRM empowers businesses to streamline their operations and enhance customer engagement.
