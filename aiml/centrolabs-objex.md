## 📝 Summary

ObjeX is an open-source, self-hosted object storage solution inspired by Amazon S3. It offers a reliable and scalable storage platform for developers to store and manage their data, providing a cost-effective alternative to cloud-based services. By utilizing ObjeX, users can maintain control over their data while enjoying a user-friendly interface and robust features.
