## 📝 Summary

Here's a 2-3 sentence summary of the project:

The Kicad-JLCPCB Tools plugin is an open-source extension for the Kicad electronics design software, streamlining the process of generating Bill of Materials (BOM) and Component Placement List (CPL) files for JLCPCB. It seamlessly integrates with the JLCPCB parts database, allowing users to directly assign LCSC part numbers and access relevant datasheets. This plugin simplifies the design-to-manufacturing workflow, saving time and effort for electronics designers and manufacturers.
