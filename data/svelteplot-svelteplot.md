## 📝 Summary

Svelteplot is an open-source, data visualization framework that provides a native plotting experience in Svelte applications, allowing for the creation of high-quality, interactive visualizations. Built on the principles of the grammar of graphics, it enables developers to easily and effectively communicate complex data insights. With svelteplot, users can create a wide range of visualization types, from simple plots to complex, multi-scale dashboards.
