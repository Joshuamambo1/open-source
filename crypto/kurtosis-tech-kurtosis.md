## 📝 Summary

### English

Kurtosis is an open‑source platform that simplifies the packaging, deployment, and orchestration of blockchain infrastructure, offering a “Docker‑Compose‑like” experience for complex, multi‑node networks. By defining blockchain stacks as reusable modules, developers can quickly spin up testnets, development environments, or production clusters across cloud, on‑prem, or local machines, streamlining testing, integration, and scaling of crypto applications.
