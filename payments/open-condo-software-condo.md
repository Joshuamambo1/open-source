## 📝 Summary

Condo is an open-source property management software that streamlines tasks such as ticket management, resident contact tracking, and payment processing. This comprehensive platform also enables the creation of invoices, oversight of a service marketplace, and extension through mini-apps, making it suitable for property management companies and shared property services. By providing a customizable and extendable solution, Condo aims to simplify the management of properties and associated tasks.
