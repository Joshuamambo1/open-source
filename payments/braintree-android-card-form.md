## 📝 Summary

The braintree/android-card-form project is an open-source, ready-to-use card form layout designed for Android applications. This layout simplifies the process of accepting credit and debit card payments by providing a pre-built, intuitive form that can be easily integrated into your app. It is a valuable resource for developers seeking to implement seamless payment processing within their Android applications.
