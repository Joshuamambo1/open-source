## 📝 Summary

MoonRay is an open-source, state-of-the-art production Monte Carlo ray tracing (MCRT) renderer developed by DreamWorks Animation. This award-winning tool is designed to provide high-quality visuals for film and television productions. By making MoonRay open-source, DreamWorks Animation aims to share its expertise and facilitate innovation in the field of computer-generated imagery (CGI).
