## 📝 Summary

Gearcoleco is an open-source emulator and debugger for the ColecoVision, a classic home video game console. This versatile emulator is compatible with multiple operating systems, including macOS, Windows, Linux, BSD, and can also be used within the popular RetroArch platform. Gearcoleco allows users to play and analyze ColecoVision games on a range of modern devices.
