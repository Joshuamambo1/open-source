## 📝 Summary

The 'awesome-opentui' project is an open-source, community-driven collection of valuable resources related to OpenTUI, a modern terminal user interface framework. It serves as a centralized hub, providing easy access to a wide range of OpenTUI libraries, tutorials, and other development tools. This curated list aims to facilitate the discovery and adoption of OpenTUI by developers and designers.
