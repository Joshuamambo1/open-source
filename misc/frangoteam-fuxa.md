## 📝 Summary

FUXA is an open-source, web-based Process Visualization software that enables users to create interactive and customizable dashboards, SCADA (Supervisory Control and Data Acquisition) systems, and HMI (Human-Machine Interface) applications. It offers a flexible and extensible platform for visualizing and monitoring industrial processes, providing real-time data and analytics. FUXA aims to provide a powerful and user-friendly solution for process visualization and control.
