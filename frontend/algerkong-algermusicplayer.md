## 📝 Summary

AlgerMusicPlayer is an open‑source third‑party music player that combines local playback, desktop lyric display, music downloading, and remote control capabilities into a single frontend solution. It provides a user‑friendly interface for managing and streaming music while offering customizable services for personal or community use.
