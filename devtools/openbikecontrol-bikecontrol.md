## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the OpenBikeControl project:

OpenBikeControl is an open-source project that enables virtual gear shifting and other functionalities in various rider apps, compatible with popular Zwift controllers. This project empowers users to extend the capabilities of their existing controllers, fostering a more immersive and interactive cycling experience. By providing a customizable and accessible solution, OpenBikeControl opens up new possibilities for cyclists and developers alike.
