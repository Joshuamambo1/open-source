## 📝 Summary

Partydeck is an open-source, split-screen game launcher designed for Linux and SteamOS users. It allows users to easily manage and launch their games in a split-screen layout, enhancing their gaming experience. By providing a user-friendly interface, partydeck aims to simplify the process of playing games with friends in a seamless and organized manner.
