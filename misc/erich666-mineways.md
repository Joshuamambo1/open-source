## 📝 Summary

Mineways is an open-source project that allows users to export 3D models from Minecraft for various purposes, including 3D printing and rendering. This tool provides a convenient way to extract and manipulate Minecraft maps, structures, and models, making it a valuable resource for enthusiasts and professionals alike. By leveraging Mineways, users can bring their Minecraft creations to life in the physical world or share them in a more visually engaging format.
