## 📝 Summary

cclib is an open-source project that provides parsers and algorithms for analyzing computational chemistry logfiles. It enables users to extract and manipulate data from various computational chemistry software packages, facilitating the reuse and integration of chemistry data. With cclib, researchers and developers can streamline their workflow and gain deeper insights from computational chemistry simulations.
