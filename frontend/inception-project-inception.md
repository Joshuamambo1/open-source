## 📝 Summary

INCEpTION is an open‑source, web‑based platform for semantic annotation that streamlines the creation and management of linguistic and knowledge‑rich corpora. It offers intelligent assistance—such as active learning, recommendation, and auto‑completion—to speed up annotation while providing robust tools for knowledge management and collaboration. Ideal for researchers and developers needing a flexible, frontend‑focused annotation environment.
