## 📝 Summary

OpenX32 is an open-source project that aims to create an open Linux kernel for the Behringer X32 digital mixer. This collaborative effort seeks to provide users with a free and customizable operating system for their X32 devices, potentially expanding their functionality and capabilities. By harnessing the power of an open-source kernel, users can unlock new features and possibilities for their X32 mixers.
