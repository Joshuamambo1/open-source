## 📝 Summary

The 'dsnet' project is an open-source, fast command-line tool for managing a centralized WireGuard VPN. Building on the idea of wg-quick, it streamlines the process of key generation and address allocation. This simplifies the setup and management of WireGuard VPNs for users.
