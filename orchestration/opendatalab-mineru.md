## 📝 Summary

Here's a 2-3 sentence summary for the MinerU project:

MinerU is an open-source project that simplifies the process of transforming complex documents into formats usable by Large Language Models (LLMs). By converting PDFs and Office documents into markdown/JSON, MinerU enables seamless integration with Agentic workflows, streamlining AI/ML applications and data operations. This efficient document transformation solution accelerates the development and deployment of AI-powered projects.
