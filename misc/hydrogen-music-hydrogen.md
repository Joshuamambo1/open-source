## 📝 Summary

Hydrogen is a powerful, open‑source drum machine and sequencer designed for Linux, macOS, and Windows. It offers a user‑friendly graphical interface, pattern‑based sequencing, extensive sample libraries, and real‑time control features for both beginners and professional musicians. The cross‑platform application is actively maintained and extensible, making it a versatile tool for creating and arranging drum tracks.
