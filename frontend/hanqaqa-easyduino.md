## 📝 Summary

This open-source project provides pre-designed PCB (Printed Circuit Board) files for popular microcontroller boards, including Arduino, ESP32, and RP2040. The project offers a convenient and standardized approach to electronics development by providing readily available and modifiable PCB designs. By utilizing these open-source PCBs, developers can save time and streamline their projects.
