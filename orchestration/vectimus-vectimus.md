## 📝 Summary

Vectimus is an open-source project that provides deterministic governance for AI coding agents, ensuring their actions are evaluated and aligned with predetermined rules before execution. This project utilizes a Cedar-based policy engine to intercept and assess every agent action, promoting transparency and reliability in AI decision-making processes. By doing so, Vectimus facilitates the development of trustworthy and accountable AI systems.
