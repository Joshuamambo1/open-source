## 📝 Summary

Poweradmin is an open-source, web-based control panel designed for managing PowerDNS, a popular domain name system (DNS) software. This user-friendly interface allows administrators to easily configure and monitor PowerDNS settings, making domain management more efficient and accessible. With poweradmin, users can streamline their DNS administration tasks and optimize their domain services.
