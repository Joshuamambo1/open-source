## 📝 Summary

QuantumOptics.jl is an open-source library for the numerical simulation of closed and open quantum systems. It enables users to model complex quantum dynamics using a variety of numerical methods, facilitating in-depth analysis and understanding of quantum systems. This library is a valuable resource for researchers and scientists in the field of quantum computing and quantum mechanics.
