## 📝 Summary

The achgateway project is an open-source payment gateway that facilitates automated ACH (Automated Clearing House) operations in a highly distributed and fault-tolerant manner. This allows for efficient and reliable processing of electronic checks, reducing the complexity and risk associated with traditional ACH workflows. By leveraging a distributed architecture, achgateway enables scalable and secure ACH operations.
