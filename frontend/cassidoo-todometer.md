## 📝 Summary

Cassidoo's Todometer is an innovative, open-source to-do list application that utilizes a meter-based interface to visualize tasks and progress. This unique approach provides a visually engaging way to manage and prioritize tasks, offering a fresh alternative to traditional to-do list styles. By using a meter-based system, users can easily track their progress and stay motivated to complete their tasks.
