## 📝 Summary

DOSBox-X is an open-source project that serves as a fork of the original DOSBox, a popular emulator for running classic MS-DOS games and applications on modern operating systems. This enhanced version offers improved performance and compatibility. It allows users to relive the nostalgia of their favorite retro games and software on a variety of platforms.
