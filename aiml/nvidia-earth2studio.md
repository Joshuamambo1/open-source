## 📝 Summary

Earth2Studio is an open-source deep-learning framework developed by NVIDIA, designed to facilitate the exploration, development, and deployment of AI-driven weather and climate workflows. This framework empowers users to build and customize their own climate modeling pipelines, enabling the creation of accurate and reliable predictive models. By leveraging Earth2Studio, researchers and developers can accelerate their work in weather and climate modeling, driving innovation in this critical field.
