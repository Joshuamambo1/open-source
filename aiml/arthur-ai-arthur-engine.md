## 📝 Summary

The Arthur Engine is an open-source project aimed at democratizing AI by providing a comprehensive monitoring and governance framework for Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning (AI/ML) systems. This framework enables developers to track and manage AI performance, ensuring transparency, accountability, and reliability. By making AI more accessible and controllable, the Arthur Engine helps bridge the gap between AI innovation and real-world adoption.
