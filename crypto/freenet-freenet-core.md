## 📝 Summary

Freenet is an open-source, decentralized network that allows users to share files and communicate anonymously, promoting digital freedom and independence. It uses a peer-to-peer architecture and encryption to protect user data and maintain confidentiality. By joining Freenet, users can browse, share, and store content without relying on centralized services or compromising their online identity.
