## 📝 Summary

Repowire is an open-source peer-to-peer mesh network designed for AI coding agents, enabling seamless communication and collaboration between various AI tools such as Claude Code, Codex, and Gemini CLI across different projects. This backend infrastructure facilitates the orchestration of AI coding agents, streamlining development workflows and enhancing productivity. By connecting these agents, Repowire empowers developers to tap into the collective potential of AI-driven coding capabilities.
