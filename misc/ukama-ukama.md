## 📝 Summary

The ukama project is an open-source initiative that aims to develop a cellular network from scratch, providing a decentralized and community-driven alternative to traditional wireless communication systems. This project offers a range of possibilities for users to customize and contribute to the development of the network, fostering innovation and experimentation in the field of telecommunications. By making the cellular network open-source, ukama seeks to promote transparency, collaboration, and accessibility in the field of wireless communication.
