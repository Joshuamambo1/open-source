## 📝 Summary

Here's a 2-3 sentence summary for the open-source project:

Netbirdio/kubernetes-operator is an open-source Kubernetes operator designed to simplify the management and deployment of Netbird infrastructure within Kubernetes environments. This operator provides a scalable and automated way to manage Netbird services, allowing users to easily integrate and manage their network connectivity with their Kubernetes applications. By leveraging the Kubernetes operator framework, users can enjoy a more streamlined and efficient experience for managing their Netbird services.
