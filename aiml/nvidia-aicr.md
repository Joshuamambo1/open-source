## 📝 Summary

The NVIDIA AI Compute Runtime (aicr) is an open-source project that provides tooling for optimizing, validating, and reproducing GPU-accelerated AI workloads in Kubernetes environments. This project enables developers to ensure the reliability and efficiency of their AI applications by leveraging Kubernetes' scalability and manageability features. By streamlining AI runtime deployment and management, aicr helps accelerate the development and deployment of AI workloads at scale.
