## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary of the project:

The ebrasha/free-v2ray-public-list is an open-source project that provides a publicly accessible list of free, working V2Ray servers for various protocols, including SS, SSR, Trojan, VLESS, and VMess. This list is updated every 15 minutes, eliminating the need for users to search for working servers or sign up for services. Users can easily connect by copying and pasting the server list into their V2Ray configurations.
