## 📝 Summary

OpenPanel is an open-source analytics platform that provides comprehensive web and product analytics capabilities, making it an accessible alternative to proprietary solutions like Mixpanel. With optional self-hosting, users can maintain full control over their data and analytics tools, ensuring enhanced flexibility and security. OpenPanel offers a robust solution for businesses and organizations seeking to monitor and optimize their digital products.
