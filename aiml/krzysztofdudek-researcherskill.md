## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary of the ResearcherSkill project:

ResearcherSkill is an open-source project that enables your AI agent to simulate scientific research, conducting over 30 experiments while your computer is idle, allowing for extensive research and data analysis. This single-file tool combines AI and machine learning capabilities to mimic a scientist's work, streamlining research processes. By harnessing the power of your AI agent, ResearcherSkill accelerates research and development, making it an invaluable resource for AI/ML developers and researchers.
