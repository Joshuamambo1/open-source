Works with Obsidian, Logseq, etc. or just folders on your local drive. Compiled memory for your LLM sessions. Crossplatform. GUI install on Claude Desktop, no terminal, no code.

## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the open-source project:

Transform your digital knowledge repository into a dynamic, compounding wiki with LLM-Wiki-Skills. This innovative project, inspired by Karpathy's concept, integrates six agent skills that continuously expand your knowledge base with every conversation. Compatible with popular note-taking applications like Obsidian and Logseq, or local folders, this open-source tool enables seamless, cross-platform knowledge growth.
