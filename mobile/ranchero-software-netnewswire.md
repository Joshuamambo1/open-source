NetNewsWire is a free, open-source RSS reader available on both macOS and iOS platforms. This versatile app allows users to efficiently manage and stay up-to-date with their favorite news sources and blogs, making it an ideal choice for those looking to streamline their online news consumption. With NetNewsWire, users can easily discover, organize, and read the latest articles from various feeds.
