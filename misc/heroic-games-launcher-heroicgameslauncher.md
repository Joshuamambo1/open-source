## 📝 Summary

The Heroic Games Launcher is an open-source, multi-platform application that allows users to easily manage and launch games from various digital stores, including GOG, Amazon, and Epic Games, across Linux, Windows, and macOS. This versatile launcher provides a streamlined experience for gamers, enabling them to access and play their favorite titles from a single interface. By supporting multiple platforms and stores, Heroic Games Launcher offers a unified gaming experience.
