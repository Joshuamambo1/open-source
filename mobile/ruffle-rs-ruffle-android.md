## 📝 Summary

Ruffle-rs/ruffle-android is an open-source Android application that brings the ruffle.rs project to mobile devices, allowing users to play classic Flash games on their Android smartphones and tablets. This project aims to preserve the nostalgia of Flash gaming and provide an accessible platform for experiencing classic Flash content on-the-go. By utilizing the ruffle.rs engine, this app ensures a seamless and immersive gaming experience on Android devices.
