## 📝 Summary

c:geo is a free, open‑source Android application that lets geocaching enthusiasts discover, log, and manage caches directly from their mobile devices. It integrates with major geocaching services, offers offline maps, automatic coordinate conversion, and a robust set of tools for tracking finds and sharing progress. The project is community‑driven, continually updated, and designed to provide a fast, reliable, and feature‑rich geocaching experience on Android.
