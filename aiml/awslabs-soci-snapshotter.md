## 📝 Summary

Here's a possible summary for the awslabs/soci-snapshotter project:

The awslabs/soci-snapshotter is an open-source containerd snapshotter plugin that enables seamless lazy loading of standard OCI images, eliminating the need for a build-time conversion step. This plugin facilitates efficient management of images in containerized environments, particularly beneficial for AI/ML and frontend applications. By streamlining the image loading process, soci-snapshotter enhances the overall performance and scalability of containerized workloads.
