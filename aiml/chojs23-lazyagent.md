## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the "chojs23/lazyagent" project:

LazyAgent is an open-source, text-based user interface (TUI) designed for monitoring and observing AI coding agents like Claude, Codex, and Opencode. This project allows developers to easily track the performance and behavior of their AI coding companions, providing valuable insights into their capabilities and limitations. With LazyAgent, users can streamline their workflow and gain a deeper understanding of their AI coding tools.
