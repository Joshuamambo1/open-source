## 📝 Summary

### English

DeepSkyStacker (deepskystacker/DSS) is an open‑source application for astrophotographers that automates the alignment, calibration, and stacking of multiple deep‑sky images to produce low‑noise, high‑detail final frames. It supports a wide range of RAW formats, offers sophisticated registration algorithms, and provides tools for light‑frame integration, gradient removal, and post‑processing. The project is community‑maintained and packaged under the “Misc” category for easy integration into Linux distributions and other software ecosystems.
