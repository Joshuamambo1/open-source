## 📝 Summary

### English

PeerBanHelper (PBH‑BTN) is an open‑source utility that automatically detects and blocks unwanted, leeching, or otherwise abnormal BitTorrent peers across popular clients such as qBittorrent, qBittorrent‑Enhanced‑Edition, Deluge, BiglyBT and BitComet. It offers built‑in cloud‑based rule sets as well as full support for user‑defined custom rules, enabling fine‑grained control over peer banning to protect download health and bandwidth.
