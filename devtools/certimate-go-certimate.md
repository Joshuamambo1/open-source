## 📝 Summary

Here's a 2-3 sentence summary for the certimate-go/certimate project:

Certimate is an open-source, self-hosted SSL certificate management tool that automates the entire process of issuance, deployment, renewal, and monitoring. This free tool provides a visually intuitive interface for managing SSL certificates across various cloud platforms, streamlining the full cycle of certificate management. With certimate, users can easily manage and secure their online presence with minimal manual intervention.
