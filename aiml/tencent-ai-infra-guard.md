## 📝 Summary

Tencent's AI-Infra-Guard is an open-source, full-stack AI Red Teaming platform that safeguards AI ecosystems against potential security threats. It provides comprehensive scanning capabilities, including OpenClaw Security Scan, Agent Scan, and LLM jailbreak evaluation, to identify vulnerabilities and ensure the integrity of AI infrastructure. This platform is designed for developers, DevOps teams, and security professionals working with AI and machine learning systems.
