## 📝 Summary

Stash is an open-source, self-hosted memory layer for AI agents that enables persistent storage of episodes, facts, and working context. Utilizing Postgres as its storage backend, Stash offers a single, cloud-free binary solution that eliminates the need for external dependencies. With its included MCP server, developers can integrate Stash into their AI projects for seamless and efficient data management.
