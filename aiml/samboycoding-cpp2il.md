## 📝 Summary

The Cpp2IL project is an open-source, work-in-progress tool aiming to reverse Unity's IL2CPP toolchain, providing a novel means of understanding and interacting with Unity's compiled code. By reversing IL2CPP, developers can gain insights into the compiled assembly and potentially unlock new possibilities for code analysis, modification, and debugging. This project holds potential for advancing Unity development and the broader field of reverse engineering.
