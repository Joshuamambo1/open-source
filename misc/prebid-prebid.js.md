## 📝 Summary

Prebid.js is an open-source project that simplifies the process of setting up and managing header bidding for advertising partners, eliminating the need for complex coding and line item configurations. This free solution allows users to streamline their ad management, making it easier to optimize revenue from digital advertising. With Prebid.js, publishers can efficiently integrate multiple ad exchanges and demand sources into their websites.
