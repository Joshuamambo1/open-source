## 📝 Summary

Authsome is an open-source local authentication proxy designed specifically for AI agents. This project enables secure and private authentication for AI models by acting as an intermediary between the model and authentication services. By utilizing Authsome, developers can ensure the confidentiality and integrity of their AI agents' interactions.
