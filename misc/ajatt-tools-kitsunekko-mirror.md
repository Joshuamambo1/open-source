## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the project:

Kitsunekko-mirror is an open-source project providing a comprehensive catalog of Japanese subtitles, offering users a valuable resource for accessing translated content. The project also serves as a backup mirror for kitsunekko, ensuring the availability of the subtitles even in case of any primary source disruptions. This catalog is a valuable contribution to the Japanese media community, fostering greater accessibility and understanding of Japanese media.
