## 📝 Summary

Here's a 2-3 sentence summary of the DORA open-source project:

DORA is an open-source middleware framework designed to simplify the development of AI-based robotic applications. It enables low-latency, composable, and distributed dataflow capabilities by modeling applications as directed graphs, or pipelines, allowing for efficient and scalable robotic system design. This architecture streamlines the creation of complex robotic applications, making it an ideal choice for developers working on AI-powered robotics projects.
