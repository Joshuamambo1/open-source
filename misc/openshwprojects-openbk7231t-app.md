## 📝 Summary

The OpenBK7231T_App project is an open-source firmware alternative for various Wi-Fi modules, allowing users to customize and control their devices. This project supports a wide range of modules from manufacturers such as BK7231T, BL2028N, and Realtek, providing a flexible option for users who want more control over their IoT devices. By replacing traditional firmwares like Tasmota or Esphome, OpenBK7231T_App enables users to tailor their devices to specific needs and preferences.
