## 📝 Summary

The WikiEduDashboard is an open-source system developed by the Wiki Education Foundation to manage and track Wikipedia editing assignments for students. This dashboard enables educators to assign and monitor student tasks, providing a centralized platform for learning and collaboration. By leveraging this tool, educators can effectively integrate Wikipedia editing into their curriculum and promote digital literacy and knowledge sharing.
