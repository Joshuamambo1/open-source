## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the AI-Novel-Writing-Assistant project:

The AI-Novel-Writing-Assistant is an open-source system designed for creating long-form fiction, utilizing a native AI engine to guide authors from initial idea to complete chapters. This comprehensive tool combines worldbuilding, structured planning, and agent-driven workflows to facilitate the novel creation process. With its agent, world, writing engine, and RAG capabilities, it aims to empower new writers to craft a complete novel.
