## 📝 Summary

Flare is an open-source project that allows users to browse multiple social media platforms, including Mastodon, Bluesky, X, Misskey, Nostr, and RSS, within a single app. It integrates multiple timelines and accounts, enabling seamless cross-posting across different networks. Flare provides a unified experience for users to manage and share content across various platforms.
