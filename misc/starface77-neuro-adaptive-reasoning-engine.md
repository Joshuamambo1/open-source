## 📝 Summary

NARE is an open-source framework that enables the conversion of Large Language Model (LLM) reasoning into efficient Python scripts. This framework essentially "crystallizes" the complex reasoning processes of LLMs into fast, executable code, allowing for streamlined AI development and deployment. By leveraging NARE, developers can tap into the power of LLMs while minimizing computational overhead.
