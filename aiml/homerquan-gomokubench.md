## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary of the open-source project:

This open-source project provides a benchmark for evaluating the performance of Large Language Models (LLMs) in playing the strategy board game Gomoku. The benchmark allows developers to test and compare the abilities of various LLMs in a standardized and repeatable manner. By leveraging this benchmark, researchers and developers can accelerate the development of LLMs for complex games and applications.
