## 📝 Summary

The VyManager project is an open-source, centralized Software-Defined Networking (SDN) controller that streamlines configuration, deployment, and monitoring of multi-site VyOS routers. This web-based interface simplifies management tasks, enhancing efficiency and reducing complexity in network operations. By leveraging VyManager, users can effectively orchestrate and manage their network infrastructure from a single, user-friendly platform.
