## 📝 Summary

### English

OpenAccountants is an open‑source library that equips large language models with comprehensive tax‑compliance capabilities, offering 371 pre‑built skills that cover the tax rules of 134 countries. The skills can be uploaded directly to any LLM or accessed through the MCP (Model Connectivity Platform), and they are organized into a quality‑tier system (Q1–Q5) to help developers select the most reliable tax logic for their applications. This project targets AI/ML and backend developers who need accurate, scalable tax calculations integrated into conversational or automated systems.
