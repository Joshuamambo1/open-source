## 📝 Summary

Mako is an open-source, lightweight Wayland notification daemon designed to provide a simple and efficient notification system for Wayland-based desktop environments. It aims to offer a low-resource alternative to existing notification daemons, making it suitable for use in embedded systems, resource-constrained devices, or minimalistic desktop setups. Mako can be used as a drop-in replacement for other notification daemons in Wayland environments.
