## 📝 Summary

The Switch_90DNS_tester is an open-source homebrew application designed to test 90DNS or Atmosphere DNS MITM functionality on the Nintendo Switch. This tool allows users to verify the setup and operation of their DNS-based exploits for homebrew and custom firmware. It serves as a utility for developers and users of the 90DNS project.
