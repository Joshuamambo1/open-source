Here's a brief summary for the open-source project:

Snaplii's agent-to-merchant-payments project revolutionizes the way AI agents interact with real-world commerce by providing a secure, tokenized payment layer. This innovative solution leverages a network of over 500 merchant gift cards, saving users up to 10% per transaction on top of existing deals and promotions. By democratizing access to seamless, cost-effective payments, Snaplii empowers AI agents to engage with the physical world in a more efficient and streamlined way.
