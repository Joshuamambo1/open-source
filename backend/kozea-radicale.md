## 📝 Summary

Here is a 2-3 sentence summary for the Kozea/Radicale project:

Radicale is an open-source CalDAV and CardDAV server that enables users to manage their calendars and contacts across multiple devices. This simple and lightweight server supports standard protocols for calendar and contact synchronization, making it a great solution for small to medium-sized deployments. With Radicale, users can easily share and access their calendar and contact information online.
