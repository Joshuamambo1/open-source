## 📝 Summary

KeyMapper is an open-source Android application that allows users to remap buttons on their devices, granting greater flexibility and customization options. With this app, users can assign new functions to buttons, keys, or even gestures, tailoring their device to their specific needs and preferences. By providing a high degree of customization, KeyMapper empowers users to optimize their Android experience.
