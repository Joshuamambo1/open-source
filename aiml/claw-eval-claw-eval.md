## 📝 Summary

Claw-Eval is an open-source evaluation harness designed to assess the performance of Large Language Models (LLMs) as agents in various tasks. By leveraging human-verified tasks, Claw-Eval provides a reliable framework for evaluating the capabilities of LLMs and fostering their development. This tool aims to standardize the evaluation process, facilitating the comparison and improvement of LLMs across different applications.
