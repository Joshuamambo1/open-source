OpenCart is an open-source, PHP-based e-commerce platform that provides a free shopping cart system for online businesses. This versatile solution allows merchants to easily create and manage their online stores, offering a range of customizable features and extensions to suit various needs. With OpenCart, entrepreneurs can establish a professional online shopping experience without incurring significant development costs.
