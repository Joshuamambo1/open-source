## 📝 Summary

This Java library, creditcardnumber, is an open-source project that provides a programmatic way to extract and analyze details from bank-issued credit card numbers. It enables developers to easily verify the validity of credit card numbers, identify card types, and retrieve associated information such as cardholder names, expiration dates, and more. By leveraging this library, developers can streamline payment processing and enhance their applications with robust credit card validation capabilities.
