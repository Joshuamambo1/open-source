## 📝 Summary

The **Open‑Plan Annotator** is an open‑source, local‑first tool that lets users create, edit, and enrich agent plans with rich annotations without relying on external services. Built with privacy‑by‑design principles, it processes plans entirely on the user's machine, making it ideal for AI/ML workflows that require secure, offline handling of planning data.
