## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the NJUThesis project:

NJUThesis is an open-source thesis template developed for Nanjing University students. It provides a standardized and easily customizable format for writing academic theses, saving students time and effort in formatting their documents. This project aims to facilitate the writing process for Nanjing University students and promote academic excellence.
