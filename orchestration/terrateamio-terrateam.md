Terrateam is an open‑source GitOps platform that streamlines infrastructure orchestration by linking GitHub pull‑request workflows with Terraform, OpenTofu, CDKTF, Terragrunt, and Pulumi. It automates plan, apply, and validation steps directly from code reviews, enabling teams to manage IaC safely and consistently. The project sits at the intersection of DevOps, infrastructure automation, and AI‑assisted operations.
