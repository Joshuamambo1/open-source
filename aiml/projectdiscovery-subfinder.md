## 📝 Summary

Project subfinder is an open-source, fast passive subdomain enumeration tool designed for discovering potential attack surfaces of an organization. It leverages multiple data sources to identify subdomains, making it a valuable asset for security researchers and penetration testers. By providing a comprehensive list of potential subdomains, subfinder helps organizations to identify and mitigate potential vulnerabilities.
