## 📝 Summary

sameer/svg2gcode is an open-source project that enables the conversion of vector graphics into g-code files, making it compatible with pen plotters, laser engravers, and other CNC machines. This project facilitates the translation of SVG files into machine-readable code, streamlining the process of creating custom designs and projects. By leveraging this tool, users can efficiently produce intricate designs and patterns with precision and accuracy.
