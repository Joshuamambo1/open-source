## 📝 Summary

### English

FinRobot is an open‑source platform that equips users with a customizable AI agent for financial analysis, leveraging large language models (LLMs) to retrieve data, generate insights, and automate reporting tasks. It provides modular tools for data ingestion, market sentiment extraction, portfolio evaluation, and decision‑support workflows, making advanced AI‑driven finance accessible to developers and analysts alike.
