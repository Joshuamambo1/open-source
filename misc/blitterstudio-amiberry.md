## 📝 Summary

BlitterStudio/amiberry is a high‑performance, open‑source emulator that faithfully recreates the Amiga computer environment on modern hardware. It focuses on speed and compatibility, offering extensive configuration options, hardware acceleration, and support for a wide range of Amiga models and peripherals. The project aims to provide an optimized platform for both casual gaming and development on classic Amiga software.
