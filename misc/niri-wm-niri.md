## 📝 Summary

Niri is an open-source Wayland compositor that supports tiling window management and scrollability. It provides a customizable and efficient desktop environment for users who prefer a flexible and interactive experience. Niri is designed to work seamlessly with Wayland protocols, making it a viable alternative to traditional desktop environments.
