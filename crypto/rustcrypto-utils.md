## 📝 Summary

RustCrypto/utils is an open-source collection of utility crates used in the RustCrypto project, providing essential functionality for cryptographic applications. These utility crates aim to simplify and standardize common cryptographic tasks, making it easier to implement secure and efficient cryptographic protocols. By offering a shared foundation for cryptographic development, RustCrypto/utils facilitates collaboration and contributes to the advancement of cryptographic research and implementation.
