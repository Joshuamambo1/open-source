## 📝 Summary

Grafana Kiosk is an open-source utility designed to transform Grafana into a secure, locked-down kiosk mode for mobile devices. This feature enables organizations to securely display Grafana dashboards on public-facing devices, such as in-store kiosks or event displays, without worrying about unauthorized access or tampering. By leveraging Grafana Kiosk, users can ensure that their data remains secure and only accessible through authorized channels.
