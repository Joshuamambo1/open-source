## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the iBUHub/AIStudioToAPI project:

iBUHub/AIStudioToAPI is an open-source wrapper that allows developers to leverage Google AI Studio's capabilities through compatible APIs, mirroring those of OpenAI, Gemini, and Anthropic. This tool enables seamless integration with existing AI models and services, promoting interoperability and flexibility in AI development. By wrapping AI Studio in a compatible API, developers can tap into a broader range of AI capabilities from a single interface.
