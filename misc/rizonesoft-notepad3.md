## 📝 Summary

Notepad3 is a lightweight, Scintilla‑based text editor that offers the classic Notepad experience with modern features such as syntax highlighting, line numbering, and customizable themes. It builds on the codebases of Notepad2 and MiniPath (itself derived from metapath), delivering a fast, portable editor for Windows users. The project is open‑source and categorized under miscellaneous utilities.
