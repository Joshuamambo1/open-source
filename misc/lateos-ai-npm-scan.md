## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary of the NPM-Scan project:

NPM-Scan is an open-source tool designed to detect and prevent common security threats in npm packages, including dependency confusion, typosquatting, and credential harvesting. This tool helps developers identify and mitigate potential vulnerabilities in their dependency chains, ensuring the security and integrity of their applications. By utilizing NPM-Scan, developers can proactively identify and address security risks before they become major issues.
