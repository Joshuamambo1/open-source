## 📝 Summary

RetroArch is a cross-platform, open-source frontend for the libretro API, offering a sophisticated and versatile platform for emulating a wide range of classic video games and consoles. With its GPLv3 license, RetroArch allows users to run their favorite retro games on various devices, from PC to handheld consoles. This powerful frontend provides a customizable and extensible environment for accessing and playing classic games.
