## 📝 Summary

The Siemens Industrial Experience (ix) is an open-source design system created to streamline the development of digital experiences for industrial software products. This design system serves as a comprehensive resource for designers and developers, enabling them to consistently craft intuitive and high-quality interfaces. By leveraging ix, users can efficiently create unified digital products that meet the unique needs of industrial applications.
