## 📝 Summary

The bitxeno/atvloadly project is an open-source tool designed to simplify the process of sideloading IPAs onto Apple TV devices. This allows users to bypass traditional app store restrictions and load custom or third-party applications onto their Apple TV. With atvloadly, users can easily transfer and install IPA files on their Apple TV.
