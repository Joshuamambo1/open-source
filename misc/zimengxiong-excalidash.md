## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary of the ExcaliDash project:

ExcaliDash is an open-source, self-hosted dashboard and organizer designed for Excalidraw users. It enables multi-user collaboration and scoped sharing, allowing teams to work together seamlessly on Excalidraw files. This project provides a centralized platform for managing Excalidraw content, enhancing productivity and collaboration within organizations.
