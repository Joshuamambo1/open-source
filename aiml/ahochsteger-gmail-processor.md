## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary of the Gmail Processor project:

The Gmail Processor is an open-source, Google Apps Script library that enables automated processing of Gmail messages and attachments based on customizable criteria. This powerful tool allows users to execute various actions such as storing attachments in Google Drive or logging information in a spreadsheet, streamlining email management and enhancing productivity. With its flexible design, Gmail Processor is suitable for a wide range of applications, including AI/ML and marketing workflows.
