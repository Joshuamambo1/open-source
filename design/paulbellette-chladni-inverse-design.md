## 📝 Summary

This open-source project, titled Inverse Chladni Energy Landscape Design, utilizes creative computational methods to design visually striking patterns inspired by the work of Ernst Chladni, an 18th-century physicist. By inverting traditional design approaches, the project generates intricate energy landscapes that could be applied in various fields such as art, design, and even architecture. The project's open-source nature allows designers and researchers to explore and contribute to this innovative design technique.
