## 📝 Summary

The rubyforgood/casa project is an open-source volunteer management system designed specifically for Court Appointed Special Advocates (CASA) organizations, which provide critical support to foster youth across the United States. This platform aims to streamline volunteer coordination and management, ultimately enhancing the lives of those served by CASA. By leveraging AI/ML and DevOps/Infra expertise, the project seeks to create a scalable and efficient solution for CASA organizations nationwide.
