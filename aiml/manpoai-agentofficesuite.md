## 📝 Summary

ManpoAI AgentOfficeSuite (AOSE) is an open‑source, web‑based office suite designed for seamless collaboration between human users and AI agents. It provides fully editable documents, spreadsheets, presentations, flowcharts, canvas boards, and video components, all built with a modern frontend that lets AI assistants interact with and modify content in real time. The platform targets AI/ML and frontend developers seeking to integrate intelligent agents into everyday productivity tools.
