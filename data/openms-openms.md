## 📝 Summary

OpenMS is an open-source software framework for mass spectrometry data analysis. It provides a comprehensive set of tools and algorithms for the processing, analysis, and visualization of large-scale mass spectrometry data. By offering a flexible and modular architecture, OpenMS enables researchers to seamlessly integrate their own algorithms and workflows into the framework.
