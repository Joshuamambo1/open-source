## 📝 Summary

The Online-Forever project is an open-source initiative that aims to keep Discord accounts online 24/7, ensuring users never miss important messages or events. By utilizing the project's backend functionality, users can maintain a persistent online presence without the need for manual intervention. This tool is particularly useful for communities and individuals who rely on real-time communication in Discord.
