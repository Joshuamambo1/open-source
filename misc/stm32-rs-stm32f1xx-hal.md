## 📝 Summary

### English

The **stm32-rs/stm32f1xx-hal** crate provides a pure‑Rust hardware abstraction layer (HAL) for the STM32F1 microcontroller family, built on top of the `embedded‑hal` traits and extending the original work from japaric’s `stm32f103xx-hal`. It offers type‑safe, zero‑cost drivers for peripherals such as GPIO, timers, I²C, SPI, and USART, enabling rapid development of embedded applications while preserving full control over low‑level hardware. The library is open‑source, actively maintained, and integrates seamlessly with the broader Rust embedded ecosystem.
