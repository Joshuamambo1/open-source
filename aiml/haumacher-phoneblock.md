Here's a brief summary for the open-source project haumacher/phoneblock:

This innovative tool aims to provide users with a more controlled and secure mobile experience, free from the nuisance of spam. By leveraging AI/ML capabilities, phoneblock offers an effective solution to combat the growing problem of mobile spam.
