## 📝 Summary

VirtualC64 is an open-source, cycle-accurate Commodore 64 emulator for macOS, designed to provide a precise and authentic gaming experience. Developed by dirkwhoffmann, this project is ideal for retro gaming enthusiasts seeking an accurate representation of the classic C64 console. By emulating the original hardware with precision, VirtualC64 allows users to play their favorite C64 games on modern computers.
