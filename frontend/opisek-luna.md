## 📝 Summary

Luna is an open-source, self-hosted calendar aggregator and frontend solution, providing users with a centralized platform to manage multiple calendars. This Frontend project allows users to consolidate their personal and professional schedules in one place, promoting organization and productivity. By hosting Luna locally, users maintain control over their calendar data and can customize the experience to suit their needs.
