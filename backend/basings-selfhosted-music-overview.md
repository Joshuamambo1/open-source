## 📝 Summary

This project, Self-Hosted Music Overview, provides a comprehensive table of software network services that can be hosted on your own servers, offering a one-stop resource for individuals looking to create a personalized music streaming setup. The table includes a range of services, from music libraries and players to streaming platforms and more. By self-hosting these services, users can maintain control and customization over their music experience.
