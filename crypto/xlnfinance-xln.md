## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary of the open-source project:

xln is a Layer 2 (L2) network designed for credit and collateral netting, enabling seamless transactions across various EVM (Ethereum Virtual Machine) and CBDC (Central Bank Digital Currency) jurisdictions. This innovative protocol ensures efficient and secure transactions with O(1) latency per hop, while also mitigating link-capped risk and maintaining local state control. The xln network aims to provide a robust and sovereign infrastructure for future payment systems.
