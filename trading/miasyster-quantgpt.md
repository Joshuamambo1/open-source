## 📝 Summary

QuantGPT is an open-source project that leverages Large Language Models (LLMs) to automate the alpha discovery process. It enables LLMs to autonomously design, backtest, and submit trading factors to the WorldQuant BRAIN platform, streamlining the alpha factory workflow. This project combines AI/ML capabilities with trading applications to facilitate the development of quantitative trading strategies.
