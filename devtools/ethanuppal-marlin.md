## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the open-source project:

Marlin is an open-source hardware testing and simulation framework developed in Rust, providing a no-nonsense approach to verifying the behavior of digital circuits. It supports various hardware description languages, including Verilog, Spade, and Veryl, making it a versatile tool for digital circuit designers and testers. Marlin aims to simplify the hardware testing and simulation process, making it easier to identify and fix issues in digital circuit designs.
