## 📝 Summary

This open-source project, "discord-rich-presence-plex", allows users to display their current Plex status on Discord using Rich Presence, enabling friends and family to see what they're watching or listening to in real-time. The project integrates Plex with Discord, providing a seamless and convenient way to share media activity. By using this tool, users can easily keep their friends informed about their media consumption.
