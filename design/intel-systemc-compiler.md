## 📝 Summary

### English

intel/systemc-compiler is an open‑source translation tool that converts synthesizable SystemC models into equivalent synthesizable SystemVerilog code, enabling designers to move seamlessly between high‑level C++‑based hardware descriptions and industry‑standard RTL. It automates the generation of Verilog‑compatible RTL while preserving synthesis semantics, facilitating verification, synthesis, and integration into existing FPGA/ASIC design flows.
