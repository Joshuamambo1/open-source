## 📝 Summary

The libretro/beetle-psx-libretro project provides a standalone port of Mednafen PSX, a popular PlayStation emulator, to the Libretro API, allowing for seamless integration with various frontends and platforms. This open-source project enables users to play PlayStation games on a wide range of devices, from computers to handheld consoles, with high fidelity and precision. By utilizing the Libretro API, the project offers a flexible and user-friendly solution for emulating PlayStation games on various platforms.
