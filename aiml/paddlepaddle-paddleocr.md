## 📝 Summary

PaddlePaddle/PaddleOCR is an open-source, lightweight OCR toolkit that enables the extraction of structured data from images and PDFs, bridging the gap between visual documents and large language models (LLMs). This powerful tool supports over 100 languages, making it a versatile solution for various applications, including data processing, marketing, and AI development. By leveraging PaddlePaddle/PaddleOCR, developers can efficiently convert unstructured visual content into actionable insights.
