## 📝 Summary

The daktilo project is an open-source initiative that transforms a standard keyboard into a functional typewriter emulator. This project mimics the experience of traditional typewriters, allowing users to engage with their digital devices in a nostalgic and tactile way. By recreating the look and feel of vintage typing, daktilo offers a unique interaction method for keyboard users.
