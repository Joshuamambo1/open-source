## 📝 Summary

FastVideo is an open-source framework designed to accelerate video generation by providing a unified platform for inference and post-training. It enables developers to leverage AI and machine learning capabilities for efficient video creation, streamlining the process from model training to final output. This framework aims to improve video generation speed and quality, making it suitable for various applications such as entertainment, education, and more.
