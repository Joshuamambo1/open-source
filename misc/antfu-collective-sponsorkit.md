## 📝 Summary

Sponsorkit is an open-source toolkit designed to help users generate high-quality sponsor images. This tool provides a convenient solution for creating visually appealing sponsor graphics, making it easier to showcase supporters and sponsors on websites, social media, or other platforms. By streamlining the process, Sponsorkit aims to make it simple for everyone to acknowledge and appreciate their sponsors.
