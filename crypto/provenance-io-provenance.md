## 📝 Summary

The Provenance blockchain is an open-source, distributed proof of stake platform specifically designed for the financial services industry. It aims to provide a secure, scalable, and transparent solution for financial institutions, leveraging advanced technologies such as AI and ML to enhance security and efficiency. By utilizing proof of stake consensus, Provenance promotes energy efficiency and faster transaction processing times.
