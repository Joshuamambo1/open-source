## 📝 Summary

Wezterm is a free, open-source terminal emulator and multiplexer that leverages GPU acceleration to provide a seamless and efficient user experience across various platforms. Written in Rust, this cross-platform application offers a high-performance terminal solution for users who require fast and responsive command-line interactions. With its GPU acceleration capabilities, wezterm is well-suited for demanding terminal applications.
