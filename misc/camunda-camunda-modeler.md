## 📝 Summary

Camunda Modeler is an open-source modeling solution for Business Process Model and Notation (BPMN), Decision Model and Notation (DMN), and Forms, built on bpmn.io. This tool serves as a companion to popular Integrated Development Environments (IDEs), aiding users in implementing solutions with Camunda. By leveraging Camunda Modeler, users can effectively design and implement business processes and decisions.
