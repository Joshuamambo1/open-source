MugglePay is an open-source project that enables seamless crypto-native payments, allowing merchants and AI agents to easily accept cryptocurrencies and increase sales through this innovative technology. By integrating crypto payments, MugglePay aims to bridge the gap between traditional payment methods and the growing demand for digital currencies. This project utilizes AI/ML capabilities to streamline the payment process and enhance user experience.
