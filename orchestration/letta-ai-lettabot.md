## 📝 Summary

Lettabot is an open-source AI assistant project that integrates with popular messaging platforms such as Telegram, Slack, WhatsApp, and Signal, allowing users to access and recall conversations and information across all connected services. This personal AI assistant, developed by letta-ai, uses orchestration and AI/ML capabilities to provide a seamless and unified experience. By leveraging these technologies, Lettabot enables users to easily search, organize, and access their conversations and data across multiple platforms.
