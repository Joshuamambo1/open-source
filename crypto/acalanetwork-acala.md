## 📝 Summary

The Acala Network is an open-source, cross-chain DeFi hub and stablecoin solution built on top of the Substrate framework for Polkadot and Kusama. It aims to provide a robust platform for decentralized financial applications, enabling seamless transactions and liquidity across multiple blockchain networks. By leveraging AI/ML capabilities, Acala seeks to enhance the efficiency and security of its DeFi hub.
