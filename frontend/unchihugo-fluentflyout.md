## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary of the open-source project:

FluentFlyout is an open-source Windows 11 app that leverages the Fluent 2 Design principles to provide a modern flyout experience. This project includes features such as Media Flyouts and Taskbar Widgets, allowing users to customize their Windows 11 desktop. It serves as a showcase for Fluent Design principles in action, providing a visually appealing and user-friendly interface.
