## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary of the project:

The codex-oauth-automation-extension is an open-source Chrome extension that automates OAuth registration and validation for OpenAI, including CAPTCHA solving and CPA callback verification. This extension aims to simplify the process of accessing OpenAI services by handling tedious authentication and verification tasks. By utilizing this extension, users can enhance their productivity and streamline their OpenAI development workflows.
