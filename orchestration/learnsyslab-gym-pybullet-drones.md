## 📝 Summary

The learnsyslab/gym-pybullet-drones project is an open-source initiative that provides PyBullet Gymnasium environments for training quadcopter control using single and multi-agent reinforcement learning techniques. These environments offer a simulated platform for researchers and developers to experiment with drone control, paving the way for advancements in autonomous flight and aerial robotics. By leveraging Gymnasium, the project facilitates seamless reinforcement learning workflows and fosters innovation in the field of drone control.
