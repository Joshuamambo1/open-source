## 📝 Summary

Here is a brief summary of the open-source project:

GitOps Promoter is an open-source tool that streamlines the process of promoting environments in a GitOps workflow, allowing developers to focus on defining desired states rather than the underlying implementation details. This tool aims to simplify the complex process of environment promotion, making it easier to manage and automate the deployment of applications across different environments. By abstracting away the complexities, GitOps Promoter enables teams to focus on what matters most: delivering high-quality software.
