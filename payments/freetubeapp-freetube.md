## 📝 Summary

FreeTube is an open-source, privacy-focused YouTube app that allows users to browse and watch their favorite videos without compromising their personal data. By providing a secure alternative to the official YouTube app, FreeTube gives users more control over their online presence and data protection. This free and customizable app is maintained by a community of developers who prioritize user privacy and security.
