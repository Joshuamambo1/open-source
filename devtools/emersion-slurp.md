## 📝 Summary

### English

**Summary:**  
`emersion/slurp` is a lightweight utility for Wayland compositors that lets users interactively select a rectangular region on the screen. It outputs the geometry of the chosen area, making it ideal for feeding coordinates into screenshot tools, screen recorders, or other automation scripts.
