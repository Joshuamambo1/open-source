## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the open-source project:

SmartBFT is an open-source consensus library that enables secure and efficient distributed consensus in decentralized systems. Developed by the Hyperledger Labs team, SmartBFT is based on the research paper "SmartBFT: Byzantine Fault Tolerance with Adaptive and Efficient Agreement" (2021) and provides a robust solution for achieving consensus in the presence of malicious actors. By leveraging cutting-edge cryptography and AI/ML techniques, SmartBFT aims to improve the scalability and resilience of decentralized networks.
