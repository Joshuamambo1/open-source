## 📝 Summary

DuckStation is an open-source PlayStation 1 emulator designed for various architectures, including x86-64, AArch32, AArch64, and RV64. This high-performance emulator prioritizes speed and compatibility, allowing users to play a wide range of PS1 games on different platforms. It is available as an open-source project, encouraging community contributions and development.
