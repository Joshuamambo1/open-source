## 📝 Summary

Sealdice-Core is an open-source, lightweight, and user-friendly TRPG dice bot designed for the next generation of tabletop role-playing games. It supports a wide range of platforms, including Windows, Linux, macOS, and Android, as well as various IM platforms, making it a versatile and accessible solution for players and game masters alike.
