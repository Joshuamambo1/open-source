## 📝 Summary

The multigres-operator is an open-source Kubernetes operator that simplifies the management of horizontally scalable PostgreSQL clusters. It provides automated topology orchestration, drain-safe rolling updates, and admission webhooks to ensure seamless cluster management and data availability. This operator enables efficient deployment, scaling, and maintenance of PostgreSQL clusters, making it an ideal solution for AI/ML and data-intensive applications.
