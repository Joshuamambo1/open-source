## 📝 Summary

`sorairolake/qrtool` is a lightweight command‑line utility that lets users generate QR codes from text or files and decode existing QR images back into their original data. It provides fast, script‑friendly operations for encoding and decoding QR codes, making it useful for automation, testing, and quick data exchange in development workflows.
