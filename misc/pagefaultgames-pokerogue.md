## 📝 Summary

"Pokerogue" is an open-source, browser-based Pokémon fangame that combines elements of the roguelite genre with the classic Pokémon experience. Players explore procedurally generated worlds, battling Pokémon and navigating randomized challenges in this unique take on the beloved franchise. The game offers a fresh, browser-based twist on the traditional Pokémon formula.
