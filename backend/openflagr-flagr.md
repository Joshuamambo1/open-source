## 📝 Summary

Flagr is an open-source, microservice-based solution for managing feature flagging, A/B testing, and dynamic configuration. It enables developers to easily control and experiment with different features of their applications in a flexible and scalable manner. With Flagr, teams can streamline their development processes and make data-driven decisions to optimize their products.
