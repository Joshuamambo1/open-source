## 📝 Summary

The seerge/g-helper is an open-source control tool designed for ASUS laptops and the ROG Ally. It provides a comprehensive management system for various settings, including performance modes, fan speeds, GPU settings, battery life, and RGB lighting, making it suitable for a wide range of ASUS models such as Zephyrus, Flow, TUF, Strix, and Scar. This lightweight tool offers users a convenient way to customize and optimize their devices for optimal performance and experience.
