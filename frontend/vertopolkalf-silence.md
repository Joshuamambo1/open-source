## 📝 Summary

A lightweight WinUI application that lets you mute your microphone system‑wide, affecting every program that uses audio input. It includes customizable sound cues, an on‑screen overlay, and various UI tweaks for a personalized experience. Ideal for users who need a quick, reliable way to silence their mic across all apps.
