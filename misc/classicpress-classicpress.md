## 📝 Summary

ClassicPress is an open-source content management system (CMS) designed specifically for creators, offering a lightweight and stable platform. Forked from WordPress, ClassicPress provides an instantly familiar user experience, making it easy for developers and users to transition from its parent CMS. Its streamlined approach allows for efficient website management and customization.
