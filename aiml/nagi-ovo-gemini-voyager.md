## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary of the Nagi-ovo/Gemini-Voyager project:

Nagi-ovo/Gemini-Voyager is an open-source extension that enhances the functionality of Google Gemini and AI Studio, offering a comprehensive suite of features including timeline navigation, folder management, a prompt library, and chat export capabilities. This all-in-one plugin aims to boost productivity and efficiency for users working with these AI-powered tools. By consolidating essential features into a single extension, users can streamline their workflow and maximize their AI Studio experience.
