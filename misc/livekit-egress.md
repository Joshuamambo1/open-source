The livekit/egress project is an open-source tool that facilitates the export and recording of WebRTC sessions and individual tracks. It allows developers to capture and process WebRTC data, enabling the creation of custom solutions for session management and data analytics. By leveraging egress, users can unlock new possibilities for WebRTC-based applications.
