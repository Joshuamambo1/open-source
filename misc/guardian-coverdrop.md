## 📝 Summary

Coverdrop is an open-source project that enables users to anonymously report sensitive information through a news app, safeguarding their identity. This secure platform allows individuals to 'blow the whistle' on important stories, providing a safe space for whistleblowers to share their concerns without fear of retaliation. By using Coverdrop, users can promote transparency and accountability within their communities.
