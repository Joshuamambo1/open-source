## 📝 Summary

Junhoyeo’s **tokscale** is a command‑line utility that monitors and aggregates token consumption across a wide range of AI coding assistants—including OpenCode, Claude Code, OpenClaw, Pi, Codex, Gemini, Cursor, AmpCode, Factory Droid, Kimi, and others. It features a global leaderboard and visual 2D/3D contribution graphs, giving developers quick insights into their usage patterns and comparative performance. The tool is positioned at the intersection of AI/ML, frontend visualization, and developer tooling.
