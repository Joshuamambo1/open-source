## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the bjarneo/cliamp project:

cliamp is an open-source, terminal-based music player that draws inspiration from the popular Winamp music player. This Orchestration project allows users to manage and play music from the comfort of their command line interface, making it a convenient option for those who prefer a minimalistic experience. With its ease of use and flexibility, cliamp offers a unique solution for music enthusiasts who want to control their music library from the terminal.
