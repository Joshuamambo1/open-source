## 📝 Summary

The folke/neoconf.nvim plugin is an open-source tool designed for Neovim users to efficiently manage their global and project-local settings. This plugin simplifies configuration management by providing a centralized way to store and apply settings across various projects and environments. By streamlining configuration, users can minimize errors and maintain consistency across their Neovim setups.
