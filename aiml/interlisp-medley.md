## 📝 Summary

Medley Interlisp is an open-source project focused on reviving the Interlisp programming language, a key precursor to the development of artificial intelligence and machine learning. The project aims to create a functional implementation of the language, leveraging its unique features and capabilities to advance AI research. By preserving and rebuilding this classic language, Medley Interlisp seeks to inspire new innovations in the field of AI and ML.
