## 📝 Summary

Urunc is an open-source, simple container runtime designed for unikernels, aiming to offer a comprehensive alternative to traditional container runtimes like runc. Its primary goal is to provide a flexible, lightweight environment for running AI and ML workloads on unikernels, enabling efficient and streamlined deployment. By leveraging unikernel technology, Urunc seeks to optimize performance and reduce overhead for resource-intensive AI and ML applications.
