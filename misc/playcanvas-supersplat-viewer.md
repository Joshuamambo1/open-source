## 📝 Summary

The supersplat-viewer is an open-source, user-friendly application designed for high-performance visualization of 3D Gaussian splats. This viewer allows for the efficient rendering and exploration of complex 3D data, providing a valuable tool for researchers and developers working with Gaussian splat-based techniques. By leveraging playcanvas, the viewer offers a seamless and interactive experience for users to navigate and analyze 3D data.
