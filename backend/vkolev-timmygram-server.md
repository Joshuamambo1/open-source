## 📝 Summary

This open-source project provides a self-hosted video feed solution for parents and caregivers, offering a secure and private way to keep an eye on children. The platform aims to give users control over their data and allow for customizable content filtering. By hosting the video feed locally, users can ensure that their children's online activity remains private and secure.
