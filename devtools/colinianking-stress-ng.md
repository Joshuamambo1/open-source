## 📝 Summary

stress-ng is an open-source project that allows users to stress test their computer system in various ways. It was designed to exercise physical subsystems and operating system kernel interfaces, providing a thorough assessment of a system's performance. This tool is useful for developers and system administrators who want to evaluate the reliability and stability of their computer systems under heavy loads.
