## 📝 Summary

Cronboard is an open-source, terminal-based dashboard designed to streamline cron job management for local systems and remote servers. It provides a user-friendly interface for creating, editing, and monitoring cron jobs, simplifying the process of automating tasks and reducing the risk of errors. With Cronboard, users can efficiently manage and visualize their cron jobs from a single terminal interface.
