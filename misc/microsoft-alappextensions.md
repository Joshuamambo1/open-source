## 📝 Summary

The Microsoft ALAppExtensions project is an open-source repository that facilitates collaboration on application add-ons and localization extensions for Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central. This project enables developers to contribute and share extensions that enhance the functionality and usability of Business Central, a cloud-based business management platform. By participating in this project, developers can help expand the capabilities of Business Central and improve its global reach through localization.
