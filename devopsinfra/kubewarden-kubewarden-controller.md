## 📝 Summary

Kubewarden is an open-source project that simplifies the management of admission policies in Kubernetes clusters, enhancing cluster security. It provides an easy-to-use interface for defining and enforcing policies, allowing users to customize access and permissions within their cluster. With Kubewarden, developers and administrators can streamline their admission control process and maintain a more secure and compliant Kubernetes environment.
