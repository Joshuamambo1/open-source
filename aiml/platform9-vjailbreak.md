## 📝 Summary

Platform9's vJailbreak is an open-source project that facilitates a seamless transition for VMware users to alternative hypervisors, allowing them to switch to a more customizable and cost-effective environment. This migration tool leverages AI and machine learning to ensure a smooth and efficient transfer of virtual machines and configurations. By utilizing vJailbreak, users can break free from VMware's proprietary ecosystem and explore alternative solutions that better suit their needs.
