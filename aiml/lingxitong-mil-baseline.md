## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the lingxitong/MIL_BASELINE project:

MIL_BASELINE is an open-source library that integrates multiple Multiple Instance Learning (MIL) methods into a unified framework, enabling researchers and developers to easily compare and build upon existing techniques. This library, submitted to ACM-MM 2024 and published in JMLR, provides a comprehensive platform for MIL research and applications. It aims to facilitate the adoption and advancement of MIL methods in various AI and ML tasks.
