## 📝 Summary

The open-agreements project is an open-source platform that enables users to fill and generate standard legal agreement templates, producing signable DOCX files for various purposes. It currently offers 25 templates, including non-disclosure agreements (NDAs), cloud terms, SAFEs, and NVCA financing documents. This tool streamlines the process of creating and managing legal agreements, making it accessible and efficient for developers and businesses.
