## 📝 Summary

### English

Meta Agents Research Environments (MARE) is an open‑source platform that lets researchers test AI agents in evolving, realistic scenarios rather than static benchmarks. It provides a suite of dynamic environments where agents must continuously adapt their strategies as new information emerges, closely mirroring real‑world challenges. The project spans orchestration, AI/ML, and mobile domains, offering extensible tools for building and evaluating adaptive agents.
