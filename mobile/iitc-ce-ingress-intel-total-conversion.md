## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary of the IITC-CE/ingress-intel-total-conversion project:

IITC-CE is an open-source user script that provides a total conversion of intel.ingress.com, offering a range of new features to enhance your experience on the site. This script aims to make it easier to extend and customize the Intel map, providing users with more flexibility and control. By extending the functionality of the original site, IITC-CE seeks to improve the overall usability and functionality of the Intel map.
