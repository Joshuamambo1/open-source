## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the open-source project:

Topolvm is an open-source, capacity-aware CSI (Container Storage Interface) plugin for Kubernetes. It enables users to integrate their Kubernetes clusters with storage systems, taking into account the capacity of each storage device to ensure efficient resource utilization and optimal performance. By leveraging Topolvm, users can simplify storage management and deployment in their Kubernetes environments.
