## 📝 Summary

MobiFlight is an open-source project that empowers users to design and build their own home cockpits for flight simulators, offering a flexible, affordable, and user-friendly approach. This project enables individuals to customize their cockpit experience, providing a personalized and immersive flight simulation environment. With MobiFlight, enthusiasts can bring their virtual flying dreams to life in a cost-effective and accessible way.
