## 📝 Summary

Here's a summary of the project in English:

OpenBiliClaw is an open-source alternative to Bilibili's recommendation algorithm, running on your personal computer. Unlike traditional click-based systems, it uses psychological profiling to understand your preferences and proactively discovers new, undiscovered content tailored to your interests. This AI/ML project aims to provide a more personalized and engaging experience for Bilibili users.
