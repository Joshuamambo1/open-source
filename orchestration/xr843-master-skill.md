## 📝 Summary

The Master-skill project is an open-source initiative that leverages AI/ML technology to generate teaching roles based on Chinese Buddhist classics. Powered by FoJin, this tool aims to recreate the wisdom and teachings of revered Buddhist masters, facilitating a deeper understanding of the subject matter. By harnessing the power of machine learning, Master-skill seeks to make these ancient teachings more accessible and engaging for modern audiences.
