## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the RoaringBitmap/CRoaring project:

RoaringBitmap/CRoaring is an open-source project providing high-performance Roaring bitmaps in C (and C++), optimized with SIMD instructions for maximum efficiency. It is widely adopted by prominent databases and analytics platforms, including Apache Doris, ClickHouse, and StarRocks. This library enables fast and memory-efficient storage and querying of large bitmasks.
