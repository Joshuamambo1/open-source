## 📝 Summary

The truST Platform is an open-source tooling suite that provides development and runtime support for IEC 61131-3 Structured Text (ST) programs. It enables the creation, compilation, and execution of industrial automation software, facilitating collaboration and efficiency in the development process. The platform offers comprehensive front-end, back-end, and developer tools to streamline the development of ST-based applications.
