## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the malbiruk/driftwm project:

Driftwm is an open-source, trackpad-first infinite canvas Wayland compositor that provides a unique and versatile workspace experience. With its focus on gesture-based navigation and seamless canvas interaction, driftwm is designed to simplify multitasking and productivity. This project offers a refreshing alternative to traditional window managers, catering to users seeking a modern and intuitive computing experience.
