## 📝 Summary

The bellingcat/toolkit is an open-source project that provides a collection of tools and resources for investigative journalists and researchers to aid in their fact-finding and verification processes. This toolkit offers a range of functionalities, including but not limited to, image and video analysis, data collection, and online research. By making these tools accessible, the bellingcat/toolkit aims to promote transparency, accountability, and investigative journalism.
