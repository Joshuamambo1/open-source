## 📝 Summary

Hallucinator is an open-source tool designed to identify potentially fabricated or hallucinated references in academic PDF papers. It aims to promote academic integrity and authenticity by detecting suspicious citation patterns and reference inconsistencies. By using Hallucinator, researchers and scholars can ensure the credibility of their sources and maintain the trustworthiness of academic research.
