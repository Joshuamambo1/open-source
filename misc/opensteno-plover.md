## 📝 Summary

Plover is an open-source stenotype engine designed to help users master the stenotype writing method. This free software translates stenotype inputs into written text, allowing users to focus on developing their stenographic skills. With Plover, individuals can practice and improve their stenotype writing in a flexible and customizable environment.
