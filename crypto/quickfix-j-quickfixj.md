## 📝 Summary

QuickFIX/J is an open-source messaging engine that implements the FIX protocol, a widely-used standard for electronic communication in the financial industry. This Java-based solution provides a full-featured and reliable platform for trading and messaging applications, catering to the needs of cryptocurrency and trading businesses. With its robust functionality and open-source nature, QuickFIX/J enables developers to build scalable and efficient FIX protocol compliant systems.
