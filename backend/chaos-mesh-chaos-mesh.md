## 📝 Summary

Chaos Mesh is an open-source Chaos Engineering platform designed to help operators and developers identify potential issues in Kubernetes clusters. It enables users to inject faults into their systems to test resilience, detect bottlenecks, and improve overall system reliability. By simulating failures and observing system behavior, users can proactively address weaknesses before they become critical issues.
