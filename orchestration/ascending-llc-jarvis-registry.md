Here's a brief summary for the open-source project:

This MCP/Agent gateway provides built-in identity, access control, and full observability, streamlining the connection process while maintaining security and visibility. With Jarvis Registry, businesses can confidently connect their AI and autonomous systems to various tools, enhancing productivity and efficiency.
