## 📝 Summary

The hys project is a terminal-based RSS reader built with Zig, designed for digital minimalists seeking to break free from the cycle of endless scrolling. This open-source tool allows users to stay informed without getting overwhelmed by the constant stream of information, promoting a more mindful approach to online consumption. By reading RSS feeds in a distraction-free terminal environment, users can regain control over their online experience.
