## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the project:

Agentle is an open-source framework for building production-ready AI agents quickly and efficiently. With its minimal code requirements and Python 3.13+ compatibility, developers can create AI assistants and complex multi-agent systems in days, rather than months, and deploy them with confidence. This platform streamlines the development process, saving time and reducing the complexities associated with large-scale AI deployments.
