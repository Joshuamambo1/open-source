## 📝 Summary

Here's a 2-3 sentence summary for the open-source project:

Feikong Teams is an open-source, multi-agent collaborative AI assistant that supports both command-line and web interfaces. It offers three modes of operation: team-based collaboration, customizable conference-based collaboration, and multi-agent discussion modes (roundtable conferences), all of which leverage the power of multiple specialized agents working together to tackle complex programming and system tasks. This project enables efficient and collaborative AI-driven workflows through orchestration and AI/ML capabilities.
