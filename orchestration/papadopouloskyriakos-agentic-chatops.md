## 📝 Summary

This open-source project, "agentic-chatops," is a 3-tier implementation of ChatOps, utilizing n8n, GPT-4, and Claude Code to automate tasks and streamline workflows. Based on the "Agentic Design Patterns," it integrates 21 patterns to enable efficient management of complex systems. The project demonstrates the ability to manage 137 devices with a single operator, showcasing its potential for scalability and automation.
