## 📝 Summary

This open-source project is a multi-platform port of the updated version of The Legend of Zelda: Link's Awakening DX in high definition (HD), originally released for the Game Boy Color. The project provides patches for Windows, Android, and Linux, making it accessible on a wide range of devices. It offers a nostalgic gaming experience with enhanced visuals, catering to fans of the classic Zelda series.
