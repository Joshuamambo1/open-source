## 📝 Summary

**clawd‑on‑desk** is an open‑source, pixel‑art desktop companion that monitors the activity of AI coding assistants such as Claude Code, Codex, Cursor, and similar agents, keeping you informed about their actions without constant manual checking. It runs quietly on your desktop, displaying real‑time status updates and notifications so you can stay focused on your work while the pet “watches” the AI tools for you.
