## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the HyperList project:

HyperList is an open-source, text-based user interface (TUI) designed to efficiently manage and edit HyperLists, a type of hierarchical note-taking system. This powerful tool enables users to create, edit, and organize their HyperLists with ease, making it an ideal solution for those who prefer a command-line interface. With its flexible and customizable design, HyperList is perfect for users seeking a streamlined and efficient way to manage their notes and tasks.
