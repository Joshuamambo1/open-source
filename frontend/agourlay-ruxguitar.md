## 📝 Summary

The ruxguitar project is an open-source, web-based Guitar Pro tablature player designed for users to view, play, and practice their favorite guitar tabs. This frontend-focused application enables users to easily access and interact with Guitar Pro files, making it a valuable resource for musicians and guitar enthusiasts. By utilizing ruxguitar, users can enhance their music practice and exploration experience.
