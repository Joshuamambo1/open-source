## 📝 Summary

The yc-bench project is an open-source benchmark designed to evaluate AI agent capabilities in managing resources and maximizing returns over long horizons, mimicking the challenges of a tech startup founder. This simulated time benchmark enables developers to test and compare the performance of their AI solutions in a realistic and controlled environment. By leveraging yc-bench, the AI community can advance the development of more effective and efficient resource management strategies.
