## 📝 Summary

Lambda ERP is an open-source enterprise resource planning (ERP) system that allows users to interact and manage their business operations through a chat interface. This innovative solution provides a user-friendly and accessible way to manage tasks, projects, and resources, leveraging the convenience of chat-based communication. By offering a flexible and customizable platform, Lambda ERP aims to simplify business management for organizations of all sizes.
