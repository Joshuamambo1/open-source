## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary of the Shadowbroker project:

Shadowbroker is an open-source intelligence platform that aggregates and unifies various sources of global information, including corporate jet tracking, spy satellite data, seismic events, and more. This comprehensive interface provides a centralized hub for knowledge, making it accessible to the public for the first time. By leveraging AI and machine learning, Shadowbroker aims to facilitate a deeper understanding of global events and activities.
