## 📝 Summary

Hortusfox-web is an open-source, self-hosted platform designed to help plant enthusiasts manage and track their plant collections. This collaborative system allows users to organize and monitor their plants, making it an ideal tool for gardeners, nurseries, and botanical enthusiasts. By hosting Hortusfox-web on their own servers, users can maintain control and flexibility over their plant data.
