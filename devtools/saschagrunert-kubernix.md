## 📝 Summary

Kubernix is an open-source project that enables the creation of isolated, single-dependency Kubernetes clusters for local testing, experimentation, and development purposes. This tool simplifies the process of spinning up a Kubernetes environment, allowing developers to focus on their projects without the complexity of a full-fledged cluster setup. With Kubernix, developers can quickly and easily spin up test clusters for prototyping and validation.
