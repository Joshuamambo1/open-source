## 📝 Summary

HabitRPG/habitica-ios is an open-source, native iOS app that brings the popular Habitica experience to mobile devices. This app allows users to track their habits, to-do lists, and daily activities within a fantasy-themed environment, providing a fun and engaging way to stay organized and motivated. By utilizing an open-source model, the community can contribute to the app's development and improve its features.
