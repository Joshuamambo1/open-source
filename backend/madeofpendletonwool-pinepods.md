## 📝 Summary

PinePods is a free, open-source podcast management system designed for self-hosting on your own server. This comprehensive platform enables users to play, download, and track their favorite podcasts in a centralized and customizable environment. With PinePods, you can take control of your podcast library and enjoy seamless management of your favorite shows.
