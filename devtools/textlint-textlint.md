## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the textlint project:

Textlint is an open-source, pluggable linter designed to enforce quality and consistency in natural language text. It provides a flexible framework for developers to create custom linting rules, allowing for tailored grammar and style checks. With textlint, writers and developers can ensure high-quality text across various applications and projects.
