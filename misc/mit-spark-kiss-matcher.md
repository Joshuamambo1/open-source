### English

KISS‑Matcher is an open‑source library that provides fast, robust and scalable point‑cloud registration algorithms, together with ready‑to‑run ROS 2 SLAM demos. It focuses on simplicity (Keep It Simple, Stupid) while delivering high‑performance matching suitable for real‑time robotics applications, and can be easily integrated into existing ROS 2 pipelines.
