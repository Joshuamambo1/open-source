## 📝 Summary

OpenCLAW / gogcli is a command‑line interface that brings the core Google Workspace services—Gmail, Google Calendar, Google Drive, and Google Contacts—directly into the terminal. It lets developers and power users list, search, create, modify, and delete resources across these services without leaving the shell, making automation and scripting of Google Suite tasks fast and reliable. The tool is open‑source, cross‑platform, and designed for developers who need a lightweight, scriptable alternative to the web UI.
