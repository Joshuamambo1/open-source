## 📝 Summary

### English

PreMiD/Activities is an open‑source library that provides a centralized storage system for “Activities” – reusable, metadata‑rich components used throughout the PreMiD ecosystem. It offers a structured activity catalog, easy retrieval APIs, and seamless integration with AI/ML workflows that consume or generate activity data. This makes it simple for developers to manage, share, and extend activity definitions across projects.
