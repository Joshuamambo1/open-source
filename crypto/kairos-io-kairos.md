## 📝 Summary

Kairos is an open-source, immutable Linux meta-distribution optimized for edge Kubernetes environments. It provides a secure and reliable foundation for deploying and managing edge computing applications, particularly those in AI/ML and DevOps/Infra contexts that require high levels of cryptography. By leveraging Kubernetes, Kairos enables streamlined deployment, scaling, and management of edge workloads.
