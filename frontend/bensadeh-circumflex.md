## 📝 Summary

The circumflex project is an open-source terminal application that brings the experience of Hacker News to your local command line. It provides a terminal-based interface to browse and interact with the Hacker News community, offering a unique way to engage with technology news and discussions. This project caters to developers and tech enthusiasts who prefer a more minimalist and accessible approach to browsing online content.
