## 📝 Summary

Ardour is an open-source digital audio workstation (DAW) that provides a comprehensive platform for recording, editing, and mixing audio. This project offers a mirror of the Ardour source code, allowing developers to access and contribute to the software's development. With Ardour, users can create and produce high-quality audio content, from music and podcasts to post-production and live recordings.
