## 📝 Summary

The watgbridge project is an open-source forwarder that enables seamless communication between WhatsApp and Telegram, allowing users to send and receive messages between the two platforms. Written in GoLang, this forwarder simplifies the process of bridging the two popular messaging services. By facilitating cross-platform communication, watgbridge enhances the flexibility and convenience of users.
