## 📝 Summary

EcuBus-Pro is an open-source, automotive ECU development tool providing comprehensive support for UDS, CAN-TP, DOIP, and LIN protocols. This powerful tool also features scripting capabilities similar to CAPL and HIL (Hardware-in-the-Loop) testing functionality, making it a versatile solution for automotive engineers and developers. With its open-source nature, EcuBus-Pro offers a flexible and customizable platform for ECU development and testing.
