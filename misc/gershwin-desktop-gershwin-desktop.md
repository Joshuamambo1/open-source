## 📝 Summary

Gershwin-desktop is an open-source, desktop environment built on the GNUstep framework, designed to provide a welcoming and accessible experience for users transitioning from other platforms. This project aims to deliver a user-friendly interface while maintaining the flexibility and customization options offered by the GNUstep foundation. By leveraging the strengths of GNUstep, Gershwin-desktop seeks to offer a unique and refreshing alternative for users seeking a free and open-source desktop environment.
