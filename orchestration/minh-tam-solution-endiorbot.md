## 📝 Summary

EndiorBot is an open-source, local-first AI agent orchestrator designed to streamline Software Development Life Cycle (SDLC) workflows. This tool-agnostic platform offers 14 role-based agents, 5 communication channels, and 39 unified commands to enhance automation and efficiency. Built under the MIT license, EndiorBot is a highly customizable solution for developers seeking to integrate AI capabilities into their workflows.
