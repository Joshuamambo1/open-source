## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the VieNeu-TTS project:

VieNeu-TTS is an open-source Vietnamese text-to-speech (TTS) project that enables real-time, on-device voice cloning with 24kHz audio quality. This AI/ML-based system allows users to convert written text into natural-sounding Vietnamese speech, facilitating seamless communication and accessibility. With its instant voice cloning capabilities, VieNeu-TTS has the potential to revolutionize speech technology in Vietnamese-speaking communities.
