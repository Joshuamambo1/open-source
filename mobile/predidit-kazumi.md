## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary of the Predidit/Kazumi project:

Predidit/Kazumi is an open-source, mobile app for anime and drama enthusiasts that utilizes custom rules for content collection. It features streaming capabilities, including live broadcast and real-time high-resolution video processing. Additionally, the app supports real-time subtitles and annotations (dannek) for an enhanced viewing experience.
