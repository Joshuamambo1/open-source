## 📝 Summary

This open-source project presents a Multi Kanban Task Board, designed to visualize and manage tasks across multiple boards and teams. Additionally, it includes a MCP (Master Control Panel) Server, allowing for centralized control and administration of the task board system. The project is built with a focus on scalability and ease of use, making it a valuable resource for teams and organizations looking to streamline their task management processes.
