## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary of the open-source project:

The webnovel-writer is an open-source, long-form web novel writing assistant built on top of Claude Code. It addresses the issues of "forgetfulness" and "hallucinations" in AI writing, enabling users to create serialized works of up to 2 million characters. This AI/ML tool supports large-scale, ongoing writing projects.
