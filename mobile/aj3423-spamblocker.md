## 📝 Summary

The SpamBlocker is an open-source Android application designed to block unwanted calls and texts, providing users with a simple and effective solution to manage their mobile communication. This feature-rich tool allows users to filter out unsolicited calls and messages, helping to minimize distractions and promote a healthier mobile experience. With its customizable settings and intuitive interface, SpamBlocker empowers users to take control of their phone's notifications.
