## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the ruzin/stenoai project:

stenoai is an open-source, privacy-focused AI notepad that utilizes locally hosted Small Language Models to create beautiful, confidential notes. Designed with professionals in mind, particularly those in government, finance, and legal sectors, stenoai ensures your sensitive data remains secure and private. By hosting models locally, users maintain full control over their data and its usage.
