## 📝 Summary

Here's a 3-sentence summary of the iPythoning/b2b-sdr-agent-template open-source project:

The iPythoning/b2b-sdr-agent-template is an open-source AI-powered Software-Defined Radio (SDR) template designed for B2B export, featuring a 10-stage sales pipeline and real-time automation capabilities. Built on OpenClaw, this multi-channel platform integrates WhatsApp, Telegram, and Email to streamline communication and sales processes. With its 4-engine memory and 10 cron jobs, this template offers a robust and efficient solution for businesses seeking to leverage AI-driven sales strategies.
