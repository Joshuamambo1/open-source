The AllanChain/zotero-arxiv-workflow project is an open-source tool designed to simplify the process of managing arXiv papers within the Zotero citation management platform. It enables users to easily import, organize, and maintain a collection of arXiv papers, making it an invaluable resource for researchers and scholars in the AI/ML community. This workflow streamlines research workflows by integrating arXiv with Zotero's robust citation management capabilities.
