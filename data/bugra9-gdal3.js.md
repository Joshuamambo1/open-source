## 📝 Summary

The gdal3.js project is an open-source, browser-based library that enables the conversion of geospatial data, including raster and vector formats, to various coordinate systems and formats. With this library, users can manipulate and transform geospatial data directly within their web applications without relying on server-side operations. By doing so, it facilitates seamless integration of geospatial capabilities within web-based projects and applications.
