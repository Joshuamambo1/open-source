## 📝 Summary

Rezivure/Grid-Mobile is a private location sharing mobile app available for both iOS and Android devices. It utilizes end-to-end encryption (E2EE) through the Matrix protocol to ensure secure sharing of users' locations. This open-source project enables individuals to share their location with trusted contacts while maintaining control over their personal data.
