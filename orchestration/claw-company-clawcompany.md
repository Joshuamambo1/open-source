## 📝 Summary

Clawcompany is an open-source operating system for artificial intelligence companies, designed to provide a comprehensive platform for AI/ML development and orchestration. It features a four-layer memory structure, allowing AI companies to store and recall information locally, and includes six customizable templates and thirty-eight predefined roles. With a simple command-line interface ("npx clawcompany"), users can easily deploy their AI company and start building their next-generation AI solution.
