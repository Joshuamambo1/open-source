## 📝 Summary

Disenchanter is an open-source tool designed to efficiently mass disenchant Champion Shards and other loot items in League of Legends. It streamlines the process, saving time and effort for players who frequently encounter these items. This tool is particularly useful for those who engage in high-level gameplay, such as summoner ranked matches or competitive play.
