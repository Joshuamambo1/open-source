## 📝 Summary

OpenKB is an open-source, large language model (LLM) knowledge base that allows users to easily store, organize, and query knowledge in a structured and scalable manner. This project is designed to facilitate the integration of LLMs with external knowledge bases, enabling the development of more robust and accurate AI applications. By providing an open framework for knowledge storage and retrieval, OpenKB aims to accelerate the advancement of AI research and development.
