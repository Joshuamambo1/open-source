## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the 4gray/iptvnator project:

IPTVNator is an open-source, cross-platform IPTV player application offering a range of features for a seamless viewing experience. It supports playback of m3u and m3u8 playlists, as well as additional functionalities such as favorites, a TV guide, and a TV archive/catchup feature. This application aims to provide an intuitive and feature-rich solution for users to access and manage their IPTV content.
