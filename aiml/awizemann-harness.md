## 📝 Summary

### English

Harness is an open‑source dev‑tool that lets you define a plain‑language goal and then uses a large‑language‑model agent to automatically drive the UI of iOS simulators, macOS 14+ apps, and web applications, reporting any points of friction it encounters. It combines AI/ML with Swift 6 to provide automated user‑testing and UI‑validation across Apple platforms and browsers, helping developers catch usability issues early in the development cycle.
