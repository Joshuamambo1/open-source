## 📝 Summary

ALVR is an open-source project that allows users to stream VR games from their PC to their headset over Wi-Fi, enabling a seamless gaming experience without the need for a direct connection or cables. This innovative solution provides a convenient and wireless way to enjoy high-quality VR gaming on a variety of headsets. By leveraging the power of their PC, users can experience immersive VR gameplay without the hassle of cables.
