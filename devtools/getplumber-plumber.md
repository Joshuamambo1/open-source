## 📝 Summary

The plumber project is an open-source Command Line Interface (CLI) tool designed to verify the compliance of GitLab CI/CD pipelines and repositories. It enables users to check their pipelines and repositories for adherence to predetermined standards and best practices. With plumber, users can streamline pipeline optimization and ensure their workflows are secure and efficient.
