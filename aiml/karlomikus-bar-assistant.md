## 📝 Summary

The karlomikus/bar-assistant project is an open-source, all-in-one solution for managing your home bar, providing a comprehensive platform for various bar-related tasks. This AI-powered backend system aims to streamline and enhance the home bar experience by offering features such as recipe suggestions, drink pairing recommendations, and inventory management. By leveraging AI and machine learning, bar-assistant strives to make bartending more accessible and enjoyable for enthusiasts of all levels.
