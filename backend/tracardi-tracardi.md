## 📝 Summary

Tracardi is an open‑source, API‑first customer data platform (CDP) designed to serve as a flexible, composable backend for businesses that need an affordable way to collect, unify, and act on customer data. It provides a modular architecture for ingesting, processing, and routing data across various services, making integration and customization straightforward for developers.
