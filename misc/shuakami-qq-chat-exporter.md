## 📝 Summary

The qq-chat-exporter is an open-source tool designed to automate the extraction and export of QQ chat records, emoticon packages, and image messages. It supports efficient backup and export of data in TXT and JSON formats, with compatibility for both QQ and NT QQ accounts. This utility simplifies the process of preserving and managing your QQ chat history.
