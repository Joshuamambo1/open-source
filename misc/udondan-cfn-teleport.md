## 📝 Summary

The cfn-teleport project is an open-source command-line tool designed to simplify the management of CloudFormation resources. It allows users to easily copy, update, and manage resources across and within stacks, streamlining the process of maintaining complex CloudFormation configurations. This tool aims to reduce the administrative burden associated with CloudFormation management.
