## 📝 Summary

The WeightWatcher is an open-source tool designed to predict the accuracy of Deep Neural Networks. It utilizes a novel approach to analyze the behavior of neural network weights, allowing developers to anticipate potential performance issues and improve model robustness. By leveraging this predictive capability, Data Scientists and Machine Learning Engineers can optimize their models more efficiently and effectively.
