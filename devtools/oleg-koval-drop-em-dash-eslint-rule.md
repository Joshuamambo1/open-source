## 📝 Summary

This open-source project aims to eradicate the overuse of the em dash (—) in coding by introducing a custom ESLint rule. The rule is designed to detect instances where multiple em dashes are used in a single line of code, a common issue often referred to as the "ChatGPT em dash." By integrating this rule, developers can maintain cleaner and more visually appealing codebases.
