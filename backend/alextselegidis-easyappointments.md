## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the open-source project:

Easy!Appointments is a self-hosted appointment scheduler that provides a simple and convenient way to manage appointments and bookings. This open-source project allows users to schedule appointments with ease, making it an ideal solution for small businesses, clinics, and other organizations that require a robust appointment management system. With Easy!Appointments, users can customize and deploy the solution on their own servers, ensuring data security and control.
