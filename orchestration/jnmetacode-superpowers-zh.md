## 📝 Summary

jnMetaCode’s **superpowers-zh** is a fully Chinese‑localized fork of the popular “superpowers” AI‑assisted coding framework (over 116 k stars). It adds six original China‑specific skills and integrates with a wide range of tools—including Claude Code, Copilot CLI, Hermes Agent, Cursor, Windsurf, Kiro, Gemini CLI, and others—so that these 16 AI programming assistants can operate effectively in Mandarin environments. The project focuses on orchestration, AI/ML, and developer tooling to boost productivity for Chinese‑speaking developers.
