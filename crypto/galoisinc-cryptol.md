## 📝 Summary

Cryptol is an open-source, formal language for specifying and verifying cryptographic protocols and algorithms. It allows users to express complex cryptographic concepts in a precise and readable way, facilitating the verification of their security and correctness. By using Cryptol, developers can design, analyze, and implement cryptographic systems with confidence in their reliability and security.
