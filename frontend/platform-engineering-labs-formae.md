Here's a brief summary for the project:

Formae is an open-source Infrastructure-as-Code (IaC) platform designed to simplify the management of complex infrastructure configurations. Built with future scalability in mind, Formae empowers users to define, deploy, and manage infrastructure through code, streamlining the process of infrastructure provisioning and management. This platform caters to DevOps and infrastructure teams seeking a flexible and efficient IaC solution.
