## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary of the open-source project:

Epistemic-protocols is an open-source initiative that aims to improve human-AI interaction quality by providing a framework for structured decision-making. This framework, centered around Claude Code, enables the integration of epistemic protocols that enhance transparency, accountability, and trust in AI-driven decision points. By promoting quality interactions between humans and AI, epistemic-protocols seeks to advance the development of reliable and trustworthy AI systems.
