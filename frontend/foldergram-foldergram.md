## 📝 Summary

Foldergram is an open-source, self-hosted platform for showcasing photos and videos in a visually appealing, Instagram-style gallery. It allows users to easily organize and share their favorite media within a folder-based structure, providing a seamless and customizable experience. By hosting Foldergram on your own infrastructure, you maintain full control over your content and its management.
