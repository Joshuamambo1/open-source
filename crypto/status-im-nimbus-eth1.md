## 📝 Summary

Nimbus is an open-source, Ethereum execution client designed for resource-restricted devices. It enables users to interact with the Ethereum network on low-end hardware, making it a valuable solution for constrained environments. By providing a lightweight Ethereum client, Nimbus aims to increase accessibility and usability of Ethereum for a broader range of users.
