## 📝 Summary

LLMForEverybody is an open‑source repository that demystifies large language models (LLMs) for a broad audience, offering clear explanations, practical insights, and interview‑ready knowledge. It serves as a go‑to resource for anyone preparing for LLM‑focused technical interviews—especially during the spring and autumn hiring seasons—enabling candidates to discuss model concepts confidently with recruiters and interviewers.
