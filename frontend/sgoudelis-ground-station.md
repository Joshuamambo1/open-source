## 📝 Summary

The Ground Station is an open-source, all-in-one satellite monitoring suite designed to provide a comprehensive platform for tracking and monitoring satellites. This frontend-focused project offers a user-friendly interface for accessing and analyzing data related to satellite positions, orbits, and other relevant information. By leveraging Ground Station, users can gain valuable insights into satellite operations and performance.
