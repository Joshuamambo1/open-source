Foundry-Local is an open-source project from Microsoft that focuses on artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning (ML) capabilities. This project aims to provide a local AI infrastructure, allowing users to deploy and manage AI workloads directly on their machines, enhancing data privacy and reducing latency. By leveraging Foundry-Local, users can enjoy more control and flexibility over their AI applications.
