## 📝 Summary

The cocotb project is an open-source, Python-based framework designed for verifying digital circuits and chip-level designs at the Register-Transfer Level (RTL). It enables users to write testbenches in Python, simplifying the process of verifying complex digital circuits and improving overall verification efficiency. By leveraging the Pytest testing framework, cocotb provides a robust and flexible environment for simulating and verifying digital designs.
