## 📝 Summary

MouseLand/cellpose is an open-source project that offers a generalist algorithm for cellular segmentation, capable of automatically identifying and outlining cells in images with high accuracy. This algorithm is designed to be user-friendly and allows for human-in-the-loop capabilities, enabling researchers to correct and refine segmentation results as needed. By leveraging AI and human expertise, cellpose empowers scientists to gain deeper insights into cellular structures and behaviors.
