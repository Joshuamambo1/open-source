## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary of the open-source project:

The project "s-theo/xx.theojs.cn" is an open-source collection of traditional Chinese spiritual texts, specifically focusing on the five arts of Chinese divination (shān yī, mìng xiàng, and bù) and classic literature. This repository aims to preserve and make accessible the cultural heritage of China's traditional knowledge and wisdom. It serves as a valuable resource for scholars, researchers, and enthusiasts interested in Chinese philosophy, history, and spirituality.
