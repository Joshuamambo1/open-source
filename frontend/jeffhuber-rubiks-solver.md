## 📝 Summary

This project, jeffhuber/rubiks-solver, is an open-source browser-based Rubik's Cube solver that allows users to upload a flat-net image of their cube and receive step-by-step instructions for solving it. The tool provides a convenient and accessible solution for individuals looking to solve their Rubik's Cube. With its front-end focused design, this project simplifies the process of cracking the cube.
