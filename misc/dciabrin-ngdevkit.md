## 📝 Summary

The ngdevkit is an open-source project focused on developing tools and software for the Neo-Geo arcade console. It aims to provide a platform for game developers, enthusiasts, and researchers to create, modify, and study Neo-Geo games, promoting the preservation and understanding of this classic gaming system. Through community-driven development, the project seeks to expand the capabilities and accessibility of the Neo-Geo.
