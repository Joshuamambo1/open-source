## 📝 Summary

The obsidian-llm-hub is an open-source Obsidian plugin that integrates various large language models for AI chat, workflow automation, and semantic search. With support for multiple backends, including Gemini, OpenAI, OpenRouter, Grok, Ollama, and CLI, users can leverage the capabilities of these models to enhance their research, productivity, and workflow. This plugin enables seamless interaction with AI-powered tools within the Obsidian knowledge management platform.
