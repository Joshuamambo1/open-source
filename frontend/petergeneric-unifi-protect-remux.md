## 📝 Summary

This open-source project, "unifi-protect-remux," enables the conversion of Ubiquiti's proprietary video files (.ubv) into standard MP4 format. This conversion allows users to freely play and manage these videos without being restricted by the proprietary format. By utilizing this tool, users can easily access and manipulate their video footage from Ubiquiti's UniFi Protect system.
