## 📝 Summary

Rundeck is an open-source project that enables self-service operations by providing users with access to existing tools, services, and scripts. This orchestration platform allows administrators to grant specific users the ability to execute and manage tasks, promoting automation and efficiency in their workflow. By using Rundeck, teams can streamline their operations and enhance collaboration.
