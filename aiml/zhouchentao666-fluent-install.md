## 📝 Summary

Fluent‑Install is a free, open‑source front‑end tool derived from the backend of cai‑install, designed to streamline the process of adding items to a SteamTools library. Built by the zhouchentao666 community, it offers a custom “fluent” interface for efficient library management and is packaged under the Chinese name “流畅入库” (Fluent Install). Although categorized under AI/ML, its primary focus is on providing a user‑friendly, automated workflow for SteamTools inventory handling.
