## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the EKKOLearnAI/hermes-web-ui project:

Hermes-web-ui is an open-source web dashboard for the Hermes Agent, a multi-platform AI chat solution that offers seamless session management, scheduled jobs, usage analytics, and customizable channel configurations for popular messaging platforms like Telegram, Discord, Slack, and WhatsApp. This user-friendly interface enables administrators to efficiently manage their AI chat operations and gain valuable insights into their platform's usage. By harnessing the power of Hermes-web-ui, users can streamline their AI chat experiences and improve their overall customer engagement.
