## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the warreth/gPhotos2Immich project:

This open-source project, gPhotos2Immich, enables seamless synchronization of Google Photos albums with Immich, a platform for preserving and sharing memories. By automating the transfer of albums, users can easily keep their Google Photos content up-to-date on Immich. This tool streamlines the process of maintaining a backup of cherished moments on a decentralized platform.
