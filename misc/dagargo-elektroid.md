## 📝 Summary

The elektroid project is an open-source sample and MIDI device manager designed for users who require a simple yet effective tool to manage their audio and MIDI devices. This project allows users to efficiently connect, control, and monitor various sample and MIDI devices, streamlining creative workflows. With elektroid, artists and producers can focus on producing high-quality content without the hassle of device management.
