Dependabot/cli is an open-source tool designed to facilitate testing and debugging of Dependabot update jobs. This utility enables developers to simulate and verify the behavior of Dependabot updates, ensuring seamless integration with their existing workflows. By using dependabot/cli, developers can streamline their Dependabot configuration and resolve potential issues.
