## 📝 Summary

Here is a brief summary of the open-source project:

This project, "swift-paperless," is a native iOS app that allows users to interact with their Paperless-ngx installation, providing a seamless way to manage documents on-the-go. Developed using Swift, the app offers a convenient mobile solution for users of the Paperless-ngx platform. With this app, users can easily access and manage their documents from their iOS device.
