## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary of the TradingGoose-Studio project:

TradingGoose-Studio is an open-source project that combines technical analysis with AI-powered agents to create automated trading workflows from live market data. This innovative platform enables users to harness the power of artificial intelligence and orchestrate trading decisions in real-time, leveraging the strengths of both human expertise and machine learning. By integrating AI agents with market data, TradingGoose-Studio aims to revolutionize the way traders make informed decisions.
