## 📝 Summary

The Conduit project is an open-source mobile client for OpenWebUI, enabling users to interact with their self-hosted AI directly on their iOS or Android devices. This mobile application provides a convenient interface for users to engage with their AI models, promoting seamless and on-the-go communication. By offering a mobile client for OpenWebUI, Conduit expands the accessibility of self-hosted AI capabilities.
