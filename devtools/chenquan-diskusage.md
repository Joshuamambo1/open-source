## 📝 Summary

The diskusage tool is an open-source utility that provides a fast and efficient way to analyze disk usage across Linux, MacOS, and Windows systems. It enables users to identify the largest directories or files on their storage devices, helping to optimize disk space management and troubleshoot storage issues. This tool is ideal for developers and system administrators seeking a reliable DevTools solution.
