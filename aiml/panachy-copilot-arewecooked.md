## 📝 Summary

Copilot-arewecooked is an open-source project that aims to raise awareness about the costs associated with using AI tools, particularly in relation to the impending June 1 deadline for paying credit fees for AI usage. This project seeks to educate users about the financial implications of relying on AI and encourages them to be more mindful of their AI credit costs. By making this information more accessible, Copilot-arewecooked hopes to help users better manage their AI expenses.
