## 📝 Summary

Here's a 2-3 sentence summary for the project:

Gemini-watermark-remover is an open-source, client-side tool that efficiently removes Gemini AI watermarks using a mathematically precise Reverse Alpha Blending algorithm. This JavaScript-based tool provides a reliable and predictable solution, eliminating the need for unpredictable AI inpainting methods. It's designed for developers and users looking to remove unwanted watermarks from Gemini AI-generated images.
