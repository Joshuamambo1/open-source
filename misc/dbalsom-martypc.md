## 📝 Summary

MartyPC is an open-source, Rust-based emulator for the IBM PC/XT, a classic 8-bit computer from the 1980s. This emulator provides a digital recreation of the original PC/XT, allowing users to run vintage software and experience the early days of personal computing. By leveraging Rust's performance and safety features, MartyPC offers a reliable and efficient way to relive the history of computing.
