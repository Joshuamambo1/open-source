## 📝 Summary

Dry-transaction is an open-source, business transaction DSL (Domain Specific Language) that provides a robust and flexible way to manage complex business logic within Ruby applications. It enables developers to write predictable, testable, and maintainable code by encapsulating transactions and their associated actions. By using dry-transaction, developers can break down complex business processes into smaller, reusable, and composable units of code.
