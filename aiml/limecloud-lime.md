## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary of the open-source project "lime":

Lime is an open-source AI-powered creative workspace that streamlines the entire creative process, from idea generation to publication, into a single platform. It combines inspiration, writing, graphic design, revision, and refinement into a seamless workflow, allowing creators to focus on their craft without switching between multiple tools. With Lime, users can go from concept to completion with ease and efficiency.
