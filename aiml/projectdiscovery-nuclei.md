## 📝 Summary

Here's a 2-3 sentence summary for the projectdiscovery/nuclei project:

Nuclei is a fast and customizable open-source vulnerability scanner that leverages the collective expertise of the global security community. Built on a simple YAML-based Domain Specific Language (DSL), Nuclei empowers users to discover trending vulnerabilities in various environments, including applications, APIs, networks, DNS, and cloud configurations. This collaborative tool streamlines the process of identifying and addressing security threats, fostering a more secure online ecosystem.
