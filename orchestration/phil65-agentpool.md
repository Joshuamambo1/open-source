## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary of the open-source project:

The agentpool project is an open-source, unified agent orchestration hub that enables the configuration and management of multiple AI agents through a YAML-based interface. This platform supports various AI technologies, including native, ACP, AGUI, and Claude Code, and exposes them through standardized protocols such as ACP/OpenCode Server. By leveraging agentpool, developers can streamline their AI workflows and integrate multiple agents seamlessly.
