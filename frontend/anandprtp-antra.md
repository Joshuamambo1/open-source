## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the Antra project:

Antra is an open-source desktop music library builder that allows users to easily collect and organize their music collections from popular streaming platforms such as Spotify, Apple Music, and Amazon Music. With Antra, users can convert URLs to lossless audio, auto-tag, transcode, and organize their music files without requiring a subscription or making any compromises. This project offers a free and flexible solution for music enthusiasts to build and manage their personal music libraries.
