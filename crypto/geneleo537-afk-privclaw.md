## 📝 Summary

PrivClaw is an open-source self-hostable AI plugin marketplace that enables developers to create, distribute, and manage their own artificial intelligence plugins. Built using FastAPI and Next.js, this project provides a flexible and customizable platform for AI plugin development and deployment. By hosting PrivClaw locally, users can maintain control over their AI data and plugins, promoting data sovereignty and security.
