## 📝 Summary

Cartography is an open-source Python tool that extracts infrastructure assets and their relationships from various sources and visualizes them in a Neo4j graph database. This project enables users to gain a comprehensive understanding of their infrastructure's complex relationships, facilitating improved management and decision-making. By leveraging Cartography, organizations can streamline infrastructure monitoring and optimize their IT environments.
