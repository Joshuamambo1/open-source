## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary of the Xero.DataComparer project:

Xero.DataComparer is an open-source, high-performance tool designed to compare generic lists in .NET, facilitating efficient data comparison and validation. This project enables developers to easily identify and manage differences between collections, making it an invaluable asset for data-driven applications. By leveraging its generic list comparator capabilities, developers can streamline their data processing workflows and reduce errors.
