## 📝 Summary

Horsicq’s **PDBRipper** is a lightweight, open‑source utility designed to extract data from Protein Data Bank (PDB) files. It parses structural files and provides convenient access to atomic coordinates, residue information, and other metadata, facilitating downstream analysis and scripting workflows. Suitable for a variety of miscellaneous bioinformatics and computational chemistry tasks.
