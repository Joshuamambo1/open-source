## 📝 Summary

The nextcloud/bookmarks project is an open-source bookmark application designed for Nextcloud users. This app allows users to manage and synchronize their bookmarks across all their Nextcloud installations, making it easy to access and share bookmarks with others. With nextcloud/bookmarks, users can organize and keep track of their favorite websites and links in a centralized and secure manner.
