## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary of the open-source project:

Clawvisor is an open-source API gateway that enables purpose-based authorization for AI agents, ensuring that tasks are executed in accordance with human-defined policies and approvals. By providing a human-centric approval mechanism and AI-native enforcement, Clawvisor provides a secure and transparent framework for AI agent interactions. Its purpose is to ensure accountability and reliability in AI decision-making processes.
