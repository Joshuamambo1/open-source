## 📝 Summary

### English

R Weekly is an open‑source newsletter that curates the latest news, packages, tutorials, and events from the R programming community. The repository maintains the website and content pipeline, allowing contributors to submit and publish R‑related resources on a weekly basis. It serves data analysts, statisticians, and developers looking to stay up‑to‑date with the R ecosystem.
