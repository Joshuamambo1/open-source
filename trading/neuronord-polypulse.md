## 📝 Summary

PolyPulse is an open-source, C++ based Trading User Interface (TUI) scalper designed to exploit oracle lag in Polymarket's BTC/ETH markets. By utilizing a tailored approach to this specific lag, PolyPulse aims to capitalize on temporary market discrepancies. This project offers a unique opportunity for traders to explore the complexities of oracle lag in cryptocurrency markets.
