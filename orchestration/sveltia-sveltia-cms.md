## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the sveltia-cms project:

Sveltia CMS is an open-source, headless content management system built on Git-based infrastructure, providing a modern user experience and robust internationalization support for seamless multilingual content management. This framework-agnostic solution offers mobile responsiveness and hundreds of improvements over its predecessor, Netlify CMS. It is free, open-source, and suitable for various applications, including orchestration and data management.
