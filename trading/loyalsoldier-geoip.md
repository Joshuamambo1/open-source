## 📝 Summary

Here is a 2-3 sentence summary for the Loyalsoldier/geoip project:

The Loyalsoldier/geoip project is an enhanced edition of GeoIP that supports various formats, including V2Ray dat, MaxMind mmdb, and custom configurations. It provides GeoIP data for multiple platforms, including Clash, Surge, and V2Ray, among others. This open-source project aims to offer flexibility and customization options for users to manage their GeoIP information.
