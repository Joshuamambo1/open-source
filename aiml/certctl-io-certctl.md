## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the certctl project:

Certctl is an open-source platform that fully automates the certificate lifecycle, from issuance to deployment, with zero human intervention. This self-hosted solution supports any certificate authority and deploys certificates to any server, ensuring that private keys remain securely stored on the user's infrastructure. By streamlining the certificate management process, certctl enhances security and reduces administrative burdens.
