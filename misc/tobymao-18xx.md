## 📝 Summary

The tobymao/18xx project is an open-source platform designed to facilitate online play of 18xx games, a series of board games focused on train-themed economic competition. This platform provides a digital space for players to engage in turn-based gameplay and interact with each other in a virtual environment. By offering a digital platform for 18xx games, this project aims to broaden accessibility and enhance the overall gaming experience.
