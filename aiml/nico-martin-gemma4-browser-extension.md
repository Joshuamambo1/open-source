## 📝 Summary

Here's a summary of the project in 2-3 sentences:

Gemma4-Browser-Extension is an open-source, on-device AI agent Chrome extension that leverages the power of Transformers.js and Gemma 4 to provide AI capabilities directly within your browser. This extension enables users to access and utilize AI-driven features without relying on cloud-based services, ensuring faster processing and enhanced data security. By harnessing the potential of on-device AI, Gemma4-Browser-Extension offers a novel solution for AI integration in web applications.
