## 📝 Summary

### English

Strukto‑AI’s **Mirage** provides a unified virtual filesystem that lets AI agents interact with heterogeneous data sources as if they were ordinary files and directories. By abstracting storage back‑ends (cloud buckets, databases, local disks, etc.) into a single, searchable namespace, Mirage simplifies data orchestration and enables seamless retrieval, manipulation, and sharing of information across AI/ML workflows. It is designed for developers building autonomous agents that need consistent, programmatic access to diverse datasets.
