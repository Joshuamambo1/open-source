## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the open-source project OhEarningsCal:

OhEarningsCal is an open-source project that allows users to subscribe to earnings calendar dates for US stocks through calendar software. This project aims to provide a convenient way for investors to stay up-to-date with important financial events and news. By leveraging a calendar interface, users can easily track and organize earnings dates and stay ahead in the stock market.
