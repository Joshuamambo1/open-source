## 📝 Summary

Seg is an open-source binary analysis tool written in Rust, designed for efficient one-command binary reconstruction in Capture The Flag (CTF) challenges and AI agent applications. It utilizes a command-line interface, simplifying the process of reverse engineering binaries and aiding in the development of AI agents that can navigate complex binary formats. This tool is particularly useful for both AI researchers and security enthusiasts looking to streamline their workflow.
