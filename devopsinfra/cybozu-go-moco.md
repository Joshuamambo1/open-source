## 📝 Summary

cybozu-go/moco is an open-source MySQL operator designed for Kubernetes, utilizing GTID-based semi-synchronous replication for reliable and efficient database management. This operator streamlines the deployment and management of MySQL clusters on Kubernetes, enhancing the overall scalability and reliability of database operations. By leveraging Kubernetes' container orchestration capabilities, moco simplifies the process of setting up and maintaining MySQL environments.
