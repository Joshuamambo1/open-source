## 📝 Summary

Pyfa is an open-source, cross-platform fitting tool designed for EVE Online players, providing a Python-based assistant to help with ship fitting and optimization in the game. This tool allows users to simulate and analyze ship configurations, making it easier to find the most suitable equipment combinations for their vessels. With Pyfa, EVE Online players can improve their gameplay experience by making informed decisions about their ship fitouts.
