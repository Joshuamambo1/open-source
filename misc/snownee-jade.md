## 📝 Summary

Snownee/Jade is an open‑source Minecraft mod that provides on‑screen information about the block, entity, or tile entity you are looking at, enhancing gameplay awareness and convenience. It originated as a fork of the popular Hwyla mod, retaining its lightweight, customizable overlay while adding new features, performance improvements, and broader compatibility with recent Minecraft versions. The project is categorized under Miscellaneous utilities.
