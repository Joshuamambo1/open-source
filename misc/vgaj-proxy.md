## 📝 Summary

This open-source project enables users to repurpose their Android phone as an HTTP proxy, allowing them to route internet traffic through their device for various purposes, such as securing their connection or bypassing network restrictions. The solution utilizes the phone's mobile network connection to establish a proxy server, providing an easily accessible and portable way to manage internet traffic. This versatile tool caters to developers, researchers, and individuals seeking flexibility in their online activities.
