## 📝 Summary

Permify is an open-source authorization platform inspired by Google Zanzibar, designed to build and manage fine-grained and scalable authorization systems for various applications. This project enables developers to create and manage complex access controls with ease, ensuring robust security and scalability. As part of FusionAuth, Permify offers a robust solution for organizations seeking to enhance their authorization systems.
