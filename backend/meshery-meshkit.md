## 📝 Summary

Meshkit is an open-source, common microservices framework designed for Meshery components, providing a standardized foundation for building and integrating microservices-based applications. This framework facilitates seamless collaboration and automation across various components, enabling developers to streamline their workflows and enhance overall system efficiency. By leveraging Meshkit, developers can create scalable, secure, and agile microservices architecture.
