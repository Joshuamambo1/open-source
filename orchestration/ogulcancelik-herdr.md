## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary of the 'herdr' project:

Herdr is an open-source, terminal-based agent multiplexer designed to simplify the management and orchestration of multiple agents. This tool enables users to centralize and streamline the operation of various agents, including those used in AI/ML workflows, from a single interface. By leveraging herdr, users can improve their productivity and efficiency when working with multiple agents.
