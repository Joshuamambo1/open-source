## 📝 Summary

Here is a brief summary in English:

Noterx is an open-source AI project developed by a team of 13-year-old high school students, aimed at analyzing and improving online note-taking content. It combines a self-trained predictive model, a comment image engine, and a multi-round AI debate system to diagnose why certain notes on Xiaohongshu (a popular Chinese e-commerce platform) are not gaining traction. The project offers an interactive online experience and showcases its research findings at noterx.muran.tech.
