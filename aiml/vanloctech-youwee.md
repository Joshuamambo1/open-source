## 📝 Summary

vanloctech/youwee is an open‑source, cross‑platform graphical front‑end for yt‑dlp that lets users download videos from YouTube, TikTok, Instagram and more than 1,800 other sites. It adds modern conveniences such as AI‑generated video summaries and built‑in post‑processing tools, all wrapped in a clean, user‑friendly UI. The project combines AI/ML features with a polished frontend to streamline media retrieval and editing.
