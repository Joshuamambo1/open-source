## 📝 Summary

### English

Serial Studio is an open‑source telemetry dashboard that lets developers visualize and interact with data from a wide range of communication protocols—including UART, Bluetooth Low Energy, MQTT, Modbus, CAN Bus, and others. Built as a modern frontend application, it provides real‑time charts, configurable widgets, and easy exporting of logs, making it ideal for rapid prototyping and debugging of embedded and IoT systems.
