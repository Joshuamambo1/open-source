## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary of the open-source project:

The zhenxun_bot is an open-source bot developed using Nonebot2, designed for multi-platform compatibility and multi-bot management. It is a loving tribute to the beloved anime character Zhenxun (also known as Shun Shinzou) from the anime series "Great Teacher Onizuka". The bot is customizable and can be used across various platforms.
