## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the invertase/melos project:

Melos is an open-source tool designed to simplify the management of Dart projects with multiple packages. It supports automation of versioning, changelogs, and publishing via Conventional Commits, streamlining the development process. With integration in popular IDEs like IntelliJ and Vscode, Melos enhances productivity for Dart developers working on complex projects.
