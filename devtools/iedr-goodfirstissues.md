## 📝 Summary

### English

A simple web platform that helps developers discover beginner‑friendly open‑source issues, making it easier to find and start contributing to projects. By aggregating and labeling “good first issue” tickets across repositories, it guides newcomers through the contribution process and connects them with suitable projects.
