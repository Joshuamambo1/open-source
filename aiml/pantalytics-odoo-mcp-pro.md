## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the open-source project:

The odoo-mcp-pro project enables seamless integration of popular AI tools, including Claude, ChatGPT, and Cursor, with Odoo ERP systems. This AI connector uses the MCP (Message Channel Protocol) to establish a secure connection between Odoo and AI services, expanding the capabilities of Odoo with advanced AI functionalities. With odoo-mcp-pro, users can unlock new levels of automation and intelligence in their business operations.
