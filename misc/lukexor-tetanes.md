## 📝 Summary

The tetanes project is an open-source, cross-platform NES emulator built with Rust and utilizing the wgpu graphics library for efficient rendering. This emulator aims to provide a high-performance and accurate emulation experience for classic NES games on a variety of platforms. By leveraging Rust and wgpu, tetanes aims to deliver a fast and visually impressive gaming experience.
