## 📝 Summary

SeBS (Serverless Benchmarking Suite) is an open-source project that provides a comprehensive benchmarking suite for automatic performance analysis of Function-as-a-Service (FaaS) platforms. It allows developers to easily compare and evaluate the performance of various serverless platforms, enabling informed decisions for deployment and scaling. SeBS streamlines the process of performance evaluation, saving time and effort in the development and operation of serverless applications.
