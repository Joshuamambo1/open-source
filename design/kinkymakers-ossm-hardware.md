## 📝 Summary

The OSSM-hardware project is an open-source initiative focused on designing printed circuit boards (PCBs) and hardware enclosures using collaborative and community-driven approaches. This project aims to provide free and accessible designs for makers and developers, promoting innovation and creativity in the field of electronics. By sharing knowledge and resources, OSSM-hardware seeks to empower individuals to create and build their own projects using open-source hardware.
