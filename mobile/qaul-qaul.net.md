## 📝 Summary

### English

qaul.net is an open‑source mobile application that creates a decentralized, internet‑independent mesh network for peer‑to‑peer communication. By leveraging Wi‑Fi, Bluetooth, and other local radios, it lets users exchange messages, voice calls, and files without relying on traditional cellular or broadband infrastructure. The project targets mobile platforms and aims to provide resilient, secure connectivity in environments where conventional networks are unavailable or unreliable.
