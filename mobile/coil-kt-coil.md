## 📝 Summary

Coil is an open-source library for loading images on Android and Compose Multiplatform, designed to simplify image management and enhance the overall user experience. It provides a flexible and customizable solution for loading images from various sources, including URLs, local files, and caching. With Coil, developers can easily integrate high-quality image loading into their applications.
