## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the reef-pi project:

Reef-pi is an open-source, community-driven project that utilizes the Raspberry Pi to control and monitor reef aquarium systems. This user-friendly controller enables users to automate and monitor various aspects of their aquarium, such as water parameters, lighting, and temperature. By providing a customizable and accessible solution, reef-pi empowers aquarium enthusiasts to create a safe and thriving environment for their marine life.
