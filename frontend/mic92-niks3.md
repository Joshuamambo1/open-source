## 📝 Summary

Mic92/niks3 is an open-source project that provides a reliable and efficient S3-backed Nix binary cache with automatic garbage collection. This ensures that Nix users can easily access pre-built packages and maintain a clutter-free cache, optimizing their development workflow. By leveraging Amazon S3 for storage, niks3 offers scalability and redundancy for large-scale Nix deployments.
