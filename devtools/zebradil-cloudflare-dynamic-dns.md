## 📝 Summary

This project, Zebradil/cloudflare-dynamic-dns, is an open-source Dynamic DNS client specifically designed to support both IPv6 and IPv4 addresses for Cloudflare. It enables users to easily update their Cloudflare DNS records to reflect their current IP address, ensuring seamless access to their services from anywhere. This tool is particularly useful for those who frequently change their IP address or require dynamic DNS support.
