## 📝 Summary

Here's a 2-3 sentence summary for the sunnypilot open-source project:

Sunnypilot is an open-source driver assistance system designed to enhance the driving experience for over 350 supported car models. This project modifies the behaviors of driving assist features, complying with safety policies from comma.ai's openpilot to ensure accurate and safe operation. By leveraging AI and ML technology, sunnypilot offers a unique and improved driving experience for users.
