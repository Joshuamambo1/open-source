## 📝 Summary

This open-source project is a standalone Unity remake of the multiplayer "Mario vs Luigi" game mode from New Super Mario Bros DS. The project allows players to experience the classic mode on their own or with friends, bringing nostalgic Mario gameplay to modern platforms. Developed with Unity, it offers a standalone gameplay experience.
