## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the Taste-Skill open-source project:

Taste-Skill is an open-source project aimed at enhancing the aesthetic appeal of AI-generated content by preventing the creation of generic, uninteresting outputs. This initiative empowers AI systems to produce more refined and engaging results, thereby elevating their overall quality and user experience. By promoting good taste in AI-generated content, Taste-Skill seeks to bridge the gap between AI capabilities and human creative standards.
