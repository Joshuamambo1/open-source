## 📝 Summary

The yarlson/lnk project is an open-source, Git-native tool designed to manage dotfiles in a efficient and user-friendly manner. It aims to provide an alternative to traditional dotfile management solutions, offering a seamless experience within the Git workflow. With lnk, users can easily track and synchronize their dotfiles across multiple systems and repositories.
