## 📝 Summary

Openinary is an open-source, self-hostable alternative to Cloudinary, providing a flexible and customizable solution for image and video management. This project enables users to manage and optimize their media assets in-house, offering greater control and scalability. By leveraging Openinary, developers can streamline their media workflow and reduce reliance on third-party cloud services.
