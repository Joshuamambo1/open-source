## 📝 Summary

This open-source project allows users to visualize their computer's audio output in real-time on a digital piano interface. It provides a creative and interactive way to see the musical frequency and waveform of any audio being played or streamed on the computer. By mapping audio signals to a virtual piano layout, users can gain a deeper understanding of sound patterns and frequencies.
