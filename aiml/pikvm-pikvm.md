## 📝 Summary

The pikvm project is an open-source, cost-effective initiative to create a DIY IP-KVM (Keyboard, Video, and Mouse) solution utilizing the Raspberry Pi. This innovative project enables users to remotely access and control their computer systems over the internet, offering a flexible and affordable alternative to commercial KVM products. By leveraging open-source software and the Raspberry Pi's versatility, pikvm promotes a more accessible and customizable approach to remote access and management.
