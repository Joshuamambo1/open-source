## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the JoltPhysics project:

JoltPhysics is a high-performance, multi-core friendly rigid body physics and collision detection library written in C++. Designed for games and VR applications, it provides a robust and efficient solution for simulating complex physical interactions. Utilized in notable titles such as Horizon Forbidden West and Death Stranding 2, JoltPhysics is a reliable choice for developers seeking to create immersive and realistic gaming experiences.
