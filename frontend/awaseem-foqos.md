## 📝 Summary

Foqos is a free and open-source project that enables users to lock mobile apps with the tap of an NFC tag or the scan of a QR code. This innovative solution provides a secure alternative to existing app locking services, including Brick, Opal, Unpluq, and Blok. With Foqos, users can easily manage access to their apps with a simple, high-tech approach.
