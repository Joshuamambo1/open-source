## 📝 Summary

Streetmix is an open-source project that empowers communities to collaborate on designing public spaces, making it easier to visualize and shape the future of their neighborhoods. This collaborative tool enables users to create and share custom street designs, facilitating community engagement and informed decision-making. By making street design more accessible, Streetmix fosters inclusive and participatory urban planning.
