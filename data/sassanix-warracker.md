## 📝 Summary

Warracker is an open-source, self-hostable warranty tracker that allows users to monitor warranty expirations, store receipts and files, and maintain control over their data. This project empowers users to manage their warranty information independently, free from reliance on third-party services. By hosting Warracker locally, users can ensure data security and compliance with their rules.
