## 📝 Summary

The ACBR (A Comic Book Reader) is a free and open-source comic book reader and converter that supports various file formats, including CBZ, CBR, CB7, EPUB, FB2, MOBI, and PDF. This versatile tool is available for both Windows and Linux operating systems, providing users with a convenient way to read and manage their digital comic collections. With ACBR, users can easily convert and view their comics in a variety of formats.
