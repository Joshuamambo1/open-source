## 📝 Summary

Piccolo is an experimental, stack‑less Lua virtual machine written entirely in Rust, aiming to explore high‑performance, safe execution of Lua bytecode without relying on a traditional C‑based runtime. By leveraging Rust’s ownership model and zero‑cost abstractions, Piccolo provides a lightweight, memory‑safe alternative for embedding Lua, while also serving as a research platform for novel VM design concepts.
