## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the stern/stern project:

Stern is an open-source, multi-pod and container log tailing tool for Kubernetes environments. It is a friendly fork of Wercker's stern tool, designed to provide efficient log monitoring capabilities for complex Kubernetes deployments. With stern, developers and operators can easily view logs from multiple containers and pods to streamline debugging and issue resolution.
