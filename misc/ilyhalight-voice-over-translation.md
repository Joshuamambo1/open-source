## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the open-source project:

This extension, voice-over-translation, is a small open-source add-on that enables voice-over translation of videos from YaBrowser to other browsers. It allows users to access multilingual content with ease, making it a valuable tool for language learners and global audiences. This extension facilitates seamless translation of video content across different browsers.
