## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary of the PiTracLM/PiTrac project:

The PiTracLM/PiTrac project is an open-source, affordable launch monitor that utilizes a Raspberry Pi and camera hardware to track ball flight and provide accurate measurements of speed, angles, and spin. This innovative system enables users to analyze and improve their golf swing with precision, making it an attractive solution for golfers of all skill levels. By leveraging low-cost hardware, PiTracLM/PiTrac offers a cost-effective alternative to traditional launch monitors on the market.
