## 📝 Summary

This open-source project, relative-ci/bundle-stats, provides a tool for analyzing and comparing bundle statistics across various builds. It supports popular bundlers like Webpack, RSPack, Vite, RollDown, and Rollup, offering insights into bundle size, assets, modules, and packages. By leveraging this tool, developers can identify and optimize performance bottlenecks in their applications.
