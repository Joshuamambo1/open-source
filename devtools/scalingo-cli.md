## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the Scalingo/cli project:

Scalingo/cli is an open-source command-line client for Scalingo, a Platform as a Service (PaaS) that simplifies the deployment and management of applications. With this tool, users can easily interact with their Scalingo applications, scaling, configuring, and monitoring them from the comfort of their terminal. This client provides a convenient interface for managing Scalingo resources and streamlining development workflows.
