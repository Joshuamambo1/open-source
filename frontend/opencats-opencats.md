## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the OpenCATS project:

OpenCATS is an open-source applicant tracking system (ATS) and recruitment CRM designed to streamline hiring processes for staffing agencies and hiring teams. This user-friendly solution helps manage candidate relationships, automate workflows, and track recruitment metrics in a single, intuitive platform. By leveraging OpenCATS, organizations can optimize their recruitment strategies and improve efficiency.
