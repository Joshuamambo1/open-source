## 📝 Summary

The open-source project "pymc" is a Python library for Bayesian modeling and probabilistic programming. It allows users to define and fit probabilistic models using a variety of distributions and estimation techniques, enabling the estimation of model parameters and the generation of probabilistic predictions. By leveraging the power of Bayesian statistics, pymc facilitates data analysis, inference, and decision-making under uncertainty.
