## 📝 Summary

The **balance** Python package provides a straightforward workflow for correcting sample bias, enabling reliable inference from biased datasets to a target population. It includes tools for estimating and applying weighting schemes, assessing balance diagnostics, and integrating bias‑adjustment steps into typical data‑analysis pipelines. Ideal for researchers and practitioners who need to mitigate selection bias in their statistical or machine‑learning models.
