## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the open-source project:

The Boxcars project is an open-source Rocket League Replay parser developed in Rust, designed to extract and process data from Rocket League match replays. This tool aims to provide a flexible and efficient way to analyze and visualize Rocket League gameplay, making it useful for data analysis, statistics, and optimization. By leveraging the power of Rust, Boxcars offers a fast and reliable solution for the Rocket League community.
