## 📝 Summary

zoxide is an open‑source, smarter alternative to the traditional `cd` command that learns your directory usage patterns and lets you jump to frequently visited folders with just a few keystrokes. It works across all major shells (bash, zsh, fish, PowerShell, etc.) and integrates seamlessly into existing workflows, providing fast, fuzzy‑matched navigation without sacrificing compatibility. The tool is lightweight, written in Rust, and licensed under the MIT license, making it a handy addition to any developer’s DevTools toolkit.
