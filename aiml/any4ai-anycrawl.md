## 📝 Summary

AnyCrawl is a Node.js/TypeScript web crawler that converts entire websites into clean, LLM‑ready datasets and can harvest structured SERP results from search engines such as Google, Bing, and Baidu. It leverages native multi‑threading to enable fast, bulk crawling and data extraction, making it ideal for AI/ML pipelines, backend services, and large‑scale data collection.
