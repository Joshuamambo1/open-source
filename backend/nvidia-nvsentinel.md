## 📝 Summary

Here's a summary for the NVSentinel project:

NVSentinel is an open-source, cross-platform service that rapidly identifies and remediates runtime node-level issues in GPU-accelerated computing environments, ensuring optimal performance and minimizing downtime. This fault remediation service provides a robust solution for managing complex GPU workloads, enabling seamless execution and reliability. By leveraging NVSentinel, developers can streamline their GPU-accelerated computing environments and improve overall system resilience.
