## 📝 Summary

Parthenon is an open-source high-performance computing (HPC) lab project that provides a scalable and extensible AMR (Adaptive Mesh Refinement) infrastructure. This infrastructure enables researchers and scientists to efficiently simulate complex phenomena in various fields, such as physics, engineering, and climate modeling. By leveraging Parthenon, users can develop and deploy high-performance applications with ease, driving innovation and advancements in their respective domains.
