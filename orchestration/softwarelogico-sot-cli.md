## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary of the open-source project:

sot-cli is a lightweight, terminal-based AI platform that provides raw, unbridled access to Large Language Models (LLMs) and innovative memory architecture, significantly reducing token costs. This Python CLI offers a flexible and efficient way to leverage AI capabilities through agent orchestration, free from unnecessary constraints or frameworks. With sot-cli, developers can harness the full power of AI at their fingertips.
