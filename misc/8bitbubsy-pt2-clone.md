## 📝 Summary

This open-source project, 8bitbubsy/pt2-clone, is a faithful replica of the classic ProTracker 2 software, originally designed for the Amiga platform. Now available for Windows, macOS, and Linux, it aims to preserve the nostalgia of the original while providing a modern and compatible experience. The clone offers a range of features and tools for music composition and editing.
