## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the open-source project "tranxuanthang/lrcget":

lrcget is an open-source utility designed to help users mass-download LRC synced lyrics for their offline music libraries. This frontend application simplifies the process of collecting and storing lyrics, making it easier to enjoy music with accurate lyrics offline. By automating the download process, lrcget streamlines music enthusiasts' experiences and enhances their music enjoyment.
