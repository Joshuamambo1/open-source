## 📝 Summary

The Arkham Horror browser game is an open-source, unofficial adaptation of the popular card game Arkham Horror: The Card Game, carefully following its official rules and mechanics. Developed by the community, this browser-based version allows fans to explore the Lovecraftian world and engage in cooperative gameplay. It is not affiliated with or endorsed by Fantasy Flight Games, the original creators of the card game.
