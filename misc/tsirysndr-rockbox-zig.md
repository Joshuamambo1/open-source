## 📝 Summary

A modern, open‑source Music Player Daemon (MPD) built on the Rockbox firmware, offering high‑quality audio playback, extensive codec support, and a lightweight, configurable architecture for headless or embedded systems. It combines Rockbox’s proven audio engine with MPD’s network‑friendly protocol, enabling seamless integration with popular clients and remote control interfaces. The project targets developers and hobbyists looking for a flexible, DIY‑friendly music server.
