## 📝 Summary

The trouble project is an open-source, Rust-based BLE (Bluetooth Low Energy) host stack designed to provide a robust and reliable foundation for developing Bluetooth applications. This stack enables developers to create BLE devices and communicate with other BLE-enabled devices, utilizing the Rust programming language for efficiency and ease of use. With trouble, developers can focus on building innovative Bluetooth-based solutions without being hindered by the complexities of BLE protocol implementation.
