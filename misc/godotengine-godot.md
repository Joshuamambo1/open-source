## 📝 Summary

The Godot Engine is an open-source, multi-platform game engine that supports the creation of 2D and 3D games for various operating systems, including Windows, macOS, and Linux. It offers a comprehensive set of tools and features, such as a scene editor, animation system, and physics engine, allowing developers to bring their game ideas to life. With its cross-platform capabilities and flexible architecture, Godot Engine enables developers to deploy their games on a wide range of platforms.
