## 📝 Summary

ViaVersion/ViaProxy is an open-source, standalone proxy solution that enables players to seamlessly join Minecraft servers of any version, including Classic, Alpha, Beta, Release, and Bedrock. This versatile tool simplifies cross-version connectivity, making it easier for players to interact with servers that may not support their current game version. By bridging the gap between different Minecraft versions, ViaVersion/ViaProxy enhances the overall gaming experience.
