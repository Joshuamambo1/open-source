The obot-platform/obot project is an open-source, complete MCP (Multi-Chat Platform) solution that includes hosting, registry, gateway, and a chat client. It provides a comprehensive platform for building and managing chatbots, enabling developers to host, register, and integrate their AI/ML models seamlessly. This versatile platform caters to the needs of developers working on AI and ML projects.
