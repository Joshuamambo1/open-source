## 📝 Summary

OpenTitan is an open-source silicon root of trust project, specifically designed to provide a secure foundation for integrated circuits. Developed by lowRISC, it offers a secure and trustworthy platform for a wide range of applications, including data centers, cloud, and IoT devices. By making the root of trust open-source, OpenTitan enables developers to build and verify secure systems from the ground up.
