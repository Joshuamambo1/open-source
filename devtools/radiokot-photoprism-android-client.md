## 📝 Summary

Radiokot/photoprism-android-client is an open‑source Android gallery app that lets users browse, search and manage their PhotoPrism libraries directly from their mobile devices. It offers features such as offline caching, secure authentication, album organization, and quick sharing, providing a smooth, native experience for PhotoPrism enthusiasts on the go.
