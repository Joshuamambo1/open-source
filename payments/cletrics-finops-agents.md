## 📝 Summary

The finops-agents project is an open-source initiative that offers 34 specialized AI agents and 6 pre-defined playbooks for FinOps (Financial Operations) tasks. These agents can be easily integrated into various coding platforms, including Claude Code, Copilot, Cursor, Windsurf, Aider, and Gemini CLI, to streamline financial operations workflows. The project adheres to the MIT license, allowing for free use and modification.
