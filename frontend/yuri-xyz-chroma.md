## 📝 Summary

Chroma is an open-source terminal audio visualizer that utilizes shaders to create vibrant and dynamic visual effects in response to sound input. This project provides a unique and immersive experience, allowing users to see their music or other audio in a new and engaging way. With its shader-based approach, Chroma offers a high degree of customization and visual flexibility.
