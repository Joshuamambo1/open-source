## 📝 Summary

GraphOS is an open-source project that provides a visual runtime and debugger for AI agents, allowing developers to observe and understand the behavior of their artificial intelligence models in a more intuitive and interactive way. With a focus on local-first execution, GraphOS enables AI agents to run and be debugged directly on a user's machine, reducing reliance on cloud services and enhancing data privacy. This innovative tool is designed to facilitate the development and refinement of AI agents with greater transparency and control.
