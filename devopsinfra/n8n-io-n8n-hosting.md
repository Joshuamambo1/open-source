## 📝 Summary

n8n‑io/n8n‑hosting provides ready‑to‑use examples and step‑by‑step instructions for self‑hosting the n8n workflow automation platform across a range of environments, including Docker Compose, Docker Swarm, Kubernetes (Helm chart and manifests), and cloud‑native services. The repository consolidates configuration files, deployment scripts, and best‑practice guidelines to help developers and DevOps teams quickly spin up, scale, and maintain n8n in production‑grade infrastructure.
