## 📝 Summary

Jarvis is an open-source, 100% private AI voice assistant that runs offline on your computer, providing a conversational experience. It offers a wide range of capabilities, including remembering conversations, checking web information, controlling Chrome, and tracking nutrition, all while maintaining unlimited customization options without context decay. This AI assistant aims to provide a seamless, third-person-like interaction, enhancing your daily life with its intuitive voice interface.
