## 📝 Summary

Pie is an open-source, programmable Large Language Model (LLM) serving platform designed to efficiently deploy and manage AI models. It provides a flexible framework for developers to serve, manage, and scale LLM-based applications, making it suitable for a wide range of use cases. By offering a programmable interface, Pie enables developers to customize and extend its functionality to meet their specific needs.
