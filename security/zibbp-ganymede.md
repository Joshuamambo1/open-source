## 📝 Summary

Ganymede is an open-source platform for archiving Twitch VODs and live streams, providing a rendered and real-time chat for each archive. This project enables users to store and revisit their favorite Twitch content with its accompanying chat history. By utilizing Ganymede, users can securely maintain a permanent record of their Twitch streams and VODs.
