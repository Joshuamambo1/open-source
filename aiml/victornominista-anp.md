## 📝 Summary

ANP is an open-source, binary protocol designed for AI agent-to-agent price negotiation, eliminating the need for language models or tokens. This protocol facilitates efficient and secure communication between AI agents, enabling them to negotiate prices in a data-driven and transparent manner. By providing a standardized framework for price negotiation, ANP aims to accelerate the development of AI-powered marketplaces and e-commerce platforms.
