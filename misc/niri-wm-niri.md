## 📝 Summary

### English

niri is a lightweight Wayland compositor that blends tiling and scrolling paradigms, allowing windows to be arranged in a dynamic, scrollable layout rather than being confined to a fixed screen grid. It focuses on simplicity, performance, and flexibility, offering customizable keybindings and a minimal codebase that makes it easy to extend or integrate into various desktop environments.
