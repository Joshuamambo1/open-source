## 📝 Summary

Ren'Py is an open-source visual novel engine that allows users to create interactive stories with text-based interfaces, images, and audio. This versatile engine supports the development of various types of visual novels, from romance and drama to science fiction and horror. Ren'Py is widely used in the visual novel community for its ease of use and flexibility.
