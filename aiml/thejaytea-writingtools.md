The Jay Tea WritingTools project provides a cross‑platform, system‑wide grammar assistant that outperforms Apple’s Intelligence Writing Tools. It integrates the free Gemini API, local large language models, and other AI back‑ends to deliver real‑time writing assistance on Windows, Linux, and macOS. The open‑source solution is built for developers, offering a flexible backend and DevTools for easy customization and extension.
