### English

Hypfer/Valetudo is an open‑source firmware add‑on that replaces the cloud‑based services of Wi‑Fi‑enabled robot vacuums, allowing them to operate entirely locally without any external servers. By running on the robot’s own hardware, Valetudo provides users with full control over scheduling, mapping, and data privacy while maintaining compatibility with many popular vacuum models. It’s a community‑driven project aimed at keeping smart home cleaning devices secure and self‑sufficient.
