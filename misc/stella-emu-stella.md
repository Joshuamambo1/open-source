## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary of the project:

Stella is an open-source, multi-platform emulator for the iconic Atari 2600 console. It allows users to play and experience games from the 2600's extensive library on various operating systems, including Windows, macOS, and Linux. With its robust emulation capabilities, Stella is a valuable resource for retro gaming enthusiasts and historians looking to preserve the legacy of the Atari 2600.
