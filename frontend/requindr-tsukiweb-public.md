## 📝 Summary

RequinDr's tsukiweb-public project aims to bring the classic visual novel "Tsukihime" to the web, making it accessible on a wide range of devices and platforms. This open-source endeavor also incorporates Quality of Life (QoL) improvements to enhance the overall user experience. By porting the game to the web, developers can enjoy the story on various devices, from desktops to mobile devices and beyond.
