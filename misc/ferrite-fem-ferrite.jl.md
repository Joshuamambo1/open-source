## 📝 Summary

Ferrite.jl is an open-source finite element toolbox for Julia, providing a comprehensive framework for solving partial differential equations using the finite element method. This toolbox offers a flexible and efficient way to model and simulate a wide range of physical problems, from simple to complex scenarios. With Ferrite.jl, users can leverage Julia's high-performance capabilities to accelerate their computational tasks and gain insights into their simulations.
