Here's a brief summary for the project:

Raphael-rs is a Rust-based macro solver designed for use in Final Fantasy XIV. This open-source project aims to help players craft more efficiently by solving macro combinations and providing optimized solutions. By leveraging the power of programming, Raphael-rs simplifies the process of crafting and allows players to focus on their gameplay experience.
