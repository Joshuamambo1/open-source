## 📝 Summary

HarfBuzz is an open-source, high-performance text shaping engine that plays a crucial role in rendering text on various platforms and devices. It enables accurate rendering of complex scripts and languages by breaking down text into individual glyphs and positioning them correctly on the screen. With its robust design and efficient algorithms, HarfBuzz is widely used in many applications and operating systems.
