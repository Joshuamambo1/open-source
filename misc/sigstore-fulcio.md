## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the sigstore/fulcio project:

Fulcio is an open-source, OIDC (OpenID Connect) PKI (Public Key Infrastructure) solution provided by sigstore, aimed at securely managing identities and cryptographic credentials. It enables trusted authentication and authorization in software systems by issuing and verifying digital certificates. Fulcio simplifies the process of identity verification and authentication in a secure, decentralized manner.
