## 📝 Summary

Thoth is an open-source, local-first AI assistant that aims to provide a private and secure alternative to traditional cloud-based AI assistants. This project allows users to leverage AI capabilities on their local devices, maintaining control over their data and ensuring greater data protection. By utilizing a local-first approach, Thoth enables users to enjoy AI-driven assistance while preserving their digital autonomy.
