## 📝 Summary

This open‑source project compiles and analyzes the primary source material released by the U.S. Department of Defense’s “PURSUE” UAP (Unidentified Aerial Phenomena) initiative, highlighting the most significant findings and patterns uncovered in the documents. It aggregates the original PDFs, transcripts, and data tables, then provides concise commentary, visualizations, and contextual background to help researchers and the public understand what the Pentagon’s release reveals about UAP investigations.
