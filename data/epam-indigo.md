## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the epam/Indigo project:

Indigo is an open-source, universal cheminformatics toolkit that provides a comprehensive set of utilities and database search tools for cheminformatics applications. It offers a robust platform for working with chemical structures, enabling users to perform various tasks such as molecular modeling, prediction, and analysis. By leveraging Indigo, researchers and developers can efficiently tackle complex cheminformatics challenges.
