## 📝 Summary

### English

Betaflight is an open‑source firmware suite for multirotor flight controllers, delivering high‑performance, low‑latency stabilization and advanced flight features for drones and other RC aircraft. It supports a wide range of hardware platforms, offers configurable PID tuning, sensor fusion, and telemetry, and is continuously updated by a global community of developers. The project provides comprehensive documentation and tooling to help hobbyists and professionals build, customize, and safely operate their aerial vehicles.
