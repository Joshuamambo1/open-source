## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the kreuzwerker/m1-terraform-provider-helper project:

The kreuzwerker/m1-terraform-provider-helper is an open-source command-line interface designed to facilitate the download and compilation of Terraform providers on Mac devices equipped with an M1 chip. This tool helps resolve compatibility issues experienced by users of M1 Macs when working with Terraform, allowing them to seamlessly manage infrastructure and resources. By streamlining the process, developers can save time and focus on their projects.
