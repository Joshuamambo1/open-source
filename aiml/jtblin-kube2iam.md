## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the kube2iam project:

kube2iam is an open-source project that automates the assignment of AWS IAM roles to pods running on a Kubernetes cluster. This tool simplifies the process of granting fine-grained permissions to pods, ensuring secure and efficient access to AWS resources. By integrating with Kubernetes, kube2iam helps DevOps teams streamline their IAM management and improve overall cluster security.
