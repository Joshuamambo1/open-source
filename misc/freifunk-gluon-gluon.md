## 📝 Summary

Gluon is an open-source project that provides a modular framework for creating custom firmwares for wireless mesh nodes based on OpenWrt. This framework allows users to easily configure and deploy wireless mesh networks with a wide range of devices, making it ideal for community networks and other mesh networking applications. By providing a flexible and customizable solution, Gluon enables users to tailor their mesh networks to their specific needs.
