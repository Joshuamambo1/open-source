By separating semantic grounding from statistical inference, the library lets developers attach external knowledge sources, constraints, or human‑interpretable representations to guide model outputs. It provides tools and interfaces for linking language models with outside ontologies, databases, or reasoning engines, aiming to make AI responses more transparent, controllable, and aligned with real‑world meaning.
