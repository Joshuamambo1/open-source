## 📝 Summary

### English

Media Player Classic Qute Theater (mpc‑qt) is an open‑source, lightweight media player that builds upon the classic Media Player Classic interface while adding modern features such as hardware‑accelerated video decoding, extensive subtitle support, and a customizable UI. It aims to provide a familiar, low‑resource playback experience on Windows, supporting a wide range of audio/video formats through integrated codecs and plug‑ins.
