## 📝 Summary

Anchor is an open-source note-taking application designed to replace Google Keep. This project aims to provide a free and customizable alternative for users seeking a note-taking solution, as featured on the YouTube channel 'Awesome Open Source'. With Anchor, users can create and manage their notes securely and efficiently.
