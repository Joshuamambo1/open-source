## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the cntext project:

Cntext is an open-source library for social science text analysis, designed specifically for Chinese text. It provides a suite of tools for tasks such as sentiment analysis, word frequency analysis, and semantic projection to measure complex constructs like attitudes and psychological states. By leveraging word embeddings, cntext enables researchers to extract meaningful insights from large volumes of Chinese text data.
