## 📝 Summary

The research-papers-plugin is an open-source project that provides a collection management system for research papers. It is designed to integrate with Claude Code, Codex CLI, and Gemini CLI, allowing users to easily organize and access their research papers within these tools. This plugin aims to streamline research workflows and improve productivity for users in various fields.
