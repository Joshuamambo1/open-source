## 📝 Summary

SpeedyWeather is an open-source project that enables users to build and explore atmospheric models using a LEGO-like approach. This Julia-based project simplifies complex atmospheric modeling, allowing users to create and manipulate models in a modular and intuitive way. By breaking down atmospheric modeling into smaller, interchangeable components, SpeedyWeather empowers users to experiment and innovate in the field of climate and weather research.
