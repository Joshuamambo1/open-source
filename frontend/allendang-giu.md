## 📝 Summary

### English

Here's a brief summary for the AllenDang/giu project:

giu is an open-source, cross-platform rapid GUI framework for Go based on the popular Dear ImGui library. It enables developers to create intuitive and interactive graphical user interfaces for their Go applications, allowing for rapid development and prototyping. With giu, users can leverage the power of Dear ImGui's high-performance rendering and customization capabilities in their Go projects.
