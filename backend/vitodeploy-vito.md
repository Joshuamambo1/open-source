## 📝 Summary

Vito is an open-source, free, and self-hosted server management tool designed to simplify the process of managing and monitoring servers. With its intuitive interface, users can easily deploy, scale, and maintain their server infrastructure, all from a single platform. By hosting Vito yourself, you maintain complete control over your server management, reducing reliance on third-party services.
