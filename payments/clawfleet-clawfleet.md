## 📝 Summary

ClawFleet is an open-source project that allows users to deploy a fleet of AI agents on their local machine in under 10 minutes. This fleet manager utilizes OpenClaw and Hermes, enabling users to harness the power of AI without incurring cloud bills, while leveraging their existing ChatGPT subscriptions. The project also features a user-friendly browser dashboard for easy management and monitoring.
