## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the alphagov/router project:

alphagov/router is an open-source HTTP router designed to act as a proxy in front of GOV.UK, directing traffic to multiple backend servers on a single domain. This router enables efficient routing of HTTP requests, improving the overall performance and scalability of GOV.UK's infrastructure. By leveraging a single domain, it simplifies management and maintenance of backend servers.
