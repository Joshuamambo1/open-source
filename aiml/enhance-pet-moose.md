## 📝 Summary

Here's a 2-3 sentence summary for the ENHANCE-PET/MOOSE project:

ENHANCE-PET/MOOSE is an open-source, data-centric AI solution that enables systemic tuberculosis research by generating multilabel organ segmentations from whole-body 18F-FDG PET/CT images. Built on the nn-UNet pipeline, this tool has the capability to segment 120 unique tissue classes, facilitating detailed analysis and insights. By harnessing the power of AI and machine learning, ENHANCE-PET/MOOSE aims to enhance tuberculosis research and diagnosis.
