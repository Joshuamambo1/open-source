## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary of the project:

Hibiscus is a free, open-source home banking application available for Linux, Windows, and MacOS. It utilizes the HBCI4Java FinTS implementation, allowing users to access their banking services through a plugin in Jameica. This versatile application enables seamless online banking experiences across multiple platforms.
