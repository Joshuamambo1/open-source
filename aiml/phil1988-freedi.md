## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the FreeDi project:

FreeDi is an open-source LCD firmware designed specifically for QIDI printers, engineered to work seamlessly with the mainline Klipper firmware. This project enables users to unlock the full potential of their QIDI printers with a highly customizable and efficient interface. By leveraging the power of mainline Klipper, FreeDi offers improved performance, flexibility, and reliability for users seeking enhanced 3D printing experiences.
