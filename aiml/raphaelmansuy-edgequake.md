## 📝 Summary

Here is a brief summary of the EdgeQuake project:

EdgeQuake is an open-source, high-performance graph-based knowledge management system written in Rust. It is inspired by LightRag and utilizes GraphRAG to transform documents into intelligent knowledge graphs, enabling superior retrieval and generation capabilities. This AI/ML project aims to provide a scalable and efficient solution for knowledge graph construction and querying.
