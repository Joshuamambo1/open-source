## 📝 Summary

Morphe-cli is an open-source, console-based tool for patching iOS and iPadOS devices. It allows users to quickly and easily modify system settings and configurations without requiring a physical connection to a computer. This makes it a valuable resource for developers and enthusiasts looking to customize their mobile devices.
