## 📝 Summary

The pvpoke project is an open-source, user-friendly platform for simulating battles, tracking rankings, and building teams in Pokémon GO's Player Versus Player (PvP) mode. This frontend-focused application provides a valuable tool for trainers to analyze strategies, test out new team compositions, and stay up-to-date with the meta game. With pvpoke, users can enhance their Pokémon GO PvP experience and improve their chances of success in battles.
