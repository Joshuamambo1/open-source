## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary:

eko/pihole-exporter is an open-source tool that facilitates the integration of PI-Hole's Raspberry PI ad blocker with Prometheus monitoring systems, enabling users to track and analyze ad blocking performance. This exporter provides valuable insights into ad blocking metrics, allowing for more effective management and optimization of PI-Hole environments. By monitoring ad blocking activity, users can make data-driven decisions to enhance their online security and performance.
