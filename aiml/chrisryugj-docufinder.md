## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the Docufinder open-source project:

Docufinder is an open-source application that enables lightning-fast searching of large collections of documents, including HWPX, PDF, and Office files, in under a second. Utilizing a local, offline approach, Docufinder provides a seamless search experience without relying on internet connectivity. Built with Tauri, React, and Rust, this innovative project showcases the power of modern web technologies and AI/ML capabilities.
