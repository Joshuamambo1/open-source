## 📝 Summary

### English

electh/nextflux is an open‑source, web‑based frontend designed to interact with the Miniflux RSS reader. It offers a modern, lightweight UI that enhances the browsing and management of feeds while remaining fully compatible with Miniflux’s API. Ideal for users seeking a fresh, customizable alternative to the default Miniflux interface.
