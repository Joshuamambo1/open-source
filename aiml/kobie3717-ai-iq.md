## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary of the AI-IQ project:

AI-IQ is an open-source, zero-configuration system that leverages hybrid search and graph intelligence to provide AI coding assistants with persistent context. This allows AI to focus on relevant information rather than requiring extensive knowledge. By utilizing full-text search and semantic analysis, AI-IQ enhances the efficiency and effectiveness of AI coding assistants.
