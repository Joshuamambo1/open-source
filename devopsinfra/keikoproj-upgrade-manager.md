## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary of the open-source project:

Upgrade Manager is a reliable and extensible solution for rolling out updates to Autoscaling groups in Kubernetes. This open-source tool enables smooth and automated scaling of applications during upgrades, minimizing downtime and ensuring high availability. By leveraging Upgrade Manager, DevOps teams can streamline their upgrade processes and maintain efficient Kubernetes environments.
