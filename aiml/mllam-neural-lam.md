## 📝 Summary

The neural-lam project is an open-source research software designed for neural weather prediction in limited area modeling. It utilizes machine learning and AI techniques to improve forecasting accuracy and precision in local weather conditions. By integrating neural networks with traditional weather modeling algorithms, neural-lam aims to enhance the reliability and effectiveness of weather prediction in specific geographic regions.
