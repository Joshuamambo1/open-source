## 📝 Summary

Here's a 2-3 sentence summary of the echook project:

Echook is an open-source project that enables AI-operated audio notifications for popular coding tools, including Claude Code, Cursor IDE, and Codex CLI. It offers a wide range of customizable features, such as voice and chime themes, text-to-speech (TTS), webhooks, and rate-limit alerts, all controllable through natural language commands. By integrating with AI agents, echook provides a seamless and efficient way to stay informed about code development.
