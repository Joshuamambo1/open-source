## 📝 Summary

The crosspoint-reader project is an open-source firmware designed for the Xteink X4 e-paper display reader. This firmware enables users to customize and extend the functionality of their X4 device, providing a flexible and community-driven solution. By contributing to and using the crosspoint-reader project, users can unlock new features and improve the overall experience of their e-paper display reader.
