## 📝 Summary

This open-source project is an interactive digital representation of a Marimo Moss Ball, allowing users to explore and engage with the unique, spherical algae formation. The project leverages technology to mimic the appearance, texture, and interactive properties of a real Marimo Moss Ball. By offering a digital version, users can learn about and appreciate this natural wonder without the need for physical care and maintenance.
