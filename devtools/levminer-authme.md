## 📝 Summary

Levminer/authme is a free, open-source two-factor authenticator app designed for desktop use on multiple platforms. This simple yet effective tool provides an additional layer of security by generating secure one-time passwords, helping protect users from unauthorized access to their accounts. By utilizing 2FA authentication, users can significantly enhance their online security and peace of mind.
