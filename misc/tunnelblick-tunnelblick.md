## 📝 Summary

Tunnelblick is an open-source, free VPN solution for macOS. It provides a user-friendly interface for connecting to and managing OpenVPN configurations, allowing users to easily establish secure and encrypted connections to remote servers. With Tunnelblick, users can protect their online privacy and security with a simple and intuitive VPN client.
