## 📝 Summary

Substrait is an open‑source, cross‑platform specification that standardizes the representation of data transformations, relational algebra, and record‑level expressions and execution plans. By providing a common, language‑agnostic format, it enables seamless interoperability among AI/ML and data‑processing engines, simplifying query exchange, optimization, and execution across diverse systems.
