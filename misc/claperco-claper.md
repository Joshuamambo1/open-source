## 📝 Summary

Claper is an open‑source, web‑based platform designed to boost audience engagement during presentations, workshops, and live events. It lets presenters share slides, PDFs, or images in real time, while participants can interact via live polls, quizzes, Q&A, and collaborative drawing tools—all without requiring any software installation. The project aims to provide a simple, customizable solution for seamless, interactive communication between speakers and their audiences.
