## 📝 Summary

This open-source project utilizes Large Language Models (LLMs) to aid in the reconstruction of partially decompiled Minecraft 26.1.2. By leveraging AI/ML capabilities, the project aims to fill gaps in the decompilation process and provide a more complete understanding of the game's code. The goal of this initiative is to advance knowledge of decompilation techniques and potentially benefit the broader reverse engineering community.
