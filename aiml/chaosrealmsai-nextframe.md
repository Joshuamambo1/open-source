## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the NextFrame project:

NextFrame is an open-source AI video engine that enables the creation of videos from structured data. It takes JSON timelines and transforms them into visually engaging videos by combining scene components with HTML and MP4 capabilities. This innovative tool empowers developers to bring data to life through dynamic video storytelling.
