## 📝 Summary

This open-source project creates a terminal feed reader that aggregates news from popular platforms such as Hacker News, Reddit, and Lobste.rs. Using Claude Code, it provides a simple and accessible way to stay updated on the latest discussions and trends in these communities. This terminal-based feed reader aims to offer a distraction-free alternative to traditional web-based news aggregators.
