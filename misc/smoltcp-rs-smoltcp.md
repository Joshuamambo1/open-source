## 📝 Summary

Smoltcp is an open-source, lightweight TCP/IP stack written in Rust, designed to provide a simple and efficient networking solution. It offers a robust and scalable implementation of the TCP/IP protocol suite, enabling developers to easily integrate reliable and secure network communication into their applications. Smoltcp is ideal for resource-constrained systems and embedded devices.
