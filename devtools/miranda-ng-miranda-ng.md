## 📝 Summary

Miranda NG is an open-source, next-generation messaging client that builds upon the legacy of the popular Miranda IM. It offers a highly customizable and extensible platform for instant messaging, file transfer, and more, with support for various protocols such as ICQ, IRC, and Jabber. This project enables users to stay connected across different platforms and services.
