ANISE is an open-source toolkit designed to provide a modern replacement for the NAIF SPICE toolkit. It offers a comprehensive collection of tools and files for handling Attitude, Navigation, Instrument, Spacecraft, and Ephemeris data, making it a valuable resource for space-related projects and research. With its focus on space data management, ANISE aims to streamline and simplify tasks in this field.
