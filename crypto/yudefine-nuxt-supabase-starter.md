## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the open-source project:

YuDefine's nuxt-supabase-starter is a rapid development template that combines the strengths of Nuxt.js and Supabase, a powerful backend-as-a-service. This template is equipped with cutting-edge features such as Claude Code's AI-assisted workflow, Test-Driven Development (TDD), and Spectra (OpenSpec) for robust and efficient development. Ideal for projects in the crypto and AI/ML spaces, this template accelerates frontend development and streamlines the process with its intuitive workflow.
