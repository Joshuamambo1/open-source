## 📝 Summary

GDL is an open-source, freely available implementation of the IDL (Interactive Data Language) programming language, designed to provide a high-level interface for data analysis, visualization, and manipulation. This project, known as GDL, offers a cross-platform alternative to commercial IDL products, allowing users to develop and execute complex scientific applications with ease. It supports a wide range of data types, including numerical, graphical, and text data.
