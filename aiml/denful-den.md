## 📝 Summary

### English

Den is an open‑source framework that brings aspect‑oriented, context‑driven configuration to Nix through a dendritic (tree‑like) model, enabling developers to compose, override, and reuse system settings in a modular and expressive way. By leveraging Nix’s functional package management and adding a high‑level DSL, Den simplifies the creation of complex, reproducible environments for AI/ML and frontend projects while keeping configurations readable and maintainable. The project is maintained by a community of contributors including @vic, @sini, @HeitorAugustoLN, @Gwenodai, @theutz, and @DylanRJohnston.
