## 📝 Summary

This open-source project, Unity-AI-ProBuilder, integrates AI-powered tools into Unity's ProBuilder, enabling users to create and manipulate 3D meshes through natural language commands. With this toolset, users can perform various operations such as creating primitive shapes, applying materials, and even advanced mesh operations like bridging and subdivision. This enhances the 3D modeling experience in Unity, making it more efficient and accessible to users.
