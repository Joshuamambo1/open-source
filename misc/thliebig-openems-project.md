## 📝 Summary

The openEMS project is a free, open-source electromagnetic field solver that utilizes the Finite Difference Time Domain (FDTD) method to simulate and analyze electromagnetic phenomena. This software provides a powerful tool for researchers and engineers to model and optimize electromagnetic systems, from simple circuits to complex antennas and waveguides. By leveraging the FDTD method, openEMS enables accurate and efficient simulations of electromagnetic fields.
