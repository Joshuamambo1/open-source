## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the GoogleCloudPlatform/vertex-ai-creative-studio project:

The GenMedia Creative Studio is an open-source, user-friendly experience built on Vertex AI that showcases the capabilities of Google Cloud's generative media APIs, including Gemini, Veo, and Gemini Image. This platform enables users to explore and experiment with various AI-powered media tools, such as text-to-speech and image generation. By leveraging these APIs, developers can create innovative and engaging applications on Google Cloud.
