## 📝 Summary

Zeitgeist is an open-source, language-agnostic dependency checker developed by the Kubernetes SIGs community. This tool helps ensure that dependencies in a project are properly declared and managed, reducing the risk of unexpected issues and vulnerabilities. By being language-agnostic, Zeitgeist provides a versatile solution for developers to maintain consistent and secure dependency management across their projects.
