## 📝 Summary

DotRush is a lightweight, open-source C# development environment designed for use in VSCode. This project provides a streamlined and efficient workspace for developers, allowing them to focus on coding without the burden of a bloated IDE. By leveraging the power of VSCode, DotRush offers a flexible and customizable environment for building and maintaining C# applications.
