## 📝 Summary

This open-source project, xdg-ninja, is a shell script designed to scan your home directory for unwanted files and directories. It helps maintain organization by identifying and potentially deleting unnecessary items, promoting a clean and clutter-free environment. By automating this process, xdg-ninja simplifies the task of keeping your home directory tidy.
