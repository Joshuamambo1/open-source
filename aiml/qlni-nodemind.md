## 📝 Summary

NodeMind is an open-source project that offers a binary document index, showcasing significant storage efficiency by being approximately 48 times smaller than float32 RAGs. This project's innovative approach eliminates the need for GPU acceleration, making it more accessible for users. NodeMind is a valuable resource in the realm of AI and ML, particularly for frontend applications.
