## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary of the IP-Sentinel project:

IP-Sentinel is an open-source, lightweight, and modular distributed VPS asset management system that addresses IP location offset and high risk scores with geolocation signal anchoring and native-like traffic injection. It combines with Telegram to enable automated and unmanned global asset management across multiple nodes with low power consumption and high realism. This solution caters to the needs of users seeking seamless and hands-free VPS management.
