## 📝 Summary

The acados project is an open-source software library providing fast and embedded solvers for nonlinear optimal control and nonlinear model predictive control problems. It is designed to efficiently solve complex control problems in various fields, including robotics, aerospace, and automotive. With its flexible and modular framework, acados enables researchers and practitioners to develop and deploy high-performance control systems.
