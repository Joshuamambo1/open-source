## 📝 Summary

### English

I2P (Invisible Internet Project) is an open‑source, decentralized anonymizing network that provides a secure, encrypted overlay for identity‑sensitive applications. By routing traffic through multiple dynamic, encrypted tunnels, it ensures privacy without relying on any trusted third parties. The project focuses on cryptographic techniques, data protection, and overall security.
