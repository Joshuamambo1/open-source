## 📝 Summary

DropSnorz/OwlPlug is an open-source desktop application designed to manage VST, AU, and LV2 plugins across multiple platforms, including Windows, MacOS, and Linux. This comprehensive plugin manager allows users to easily organize, install, and update their audio plugins in a centralized and user-friendly interface. With DropSnorz/OwlPlug, audio producers and musicians can streamline their workflow and focus on creating music.
