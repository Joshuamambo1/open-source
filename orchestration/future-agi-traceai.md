## 📝 Summary

The future-agi/traceAI project is an open-source AI tracing framework designed to provide visibility and insights into AI applications and frameworks using OpenTelemetry. This framework enables developers to monitor and analyze the performance of complex AI systems, facilitating efficient debugging, optimization, and scalability. With traceAI, developers can gain a deeper understanding of their AI workflows and make data-driven decisions to improve their applications.
