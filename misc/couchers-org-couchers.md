## 📝 Summary

Couchers is an open-source, non-profit platform that offers a modern alternative to traditional couch surfing services. This community-led project aims to provide a free and accessible way for people to connect with hosts and travelers worldwide. By being open-source, it encourages collaboration and contributions from the community to improve and maintain the platform.
