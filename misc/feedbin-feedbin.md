## 📝 Summary

Feedbin is an open-source web feed reader that allows users to easily subscribe to and manage their favorite RSS and Atom feeds. With its user-friendly interface, users can stay up-to-date with the latest content from their preferred news sources and websites. This project aims to provide a convenient and accessible platform for reading and organizing online content.
