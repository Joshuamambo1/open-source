## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the open-source project:

The lazyagent project provides a centralized monitoring platform for various coding agents, offering a single-point console to oversee and manage multiple tools such as Claude Code, Cursor, OpenCode, and pi. This orchestration tool enables developers to streamline their workflow and improve productivity by tracking the status and performance of their coding agents in real-time. With lazyagent, users can easily monitor, control, and optimize their coding experience from a single terminal.
