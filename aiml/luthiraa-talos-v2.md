## 📝 Summary

This open-source project showcases the achievement of running MicroGPT, a hardware implementation of the GPT-3 language model, at an impressive 50,000 transactions per second (Tkps) on a Cyclone V Field-Programmable Gate Array (FPGA). This feat represents a significant milestone in the pursuit of hardware-accelerated natural language processing (NLP) capabilities. The project's success demonstrates the potential for FPGAs to accelerate complex AI tasks.
