## 📝 Summary

The FLT (Fermat's Last Theorem) project is an ongoing open-source effort led by Imperial College London to formally prove Andrew Wiles' celebrated solution to the theorem. This work utilizes Lean, a powerful formal proof system, to provide a rigorous and verifiable proof of Fermat's Last Theorem. By formalizing this proof, the project aims to increase confidence in its validity and demonstrate the potential of formal mathematics.
