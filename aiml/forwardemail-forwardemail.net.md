## 📝 Summary

### English

ForwardEmail is an open‑source, privacy‑first email platform that lets users send and receive encrypted messages without relying on commercial providers. It combines the core functions of Gmail, Mailchimp, and Sendgrid into a single, self‑hosted service, offering secure routing, mailing‑list management, and transactional‑email capabilities. Built with modern web technologies, it aims to make encrypted email accessible to developers and everyday users alike.
