## 📝 Summary

The tronbyt/server project is an open-source solution that allows users to manage their apps on Tronbyt devices (flashed Tidbyt) independently, without relying on Tidbyt's backend servers. This decentralized approach provides users with full control over their device and app management. By using tronbyt/server, users can maintain their Tronbyt's functionality and customizations without being dependent on external services.
