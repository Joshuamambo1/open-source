## 📝 Summary

Keep is an open-source project that provides a reflective memory system for AI agents. This system enables AI agents to learn from past experiences, integrate new information, and adapt to changing environments. By providing a framework for AI agents to retain and reflect on their memories, Keep aims to enhance their decision-making capabilities and overall performance.
