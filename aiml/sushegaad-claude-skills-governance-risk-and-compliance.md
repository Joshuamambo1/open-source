Sushegaad/Claude-Skills-Governance-Risk-and-Compliance is an open‑source library that equips Claude (the Anthropic LLM) with specialized “skills” for delivering expert‑level guidance on a wide range of Governance, Risk, and Compliance frameworks—including ISO 27001, SOC 2, FedRAMP, GDPR, HIPAA, NIST CSF, PCI DSS, EU AI Act, ISO 42001, ISO 27701, DORA, CSRD, India’s DPDPA, CMMC 2.0, NIST AI Risk, SWIFT, Australia’s ISM, EU NIS2, and CCPA/CPRA.
