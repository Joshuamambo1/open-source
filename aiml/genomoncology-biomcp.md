## 📝 Summary

BioMCP, an open-source project, is a Biomedical Model Context Protocol that aims to standardize the representation and exchange of model context information. This protocol facilitates seamless communication and collaboration among researchers, clinicians, and developers in the biomedical field, enhancing the development and deployment of AI/ML models. By establishing a common language, BioMCP accelerates the adoption of AI in healthcare and biomedical research.
