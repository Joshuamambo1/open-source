## 📝 Summary

LinkAce is an open‑source, self‑hosted web application that lets you archive, organize, and tag the URLs of your favorite sites in a searchable personal library. Designed for easy deployment on typical DevOps infrastructures, it provides a clean interface, tag‑based categorization, full‑text search, and import/export capabilities for managing link collections securely on your own server.
