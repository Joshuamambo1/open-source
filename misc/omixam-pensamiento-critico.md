## 📝 Summary

Here's a 2-3 sentence summary of the project:

The "Pensamiento Crítico" project is an open-source tool designed to analyze argumentative texts using the Paul-Elder framework, which evaluates 8 key elements and intellectual standards. Additionally, it detects cognitive biases and fallacies, while also acknowledging the non-neutrality of its own analysis. This tool is built to promote critical thinking and provide insights into the structure and content of argumentative texts in Spanish.
