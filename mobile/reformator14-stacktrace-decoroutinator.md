## 📝 Summary

The stacktrace-decoroutinator is a lightweight, open-source library designed to recover and display stack traces in exceptions thrown by Kotlin coroutines. This tool helps developers diagnose and troubleshoot issues related to coroutine execution, facilitating a more efficient debugging process. By utilizing this library, developers can gain a deeper understanding of their coroutine-based applications.
