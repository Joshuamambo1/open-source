## 📝 Summary

The sbproxy project is an open-source AI Governance Engine that serves as a self-hostable gateway for managing AI traffic, APIs, and AI crawlers. This comprehensive platform provides a centralized control point for monitoring and regulating AI-related activities, ensuring secure, efficient, and transparent data exchange. By hosting sbproxy on-premises, organizations can maintain control and compliance over their AI operations.
