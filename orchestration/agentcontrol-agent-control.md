## 📝 Summary

The agent-control project is an open-source, centralized control plane designed to govern the behavior of runtime agents at scale. Configurable and extensible, this platform enables organizations to manage complex agent interactions and ensure seamless operation in production environments. By providing a centralized governance framework, agent-control facilitates efficient and scalable management of AI/ML and other products.
