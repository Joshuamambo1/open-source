## 📝 Summary

The IVPN Desktop app is an official, open-source client for secure and private internet access. This desktop application provides users with a user-friendly interface to manage their IVPN subscriptions, enabling them to protect their online identity and data with end-to-end encryption. By utilizing the app, users can browse the internet securely and access geo-restricted content.
