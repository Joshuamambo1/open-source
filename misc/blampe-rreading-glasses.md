## 📝 Summary

The rreading-glasses project is an open-source initiative aimed at creating digital "reading glasses" for individuals who may be difficult to deal with. Its primary function is to serve as a humorous, in-app annotation tool that allows users to flag and label annoying or grumpy behavior in others. This project aims to provide a lighthearted way to document and share experiences with curmudgeonly individuals.
