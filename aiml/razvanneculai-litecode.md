## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the 'litecode' project:

Litecode is an open-source Command-Line Interface (CLI) tool designed to work with 8K context AI models. It provides a user-friendly interface to interact with these advanced models, facilitating development, testing, and deployment of AI-powered applications. With litecode, developers can efficiently integrate and utilize the capabilities of 8K context AI models in their projects.
