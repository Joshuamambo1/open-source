## 📝 Summary

### English

SkepticMystic/breadcrumbs is an open‑source Obsidian plugin that lets you create typed, hierarchical links—called “breadcrumbs”—between notes, enabling a clear, navigable structure for your knowledge base. By defining link types (e.g., parent, child, related) and automatically generating context‑aware navigation, the plugin helps you maintain organized, inter‑connected markdown files within Obsidian.
