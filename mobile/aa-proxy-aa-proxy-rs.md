## 📝 Summary

The aa-proxy/aa-proxy-rs project is an open-source solution that enables users to proxy Android Auto connections wirelessly or through a wired connection, providing a flexible and customizable experience for those who want to use Android Auto on their preferred devices. This project allows users to seamlessly access Android Auto on various platforms, enhancing their in-car entertainment and navigation experience. By utilizing aa-proxy/aa-proxy-rs, users can bypass the limitations of Android Auto's native compatibility requirements.
