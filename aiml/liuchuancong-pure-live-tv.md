## 📝 Summary

Pure Live TV is an open‑source streaming aggregator that pulls live video feeds from a wide range of Chinese platforms—including Bilibili, Huya, Douyu, Kuaishou, TikTok (Douyin), NetEase CC, M38, and custom sources—into a unified interface. It offers a simple, customizable solution for users who want to watch multiple live streams without switching between different apps or websites. The project is primarily geared toward developers interested in multimedia handling and integration, fitting within the AI/ML category for potential content analysis and recommendation extensions.
