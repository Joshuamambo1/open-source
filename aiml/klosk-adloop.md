## 📝 Summary

The kLOsk/adloop project is an open-source MCP (Machine-to-Machine) server that enables AI assistants to access and interact with Google Ads and Google Analytics 4 (GA4) data in read-write mode. This integration is safeguarded by built-in "safety guardrails" that prevent accidental or unauthorized spending on Google Ads. By providing a secure and controlled interface, kLOsk/adloop facilitates more efficient and effective AI-driven advertising and analytics management.
