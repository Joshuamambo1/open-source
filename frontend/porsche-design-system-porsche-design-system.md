## 📝 Summary

This open-source project, Porsche Design System, provides a centralized library of reusable web components and UXI guidelines, empowering designers and developers to create cohesive, user-friendly digital products that embody the Porsche brand. By leveraging this design system, users can build high-quality digital experiences that meet Porsche's distinctive design standards. It aims to streamline collaboration between designers and developers, fostering consistency and efficiency across digital products.
