## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the open-source project:

Lightning Loop is an open-source, non-custodial off/on-chain bridge that enables seamless transactions between on-chain and off-chain Lightning Network payments. This decentralized bridge allows users to securely transfer funds between the two ecosystems, providing greater flexibility and accessibility in the world of cryptocurrencies. By leveraging AI/ML technologies, Lightning Loop aims to further enhance the security and efficiency of its bridge protocol.
