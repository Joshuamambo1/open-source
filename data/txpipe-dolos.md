## 📝 Summary

The txpipe/dolos project is an open-source, Cardano-based data node designed to facilitate the secure storage and management of data on the Cardano blockchain. This initiative aims to establish a decentralized data infrastructure for Cardano, enabling users to store and retrieve data in a secure, transparent, and community-driven manner. By leveraging the Cardano network, dolos seeks to promote data sovereignty and empower users with control over their data.
