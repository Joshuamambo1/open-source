## 📝 Summary

Flowctl is an open-source self-service workflow execution platform that provides users with the ability to manage and orchestrate complex workflows in a flexible and scalable manner. This platform offers a user-friendly interface, allowing users to define, execute, and monitor workflows without requiring extensive IT knowledge or support. By leveraging flowctl, organizations can streamline business processes and increase efficiency.
