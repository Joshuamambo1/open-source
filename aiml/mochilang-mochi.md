## 📝 Summary

Mochi is an open-source, embeddable programming language optimized for agent-based, data-driven, and AI applications. With its unique blend of functional syntax, stream-first semantics, and native support for datasets, graphs, and simulation, Mochi enables developers to create efficient and scalable programs. This versatile language is ideal for tasks such as data analysis, modeling, and simulation in various fields, including AI, data science, and more.
