## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the trzsz-go project:

trzsz-go is an open-source project that brings the functionality of trzsz to various terminals that support local shells, making it accessible across different platforms. This Go version of trzsz allows users to enjoy its features and benefits in terminals that previously lacked support. By bridging the gap, trzsz-go expands the usability of trzsz.
