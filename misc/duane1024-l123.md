## 📝 Summary

L123 is an open-source project that brings back the functionality of classic spreadsheets like Lotus 1-2-3, while also offering modern compatibility with Excel. This terminal-based spreadsheet is designed to provide a simple, text-based interface for data analysis and manipulation. With its unique blend of old-school charm and modern utility, L123 aims to fill a niche for users seeking a lightweight, command-line spreadsheet solution.
