## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary of the open-source project Look4Sat:

Look4Sat is an open-source satellite tracker and pass predictor for Android, designed to provide users with accurate location and timing information for visible satellites in the sky. Inspired by Gpredict, this project utilizes AI/ML and data-driven approaches to offer a user-friendly and reliable experience. By leveraging Android's capabilities, Look4Sat enables users to track and predict satellite passes with ease.
