## 📝 Summary

The Prime Gap Structure project is an open-source initiative that explores the distribution and patterns of prime number gaps. By analyzing the differences between consecutive prime numbers, this project aims to reveal insights into the underlying structure of prime numbers. Its open-source nature invites contributions from the community to further our understanding of prime number theory.
