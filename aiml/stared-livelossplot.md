## 📝 Summary

The "stared/livelossplot" open-source project enables real-time visualization of training losses in Jupyter Notebooks, supporting popular deep learning frameworks such as Keras and PyTorch. This tool allows data scientists to track the performance of their models as they train, facilitating faster iteration and improvement. By providing an interactive and dynamic loss plot, it simplifies the process of monitoring and optimizing model performance.
