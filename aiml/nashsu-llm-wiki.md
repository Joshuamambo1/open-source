## 📝 Summary

The LLM Wiki project is an open-source, cross-platform desktop application that utilizes Large Language Models (LLMs) to create a comprehensive, interconnected knowledge base from your existing documents. This innovative tool streamlines the process of information organization, eliminating the need for repetitive searches by building and maintaining a persistent wiki incrementally. By automating the knowledge management process, LLM Wiki enhances productivity and facilitates easier access to relevant information.
