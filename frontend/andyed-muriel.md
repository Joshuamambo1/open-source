## 📝 Summary

Muriel is an open-source project that enables Claude to learn and utilize various visualization-building tools, including raster, SVG, web, video, terminal, interactive, and more. With built-in features such as brand token management and an 8:1 contrast audit, Muriel facilitates the creation of accessible and visually appealing visualizations. This project aims to enhance design and product development through a wide range of visualization tools and design principles.
