## 📝 Summary

UniFace is an open-source, unified face analysis library in Python, leveraging the power of ONNX Runtime to provide efficient and accurate face analysis capabilities. Developed by @yakhyo, this actively maintained library offers a robust foundation for various AI and ML applications, including face recognition, detection, and analysis. With UniFace, developers can integrate face analysis functionality into their projects with ease.
