## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary of the open-source project:

The Zhongda Maopu is an open-source WeChat mini-program designed to provide a comprehensive and organized catalog of notable individuals at Zhongda Hospital, a prestigious hospital affiliated with Southeast University in China. This project aims to facilitate easy access and maintenance of the catalog. It is also known as Du Xing Maopu.
