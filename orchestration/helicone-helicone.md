## 📝 Summary

Helicone is an open-source LLM (Large Language Model) observability platform that enables users to monitor, evaluate, and experiment with their models using just one line of code. This platform aims to simplify the process of working with AI/ML models, making it more accessible and efficient for developers. Helicone is a part of YC W23, a prestigious startup accelerator program.
