Here's a brief summary for the Locki project:

Locki is an open-source project that provides AI sandboxing capabilities, allowing developers to safely contain and test artificial intelligence models without compromising the host system. This innovative tool enables the creation of isolated environments for AI experimentation, reducing the risk of model-induced instability or security breaches. By providing a secure sandbox for AI development, Locki empowers developers to push the boundaries of AI innovation.
