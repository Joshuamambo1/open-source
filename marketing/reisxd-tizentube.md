### English

Reisxd’s **TizenTube** is a TizenBrew module that strips advertisements from the native YouTube app on Tizen‑based smart TVs, delivering an uninterrupted viewing experience. It also integrates SponsorBlock, allowing users to automatically skip sponsored segments in videos. The project is open‑source, easy to install via TizenBrew, and tailored for Tizen TV owners who want cleaner, ad‑free streaming.
