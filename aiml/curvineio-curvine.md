## 📝 Summary

Curvine is a high‑performance, distributed multi‑tier cache written in Rust, designed to accelerate data access across cloud‑native and edge environments. It combines low‑latency in‑memory storage with persistent backing tiers, offering seamless scaling, strong consistency guarantees, and easy integration via a simple API. Ideal for AI/ML workloads, DevOps pipelines, and frontend services that demand fast, reliable caching at scale.
