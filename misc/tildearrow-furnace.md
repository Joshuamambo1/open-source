## 📝 Summary

The tildearrow/furnace project is an open-source, multi-system chiptune tracker that supports compatibility with DefleMask modules. This versatile tool enables creators to compose and arrange music for various 8-bit and 16-bit systems, offering a flexible platform for chiptune enthusiasts. With furnace, developers can work with a wide range of retro consoles and microcomputers.
