## 📝 Summary

GreycLab’s **CImg** library is a lightweight, header‑only C++ toolkit that provides a comprehensive set of image processing functions—from basic I/O and pixel manipulation to advanced filtering, geometry, and visualization. Designed for ease of integration, it supports a wide range of image formats and works across major platforms without external dependencies, making it ideal for rapid prototyping and research in computer vision and graphics.
