## 📝 Summary

This open-source project, titled "The Foundations of LLMs, 1943-2026: A Curated Collection of Seminal Papers," is a comprehensive collection of pioneering research papers on Large Language Models (LLMs). Spanning over eight decades, from 1943 to 2026, the project aims to provide a single resource for exploring the evolution and development of LLMs. By consolidating these foundational papers, the project seeks to facilitate a deeper understanding of the field and its advancements.
