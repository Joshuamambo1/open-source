The ardanlabs/service project provides an open-source starter kit for building services in Go that can be easily deployed and managed on Kubernetes platforms. This starter kit offers a foundation for writing scalable and maintainable services, simplifying the development and deployment process for Go applications in cloud-native environments. It caters to DevOps and infrastructure teams by streamlining the service creation process.
