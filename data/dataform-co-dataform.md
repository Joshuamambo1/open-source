## 📝 Summary

Dataform is an open-source framework for managing SQL-based data operations in BigQuery, enabling data teams to streamline their workflows and collaborate more effectively. It provides a scalable and customizable solution for data modeling, transformation, and governance, empowering users to build, deploy, and maintain complex data pipelines. With Dataform, data analysts and engineers can focus on high-value tasks while ensuring data integrity and consistency across their organization.
