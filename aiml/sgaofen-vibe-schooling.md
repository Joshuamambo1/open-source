## 📝 Summary

Vibe Schooling is an open-source, AI-powered academic automation toolkit designed to simplify and streamline educational processes. This innovative platform offers features such as automatic answer submission on canvas, a smart glasses exam pipeline for hands-free testing, and Claude Code, a homework methodology that utilizes AI to assist with coding tasks. By leveraging AI and automation, Vibe Schooling aims to enhance efficiency and reduce the workload for educators and students alike.
