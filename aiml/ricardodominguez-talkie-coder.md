## 📝 Summary

The talkie-coder project is an open-source initiative that aims to create a unique and interactive experience by mapping the timeline of computer science history from the 1930s to the modern era of software engineering and benchmarking. This project seeks to engage users with an immersive journey through the evolution of the field, highlighting key milestones and innovations. By exploring this interactive timeline, users can gain a deeper understanding of the history and development of computer science.
