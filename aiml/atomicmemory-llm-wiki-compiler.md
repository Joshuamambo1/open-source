## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary of the open-source project:

The LLM Wiki Compiler is an open-source tool that takes raw sources as input and generates an interlinked wiki as output. Inspired by Andrej Karpathy's LLM Wiki pattern, this compiler enables the organization and structure of knowledge in a cohesive and easily navigable format. It is a useful DevTool for AI/ML enthusiasts looking to document and share knowledge in a collaborative and accessible way.
