## 📝 Summary

The involvex/youtube-music-cli project offers a user-friendly Terminal User Interface (TUI) music player for YouTube Music, providing a seamless experience for music enthusiasts. This open-source project allows users to access and manage their YouTube Music library directly from the terminal, making it an ideal solution for those who prefer a command-line interface. With its powerful features, it enables users to easily discover, play, and control their favorite music.
