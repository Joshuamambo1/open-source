## 📝 Summary

PokéBot Gen3 is an open-source, Python-based shiny hunting bot that supports various Pokémon games, including Ruby, Sapphire, Emerald, FireRed, and LeafGreen. Utilizing libmgba and mGBA Python bindings, this bot streamlines the process of searching for rare shiny Pokémon in these classic games. The project allows users to automate their shiny hunting experience.
