## 📝 Summary

The nextcloud/contacts project is an open-source contacts application designed specifically for Nextcloud. This app provides a centralized platform for managing and syncing contacts across multiple devices, allowing users to easily access and edit their contact information within their Nextcloud environment. With nextcloud/contacts, users can maintain a seamless and secure contact management experience.
