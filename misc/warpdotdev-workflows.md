## 📝 Summary

Warps workflows let you discover, run, and share single commands or command pipelines directly from the terminal, eliminating the need to switch contexts. By providing a searchable catalog and simple execution interface, they streamline repetitive tasks and make collaborative command sharing effortless. Ideal for developers who want a lightweight, terminal‑centric workflow manager.
