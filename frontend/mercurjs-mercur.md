## 📝 Summary

Mercur is an open-source, multi-vendor marketplace platform designed for both B2B and B2C business models. Built on top of MedusaJS, this customizable solution enables developers to create their own unique marketplaces. With its flexible architecture, Mercur offers a scalable and efficient platform for entrepreneurs to launch and manage their online marketplaces.
