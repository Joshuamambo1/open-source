## 📝 Summary

AcquireMock is an open-source mock payment gateway designed for seamless testing of e-commerce integrations. It offers realistic payment simulations with features like OTP verification and HMAC-signed webhooks, allowing developers to thoroughly test and debug their applications. With a user-friendly interface, AcquireMock simplifies the testing process and ensures reliability in payment transactions.
