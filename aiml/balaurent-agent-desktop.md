BaLaurent/agent-desktop is an open-source, highly configurable desktop client for interacting with large language models (LLMs). This versatile tool enables users to leverage their preferred LLMs in a user-friendly interface, streamlining the integration of AI-powered capabilities into daily workflows. With its customizable features, the agent-desktop provides an adaptable platform for harnessing the potential of LLMs.
