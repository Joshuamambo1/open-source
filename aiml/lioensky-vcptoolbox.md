## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the VCPToolBox project:

The VCPToolBox is an open-source project that revolutionizes large language models by transforming them into complete intelligent systems with self-awareness, physical interaction capabilities, and collaborative intelligence. It acts as a bridge between AI models and frontend applications, leveraging a unified command protocol, multi-level persistence, a distributed plugin engine, and a multi-agent collaboration framework. By doing so, VCPToolBox enables large language models to operate autonomously and interact with the physical world.
