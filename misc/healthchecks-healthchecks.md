## 📝 Summary

Healthchecks is an open-source monitoring service that tracks the status of cron jobs and background tasks. It's designed to notify users when tasks fail or become unresponsive, helping ensure the reliability and stability of their systems. Built with Python and Django, Healthchecks provides a simple and scalable solution for task monitoring.
