## 📝 Summary

Rocky is an open-source, Rust-based SQL engine that offers a range of advanced features, including support for data branching, replay capabilities, and column lineage. This engine enables developers to manage data complexity and collaborate more effectively across different projects and versions. By leveraging Rust, Rocky aims to provide a fast and secure foundation for SQL operations.
