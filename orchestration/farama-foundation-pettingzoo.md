## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the PettingZoo project:

PettingZoo is an open-source API standard for multi-agent reinforcement learning environments, providing a unified interface for experimenting with complex scenarios. It offers a variety of popular reference environments and utilities to facilitate the development and comparison of multi-agent AI models. By standardizing multi-agent environments, PettingZoo enables seamless collaboration and innovation in this rapidly evolving field.
