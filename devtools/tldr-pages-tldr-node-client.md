## 📝 Summary

### English

tldr‑node‑client is a lightweight, open‑source command‑line tool written in Node.js that lets developers quickly view simplified, community‑maintained “tldr” cheat‑sheet pages for common command‑line utilities. It fetches, caches, and formats the pages for easy offline access, providing a fast alternative to the full man pages. The project is maintained under the tldr‑pages organization and is categorized as a DevTools utility.
