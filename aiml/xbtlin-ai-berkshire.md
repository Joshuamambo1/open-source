## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary of the open-source project:

The xbtlin/ai-berkshire project is an open-source research framework for value investing, inspired by the methods of four legendary investors: Warren Buffett, Charlie Munger, Yiqing Shen, and Li Lu. Built on top of Claude Code, this framework leverages AI and multi-agent parallel research to provide insights and strategies for value investors. By combining the expertise of these four masters, ai-berkshire aims to provide a comprehensive and data-driven approach to value investing.
