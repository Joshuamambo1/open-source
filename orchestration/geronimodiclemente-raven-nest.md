## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the project:

Raven-Nest is an open-source, multi-agent terminal that enables developers to seamlessly manage and interact with multiple AI agents in parallel, including Claude Code, Codex, and Gemini. Built with Electron and React, this desktop application streamlines the experience of running multiple AI agents concurrently. By providing a centralized interface, Raven-Nest simplifies the process of AI orchestration for developers.
