## 📝 Summary

This open-source project explores the performance of large language models (LLMs) in classical hyperparameter optimization, discovering that they often fall short of traditional algorithms. The project's findings challenge the conventional wisdom that LLMs can outperform traditional methods in this domain. By comparing the efficacy of LLMs to established hyperparameter optimization algorithms, the project sheds new light on the effectiveness of these approaches.
