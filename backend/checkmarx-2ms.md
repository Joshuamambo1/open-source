## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the Checkmarx/2ms project:

Too many secrets (2MS) is an open-source project designed to help protect sensitive information, known as secrets, across various platforms and systems, including files, Content Management Systems (CMS), chats, and Git repositories. By leveraging this tool, users can effectively conceal and manage their sensitive data, reducing the risk of unauthorized exposure. This project is part of Checkmarx's efforts to enhance security and data protection.
