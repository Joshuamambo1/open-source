## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the gen0sec/synapse project:

Synapse is an open-source, active Network Detection and Response (NDR) solution that leverages eBPF technology to provide comprehensive protection against east-west and north-south threats. It offers active fingerprint blocking, firewall, and proxy functionality to safeguard systems from various security risks. With Synapse, users can proactively detect and respond to potential security incidents in real-time.
