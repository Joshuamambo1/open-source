## 📝 Summary

The avaly/paddle-sdk is an open-source Node.js SDK for integrating with Paddle.com, a payment gateway that facilitates online transactions. This SDK enables developers to easily incorporate Paddle's payment processing capabilities into their applications, streamlining the payment experience for their users. By leveraging this SDK, developers can focus on building their core product while relying on a reliable and scalable payment solution.
