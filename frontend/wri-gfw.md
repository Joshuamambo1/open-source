## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the wri/gfw project:

The wri/gfw project is an open-source, online platform for near-real-time global forest monitoring. This tool allows for the tracking of forest cover changes, deforestation, and land use patterns worldwide, providing valuable insights into the health of our planet's forests. By utilizing this data, users can engage in informed decision-making and conservation efforts to protect the world's precious forest ecosystems.
