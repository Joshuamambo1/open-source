Here's a brief summary for the Milkshiift/GoofCord open-source project:

This project offers a customizable and secure alternative to traditional Discord clients, prioritizing user privacy and flexibility. With GoofCord, users can personalize their Discord interface and enjoy a more private and streamlined experience.
