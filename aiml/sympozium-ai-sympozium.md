Here's a brief summary for the sympozium project:

This tool allows for agentical administration of the cluster, providing a flexible and scalable platform for AI-driven automation and decision-making. By leveraging Kubernetes, Sympozium facilitates the efficient operation and control of AI agents in a distributed environment.
