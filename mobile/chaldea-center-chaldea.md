## 📝 Summary

Chaldea is an open-source project offering a Material Planner and Battle Simulator for Fate/Grand Order (FGO), a popular mobile game. This tool allows players to plan and optimize their in-game resources, as well as simulate battles to test strategies and improve gameplay. By providing a community-driven platform, Chaldea aims to enhance the FGO experience for players worldwide.
