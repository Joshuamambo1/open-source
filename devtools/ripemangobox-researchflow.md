## 📝 Summary

ResearchFlow is an open-source project that aims to simplify complex research workflows by providing a semi-automated research assistant and local knowledge base. This tool supports multiple stages of the research process, including paper analysis, ideation, coding, experimentation, writing, and publication management. By streamlining these tasks, ResearchFlow helps researchers focus on their work while maintaining a centralized and organized knowledge base.
