## 📝 Summary

The joypad-os project is an open-source, universal firmware core designed for controller adapters and custom controllers. Its primary goal is to provide a flexible and extensible foundation for developing adaptive and AI-driven gaming experiences. By leveraging AI/ML capabilities, joypad-os enables controllers to learn and adapt to users' preferences, creating a more personalized and immersive gaming experience.
