## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the open-source project:

Out Loud is an open-source desktop text-to-speech (TTS) application available for macOS, Windows, and Linux. This free and customizable tool enables users to convert written text into spoken words, offering a convenient solution for those who prefer to listen to digital content. Developed through community contributions, Out Loud is a versatile and user-friendly TTS app.
