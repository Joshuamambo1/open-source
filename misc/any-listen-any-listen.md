## 📝 Summary

Any-Listen is an open-source, cross-platform private music playback service designed to provide a secure and personalized music listening experience. This project allows users to stream their own music collections from their local devices, ensuring that their music libraries remain private and accessible only to them. By harnessing the power of open-source technology, Any-Listen offers a flexible alternative to traditional music streaming services.
