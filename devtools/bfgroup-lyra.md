## 📝 Summary

Lyra is an open-source, command-line parser for C++ 11 and beyond, designed to be simple and composable. It allows developers to easily create robust and user-friendly command-line interfaces for their applications. With Lyra, users can focus on building their application's logic without worrying about the intricacies of command-line parsing.
