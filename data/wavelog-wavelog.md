## 📝 Summary

Wavelog is an open‑source, web‑based logging platform designed for amateur radio operators to record, manage, and analyze their contacts from any device with internet access. It offers a feature‑rich interface—including real‑time QSO tracking, award calculations, DXCluster integration, and customizable reports—while storing all data securely in a centralized database. Ideal for hams seeking a flexible, cloud‑friendly solution for their logging needs.
