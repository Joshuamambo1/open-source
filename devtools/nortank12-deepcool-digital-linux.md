## 📝 Summary

This open-source project, "deepcool-digital-linux," is a Linux version of the DeepCool Digital Windows software, providing users with a similar experience on Linux-based operating systems. The project is designed to bridge the gap for DeepCool users who prefer or require Linux, allowing them to control and monitor their DeepCool devices seamlessly. It falls under the DevTools category, aimed at developers and enthusiasts interested in creating and customizing Linux software.
