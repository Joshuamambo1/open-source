## 📝 Summary

This open-source project, dasniko/keycloak-extensions-demo, is a collection of demos, examples, and playgrounds designed to facilitate learning and experimentation with Keycloak extensions, providers, and SPI (Service Provider Interface) implementations. It serves as a practical resource for developers looking to extend Keycloak's functionality and integrate custom features into their identity and access management systems. By utilizing this project, developers can explore and test various Keycloak extensions in a controlled environment.
