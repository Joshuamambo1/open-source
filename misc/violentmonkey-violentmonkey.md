## 📝 Summary

Violentmonkey is a free and open-source userscript manager that enables users to customize and personalize their browsing experience by running scripts on web pages. This versatile extension supports browsers with WebExtensions capabilities, allowing users to access a wide range of userscripts that can enhance, modify, or automate web interactions. Violentmonkey provides users with flexibility and control over their online activities.
