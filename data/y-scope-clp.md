## 📝 Summary

The y-scope/clp (Compressed Log Processor) is an open-source log management tool designed to efficiently compress and store log data. Its key feature is the ability to search and query compressed logs without requiring decompression, making it a valuable solution for managing large log datasets. This allows for faster data analysis and improved log management capabilities.
