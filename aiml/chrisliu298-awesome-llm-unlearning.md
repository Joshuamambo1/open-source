## 📝 Summary

The **awesome-llm-unlearning** repository curates a comprehensive collection of papers, datasets, tools, and benchmarks related to machine unlearning techniques for large language models. It serves as a one‑stop resource for researchers and practitioners seeking to understand, evaluate, and develop methods that can efficiently remove or forget specific information from LLMs while preserving overall performance.
