## 📝 Summary

The Void Player is an open-source, native macOS music player for local files that offers a lightweight alternative to other players. Weighing in at only 1.6MB, this project provides a straightforward and efficient way to play music on Mac devices. Developed and shared on GitHub, Void Player allows users to enjoy their local music collections without unnecessary features or bloated software.
