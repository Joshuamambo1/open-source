## 📝 Summary

Indico is an open-source event management system designed to handle a wide range of conference and workshop tasks, from abstract submission to online registration. Developed by CERN, the project aims to provide a robust and customizable platform for managing complex events. With its feature-rich capabilities, Indico supports various event types and formats, making it a versatile solution for the scientific community and beyond.
