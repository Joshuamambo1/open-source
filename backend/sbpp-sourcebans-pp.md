## 📝 Summary

SourceBans++ is an open-source administrative management system designed for the Source engine, allowing for efficient management of player access and communication within online servers. This comprehensive tool enables administrators to easily manage bans, configure permissions, and maintain a clear record of server activity. With SourceBans++, server administrators can streamline their workflow and provide a better experience for users.
