## 📝 Summary

HactarCE/Hyperspeedcube is an open-source project that brings a modern twist to the classic Rubik's cube, offering a beginner-friendly 3D and 4D simulator. This immersive experience allows users to explore and solve complex Rubik's puzzles in a visually engaging and interactive environment. By simulating a wide range of cube dimensions, Hyperspeedcube provides an innovative platform for cubing enthusiasts to learn and master the art of speedcubing.
