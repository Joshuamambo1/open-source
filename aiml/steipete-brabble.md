## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the steipete/brabble project:

Brabble is an open-source, locally run AI application inspired by the iconic "Hey, Computer" from Star Trek, allowing users to interact with their local machine as if it were an intelligent assistant. The project enables users to run custom hooks after specific trigger commands, giving them a high degree of control and flexibility. With Brabble, users can take advantage of local AI capabilities without relying on external services.
