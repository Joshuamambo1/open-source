## 📝 Summary

The okta/terraform-provider-okta project provides a Terraform provider for managing Okta resources, allowing users to provision and manage Okta objects such as users, groups, and applications using infrastructure-as-code. This enables organizations to automate and streamline their Okta setup and configuration. By integrating Terraform with Okta, users can maintain a consistent and reproducible infrastructure setup across their organization.
