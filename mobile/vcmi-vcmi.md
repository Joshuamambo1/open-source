## 📝 Summary

The vcmi project is an open-source engine that allows players to experience the classic game Heroes of Might and Magic III on various platforms, including mobile devices. Based on the original game, vcmi offers a faithful recreation of the game's mechanics, graphics, and sound, while also providing additional features and improvements. This project aims to preserve and enhance the gaming experience of a beloved classic.
