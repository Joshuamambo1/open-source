## 📝 Summary

Seal is an open-source video and audio downloader for Android devices, built on top of the yt-dlp framework. It provides users with a seamless experience for downloading content from various platforms, allowing them to manage and store their media files locally. This mobile application offers a user-friendly interface, making it easily accessible to a wide range of users.
