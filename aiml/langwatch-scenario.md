## 📝 Summary

The langwatch/scenario project is an open-source tool designed for agentic testing of codebases, specifically tailored for agentic codebases that incorporate artificial intelligence and machine learning capabilities. This tool enables developers to simulate real-world scenarios and interactions to ensure the reliability and robustness of their code. By leveraging agentic testing, developers can identify and address potential issues before deployment.
