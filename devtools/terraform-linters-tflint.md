## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the project:

Tflint is an open-source, pluggable Terraform linter that helps users identify and fix errors and inconsistencies in their infrastructure as code (IaC) configurations. With a modular design, tflint allows users to extend its functionality with custom rules and integrations, making it a highly flexible tool for infrastructure developers. By catching potential issues early on, tflint helps ensure the quality and reliability of Terraform configurations.
