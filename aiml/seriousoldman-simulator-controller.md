## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the project:

Simulator-Controller is an open-source project that utilizes AI technology to create a realistic pit crew experience for sim racing enthusiasts. Powered by GPT, this virtual AI-based pit crew simulates real-life pit stops, allowing users to focus on the driving experience without manual pit crew management. This innovative tool brings a new level of immersion to the world of sim racing.
