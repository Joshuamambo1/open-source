## 📝 Summary

Here's a 2-3 sentence summary of the project:

HugoBlox's Hugo Theme Academic CV is an open-source academic portfolio that simplifies citation tracking and boosts online visibility. This AI-powered theme generates pages from Markdown files, allowing users to maintain full ownership of their content. Features include auto-imported BibTeX citations, support for Jupyter notebooks, LaTeX documents, and slides, as well as a free hosting option for permanent online presence.
