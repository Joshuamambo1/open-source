## 📝 Summary

The onellm project provides a unified interface for seamless interaction with hundreds of large language models (LLMs), offering caching and fallback mechanisms to enhance reliability. This open-source project simplifies the process of working with various LLMs, streamlining development and improving overall performance. By providing a standardized interface, onellm enables developers to easily integrate and switch between multiple LLMs in their applications.
