## 📝 Summary

Vincent‑Uden’s **miro** is a lightweight, native PDF viewer designed for Windows and Linux (supporting both Wayland and X11). It focuses on speed and simplicity while offering fully configurable keybindings, allowing users to tailor navigation and actions to their workflow. The project is open‑source and aims to provide a clean alternative to heavyweight PDF readers.
