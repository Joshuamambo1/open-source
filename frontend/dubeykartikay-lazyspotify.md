## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the project:

This open-source project, lazyspotify, offers a terminal-based Spotify client for macOS and Linux, providing users with a command-line interface to manage their Spotify playlists and music libraries. The application aims to simplify music management and playback through a user-friendly terminal experience. With lazyspotify, users can access and control their Spotify music from the comfort of their terminal.
