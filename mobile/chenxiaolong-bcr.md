## 📝 Summary

The BCR (Basic Call Recorder) project is an open-source call recording application designed for rooted Android devices. It provides a simple and effective way to record phone calls, allowing users to capture and manage their call history with ease. By leveraging Android root access, BCR offers a seamless call recording experience for users who require this feature on their devices.
