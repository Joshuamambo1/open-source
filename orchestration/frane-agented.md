## 📝 Summary

Agnented is an open-source text editor specifically designed for Large Language Models (LLMs), allowing for seamless interaction and customization to enhance their performance. This innovative tool provides a unique interface for LLMs to access, manipulate, and learn from text data, unlocking new possibilities for AI research and development. By leveraging Agented, developers can fine-tune and optimize their LLMs to better meet the demands of complex natural language tasks.
