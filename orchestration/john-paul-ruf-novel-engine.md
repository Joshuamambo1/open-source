## 📝 Summary

The Novel Engine is an open-source, desktop application that streamlines the novel-building process by utilizing seven AI agents to handle the editorial pipeline. This allows users to focus on developing their story, from pitch to published manuscript, without being bogged down by the intricacies of the editorial process. Powered by Claude, the Novel Engine is free, gatekeeper-free, and designed to help users bring their ideas to life.
