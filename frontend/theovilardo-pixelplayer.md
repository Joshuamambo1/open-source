## 📝 Summary

PixelPlayer is a free and open-source Android music player prioritizing user privacy. Built with Material 3 Expressive, it offers a range of features including offline playback, lyrics synchronization, customizable equalizer presets, and Chromecast support for seamless casting to other devices. This mobile application caters to users seeking a secure and feature-rich music listening experience.
