## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary of the OpenCut-app/OpenCut project:

OpenCut is an open-source video editing application, designed as a free alternative to the popular mobile video editor CapCut. This versatile tool offers a range of features to help users create professional-looking videos with ease. By leveraging its open-source nature, developers and users can collaborate to improve and expand the app's capabilities.
