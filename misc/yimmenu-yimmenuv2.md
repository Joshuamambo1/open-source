## 📝 Summary

YimMenu and YimMenuV2 are open-source, experimental menu systems designed for GTA 5: Enhanced, a popular modification for the game Grand Theft Auto V. These menus aim to provide an alternative and customizable interface for players, allowing them to personalize and enhance their gameplay experience.
