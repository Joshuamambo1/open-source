> Bella OpenAPI是一个提供了丰富的AI调用能力的API网关，可类比openrouter，与之不同的是除了提供聊天补全(chat-completion)能力外，还提供了文本向量化(text-embedding)、语音识别(ASR)、语音合成(TTS)、文生图、图生图等多种AI能力，同时集成了计费、限流和资源管理功能。且集成的所有能力都经过了大规模生产环境的验证。

## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary of the LianjiaTech/bella-openapi project:

Bella OpenAPI is an open-source API gateway that provides a wide range of AI capabilities, including text embedding, speech recognition, text-to-speech synthesis, and generative models. With a focus on production-readiness, Bella OpenAPI offers robust features such as billing, rate limiting, and resource management. Its modular design and scalable architecture make it an ideal solution for integrating AI capabilities into various applications.
