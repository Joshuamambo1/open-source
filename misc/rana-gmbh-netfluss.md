## 📝 Summary

NetFluss is a free, open-source menubar app that provides real-time insights into your system's network performance. It displays your current upload and download speeds, as well as the top apps consuming bandwidth, helping you identify potential network bottlenecks. With NetFluss, you can stay on top of your system's network activity in a simple and intuitive way.
