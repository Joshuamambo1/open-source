## 📝 Summary

This project, bevy_garage, is an open-source, gamelike car simulation platform for experimenting and developing artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning (ML) models. It utilizes the Bevy game engine as its core, providing a flexible environment for users to test and refine their AI/ML algorithms in a simulated car driving scenario. This playground is ideal for researchers and developers looking to innovate in the field of autonomous vehicles and AI.
