## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the akivacp/chatgpt-json-tree-viewer project:

The akivacp/chatgpt-json-tree-viewer is a free, offline tool that enables users to explore and visualize conversation exports from popular AI models like ChatGPT, DeepSeek, Claude, Grok, and Mistral in a user-friendly tree format. This standalone HTML viewer allows users to navigate and understand the structure of their AI-generated conversations without relying on online services. By providing a local, offline interface, users can maintain the privacy and control of their conversations.
