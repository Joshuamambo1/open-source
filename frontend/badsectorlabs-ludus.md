## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the badsectorlabs/ludus project:

Ludus is an open-source system that enables the creation of user-friendly cyber environments for testing and development purposes. It provides a platform for building and managing secure, isolated spaces for experimentation and innovation in the field of cybersecurity. With Ludus, users can easily set up and manage their own cyber environments, streamlining the process of testing and development.
