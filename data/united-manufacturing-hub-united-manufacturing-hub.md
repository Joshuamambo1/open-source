## 📝 Summary

The United Manufacturing Hub is an open-source data platform designed to support the needs of manufacturing industries. It provides a centralized hub for collecting, processing, and analyzing data to improve manufacturing efficiency, productivity, and decision-making. By offering a scalable and customizable solution, the platform aims to bridge the gap between data and manufacturing operations.
