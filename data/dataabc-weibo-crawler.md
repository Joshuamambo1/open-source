## 📝 Summary

The **dataabc/weibo-crawler** project is a Python‑based web scraper designed to collect data from Sina Weibo. It extracts posts, user information, and associated media, automatically downloading images and videos linked to each Weibo entry. Ideal for researchers and developers needing large‑scale Weibo datasets for analysis or machine‑learning tasks.
