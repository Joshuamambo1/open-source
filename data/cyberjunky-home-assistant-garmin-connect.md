## 📝 Summary

This open-source project integrates Home Assistant with Garmin Connect, enabling seamless data exchange between the two platforms. Users can now access and upload their fitness and health data from Garmin Connect to Home Assistant for a more comprehensive view of their daily activities. This integration offers a convenient and automated way to track and monitor fitness goals within Home Assistant.
