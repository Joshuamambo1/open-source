### English

Synapse AI is an open‑source platform that lets developers build, connect, and orchestrate AI agents capable of performing real‑world actions. By supporting any large language model—whether hosted locally, in the cloud, or accessed via CLI—it provides a flexible framework for creating end‑to‑end AI workflows. The project focuses on orchestration, AI/ML integration, and a user‑friendly frontend for managing agent pipelines.
