## 📝 Summary

The Neuro-Symbolic engine is an open-source project that leverages artificial intelligence to autonomously verify complex mathematical proofs, specifically the GCT (Geometric Complexity Theory) barrier in Lean 4. This innovative project combines the strengths of both symbolic and connectionist AI methods to tackle challenging mathematical problems. By achieving autonomous verification of the GCT barrier, the project contributes to advances in the field of computational complexity theory.
