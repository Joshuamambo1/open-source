## 📝 Summary

Napari is an open-source, interactive, multi-dimensional image viewer for Python, designed to provide a fast and intuitive experience for exploring and analyzing complex image data. This versatile tool allows users to visualize, manipulate, and analyze 2D and 3D images, making it an essential resource for scientists, researchers, and data analysts. With napari, users can easily navigate and interact with their image data in a highly customizable environment.
