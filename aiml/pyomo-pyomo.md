## 📝 Summary

Pyomo is an open-source, object-oriented algebraic modeling language in Python designed to simplify the creation of structured optimization problems. Its intuitive interface enables users to easily model complex systems, solve optimization problems, and visualize results, making it a powerful tool for researchers and practitioners in fields such as operations research, engineering, and artificial intelligence. By leveraging Python's extensive libraries and ecosystem, Pyomo streamlines the optimization process and facilitates the development of efficient, scalable solutions.
