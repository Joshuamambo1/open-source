## 📝 Summary

The Simulink Agentic Toolkit is an open-source project that enables AI agents to effectively interact with Simulink and Model-Based Design, providing them with the necessary tools and expertise. This toolkit allows AI agents to integrate with Simulink models, facilitating seamless collaboration and automation in the design process. By leveraging the Simulink Agentic Toolkit, users can unlock new possibilities for AI-assisted modeling and simulation.
