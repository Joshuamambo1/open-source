## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the freerouting project:

freerouting is an open-source, advanced PCB (Printed Circuit Board) auto-router designed to efficiently and effectively route complex electronics layouts. This innovative tool utilizes sophisticated algorithms to optimize routing, reducing design time and improving overall circuit performance. By harnessing the power of artificial intelligence and machine learning, freerouting streamlines the PCB design process for engineers and makers alike.
