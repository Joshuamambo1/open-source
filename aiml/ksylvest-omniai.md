## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary of the ksylvest/omniai project:

Omniai is an open-source project that standardizes APIs for multiple artificial intelligence (AI) providers, allowing developers to interact with different AI systems through a unified interface. This project currently supports popular AI services such as OpenAI's Chat GPT, Mistral's LeChat, and others, making it easier to integrate AI capabilities into various applications. By providing a standardized API, omniai aims to simplify the development process and enable seamless collaboration between developers and AI systems.
