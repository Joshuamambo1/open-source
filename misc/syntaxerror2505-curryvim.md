## 📝 Summary

Curryvim is an open-source, Neovim-based distro designed to provide a unique and customized experience for developers. Unlike other Neovim variants, Curryvim aims to stand out by offering a tailored setup that diverges from the VSCode-like interface, instead focusing on a more minimalistic and customizable approach. This distro is built to cater to users seeking a more personalized Neovim setup.
