## 📝 Summary

The RISC-V Architectural Certification Tests (ACTs) are an open-source collection of assembly language tests. These tests are designed to verify that a RISC-V implementation accurately adheres to the RISC-V specification, ensuring compliance and correctness. By utilizing the ACTs, designers and developers can validate their RISC-V-based systems and ensure they function as intended.
