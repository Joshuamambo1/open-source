## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the calrs project:

Calrs is a fast and self-hostable scheduling platform, designed to be a scalable alternative to commercial solutions like Cal.com. Built using Rust, Calrs offers a customizable and private scheduling solution for individuals and organizations. This open-source platform allows users to host and manage their own scheduling needs without relying on third-party services.
