## 📝 Summary

Vleer is a free, open-source music platform that offers a subscription-free alternative to traditional music streaming services. It provides an innovative frontend experience, allowing users to enjoy their favorite music without the added cost of subscription-based models. This project aims to promote music accessibility and democratize the way people engage with music online.
