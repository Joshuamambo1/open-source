## 📝 Summary

The Wayland Maker project is an open-source, Wayland compositor inspired by the classic Window Maker window manager. It aims to bring a similar, user-friendly interface experience to the modern Wayland desktop. By blending traditional window management with the latest Wayland technologies, Wayland Maker offers a unique blend of functionality and elegance.
