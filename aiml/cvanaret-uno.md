## 📝 Summary

Uno is an open-source project that empowers users to fully manage their Sequential Quadratic Programming (SQP) or barrier solver for nonlinearly constrained optimization tasks. This project aims to provide a high degree of control over these complex algorithms, enabling users to tailor them to their specific needs. By leveraging Uno, developers and researchers can optimize nonlinearly constrained problems with increased flexibility and customization.
