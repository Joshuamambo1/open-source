## 📝 Summary

The LSFG-Android project is an open-source application designed for Android devices. It generates Lossless Scaling Frames (LSFs) which enable seamless scaling of images and videos without compromising their quality. This mobile application leverages LSFs to provide a high-performance solution for Android users who require lossless image and video scaling.
