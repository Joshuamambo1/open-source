## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary of the project:

This open-source project, hh-applicant-tool, is a script/utility designed to automate various tasks on hh.ru, a popular Russian job search platform. It enables users to respond to suitable job postings, update their resumes, and perform other actions with ease. The project is aimed at simplifying the job search process for individuals and can be used as a starting point for developers looking to build more advanced automation tools.
