### English

qcadoo MES is an open‑source, web‑based Manufacturing Execution System that helps factories monitor, control, and optimize production processes in real time. It offers a user‑friendly interface for managing orders, workstations, inventory, and quality checks, while providing integration hooks for ERP, PLCs, and other shop‑floor equipment. The platform is built to be extensible and customizable, making it suitable for a wide range of manufacturing environments.
