## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary of the MetaScreener project:

MetaScreener is an open-source, AI-powered tool designed to streamline the abstract and PDF screening process in systematic reviews. By leveraging machine learning algorithms, this platform aims to increase efficiency and accuracy in identifying relevant studies, thereby reducing the time and labor required for manual screening. With its flexible and customizable architecture, MetaScreener is an invaluable resource for researchers and reviewers seeking to enhance their systematic review workflows.
