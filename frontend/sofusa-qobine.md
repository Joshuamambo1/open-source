## 📝 Summary

SofusA/qobine is an open-source multimedia player that provides users with a variety of playback options, including a terminal-based interface (TUI), web-based interface, and RFID-enabled capabilities. It is specifically designed to play music from Qobuz, a popular music streaming service. This project caters to users seeking a versatile and flexible music player for their Qobuz account.
