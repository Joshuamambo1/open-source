## 📝 Summary

TeleMem is an open‑source, high‑performance drop‑in replacement for Mem0 that provides semantic deduplication, persistent long‑term dialogue memory, and multimodal video reasoning capabilities. Designed for orchestration and AI/ML workflows, it enables developers to store, retrieve, and reason over large volumes of textual and visual context efficiently. The project offers seamless integration with existing systems while delivering fast, scalable memory management for advanced conversational agents.
