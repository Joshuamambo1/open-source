## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary of the open-source project:

AgentPlane is an open-source, governed CLI framework that enables deterministic and auditable AI agent workflows. This framework facilitates the orchestration of AI/ML tasks, ensuring reproducibility and transparency throughout the process. By providing a structured and accountable environment, AgentPlane empowers developers to build and deploy AI solutions with confidence.
