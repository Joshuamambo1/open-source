## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the fireshare project:

Fireshare is an open-source project that enables users to self-host their media and share it with unique links, providing a decentralized alternative to traditional file-sharing platforms. This self-hosted solution offers users greater control over their content and data, promoting online privacy and security. By hosting their own media, users can easily share files without relying on external services.
