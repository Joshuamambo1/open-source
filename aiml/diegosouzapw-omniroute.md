## 📝 Summary

OmniRoute is an open-source AI gateway that enables seamless integration with multiple large language models (LLMs) from different providers, including OpenAI. It features smart routing, load balancing, and retries to ensure reliable and efficient inference, while also providing policies, rate limits, caching, and observability for cost-aware and high-performance AI operations. This versatile gateway empowers developers to build robust and scalable AI applications.
