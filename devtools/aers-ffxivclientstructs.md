## 📝 Summary

This open-source project, aers/FFXIVClientStructs, provides essential resources for reverse-engineering the native classes of the Final Fantasy XIV (FFXIV) client. It offers a valuable collection of information and data for developers and researchers seeking to understand the internal workings of the game's client-side architecture. By contributing to this project, the FFXIV modding and development community can advance their knowledge and create new content for the popular MMORPG.
