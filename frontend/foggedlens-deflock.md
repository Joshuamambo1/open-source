## 📝 Summary

Deflock is an open-source crowdsourced tool designed to help locate and report Automatic License Plate Readers (ALPRs). This platform empowers users to contribute to a collective effort to monitor and track ALPRs, providing valuable insights into their presence and usage. By leveraging community-driven data, Deflock aims to promote transparency and accountability regarding the use of ALPR technology.
