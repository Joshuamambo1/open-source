## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the MacCalendar project:

MacCalendar is a free and open-source, offline macOS calendar application that integrates beautifully into the menu bar. It supports both the Gregorian calendar and the traditional Chinese lunar calendar, as well as displays Chinese holidays and customizable vacation schedules. The app also integrates with system events and allows users to view their schedule at a glance.
