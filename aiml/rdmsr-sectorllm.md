## 📝 Summary

The sectorllm project is an open-source, ultra-compact implementation of the Llama2 AI model, specifically designed to run on x86 assembly within a remarkably small footprint of under 1500 bytes. This achievement showcases the potential for efficient and lightweight AI inference in resource-constrained environments. By leveraging optimized assembly code, sectorllm enables fast and accurate Llama2 inference in a remarkably compact package.
