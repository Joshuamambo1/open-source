## 📝 Summary

The Orallexa AI Trading Agent is an open-source, multi-agent system that employs machine learning models and large language models to facilitate a dialogue between rival trading strategies, Bull and Bear, before making investment decisions. This cutting-edge approach utilizes a combination of 9 ML models, real-time dashboard monitoring, and adaptive strategy evolution to optimize trading outcomes. The Orallexa platform offers a unique AI-powered trading experience that leverages debate and strategy evolution to drive informed investment decisions.
