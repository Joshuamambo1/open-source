## 📝 Summary

Emberstack/kubernetes-reflector is an open-source, custom Kubernetes controller designed to simplify the replication of sensitive resources such as secrets, configmaps, and certificates across multiple environments. This tool provides a scalable solution for DevOps teams to maintain consistent and secure configuration across their clusters. By automating the replication of these resources, developers can focus on application development, while infrastructure management is streamlined.
