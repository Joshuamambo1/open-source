## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the open-source project:

Swiftide is a high-performance, open-source Rust library for fast indexing, querying, and integrating Large Language Models (LLMs) into various applications. It enables efficient, streaming-based operations for LLMs, making it suitable for a wide range of AI and data-intensive use cases. With Swiftide, developers can build scalable and responsive applications that leverage the power of LLMs.
