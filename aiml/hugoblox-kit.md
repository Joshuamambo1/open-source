## 📝 Summary

HugoBlox/kit is an open-source project that enables users to create websites by snapping together pre-designed blocks, similar to building with Legos. This innovative platform leverages AI to generate websites from Markdown, allowing users to focus on content rather than coding. With support for various templates such as landing pages, blogs, and portfolios, HugoBlox/kit offers a flexible and user-friendly way to deploy websites.
