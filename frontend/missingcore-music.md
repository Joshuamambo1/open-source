## 📝 Summary

MissingCore/Music is an open-source, local music player inspired by the popular mobile app Nothing. This project offers a minimalistic interface and a seamless music listening experience, allowing users to easily manage and play their local music collections. By leveraging a locally-focused approach, MissingCore/Music provides a faster and more private alternative to cloud-based music streaming services.
