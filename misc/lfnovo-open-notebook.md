## 📝 Summary

Open-Notebook is a self-hosted notebook project that caters to the needs of developers who want to run NotebookLM on their own servers. This project was highlighted by the YouTube channel "Better Stack" in their video "I Found the Self-Hosted NotebookLM Devs Actually Want (Open-Notebook)". With Open-Notebook, users can have full control over their data and customization options, making it an attractive alternative to cloud-based solutions.
