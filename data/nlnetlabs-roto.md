## 📝 Summary

Roto is an open-source, statically-typed, compiled embedded scripting language designed for Rust. It is used by the Rotonda project and provides a robust and efficient solution for scripting within Rust applications. With its compiled nature, Roto offers a balance between flexibility and performance.
