## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the open-source project:

"Intel's tsffs is an open-source, coverage-guided fuzzer that utilizes SIMICS to efficiently test and identify vulnerabilities in UEFI, kernel, firmware, and BIOS software. This snapshotting fuzzer leverages machine learning to guide its testing, ensuring comprehensive coverage and rapid discovery of potential security issues. By utilizing tsffs, developers can significantly improve the reliability and security of complex software systems."
