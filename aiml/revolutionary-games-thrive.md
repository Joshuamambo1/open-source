## 📝 Summary

Thrive is an open-source evolution game that simulates the development of life on Earth, from single-celled organisms to complex ecosystems. Developed by the Revolutionary Games community, the game utilizes AI and machine learning to create a dynamic and realistic environment that showcases the evolution of life over millions of years. By exploring the game, players can gain a deeper understanding of the intricate processes that shape the natural world.
