## 📝 Summary

Kale is an open-source platform designed by Kubeflow to support data scientists in their workflow, providing a set of tools and features to streamline data preparation, model development, and deployment. This platform aims to simplify the data science process, making it more efficient and accessible for users. With Kale, data scientists can focus on building and refining their models without getting bogged down in infrastructure and logistics.
