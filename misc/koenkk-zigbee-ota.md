## 📝 Summary

The Koenkk/zigbee-OTA project is an open-source collection of Zigbee Over-The-Air (OTA) files, providing developers and users with access to firmware updates for Zigbee devices. This repository serves as a central hub for sharing and maintaining Zigbee OTA files, facilitating the development and improvement of connected devices. By contributing to this project, users can help ensure the longevity and compatibility of their Zigbee-enabled products.
