## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the intro-skipper project:

intro-skipper is an open-source tool designed to automatically detect and skip intro/credit sequences in Jellyfin, allowing users to quickly access their desired content without interruptions. This project aims to enhance the viewing experience by streamlining the process of navigating through video introductions. By leveraging machine learning or other detection methods, intro-skipper aims to minimize user interaction.
