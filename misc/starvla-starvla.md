## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the starVLA project:

StarVLA is an open-source project that aims to simplify the development of vision-language-action models through a modular, Lego-like codebase. This framework enables researchers and developers to assemble and customize their models using a variety of pre-built components, streamlining the process of creating and testing complex AI systems. By providing a flexible and extensible foundation, StarVLA facilitates innovation and collaboration in the field of vision-language-action research.
