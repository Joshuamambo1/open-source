## 📝 Summary

The LLM Zoomcamp is a free, 10-week online course that teaches real-life applications of Large Language Models (LLMs). Through this course, participants will learn how to build an AI system that effectively answers questions about a knowledge base, gaining hands-on experience in AI/ML, frontend development, and data handling. By the end of the course, learners will be equipped with the skills to apply LLMs in practical scenarios.
