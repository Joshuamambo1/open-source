## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the stencila/stencila open-source project:

Stencila is an open-source platform designed to create, collaborate, and manage documents with embedded scientific intelligence, enabling users to incorporate data, code, and visualizations directly into their documents. This innovative tool aims to enhance the way researchers, scientists, and educators communicate complex ideas by providing a seamless integration of content and computational capabilities. By doing so, Stencila facilitates more efficient and effective knowledge sharing in various fields.
