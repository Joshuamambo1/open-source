## 📝 Summary

Llamatik is an open-source project that enables on-device AI capabilities for Kotlin Multiplatform applications across various platforms, including Android, iOS, desktop, JVM, and WASM. It leverages llama.cpp, whisper.cpp, and stable-diffusion.cpp to provide features such as Large Language Models (LLM), Speech-to-Text, and Image Generation. This project empowers developers to integrate AI into their cross-platform applications.
