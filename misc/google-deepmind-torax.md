## 📝 Summary

TORAX is an open-source project that utilizes the JAX library to simulate transport phenomena in a Tokamak, a type of nuclear fusion reactor. This simulator aims to model and analyze the behavior of plasma within the Tokamak, providing valuable insights for the development of future fusion reactors. By leveraging JAX for efficient and flexible computations, TORAX enables researchers to explore complex transport dynamics in Tokamaks.
