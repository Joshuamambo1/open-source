## 📝 Summary

Helix is an open‑source framework that automates the management and recovery of AI‑agent payment pipelines, ensuring continuous operation with a 90.3 % auto‑recovery rate. By integrating crypto‑based transaction handling with orchestration tools, it provides self‑healing infrastructure that detects failures, rolls back problematic states, and restores services without manual intervention. The project is aimed at developers building resilient, decentralized payment systems for AI agents.
