## 📝 Summary

ORIPA is an open-source drawing software designed specifically for creating and visualizing origami crease patterns. Its unique feature allows users to calculate the folded shape of the paper from the pattern, enabling precise and efficient origami design. By leveraging ORIPA, origami enthusiasts and designers can bring their creations to life with ease.
