## 📝 Summary

UglyFeed is an open-source project that leverages Large Language Models to retrieve, aggregate, filter, evaluate, rewrite, and serve RSS feeds, providing a versatile tool for research, learning, and experimentation. This project aims to push the boundaries of AI-powered RSS feed management, enabling users to tap into the potential of language models for data processing and analysis. By harnessing the capabilities of Large Language Models, UglyFeed empowers users to explore new frontiers in RSS feed manipulation and analysis.
