## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the fosrl/gerbil project:

This backend application is built to cater to the needs of Pangolin users, offering a hassle-free experience for configuring and managing WireGuard interfaces. By leveraging Gerbil, users can efficiently manage their WireGuard settings with ease.
