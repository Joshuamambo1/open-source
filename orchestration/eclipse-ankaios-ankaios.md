## 📝 Summary

Eclipse Ankaios is an open-source project that offers a workload and container orchestration solution specifically designed for embedded devices, such as automotive High-Performance Computers (HPCs). It enables efficient management and deployment of AI and ML workloads on resource-constrained devices. By providing a scalable and customizable orchestration framework, Ankaios streamlines the development and operation of intelligent systems in embedded environments.
