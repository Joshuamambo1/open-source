## 📝 Summary

The Animite project is an open-source Android application that serves as a client for the popular anime and manga database AniList. This mobile tool allows users to access and manage their AniList accounts directly from their Android devices, providing a convenient and streamlined experience. With Animite, users can easily track their anime and manga collections and stay up-to-date with the latest information from the AniList community.
