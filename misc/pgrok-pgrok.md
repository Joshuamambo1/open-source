## 📝 Summary

pgrok is an open-source, multi-tenant HTTP/TCP reverse tunnel solution that utilizes SSH remote port forwarding. It provides a simple and secure alternative to ngrok, allowing users to expose local services to the internet from behind firewalls or restricted networks. By leveraging SSH, pgrok ensures that data transmitted through the tunnel remains encrypted.
