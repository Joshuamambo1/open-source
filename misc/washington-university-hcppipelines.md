## 📝 Summary

This open-source project, Washington-University/HCPpipelines, provides processing pipelines for the Human Connectome Project (HCP). It offers a collection of tools and workflows to analyze and process HCP data, enabling researchers to efficiently and effectively interpret brain imaging data. By utilizing this project, researchers can streamline their analysis and gain valuable insights into brain function and structure.
