## 📝 Summary

### English

LunaMultiplayer is an open‑source multiplayer mod that enables players to share a single Kerbal Space Program (KSP) universe in real time, allowing collaborative building, mission planning, and flight operations. It synchronizes spacecraft, physics, and player actions across networked sessions while preserving the core KSP experience, making it easy for friends or community groups to explore space together.
