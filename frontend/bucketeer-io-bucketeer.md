## 📝 Summary

Bucketeer is an open-source feature flag management and A/B testing platform that enables developers to easily manage and experiment with new features in their applications. With Bucketeer, teams can create, manage, and roll out features to specific user segments, reducing the risk of feature launches and improving the overall user experience. By providing a robust and customizable solution, Bucketeer empowers development teams to innovate and optimize their applications with ease.
