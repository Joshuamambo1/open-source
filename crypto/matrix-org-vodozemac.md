## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary of the matrix-org/vodozemac project:

vodozemac is an open-source implementation of the Olm and Megolm cryptographic ratchets in pure Rust. These cryptographic ratchets are used for end-to-end encryption in messaging applications, providing secure and reliable communication. By using vodozemac, developers can integrate secure messaging capabilities into their applications with a robust and verified implementation.
