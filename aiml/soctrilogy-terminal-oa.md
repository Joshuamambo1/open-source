## 📝 Summary

**Summary:**  
SoCtrilogy/terminal-oa is an open‑source experiment that pairs two alien‑trained large language models (LLMs) to converse with each other in a constructed alien language. The project showcases how specialized LLMs can generate and interpret non‑human linguistic patterns, providing a playful testbed for multi‑agent communication and language emergence research.
