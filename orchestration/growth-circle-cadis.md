## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary of the Growth-Circle/cadis project:

cadis is an open-source, Rust-first runtime that enables local-first, multi-agent collaboration with a desktop Heads-Up Display (HUD) and policy-gated tools. This innovative platform allows for voice communication and worktree-isolated coding agents, facilitating seamless team collaboration and development. With its unique architecture, cadis streamlines AI/ML and frontend development workflows.
