## 📝 Summary

The srwi/keypeek project is an open-source, live on-screen keyboard overlay that dynamically reflects the active keyboard layout of QMK, Vial, or ZMK in real-time. This feature-rich overlay allows users to visualize their keyboard layout on their screen, providing a convenient and intuitive way to navigate and interact with their keyboard. By mirroring the active layers, keypeek enhances the overall keyboard experience for users.
