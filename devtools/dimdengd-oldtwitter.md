## 📝 Summary

This open-source project, OldTwitter, is an extension designed to restore the classic Twitter layout from 2015 and 2018. Users can experience the nostalgic interface of Twitter's past by installing this extension, which aims to bring back the familiar look and feel of the old platform. By installing OldTwitter, users can relive the memories of Twitter's earlier versions.
