## 📝 Summary

The Cat Is Under Mayonnaise is an open-source project that enables users to fine-tune and modify the behavior of large language models (LLMs) without the need for extensive retraining. This innovative approach allows users to inject custom logic and preferences into LLMs, making them more adaptable and responsive to specific use cases. By doing so, the project facilitates the creation of tailored models that can perform tasks more accurately and efficiently.
