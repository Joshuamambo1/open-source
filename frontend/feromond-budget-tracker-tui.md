## 📝 Summary

Feromond/budget_tracker_tui is an open-source, Rust-based application that provides a simple and intuitive command-line interface (TUI) for tracking income and expenses. This tool helps users visualize their financial transactions and gather basic insights to make informed decisions about their budget. By utilizing a TUI, it offers a distraction-free way to manage finances on any computer or terminal.
