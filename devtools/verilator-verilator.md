## 📝 Summary

Verilator is an open-source SystemVerilog simulator and lint system, designed to provide a fast and accurate way to test and verify digital circuit designs. This powerful tool allows developers to compile and execute SystemVerilog code, enabling them to simulate, debug, and optimize their designs more efficiently. With its open-source nature, Verilator provides a flexible and collaborative platform for the verification and development of digital circuits.
