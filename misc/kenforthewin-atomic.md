## 📝 Summary

The atomic project is an open-source, self-hosted personal knowledge base that enables users to organize and connect their knowledge in a semantically meaningful way. This system allows individuals to store, link, and retrieve information across various contexts, creating a dynamic and interconnected network of knowledge. By hosting their own knowledge base, users can maintain control over their data and have complete flexibility in how they structure and visualize their information.
