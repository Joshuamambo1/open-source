This open-source project explores the concept of pixel embeddings as a viable alternative to vision encoders for understanding and generating visual data. By leveraging pixel embeddings, researchers aim to achieve unified understanding and generation capabilities, potentially leading to breakthroughs in computer vision and image processing. The project's findings suggest that pixel embeddings may offer a more efficient and effective approach to visual data processing.
