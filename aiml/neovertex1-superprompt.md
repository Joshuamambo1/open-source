## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the SuperPrompt project:

SuperPrompt is an open-source initiative focused on designing effective prompts that can help elucidate the workings of AI agents. By developing and refining these prompts, researchers aim to better comprehend the decision-making processes and limitations of AI systems, ultimately driving advancements in AI understanding and development. This project contributes to the ongoing efforts in AI research and transparency.
