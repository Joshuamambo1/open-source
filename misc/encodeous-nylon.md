## 📝 Summary

The nylon project, developed by encodeous, is an open-source initiative that enables dynamic routing on WireGuard for a wide range of users. This project aims to simplify the setup and management of WireGuard's dynamic routing capabilities, making it more accessible to those who need it. By providing a user-friendly and customizable solution, nylon helps bridge the gap between WireGuard's advanced features and its ease of use.
