## 📝 Summary

TypeCellOS/BlockNote is a highly extensible React-based rich text editor that utilizes a block-based approach, reminiscent of Notion's interface. Built on top of Prosemirror and Tiptap, this open-source project provides a robust foundation for creating custom rich text editing experiences. Its modular design makes it ideal for developers looking to tailor the editor to their specific needs.
