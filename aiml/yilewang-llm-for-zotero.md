## 📝 Summary

The **llm-for-zotero** project adds an intelligent research‑assistant layer to your Zotero library, letting large language models query, summarize, and interact with your personal references and notes. By integrating directly with Zotero’s database, it enables AI‑driven literature discovery, citation generation, and knowledge extraction while keeping all data under your control. This open‑source tool is ideal for researchers who want a seamless, privacy‑preserving AI companion for managing and exploring scholarly sources.
