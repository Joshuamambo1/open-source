## 📝 Summary

This open-source project is a recreation of the music assembly player originally used in Commodore 64 games composed by Martin Galway. The player is designed to accurately play the SID (Sound Interface Device) chip sound effects from classic Commodore 64 games. By utilizing this player, users can experience the authentic music and sound effects from Martin Galway's iconic Commodore 64 game scores.
