## 📝 Summary

SingularityCE is an open-source container platform that provides a simple, fast, and secure environment for running isolated applications. This community edition of Singularity is designed to facilitate easy deployment and management of artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning (ML) workloads, as well as other applications. By leveraging SingularityCE, users can enhance their computing infrastructure with containerized environments.
