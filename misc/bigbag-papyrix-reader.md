## 📝 Summary

### English

bigbag/papyrix-reader is a lightweight, open‑source firmware designed for the Xteink X4 and X3 e‑paper readers. It adds support for popular document formats such as EPUB, FB2, Markdown and plain text, while allowing users to customize the device with their own themes and fonts. The project aims to provide a simple, community‑driven alternative to proprietary firmware, enhancing readability and personalization on low‑power e‑ink hardware.
