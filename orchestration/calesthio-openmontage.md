Here's a 2-3 sentence summary for the OpenMontage project:

OpenMontage is an innovative, open-source video production system that leverages AI to enable users to create high-quality video content with ease. Built on a robust framework with 12 pipelines, 52 tools, and over 500 agent skills, this agentic system empowers users to transform their AI coding assistants into full-fledged video production studios. By breaking down the complexity of video production, OpenMontage democratizes access to multimedia content creation.
