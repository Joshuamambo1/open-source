## 📝 Summary

Vocaluxe is an open-source singing game that offers a fun and engaging experience for music enthusiasts. Inspired by popular singing games like SingStar and Ultrastar Deluxe, Vocaluxe provides a platform for users to sing along to their favorite songs and compete with others. This free and community-driven project allows users to access a wide range of songs and features through a user-friendly interface.
