The **fw‑ai/cookbook** repository is a curated collection of “recipes” and supporting resources that show developers how to build, deploy, and fine‑tune generative AI models using the Fireworks platform. It covers end‑to‑end workflows—from data preparation and model selection to integration with frontend applications—making it a practical guide for AI/ML engineers and product teams looking to accelerate AI‑powered product development.
