## 📝 Summary

Fornjot is an early-stage, open-source b-rep (boundary representation) CAD (computer-aided design) kernel developed in Rust. This project aims to provide a foundation for 3D model creation and manipulation, focusing on performance and flexibility. By leveraging Rust's capabilities, Fornjot offers a unique approach to CAD development.
