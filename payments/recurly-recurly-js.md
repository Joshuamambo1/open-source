## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the recurly/recurly-js project:

Recurly-js is an open-source, browser-based subscription billing solution that enables developers to easily manage recurring payments and subscriptions. This simple and intuitive library streamlines the process of handling complex billing scenarios, making it ideal for businesses requiring seamless and secure transactions. With recurly-js, developers can focus on building their application while leaving the payment processing to the library.
