## 📝 Summary

The NativeScript CLI is an open-source project providing a suite of command-line tools for building, testing, and deploying NativeScript applications. These tools enable developers to simplify their workflow, streamline their development process, and efficiently manage their mobile apps across multiple platforms, including iOS and Android. With the NativeScript CLI, developers can focus on writing code, while the CLI handles the underlying complexities of mobile app development.
