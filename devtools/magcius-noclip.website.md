## 📝 Summary

noclip.website is an open-source digital museum showcasing video game levels, allowing users to explore and interact with iconic game environments. This project provides a unique platform for gamers and developers to revisit and appreciate the intricate design and detail that goes into creating immersive game worlds. By preserving and sharing these levels, noclip.website serves as a testament to the artistry and innovation in video game development.
