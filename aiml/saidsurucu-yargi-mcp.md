## 📝 Summary

Here's a 2-3 sentence summary for the open-source project:

Yargi-MCP is an open-source MCP (Machine-Readable Court Decisions) server designed to facilitate access to Turkish legal databases. This project allows for the standardized storage and querying of court decisions, enabling data-driven insights and applications for the Turkish legal community. By providing an open-source framework, Yargi-MCP promotes transparency and collaboration in the development of AI/ML and data-driven tools for the legal sector.
