## 📝 Summary

GhidrAssist is an open-source extension for the Ghidra reverse engineering framework that utilizes Large Language Models (LLMs) to provide AI-assisted analysis and insights. This tool enables users to leverage the power of AI in their reverse engineering workflows, facilitating more efficient and accurate code analysis, decompilation, and understanding. With GhidrAssist, users can tap into the capabilities of LLMs to augment their Ghidra experience.
