## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary of the noq project:

noq is an open-source, Rust-based implementation of the QUIC (Quick UDP Internet Connections) protocol, designed to provide a fast and reliable alternative to traditional TCP connections. This project aims to bring the benefits of QUIC to a wide range of applications, from web development to distributed systems. By leveraging Rust's performance capabilities, noq seeks to deliver efficient and scalable QUIC connectivity.
