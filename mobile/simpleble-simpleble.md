## 📝 Summary

SimpleBLE is an open-source, cross-platform Bluetooth library providing a unified interface for developing Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) applications. It supports a wide range of operating systems, including MacOS, iOS, Windows, Linux, and Android, making it a versatile tool for developers. With SimpleBLE, you can easily create and manage BLE connections, characteristics, and services in your projects.
