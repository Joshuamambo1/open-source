## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the androidqf project:

androidqf is an open-source tool that assists in rapidly collecting forensic evidence from Android devices to detect potential security breaches. It provides a comprehensive collection of digital artifacts, enabling investigators to reconstruct the device's history and identify potential signs of compromise. This tool is designed to streamline the forensic analysis process, making it easier to gather critical evidence in digital investigations.
