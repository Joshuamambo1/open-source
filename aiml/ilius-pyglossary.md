## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the ilius/pyglossary project:

ilius/pyglossary is an open-source tool designed to convert dictionary files, also known as glossaries, into a usable format for offline access across various operating systems and devices. This project aims to facilitate the integration of offline glossaries with open-source dictionaries, promoting cross-platform accessibility. By leveraging AI/ML capabilities, ilius/pyglossary streamlines the process of converting and utilizing offline glossaries.
