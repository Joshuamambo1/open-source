## 📝 Summary

The RTLSDR-Airband project is an open-source, multichannel demodulator designed to receive and decode AM and NFM signals. This project utilizes RTL-SDR technology to provide real-time decoding capabilities, making it an essential tool for radio enthusiasts and researchers in the fields of cryptography and AI/ML. By leveraging RTLSDR-Airband, users can gain insights into encrypted or hard-to-decipher communications.
