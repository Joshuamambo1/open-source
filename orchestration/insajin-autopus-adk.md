## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary of the Autopus-ADK project:

Autopus-ADK is an open-source, multi-model orchestration platform that empowers agents to manage and optimize their workflows. With its flexible architecture, agents can choose from various orchestration models, including consensus, pipeline, debate, and fastest, to suit their specific needs. The project also features Architecture-as-Code, Lore decision tracking, and a SPEC/EARS engine to support data-driven decision making.
