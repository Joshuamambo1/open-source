## 📝 Summary

The Z3 Theorem Prover is an open-source, general-purpose theorem prover for satisfiability (SAT), satisfiability modulo theories (SMT), and higher-order logic problems. Developed by Microsoft Research, it provides a high-performance platform for solving complex mathematical and logical problems. Z3 is widely used in formal verification, artificial intelligence, and cybersecurity applications.
