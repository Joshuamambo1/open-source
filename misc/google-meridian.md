## 📝 Summary

Meridian is an open-source framework developed by Google, specifically designed to empower advertisers with the ability to create and manage their own in-house machine learning models. This framework, known as the MMM (Model Management Module) framework, provides a flexible and scalable platform for advertisers to set up and run their own models. By leveraging Meridian, advertisers can gain greater control and insight into their advertising campaigns.
