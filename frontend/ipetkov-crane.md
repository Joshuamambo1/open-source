## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the ipetkov/crane project:

Crane is an open-source Nix library designed to simplify the process of building Cargo projects. It achieves this by utilizing incremental artifact caching, ensuring that builds are only executed when necessary and reducing duplication. By leveraging Crane, developers can optimize their build workflows and take advantage of Nix's declarative configuration management.
