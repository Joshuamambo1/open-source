## 📝 Summary

The bugzmanov/bookokrat project is an open-source, terminal-based e-book reader capable of displaying EPUB, PDF, and DJVU formats. This tool allows users to read a wide range of digital books within a command-line interface, providing a simple and accessible reading experience. By leveraging the terminal, bookokrat offers a unique approach to e-book consumption.
