## 📝 Summary

Publint is an open-source project that focuses on linting packaging errors, providing developers with a tool to identify and correct issues in their packages. This project aims to help maintain high-quality packaging by catching errors early in the development process. By utilizing publint, developers can ensure their packages are error-free and easily maintainable.
