## 📝 Summary

Here is a brief summary for the open-source project:

Greenlight is an open-source client for xCloud and Xbox home streaming, allowing users to access and control their console gaming experience from the comfort of their PC or other devices. Developed in Typescript, this client provides a seamless streaming experience, empowering users to play their favorite Xbox games on a wider range of platforms. With Greenlight, users can enjoy a more flexible and portable gaming experience.
