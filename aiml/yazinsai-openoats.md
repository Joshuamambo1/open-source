## 📝 Summary

OpenOats is an open-source AI-powered meeting note-taker that revolutionizes the way we record and engage with meeting minutes. This innovative tool utilizes machine learning to capture key takeaways, action items, and decisions made during a meeting, providing a comprehensive summary of the discussion. By talking back, OpenOats facilitates real-time feedback and ensures that all stakeholders are on the same page.
