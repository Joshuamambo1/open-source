## 📝 Summary

RELATE is an open‑source, web‑based platform designed to support educators and learners by providing an interactive environment for creating, delivering, and managing educational content. Built with modern frontend technologies and packaged with DevOps‑friendly infrastructure, it enables easy deployment, customization, and scaling for classroom or remote‑learning scenarios. The project aims to streamline the teaching workflow while fostering collaboration between instructors and students.
