## 📝 Summary

DocSpell is an open-source project that helps users organize their digital documents by automating the process of categorizing and managing documents from various sources, such as scanners and emails. It aims to minimize the effort required to maintain a structured and easily accessible document repository. With its AI/ML capabilities, DocSpell streamlines document organization, making it a valuable tool for those dealing with large volumes of electronic paperwork.
