## 📝 Summary

Sidekick is a native macOS application that lets you converse with a locally‑run large language model, enabling real‑time queries over the contents of your files, folders, and web pages without installing any additional software. Powered by llama.cpp, it brings on‑device AI assistance to everyday tasks while keeping data private and offline. The project combines AI/ML capabilities with a sleek frontend for seamless macOS integration.
