## 📝 Summary

gpustack is an open‑source GPU cluster manager that streamlines the provisioning, configuration, and orchestration of inference engines such as **vLLM** and **SGLang**. By handling GPU resource allocation, load balancing, and runtime scaling, it enables high‑performance, low‑latency deployment of large AI models across multiple nodes. The project is targeted at AI/ML teams looking to maximize inference throughput and simplify multi‑GPU infrastructure management.
