## 📝 Summary

The CourtListener project is an open-source initiative that provides a comprehensive archive of court data, offering users a fully-searchable and accessible platform to explore a vast repository of court opinions, oral arguments, and related information. This archive also includes data on judges and federal filings, aiming to increase transparency and accessibility of court records. The project's goal is to make court data more easily accessible, fostering greater understanding and analysis of the judicial system.
