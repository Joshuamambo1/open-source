## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the project:

Gofannon is an open-source web app that enables rapid prototyping of AI agents and their associated user interfaces. With Gofannon, users can build, preview, and share agent-driven experiences without being locked into a specific platform, facilitating collaboration and innovation in AI development. It combines functionality for AI/ML with frontend and backend capabilities.
