## 📝 Summary

Here's a 2-3 sentence summary for the project:

MirotalkC2C is an open-source, self-hosted WebRTC platform for secure 1-to-1 video calling. This peer-to-peer communication system ensures end-to-end privacy and limits each room to two participants for maximum security. By hosting MirotalkC2C yourself, you can enjoy real-time, immersive video conversations while maintaining complete control over your data.
