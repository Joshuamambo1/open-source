## 📝 Summary

AI Agent Passport is an open‑source specification that provides a standardized, verifiable identity for artificial‑intelligence agents, enabling them to present cryptographically signed credentials about their capabilities, provenance, and permissions. By defining a portable “passport” format and associated tooling, the project aims to foster trust, interoperability, and accountable interactions among AI services, platforms, and users across the AI/ML ecosystem.
