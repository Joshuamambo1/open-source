## 📝 Summary

The 17/agentsecrets project provides a zero‑knowledge secrets management platform designed specifically for AI agents, enabling them to generate, store, and retrieve confidential data without ever exposing the plaintext to the host system. By combining secure cryptographic primitives with a developer‑friendly API, the infrastructure lets AI‑driven applications operate autonomously while maintaining strict confidentiality, making it a versatile tool for AI/ML, frontend, and DevOps workflows.
