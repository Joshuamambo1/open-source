## 📝 Summary

HKUDS/RAG-Anything is an open-source, all-in-one framework for RAG (Reparameterized Attention Granularity) models, designed to simplify the development and deployment of large-scale language models. This comprehensive framework offers a range of tools and features to support the training, evaluation, and fine-tuning of RAG models for various NLP tasks. By providing a unified platform, HKUDS/RAG-Anything aims to accelerate research and innovation in AI and ML.
