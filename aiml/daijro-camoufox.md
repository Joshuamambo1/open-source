## 📝 Summary

Camoufox is an open-source, anti-detect browser designed to provide enhanced online anonymity and security. Developed using AI/ML technologies, this project enables users to create and manage multiple browser profiles with unique fingerprints, making it ideal for tasks that require stealth or evasion of tracking. Camoufox aims to empower users with increased control over their online presence and digital footprints.
