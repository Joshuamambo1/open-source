## 📝 Summary

The NVIDIA-RTX/NRD library is an open-source, real-time denoising solution that leverages the power of NVIDIA GPU acceleration to significantly reduce noise and artifacts in images and videos. This library, part of the NVIDIA Real-time Denoising (NRD) project, enables developers to integrate high-quality, real-time denoising capabilities into their applications and workflows. With NRD, users can enhance visual fidelity and improve overall image quality with minimal computational overhead.
