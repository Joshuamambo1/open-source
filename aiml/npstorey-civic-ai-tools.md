## 📝 Summary

The npstorey/civic-ai-tools project is an open-source platform designed to bridge the gap between artificial intelligence (AI) assistants and government open data. This platform includes a robust MCP server, a curated directory of civic MCPs, and an anti-hallucination framework to ensure accurate data extraction from all 559 Socrata portals. The project aims to facilitate seamless integration of AI with government data, promoting transparency and accountability.
