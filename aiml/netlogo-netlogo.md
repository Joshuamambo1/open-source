## 📝 Summary

NetLogo is an open-source, multi-agent modeling platform designed for individuals ranging from kids to scientists. It allows users to create simulations using basic elements such as turtles, patches, and links to model complex systems and behaviors. By providing an intuitive and accessible interface, NetLogo fosters interdisciplinary collaboration and exploration in fields like AI, data analysis, and education.
