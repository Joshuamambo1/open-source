## 📝 Summary

### English

EdgeX Go is the official monorepository for the Golang‑based microservices that form the core of the EdgeX Foundry open‑source IoT platform. It houses all production‑ready services, adapters, and supporting libraries, enabling developers to build, deploy, and extend edge‑computing solutions with a consistent, cloud‑native architecture. The repository is maintained by the EdgeX Core/Support Working Group and welcomes contributions from the community.
