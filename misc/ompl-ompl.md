## 📝 Summary

The Open Motion Planning Library (OMPL) is an open-source software library that provides a wide range of motion planning algorithms for various robotic systems. OMPL allows users to easily implement and compare different motion planning techniques, making it a valuable resource for researchers and developers in robotics and computer science. Its flexible and modular design enables efficient planning for complex motion problems.
