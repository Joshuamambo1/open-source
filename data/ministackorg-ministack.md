## 📝 Summary

Ministack is a free, open-source local emulator that mimics the functionality of Amazon Web Services (AWS), offering more than 40 services and compatibility with Terraform. This comprehensive emulator also includes real databases, making it an ideal tool for developers and DevOps professionals to test and deploy applications in a local environment. With a perpetual free license, Ministack is an accessible solution for anyone looking to streamline their development workflow.
