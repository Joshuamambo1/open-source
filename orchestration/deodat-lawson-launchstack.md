## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the LaunchStack project:

LaunchStack is an open-source AI-powered StartUp Accelerator Engine built on Next.js, LangChain, and PostgreSQL with pgvector. This cutting-edge platform enables users to upload, organize, and engage with documents through intuitive chat functionality, while also leveraging predictive analytics and role-based workflows to streamline operations. By extracting valuable insights at the page level, LaunchStack provides a comprehensive solution for startups and innovation teams to accelerate their growth.
