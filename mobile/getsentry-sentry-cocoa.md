## 📝 Summary

The Sentry Cocoa SDK is an open-source project that provides a seamless integration of error tracking and monitoring for iOS, tvOS, macOS, watchOS, iPadOS, and visionOS applications. This official SDK enables developers to collect and analyze errors, crashes, and performance issues, allowing for swift identification and resolution of issues. By utilizing Sentry, developers can improve the overall stability and user experience of their mobile apps.
