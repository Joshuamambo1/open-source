## 📝 Summary

Angelica is an open‑source mod that serves as a drop‑in replacement for OptiFine on Minecraft 1.7.10, providing performance enhancements, visual tweaks, and configurable graphics options without requiring the proprietary OptiFine library. It integrates with the GTNewHorizons modpack ecosystem, offering a fully open‑source alternative for players seeking improved frame rates and customizable rendering features.
