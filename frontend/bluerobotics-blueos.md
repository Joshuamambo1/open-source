## 📝 Summary

BlueOS is an open-source platform designed for the operation, development, and expansion of Remotely Operated Vehicles (ROVs), Unmanned Surface Vehicles (USVs), and robotic systems. This versatile platform offers a comprehensive solution for users to build, customize, and manage their robotic systems, making it an ideal choice for both beginners and experienced developers. With its modular architecture and extensive community support, BlueOS facilitates the creation of innovative robotic solutions.
