## 📝 Summary

The noah-nuebling/mac-mouse-fix project is an open-source initiative aimed at enhancing the functionality of the Mac mouse, specifically those with a price point of $10, to make them comparable or even superior to the Apple trackpad in terms of performance and usability. This project seeks to optimize the user experience of budget-friendly Mac mice, providing users with a more seamless and efficient interaction with their devices. By leveraging open-source development, this project aims to democratize access to improved Mac mouse technology.
