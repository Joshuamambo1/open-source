## 📝 Summary

### English

MouseMaster is an open‑source tool that lets you control the cursor without a physical mouse by using gestures, eye‑tracking, or other sensor inputs, while also providing deep keyboard remapping and macro capabilities. It offers a highly configurable, cross‑platform interface for creating custom shortcuts, layered keymaps, and advanced input pipelines, making it ideal for power users and accessibility enthusiasts.
