## 📝 Summary

HighwayEnv is a lightweight, open‑source simulation library that provides minimalist yet realistic driving scenarios for testing and benchmarking decision‑making algorithms in autonomous vehicles. It offers a collection of configurable highway and intersection environments, complete with vehicle dynamics, traffic agents, and customizable reward structures, making it ideal for reinforcement learning and other AI research. The package is easy to install, extensible, and integrates seamlessly with popular RL frameworks.
