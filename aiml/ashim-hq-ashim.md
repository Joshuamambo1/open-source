## 📝 Summary

The Ashim project is an open-source, self-hosted image manipulation platform that packs over 45 tools, a local AI engine, and customizable pipelines into a single Docker container. This allows users to process and transform images directly on their machine, without relying on cloud services or compromising their data's integrity. By keeping all processing local, Ashim ensures complete control and discretion over sensitive image data.
