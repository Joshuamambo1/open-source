## 📝 Summary

TetherFuseNet is an open-source, root-free solution for internet sharing, designed for mobile devices. This project enables users to share their internet connection without requiring root access, providing a convenient and secure way to connect multiple devices to the internet. By leveraging a flexible and adaptable architecture, TetherFuseNet makes internet sharing accessible to a broader range of users.
