## 📝 Summary

GT-New-Horizons-Modpack is a massive, open-source questing modpack for Minecraft 1.7.10 that seamlessly integrates with GregTech, offering an immersive and challenging gameplay experience. This modpack features a progressive structure, with a gradual increase in difficulty and complexity as players progress through the questline. By combining a vast array of mods, GT-New-Horizons-Modpack provides a rich and engaging world to explore and conquer.
