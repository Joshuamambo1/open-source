## 📝 Summary

The Realiserad/fish-ai project is an open-source tool that integrates Large Language Models (LLMs) into the Fish shell, elevating command-line interactions and providing real-time shell scripting assistance. This innovation aims to revolutionize the command-line experience by harnessing AI capabilities. By leveraging LLMs, users can receive intelligent suggestions and auto-completion for their shell commands.
