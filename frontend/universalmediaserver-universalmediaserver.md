## 📝 Summary

The UniversalMediaServer is an open-source media server that enables streaming of multimedia content across multiple platforms, including DLNA, UPnP, and HTTP(S) enabled devices. This versatile server provides a seamless media experience by allowing users to access and play their favorite content on various devices, both locally and remotely. By supporting multiple protocols, it offers users a wide range of compatibility options.
