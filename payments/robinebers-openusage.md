## 📝 Summary

OpenUsage is a free and open-source tool designed to help users track and manage their subscription services, preventing unnecessary expenses and wasted resources. It aims to make it easier to monitor and control one's subscriptions, ensuring that payments align with actual usage. By providing a clear overview of subscription services, OpenUsage helps users make more informed financial decisions.
