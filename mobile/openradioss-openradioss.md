## 📝 Summary

Here's a 2-3 sentence summary for the OpenRadioss project:

OpenRadioss is an open-source finite element solver for dynamic event analysis, offering a powerful and industry-proven solution for simulating complex events. This mobile-friendly tool enables users to analyze and predict the behavior of structures under various dynamic loads, providing valuable insights for industries such as aerospace, automotive, and construction. By leveraging OpenRadioss, users can optimize designs and improve safety, ultimately reducing costs and accelerating innovation.
