## 📝 Summary

Chainloop is an open-source project that serves as an evidence store and policy engine for software supply chain attestations and related data, including SBOMs, VEX, SARIF, and QA reports. This platform helps organizations maintain a centralized record of their software development lifecycle (SDLC) data, ensuring compliance and transparency. By supporting various data formats and attestations, Chainloop facilitates a more secure and trustworthy software development process.
