## 📝 Summary

Turmoil is an open-source project that introduces artificial instability to your tests, simulating real-world conditions by injecting failures and errors. This tool helps developers ensure their applications can withstand adverse scenarios, making them more resilient and robust. By incorporating Turmoil into your testing workflow, you can identify and fix potential issues before they impact production environments.
