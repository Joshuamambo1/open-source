## 📝 Summary

ClassifierAI is an open-source Chrome extension that uses machine learning to detect AI-generated content on the DEV platform. Developed by Two Devs and a Copilot, this prototype aims to identify and differentiate between human-written and AI-generated content, promoting transparency and authenticity in online communities. The project showcases the potential of AI/ML in content classification.
