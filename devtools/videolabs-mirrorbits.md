## 📝 Summary

### English

Mirrorbits is an open‑source, Go‑based download redirector that intelligently routes client requests to the nearest or best‑performing mirror server. By leveraging geographic and health data, it balances load across a distributed mirror network, making large file distribution faster and more reliable. Ideal for DevTools and DevOps/Infra teams needing scalable, automated mirror management.
