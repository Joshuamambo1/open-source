## 📝 Summary

Tuppr is an open-source Kubernetes controller designed to streamline the upgrade process of both Talos and Kubernetes environments. It automates upgrades by ensuring minimal downtime and provides a scalable solution for managing complex cluster upgrades. With Tuppr, users can efficiently manage their cluster upgrades and maintain a secure, up-to-date infrastructure.
