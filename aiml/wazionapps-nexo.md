## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary of the NEXO Brain project:

NEXO Brain is an open-source, 100% local AI brain that enables AI agents to share persistent memory, perform semantic reasoning, and evaluate trust scores. This shared brain utilizes a metacognitive guard, natural forgetting, and over 150 Machine Control Protocol (MCP) tools, making it compatible with popular AI clients like Claude Code, Codex, and Claude Desktop. With its focus on local processing and open-source accessibility, NEXO Brain empowers developers to create more intelligent and robust AI applications.
