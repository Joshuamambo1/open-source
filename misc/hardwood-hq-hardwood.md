## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the hardwood project:

Hardwood is an open-source, fast, and minimal dependency implementation of Apache Parquet, a widely-used columnar storage format. This lightweight alternative aims to provide a more efficient and flexible solution for working with large datasets while maintaining compatibility with Parquet files. By reducing dependencies, Hardwood makes it easier to integrate Parquet into various projects and environments.
