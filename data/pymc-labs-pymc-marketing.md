## 📝 Summary

Here's a 2-3 sentence summary for the project:

The PyMC-marketing project is an open-source Bayesian marketing toolbox developed in PyMC, aimed at providing a flexible and interpretable framework for modeling various marketing scenarios. It includes tools for Media Mix Modeling (MMM), Customer Lifetime Value (CLV) analysis, and Buy-Till-You-Die (BTYD) modeling, among others. This project enables data analysts and marketers to leverage Bayesian inference for more accurate and informed marketing decisions.
