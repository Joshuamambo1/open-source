## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary of the project:

The ai-session-governance project provides a cross-tool handoff governance framework for AI development workflows, ensuring continuity and consistency across AI tools like Codex, Claude Code, and Gemini CLI. It implements a structured workflow (PLAN→READ→CHANGE→QC→PERSIST) with anti-drift guardrails to maintain session integrity and prevent data drift. This open-source project enables seamless collaboration and version control in AI development environments.
