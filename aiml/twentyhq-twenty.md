## 📝 Summary

Twenty is an open-source project that aims to provide a free alternative to Salesforce, tailored for the integration of artificial intelligence (AI) capabilities. This innovative platform is designed to offer a more accessible and flexible solution for businesses, developers, and organizations seeking to leverage AI in their operations. By leveraging open-source principles, Twenty seeks to democratize access to AI-driven sales and customer relationship management (CRM) solutions.
