## 📝 Summary

Faker is a pure Elixir library designed to generate realistic, random data for testing, seeding, and development purposes. It offers a wide range of generators—including names, addresses, phone numbers, dates, and custom formats—making it a handy tool for developers who need mock data quickly and reliably. The project is open‑source, well‑documented, and fits within the DevTools and Data categories.
