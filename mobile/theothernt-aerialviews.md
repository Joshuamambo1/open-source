## 📝 Summary

The AerialViews project is an open-source screensaver designed for Android TV devices, including Google TV, Nvidia Shield, and Fire TV. Inspired by Apple TV's video screensaver, it provides a visually appealing experience for users. This free, customizable screensaver offers a unique and engaging way to display stunning aerial footage on Android TV devices.
