## 📝 Summary

The GameNative project is an open-source initiative that enables native PC gaming on Android devices. It integrates popular gaming platforms such as Steam, Epic, GOG, and Amazon, allowing users to access their PC games directly on their Android smartphones and tablets. This project aims to bridge the gap between PC and mobile gaming, providing a seamless and native gaming experience.
