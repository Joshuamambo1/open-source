## 📝 Summary

sherpa is an open-source, mini-package-manager designed for QNAP NAS devices. It enables users to easily install, update, and manage packages on their QNAP systems, providing a convenient and streamlined experience. sherpa aims to simplify the process of managing software on QNAP NAS devices, making it more user-friendly and efficient.
