## 📝 Summary

Kasane is an open-source drop-in frontend for Kakoune, providing an extensible UI foundation. It offers a customizable and adaptable interface for the Kakoune editor, allowing users to personalize their workflow and enhance their editing experience. This project enables seamless integration of Kakoune's powerful editing capabilities with a flexible and user-friendly interface.
