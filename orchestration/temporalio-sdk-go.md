## 📝 Summary

The temporalio/sdk-go project is an open-source, Go-based software development kit (SDK) for the Temporal orchestration platform. This SDK enables developers to integrate Temporal's workflow and task management capabilities into their applications, providing a scalable and fault-tolerant way to orchestrate complex business processes. By leveraging the temporalio/sdk-go, developers can build robust, distributed systems with ease.
