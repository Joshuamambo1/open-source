## 📝 Summary

LLEF is a plugin for LLDB, a popular open-source debugger, designed to enhance its functionality for reverse engineering (RE) and virtualization (VR) applications. This plugin aims to provide researchers and security professionals with a more powerful toolset for analyzing and understanding complex systems. By extending LLDB's capabilities, LLEF facilitates a deeper understanding of operating systems, binary formats, and system internals.
