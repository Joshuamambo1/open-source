## 📝 Summary

Here's a 2-3 sentence summary for the VLA-Handbook project:

The VLA-Handbook is an open-source project designed to provide a comprehensive, Chinese-language guide for algorithm engineers entering the Vision-Language-Action (VLA) field. Focusing on the unique challenges of robotics, this practical handbook offers a valuable resource for learning and preparing for interviews in the VLA domain. It aims to bridge the gap in existing CV/NLP interview guides by offering a specialized roadmap for career advancement in robotics.
