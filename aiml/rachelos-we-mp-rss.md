## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary of the project:

The we-mp-rss project is an open-source platform designed to act as a WeChat public account assistant, providing features like article conversion to Markdown and PDF, automated updates, and RSS feed generation for subscribed articles. It also allows for integration with Webhook, API, and AI Agent interfaces, enabling users to customize and extend its functionality. This platform caters to users' reading habits by providing a comprehensive toolset for WeChat public account management and article analysis.
