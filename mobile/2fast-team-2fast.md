## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the 2fast project:

2fast is an open-source, two-factor authenticator that supports Time-Based One-Time Password (TOTP) across multiple platforms, including Windows 10, Android, iOS, Linux, and macOS. This secure application provides an additional layer of protection against unauthorized access to your accounts, enhancing overall security and peace of mind. With 2fast, you can easily enable two-factor authentication for your online presence with a simple, yet robust solution.
