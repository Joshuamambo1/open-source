## 📝 Summary

Languagetool is an open-source style and grammar checker that supports over 25 languages, helping users refine their writing by identifying errors in grammar, spelling, and syntax. This tool is designed to be highly customizable and adaptable to various writing styles, making it a valuable resource for language learners, writers, and editors. By detecting errors and providing suggestions, Languagetool aims to improve the overall quality and clarity of written content.
