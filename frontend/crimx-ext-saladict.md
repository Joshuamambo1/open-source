## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary of the crimx/ext-saladict project:

Crimesaladict is an open-source, all-in-one dictionary and page translator that offers multiple search modes and features such as page translations, a new word notebook, and PDF selection searching. This frontend-focused application is designed to cater to users' language needs in a professional and efficient manner. It allows users to expand their vocabulary and navigate multilingual content with ease.
