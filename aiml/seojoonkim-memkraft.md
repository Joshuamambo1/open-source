## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the open-source project 'seojoonkim/memkraft':

MemKraft is an open-source, zero-dependency compound knowledge system designed to empower AI agents. This system automates the extraction, classification, and search of knowledge from plain Markdown, enabling AI agents to efficiently store and retrieve information. By leveraging MemKraft, developers can streamline the creation and maintenance of complex AI knowledge bases.
