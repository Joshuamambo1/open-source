## 📝 Summary

Rabtap is an open-source project that provides a RabbitMQ wire tap and a Swiss army knife for various tasks. It enables developers to monitor, inspect, and manipulate RabbitMQ messages, queues, and exchanges, making it a powerful tool for debugging and troubleshooting messaging systems. With rabtap, users can tap into RabbitMQ's functionality and gain deeper insights into their messaging infrastructure.
