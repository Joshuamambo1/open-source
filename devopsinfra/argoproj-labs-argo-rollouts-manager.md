## 📝 Summary

The argo-rollouts-manager is an open-source Kubernetes Operator that integrates with the Argo Rollouts controller to provide a scalable and automated deployment solution. This Operator enables users to manage rollouts across their clusters with ease, allowing for efficient deployment, scaling, and canary releases. By leveraging Argo Rollouts, developers can ensure high availability and minimize downtime during application updates.
