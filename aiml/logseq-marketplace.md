## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the Logseq marketplace project:

The Logseq Marketplace is an open-source, centralized package manager for Logseq plugins, providing a seamless and efficient way to discover, install, and manage AI and ML-powered plugins. This package manager streamlines the process of integrating external plugins into the Logseq ecosystem, making it easier for users to harness the power of AI and ML capabilities within their digital note-taking experience. By centralizing plugin management, the Logseq Marketplace enhances overall user experience and productivity.
