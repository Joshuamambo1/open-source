## 📝 Summary

The UMGCCCFCSR/CytometryInR project is an open-source initiative that aims to provide an educational platform for learning cytometry analysis in the R programming language. This platform is designed to offer a comprehensive resource for students and researchers, covering the fundamentals of flow cytometry and its applications in various fields. By leveraging the power of R, users can easily analyze and visualize cytometric data.
