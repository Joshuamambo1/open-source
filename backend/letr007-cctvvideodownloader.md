## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the open-source project:

This is a network video stream parsing and processing tool, specifically designed for China Central Television (CCTV) online videos. The project, CCTVVideoDownloader, is an open-source backend solution that allows users to download CCTV videos from the web. It aims to provide a convenient and efficient way to access and manage CCTV online content.
