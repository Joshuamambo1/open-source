## 📝 Summary

OpenGov's FormSG is an open-source form builder designed specifically for the Singapore Government. It provides a user-friendly interface for creating and managing forms, allowing government agencies to efficiently collect and process information. This front-end solution is built to meet the needs of Singapore's public sector, promoting greater transparency and accessibility.
