## 📝 Summary

This open-source project, Joplin-plugin-Jarvis, integrates advanced AI systems into Joplin, a note-taking application. It enables users to leverage the capabilities of GPT, Claude, Gemini, Ollama, and Hugging Face models, allowing for a more intelligent and interactive note-taking experience. This plugin aims to enhance productivity and creativity by harnessing the power of AI within the Joplin interface.
