## 📝 Summary

Nextcloud Photos is an open-source project that allows users to store, organize, and access their personal photos and memories within their own secure environment. This frontend application is designed to provide a user-friendly interface for managing and sharing photos, ensuring that memories remain under the user's control. Nextcloud Photos seamlessly integrates with the Nextcloud ecosystem, providing a comprehensive solution for photo management.
