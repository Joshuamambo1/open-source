## 📝 Summary

Smolvm is an open-source tool that enables the creation and execution of portable, lightweight virtual machines. These self-contained VMs offer a convenient way to develop and deploy applications, particularly in AI/ML and frontend development. By leveraging smolvm, developers can create isolated environments for their projects, reducing dependencies and improving overall efficiency.
