## 📝 Summary

avibebuilder/claude-prime is an open‑source toolkit that streamlines the setup of Claude Code’s advanced features—such as custom skills, hooks, agents, and memory systems—into a single, easy‑to‑run command. By automating the orchestration of these components, it lets developers quickly prototype and integrate Claude‑powered AI capabilities into frontend applications without needing extensive configuration. The project targets developers looking for a hassle‑free way to leverage Claude’s full potential in their AI/ML workflows.
