## 📝 Summary

Monal is an open-source, XMPP-based instant messaging client available for both iOS and macOS devices. It allows users to communicate securely over the XMPP protocol, providing a private and decentralized messaging experience. Monal offers a robust and user-friendly interface for managing contacts, conversations, and multimedia content.
