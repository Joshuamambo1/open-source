## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the fan2go project:

fan2go is an open-source daemon that enables dynamic fan speed control based on temperature sensor readings. This lightweight tool allows users to optimize their system's cooling performance by automatically adjusting fan speeds in response to changing temperatures. By providing a simple and efficient solution for fan control, fan2go helps to promote system reliability and extend its lifespan.
