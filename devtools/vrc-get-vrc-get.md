## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the vrc-get project:

vrc-get is a fast, open-source command line and graphical client for managing VRChat packages. It provides an alternative to the official Creator Companion, allowing users to easily install, update, and manage VRChat content. With vrc-get, developers and content creators can streamline their workflow and enhance their VRChat experience.
