## 📝 Summary

AgentLens is an open-source project that provides local in-depth observability for coding-agent sessions, enabling developers to gain insights into their AI and ML workflows. This tool helps users visualize and analyze the behavior of their coding agents, facilitating better understanding, optimization, and troubleshooting of complex AI and ML systems. By offering a local perspective on agent sessions, AgentLens empowers developers to make data-driven decisions and improve the performance of their applications.
