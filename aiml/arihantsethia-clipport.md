## 📝 Summary

Clipport is an open-source project that enables users to seamlessly paste screenshots directly into SSH sessions from iTerm. This productivity-enhancing tool streamlines the workflow of developers and system administrators by eliminating the need for manual image transfer and editing. With Clipport, users can quickly and easily share visual information with colleagues or troubleshoot issues remotely.
