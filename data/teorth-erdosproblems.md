## 📝 Summary

The teorth/erdosproblems repository is a community-driven database that aggregates and organizes problems from the erdosproblems.com site, providing a centralized resource for researchers and enthusiasts interested in number theory and related areas. This open-source project aims to facilitate collaboration and knowledge sharing by making the problems easily accessible and searchable. By contributing to this database, users can help build a comprehensive collection of Erdős problems and their solutions.
