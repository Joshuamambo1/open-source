## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the loopbackio/strong-soap project:

Strong-Soap is an open-source SOAP driver for Node.js, designed to provide a complete rewrite of the popular node-soap library. It offers a robust and efficient way to interact with SOAP-based web services, allowing developers to easily integrate them into their Node.js applications. With Strong-Soap, developers can take advantage of its powerful features and flexibility to build scalable and reliable web services.
