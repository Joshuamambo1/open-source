## 📝 Summary

This open-source browser extension, Fannon, enables users to efficiently search through their bookmarks, browsing history, and open tabs in one convenient interface. Key features include tagging and advanced fuzzy search capabilities, allowing for precise and intuitive query results. By streamlining the search process, Fannon aims to save users time and enhance their overall browsing experience.
