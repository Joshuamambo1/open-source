## 📝 Summary

Tasknotes is an open-source plugin for Obsidian that enables users to manage tasks and track time spent on various activities. It also integrates calendar functionality, allowing users to schedule and organize their tasks in a visually appealing way. This plugin aims to streamline productivity and time management within the Obsidian note-taking platform.
