## 📝 Summary

Schism Tracker is an open-source, old-school sample-based music composition tool designed for creating chiptune and other nostalgic electronic music styles. It features a user-friendly interface and supports various formats for loading and saving songs, samples, and instruments. This project allows users to tap into their creative potential and produce retro-style music with ease.
