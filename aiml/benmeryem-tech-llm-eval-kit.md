## 📝 Summary

The LLM-eval-kit is an open-source, distributed framework for evaluating large language models (LLMs). This project aims to provide a scalable and efficient platform for benchmarking and comparing the performance of various LLMs, facilitating advancements in the field of AI and ML research. By facilitating collaborative evaluation, LLM-eval-kit promotes the development of more accurate and reliable language models.
