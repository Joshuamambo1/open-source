## 📝 Summary

The knockout/tko project is an open-source monorepo designed specifically for Knockout.js 4.0 and above. This monorepo aims to provide a centralized and streamlined way to work with the Knockout.js framework, making it easier to manage and develop Knockout-based applications. By consolidating related projects and tools under a single repository, tko simplifies the development process and fosters collaboration among developers.
