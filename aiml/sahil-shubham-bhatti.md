## 📝 Summary

The sahil-shubham/bhatti project provides a self-hostable platform for creating and managing MicroVMs (lightweight virtual machines) that can serve as agents for artificial intelligence and machine learning applications. This open-source project enables users to host and control their own MicroVMs, offering a high degree of flexibility and autonomy. By utilizing MicroVMs, users can streamline the deployment and management of AI/ML agents, improving overall efficiency and security.
