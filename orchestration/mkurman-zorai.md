## 📝 Summary

Zorai is an open-source platform designed for building and managing complex, long-running tasks and workflows in a multi-agent environment. It provides persistent execution, auditable logging, and learning capabilities, enabling seamless ownership and management of resources, approvals, and tools. Zorai is suitable for orchestration and AI/ML applications that require high levels of automation and scalability.
