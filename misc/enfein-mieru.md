Here's a brief summary of the open-source project "mieru":

Mieru is an open-source proxy tool that enables users to bypass censorship by providing a socks5, HTTP, and HTTPS proxy. This tool allows users to access restricted content by routing their internet traffic through a secure and anonymous proxy server. With mieru, users can maintain their online freedom and anonymity.
