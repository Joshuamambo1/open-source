## 📝 Summary

This open-source project allows developers to build SaaS (Software as a Service) applications where users have control over the storage location of their data. Users can choose to store their data on-premises, in a cloud provider of their choice, or even on their personal devices. By providing this level of flexibility and data sovereignty, the project empowers users to maintain full control over their digital assets.
