## 📝 Summary

The JustAJobApp/jobseeker-analytics project is an open-source initiative that aims to streamline job seekers' experiences by automatically tracking their job searches from their Gmail inbox. This innovative tool connects to users' Gmail accounts, eliminating the need for manual data entry, and presents their job search history in a user-friendly dashboard. By leveraging AI/ML capabilities, it provides valuable insights and analytics to help job seekers optimize their job search strategies.
