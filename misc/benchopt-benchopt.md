## 📝 Summary

Benchopt is an open-source project that provides a framework for creating reproducible and comparable benchmarks. Its purpose is to help researchers and practitioners evaluate the performance of optimization algorithms and other computational methods in a consistent and reliable manner. By leveraging Benchopt, users can easily design and distribute benchmark codes that facilitate direct comparison and sharing of results.
