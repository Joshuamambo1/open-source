## 📝 Summary

Inspect is an open-source framework developed by the UK Government's BEIS department to evaluate the performance of large language models. This tool provides a comprehensive platform for assessing the accuracy, fairness, and reliability of AI models, enabling developers to improve their language processing capabilities. By facilitating rigorous testing and evaluation, Inspect aims to advance the development of trustworthy and effective AI solutions.
