## 📝 Summary

Here's a summary of the 'quincy' project:

Quincy is an open-source, post-quantum VPN solution based on the QUIC protocol, providing a secure and private connection between users. This project aims to offer robust protection against quantum computer threats, ensuring the confidentiality and integrity of data transmitted over the internet. With its QUIC foundation, Quincy enables fast and efficient VPN connections.
