## 📝 Summary

Here's a 2-3 sentence summary for the DialKit iOS project:

DialKit is an open-source SwiftUI package that enables real-time editing and previewing of interface updates, streamlining the development process for iOS apps. This innovative tool allows designers and developers to instantly see the effects of their changes, facilitating seamless collaboration and rapid iteration. By leveraging DialKit, mobile app developers can boost productivity and deliver high-quality user interfaces.
