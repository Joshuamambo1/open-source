## 📝 Summary

**awesome-gpt-image-2** is an open‑source, industrial‑grade prompt‑as‑code engine for GPT‑Image2, offering a comprehensive library of over 370 reverse‑engineered prompt examples and more than 20 high‑quality templates. Designed for developers and creators, it provides ready‑to‑use prompt templates that can be customized programmatically, accelerating image generation workflows and continuously evolving with new cases.
