## 📝 Summary

The Legal Contract Version Control project provides a system for tracking and managing changes to legal contracts, allowing users to maintain a record of revisions and updates. This open-source initiative aims to streamline the contract review and approval process by employing version control principles, commonly used in software development. By doing so, it seeks to increase transparency and reduce errors in contract management.
