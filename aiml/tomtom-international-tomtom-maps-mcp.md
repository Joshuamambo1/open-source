## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the open-source project:

The TomTom Maps MCP is an open-source server that provides TomTom's location services, including search, routing, and traffic data, to AI agents. This protocol allows developers to integrate TomTom's extensive mapping data into their artificial intelligence applications, enhancing their navigation and spatial reasoning capabilities. By leveraging this MCP server, developers can access a wealth of location-based intelligence for their AI projects.
