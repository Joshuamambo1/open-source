## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the agentjido/req_llm project:

The agentjido/req_llm project is an open-source plugin for Req, a request management tool, that enables users to query AI providers. This plugin allows developers to seamlessly integrate AI capabilities into their workflows, making it easier to harness the power of artificial intelligence in their applications. By providing a standardized interface to various AI providers, req_llm streamlines the process of leveraging AI for tasks such as text analysis, image recognition, and more.
