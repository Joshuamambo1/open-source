## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary of the project:

fftext is an open-source project that aims to provide a quick summary, fact check, and ELI5 (Explain Like I'm 5) explanation of CPU-related concepts on the Hacker News platform. This project enables users to easily grasp complex CPU topics, making it a valuable resource for those interested in computer science and technology. By offering clear and concise explanations, fftext promotes a better understanding of CPU fundamentals.
