## 📝 Summary

The RobotLocomotion/drake project is an open-source framework for model-based design and verification in robotics. It enables developers to create and analyze mathematical models of robotic systems, facilitating the design and validation of complex robotic behaviors and control strategies. By providing a unified platform for modeling and verification, drake supports the development of reliable and efficient robotic systems.
