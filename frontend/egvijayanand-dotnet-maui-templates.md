## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary of the open-source project:

This project, .NET MAUI Project and Item Templates, provides open-source templates for creating .NET MAUI projects and items in Visual Studio 2022 and 2026, as well as through the .NET CLI. The templates are designed to simplify the development process for cross-platform mobile applications. By offering these customizable templates, developers can accelerate their workflow and focus on building high-quality .NET MAUI applications.
