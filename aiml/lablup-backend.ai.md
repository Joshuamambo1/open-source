## 📝 Summary

Backend.AI is an open-source, container-based computing cluster platform designed to streamline AI and ML workloads. It supports popular frameworks and languages, as well as various accelerators including GPUs, NPUs, and TPUs, making it an efficient and versatile solution for diverse computing needs. By hosting a range of heterogeneous accelerators, Backend.AI enables seamless and scalable processing of complex AI and ML tasks.
