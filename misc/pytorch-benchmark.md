## 📝 Summary

TorchBench is an open-source collection of benchmarks designed to evaluate the performance of PyTorch, a popular deep learning framework. This comprehensive set of tests allows developers to assess and compare the efficiency of various PyTorch configurations and hardware setups. By providing a standardized evaluation framework, TorchBench facilitates the optimization of PyTorch-based projects for improved performance and scalability.
