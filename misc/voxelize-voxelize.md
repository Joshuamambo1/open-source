## 📝 Summary

Voxelize is an open-source, full-stack voxel engine designed to bring 3D voxel graphics to life. This project provides a comprehensive solution for developers to create and render voxel-based worlds, characters, and objects. With its versatile and customizable architecture, Voxelize enables the creation of a wide range of applications, from games to simulations and visualizations.
