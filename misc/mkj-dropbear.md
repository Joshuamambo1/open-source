## 📝 Summary

### English

mkj/dropbear is an open‑source implementation of the Dropbear SSH server and client, providing a lightweight, secure alternative to OpenSSH for embedded and resource‑constrained systems. It offers full SSH2 protocol support, including public‑key authentication, port forwarding, and SFTP, while maintaining a small footprint and fast performance. The project is maintained under the “Misc” category and is freely available for integration and customization.
