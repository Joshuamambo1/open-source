## 📝 Summary

The Vault Secrets Operator (VSO) is an open-source project that simplifies the integration of HashiCorp Vault with Kubernetes environments. It enables Pods to consume Vault secrets natively from Kubernetes Secrets, streamlining the secure storage and management of sensitive data. This operator facilitates a seamless and scalable approach to secrets management in Kubernetes deployments.
