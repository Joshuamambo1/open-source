## 📝 Summary

Projx is an open-source CLI tool for rapidly scaffolding production-grade projects. With Projx, users can choose from a range of popular stacks, including FastAPI, Fastify, React, and Flutter, and receive a fully configured template complete with authentication, database integration, CI/CD pipelines, and end-to-end testing. This streamlined approach enables developers to focus on their application's core logic, rather than spending time on setup and boilerplate code.
