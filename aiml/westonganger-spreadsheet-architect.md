## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the Spreadsheet Architect project:

Spreadsheet Architect is an open-source library that simplifies the process of generating XLSX, ODS, or CSV spreadsheets from various data sources, including ActiveRecord relations, Ruby objects, and tabular data. This lightweight tool is ideal for data scientists, marketers, and developers who need to quickly export data in a spreadsheet format. By leveraging Spreadsheet Architect, users can easily create and share spreadsheet files in a matter of minutes.
