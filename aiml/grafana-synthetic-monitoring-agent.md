## 📝 Summary

This open-source project, the Synthetic Monitoring Agent, is a tool designed to provide proactive monitoring and detection of issues in applications and services. It uses AI/ML capabilities to simulate user interactions and monitor system performance, allowing for early identification and resolution of potential problems. By leveraging this synthetic monitoring approach, users can ensure high uptime and reliability for their applications.
