## 📝 Summary

Claude Desktop Buddy is an open-source project that allows users to easily interact with their Claude AI model on their local desktop. This project provides a convenient way to access and utilize the capabilities of Claude, a cutting-edge AI model, directly on one's computer. By offering a desktop interface, users can streamline their workflow and leverage the power of Claude AI without relying on online platforms.
