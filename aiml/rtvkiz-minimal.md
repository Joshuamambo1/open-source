## 📝 Summary

This open-source project, rtvkiz/minimal, offers a curated collection of minimal CVE (Common Vulnerabilities and Exposures) hardened container images. These images are designed to provide a secure foundation for AI and ML applications, minimizing the attack surface and ensuring a robust development environment. By leveraging minimalistic images, developers can improve the overall security and reliability of their projects.
