## 📝 Summary

OpenSfM is an open-source, free-to-use Structure-from-Motion (SfM) pipeline that enables the extraction of 3D geometry and camera poses from unordered collections of images. Developed by the Mapillary team, this robust and efficient pipeline can be used for various applications, including photogrammetry, 3D mapping, and computer vision research. By leveraging OpenSfM, users can reconstruct accurate 3D models from large datasets of images.
