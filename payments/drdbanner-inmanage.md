## 📝 Summary

DrDBanner/inmanage is an open-source command-line interface (CLI) for Invoice Ninja, a self-hosted invoicing and billing system. This project allows users to easily install, configure, update, and backup their Invoice Ninja instance, as well as receive notifications and monitor its performance. By utilizing this CLI, users can streamline their Invoice Ninja management experience.
