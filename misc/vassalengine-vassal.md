## 📝 Summary

VASSAL is an open-source board game engine designed to facilitate online and offline gaming experiences. It allows users to create and play a wide variety of board games, from popular titles to custom creations, with features such as 3D game boards, multiplayer capabilities, and customizable rules. This versatile engine enables gamers to connect, play, and share their favorite games in a collaborative and accessible environment.
