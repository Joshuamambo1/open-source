## 📝 Summary

EmDash is an open-source, full-stack content management system (CMS) built on TypeScript and Astro, designed to be the spiritual successor to WordPress. This CMS offers a robust platform for managing and publishing content, leveraging the power of both TypeScript and Astro to provide a modern and scalable solution. With EmDash, users can create, edit, and publish content with ease, taking advantage of its flexible and customizable architecture.
