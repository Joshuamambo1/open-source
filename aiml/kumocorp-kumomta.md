## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the kumomta project:

Kumomta is an open-source, high-performance Mail Transfer Agent (MTA) designed for high-volume email sending environments. Built from the ground-up with scalability and reliability in mind, kumomta leverages AI/ML capabilities to optimize email delivery and minimize latency. This powerful MTA solution aims to provide a fast, secure, and efficient way to manage large-scale email sending operations.
