## 📝 Summary

FeurStagram is an open-source, customizable Android Instagram alternative that incorporates a built-in distraction blocking feature. This project allows users to modify the code and tailor their experience to reduce digital distractions, promoting a more focused and productive Instagram experience. By leveraging the power of open-source, FeurStagram empowers users to take control of their social media consumption.
