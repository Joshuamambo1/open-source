## 📝 Summary

This open-source project, "Train a LLM from Scratch," enables users to train a large language model (LLM) from scratch, allowing for customization and fine-tuning of the model's architecture and parameters. By providing a flexible framework, users can experiment with various models and techniques to achieve optimal results in natural language processing tasks. The project aims to democratize access to LLM training, making it more accessible to researchers and developers.
