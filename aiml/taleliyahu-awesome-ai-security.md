It aggregates the latest research, best‑practice guidelines, and open‑source security utilities to help developers, researchers, and practitioners assess, harden, and monitor AI models throughout their lifecycle. The repository serves as a go‑to resource hub for anyone looking to understand and improve the security posture of AI applications.
