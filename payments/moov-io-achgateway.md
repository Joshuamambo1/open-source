## 📝 Summary

moov‑io/achgateway is an open‑source, highly available payment gateway that streamlines Automated Clearing House (ACH) processing for businesses. It provides a distributed, fault‑tolerant architecture that handles file creation, validation, and transmission, enabling reliable, automated ACH operations at scale. Ideal for fintechs and enterprises looking to integrate secure, low‑cost ACH capabilities into their payment stacks.
