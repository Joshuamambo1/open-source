## 📝 Summary

The rr-debugger/rr project is an open-source record and replay framework that enables developers to reproduce and debug system crashes, freezes, and other issues. It captures a snapshot of the system's state at a specific point in time, allowing for precise reproduction of the issue and efficient debugging. By replaying the recorded state, developers can examine the system's behavior and identify the root cause of problems.
