## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary of the inaturalist project:

The inaturalist project is an open-source Rails application that powers the popular citizen science platform, iNaturalist.org, which utilizes AI/ML technologies to facilitate the identification and cataloging of natural world observations. This platform allows users to contribute and share information about the plants, animals, and other organisms they encounter, promoting biodiversity research and conservation. The project's open-source nature fosters collaboration and innovation among developers and researchers.
