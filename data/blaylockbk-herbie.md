## 📝 Summary

The **Herbie** library provides a simple, command‑line and Python interface for automatically locating and downloading the latest numerical weather prediction (NWP) files—from sources such as NOAA’s NOMADS, partner cloud archives (Amazon, Google, Microsoft), ECMWF open data, and the University of Utah’s Pando Archive. It streamlines access to a wide range of model datasets (HRRR, RAP, GFS, IFS, etc.), making it easier for developers, researchers, and marketers to integrate up‑to‑date forecast data into their applications.
