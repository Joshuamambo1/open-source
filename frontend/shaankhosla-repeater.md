## 📝 Summary

The shaankhosla/repeater project is an open-source terminal-based spaced repetition tool that utilizes the concept of repetitive learning to help users absorb and retain information more effectively. This tool enables users to review and practice their knowledge at optimized intervals, leveraging the scientifically-backed spaced repetition technique. By utilizing the terminal, repeater provides a simple and accessible way to manage and reinforce learning.
