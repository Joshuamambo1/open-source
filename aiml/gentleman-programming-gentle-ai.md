## 📝 Summary

The open-source project "gentle-ai" is a gentle introduction to artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning (ML) concepts, designed to be accessible and easy to understand for developers of all skill levels. This project aims to bridge the gap between theory and practice by providing a simple and intuitive interface to explore various AI and ML techniques. With "gentle-ai," users can experiment and learn from real-world examples, making AI and ML more approachable and enjoyable.
