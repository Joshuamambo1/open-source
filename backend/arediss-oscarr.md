## 📝 Summary

Oscarr is an open-source, modern media request interface designed to integrate seamlessly with Radarr and Sonarr. This project aims to provide a user-friendly interface for managing movie and TV show requests, enhancing the overall experience of media enthusiasts. By combining the strengths of Oscarr with Radarr and Sonarr, users can efficiently track and retrieve their desired media content.
