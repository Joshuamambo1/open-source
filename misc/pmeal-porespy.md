## 📝 Summary

PMEAL/porespy is an open-source project that provides a set of tools for characterizing and analyzing 3D images of porous materials. This project enables users to visualize, segment, and quantify the structure and properties of complex porous systems, facilitating a deeper understanding of their behavior and performance. By leveraging porespy, researchers and engineers can efficiently analyze and optimize the design of porous materials for various applications.
