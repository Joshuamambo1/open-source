## 📝 Summary

Dockit is an open-source, multi-platform GUI client for managing ElasticSearch, OpenSearch, and DynamoDB databases. This user-friendly interface allows developers to easily interact with and visualize their data across various platforms, including Mac, Windows, and Linux. With Dockit, users can streamline their database management tasks with a simple and intuitive interface.
