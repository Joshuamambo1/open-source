## 📝 Summary

HomeBox is an open-source inventory and organization system designed specifically for home users. This project aims to provide a user-friendly interface for managing and tracking personal belongings, making it easier to keep track of possessions and maintain a sense of organization in the home. By utilizing HomeBox, users can streamline their household management and declutter their living spaces.
