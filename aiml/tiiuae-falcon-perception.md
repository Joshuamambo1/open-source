## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary of the Falcon-Perception project:

Falcon-Perception is an open-source inference repository for advanced multimodal models, including Falcon-Perception and Falcon-OCR. These models leverage dense Autoregressive Transformer architectures, enabling early-fusion and native multimodal capabilities. By providing a platform for inference, Falcon-Perception aims to facilitate the development and deployment of cutting-edge AI applications.
