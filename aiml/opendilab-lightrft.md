## 📝 Summary

LightRFT is an open-source AI/ML framework that enables efficient, omni-modal reinforcement fine-tuning of reinforcement learning models. It utilizes a reward-model driven approach to optimize model performance, resulting in improved learning efficiency and adaptability. By providing a flexible and modular platform, LightRFT facilitates the integration of various reinforcement learning techniques and environments.
