## 📝 Summary

Glassbox is an open-source tool that enables humans to review and evaluate AI-generated code in a local environment. This platform helps developers scrutinize the quality and maintainability of AI-coded projects, fostering a more transparent and collaborative approach to AI-driven development. By using Glassbox, developers can gain a deeper understanding of the AI coding process and identify potential issues or areas for improvement.
