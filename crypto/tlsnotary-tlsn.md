## 📝 Summary

The TLSNotary project, implemented in Rust as tlsn, provides a secure method for verifying the authenticity of TLS server certificates through the TLSNotary protocol. This open-source initiative allows users to verify the authenticity of TLS certificates, ensuring the identity and trustworthiness of remote connections. By utilizing the TLSNotary protocol, developers can enhance the security and integrity of their applications and systems.
