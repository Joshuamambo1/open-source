## 📝 Summary

CoolProp is an open-source project that provides a comprehensive library of thermophysical properties for a wide range of working fluids, including refrigerants, pure substances, and mixtures. This library enables developers to easily access and utilize accurate and reliable property data in their applications, facilitating the design and optimization of various systems such as refrigeration, air conditioning, and power generation. With CoolProp, users can leverage a vast collection of thermodynamic properties in a flexible and user-friendly manner.
