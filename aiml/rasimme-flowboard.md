## 📝 Summary

FlowBoard is an open-source project that provides project workspaces for AI agents, specifically designed for OpenClaw. It offers a suite of tools for context management, Kanban boards, specification documentation, and an idea canvas to facilitate collaboration and organization within AI projects. This platform aims to streamline AI development processes, enhancing productivity and efficiency for teams working with AI and machine learning technologies.
