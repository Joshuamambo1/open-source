### English

opengeos / geospatial-data-catalogs is an open‑source, curated collection of publicly available geospatial datasets hosted across major platforms such as AWS, Google Earth Engine, Microsoft Planetary Computer, NASA’s CMR, and the STAC Index. It provides a searchable, categorized inventory that helps developers, analysts, and researchers quickly discover and access high‑quality spatial data for AI/ML, mapping, and scientific projects. The repository is community‑maintained and encourages contributions to keep the catalog up‑to‑date.
