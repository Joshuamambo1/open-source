## 📝 Summary

Liblouis/liblouis is an open-source project that provides a robust braille translation and back-translation system. This tool allows users to convert text into braille and vice versa, supporting various braille codes and languages. By utilizing liblouis, developers can integrate braille support into their applications and improve accessibility for individuals who are blind or have low vision.
