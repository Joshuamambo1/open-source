## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary of the open-source project in 2-3 sentences:

onionlink is an open-source C++ library designed to facilitate connections to v3 onion services. This library leverages the lightweight libsodium and mbedTLS cryptographic libraries to provide a secure and efficient way to establish communication with Tor-hidden services. With over 2,500 lines of code, onionlink offers a robust solution for developers looking to integrate onion services into their applications.
