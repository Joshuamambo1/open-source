## 📝 Summary

The openarm project is an open-source, fully integrated humanoid arm designed for physical AI research and deployment in environments where interaction with physical objects is crucial. This project enables researchers and developers to explore and advance the field of physical AI by providing a versatile, customizable platform for testing and refining artificial intelligence and machine learning algorithms. The openarm facilitates the integration of AI models with physical systems, driving innovation in areas such as robotics, human-computer interaction, and automation.
