## 📝 Summary

Awesome-42 is an open-source project that aggregates useful resources, links, and tools for students at the @42School, a well-known French coding bootcamp. The project aims to provide a centralized hub for students to access relevant information, tutorials, and community support. By sharing knowledge and resources, Awesome-42 seeks to promote learning and success among 42School students.
