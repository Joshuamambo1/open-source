## 📝 Summary

### English

A lightweight, modular button component for ESPHome that leverages LVGL (Light and Versatile Graphics Library) to create customizable, touch‑enabled buttons on ESP32/ESP8266 devices. It provides a clean, reusable platform for defining button styles, actions, and callbacks directly in ESPHome YAML, making UI development faster and more maintainable. Ideal for hobbyists and developers looking to integrate sophisticated graphical controls into their Home Assistant‑linked projects.
