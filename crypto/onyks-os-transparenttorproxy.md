## 📝 Summary

This open-source project, version 0.1.0, provides a stateless, system-wide transparent Tor proxy for Linux. It enables users to browse the internet anonymously through the Tor network, encrypting their internet traffic and masking their IP addresses. This project allows users to easily maintain their online anonymity with minimal configuration required.
