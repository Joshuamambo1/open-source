## 📝 Summary

The expressvpn/lightway project is an open-source workspace that provides a Rust implementation of the Lightway protocol, a high-performance VPN protocol developed by ExpressVPN. This workspace serves as a central hub for contributing to and maintaining the Lightway Rust library, facilitating collaboration and development among the community. By open-sourcing Lightway, ExpressVPN aims to promote transparency and community involvement in the development of the protocol.
