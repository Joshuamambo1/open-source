## 📝 Summary

Videodl is a lightweight, pure‑Python video downloader that prioritizes high‑definition, watermark‑free streams. It supports a vast array of platforms—including TikTok, YouTube, Bilibili, Douyin, Kuaishou, and many Chinese and international media sites—allowing users to fetch videos from virtually any online source. The tool is open‑source, easy to integrate into backend workflows, and is suitable for developers needing a simple, cross‑platform solution for bulk video retrieval.
