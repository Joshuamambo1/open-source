## 📝 Summary

The momentum library is an open-source project developed by Facebook Research, aimed at facilitating human kinematic motion and numerical optimization solvers. This library enables efficient application of human motion in various fields, such as computer graphics, robotics, and simulation. By providing a comprehensive set of tools and algorithms, momentum streamlines the process of planning and simulating human movements.
