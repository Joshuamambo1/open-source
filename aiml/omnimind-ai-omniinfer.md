## 📝 Summary

OmniInfer is an open-source project that enables fast, private, and accessible Large Language Model (LLM) and Vision Language Model (VLM) inference on various devices. This project aims to bridge the gap between powerful AI models and resource-constrained devices, making AI-powered applications more inclusive and efficient. With OmniInfer, developers can easily integrate AI capabilities into their projects without compromising on performance or data security.
