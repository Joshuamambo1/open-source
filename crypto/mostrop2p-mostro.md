## 📝 Summary

### English

Mostro is an open‑source, peer‑to‑peer exchange built on the Lightning Network and integrated with the Nostr decentralized protocol. It enables users to create, discover, and fulfill buy‑ and sell‑offers for Bitcoin in a trustless, censorship‑resistant environment, leveraging Lightning for instant, low‑fee payments and Nostr for decentralized order broadcasting. The platform is aimed at crypto traders seeking a secure, permission‑less marketplace for on‑chain asset swaps.
