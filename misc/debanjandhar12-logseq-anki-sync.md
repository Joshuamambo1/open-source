## 📝 Summary

A lightweight Logseq plugin that automatically syncs your notes with Anki, creating flashcards on the fly. It supports advanced features such as image‑occlusion cards, customizable card direction, incremental card generation, and many other enhancements to streamline spaced‑repetition learning.
