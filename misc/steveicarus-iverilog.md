## 📝 Summary

Icarus Verilog is an open-source Verilog simulator, allowing users to design, model, and test digital circuits through the execution of Verilog code. This project provides a robust and reliable tool for verifying and debugging digital systems, making it a valuable resource for engineers and researchers. By utilizing Icarus Verilog, developers can accelerate the development and testing of complex digital designs.
