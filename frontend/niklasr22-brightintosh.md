## 📝 Summary

BrightIntosh is an open-source project that aims to unlock the full brightness potential of MacBook Pro XDR displays, allowing users to experience the maximum brightness their device can offer. This project targets the frontend aspect of the MacBook Pro's display capabilities, providing a solution for users looking to push their display's brightness to its limits. By doing so, it enables users to enjoy a more vibrant and immersive visual experience on their device.
