## 📝 Summary

CoreELEC is an open‑source, lightweight Linux distribution designed to run the Kodi media center on a wide range of low‑power hardware, such as single‑board computers and set‑top boxes. It provides a streamlined, read‑only OS with built‑in drivers and optimized configurations to deliver fast, reliable playback and a smooth Kodi experience out of the box. The project is community‑driven and regularly updated, making it a popular choice for DIY home‑theater PC builds.
