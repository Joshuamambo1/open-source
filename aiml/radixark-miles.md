## 📝 Summary

Miles is an open-source, enterprise-facing reinforcement learning framework designed for post-training fine-tuning of Large Language Models (LLMs) and Visual Language Models (VLMs). Built upon the foundation of Slime, Miles enables the efficient and scalable enhancement of AI models to meet specific business objectives. Its collaborative development aims to accelerate the application of reinforcement learning in real-world enterprise settings.
