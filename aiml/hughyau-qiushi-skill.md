Qiushi-Skill is an open-source AI/ML framework that distills classical dialectical materialism and practical philosophy into a unifying principle and nine key methods. By applying these principles, developers can build intelligent agents that investigate, prioritize, and validate their approach through practical experience. This framework equips AI systems with a systematic approach to problem-solving, empowering them to tackle complex challenges effectively.
