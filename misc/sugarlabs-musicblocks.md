## 📝 Summary

Music Blocks is an open-source project that provides a musical microworld, allowing users to create and explore music in a visually engaging and interactive environment. This project facilitates learning and creativity in music through its intuitive interface and modular design. By offering a unique platform for music composition and experimentation, Music Blocks empowers users to express themselves and develop their musical skills.
