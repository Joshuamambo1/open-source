**edonyzpc/personal-assistant** is an open‑source Obsidian plugin that leverages AI agents and advanced streamlining techniques to automate note‑taking, organization, and workflow tasks within the Obsidian knowledge base. By integrating generative AI, the plugin can intelligently create, tag, link, and update notes, allowing users to manage their vault with minimal manual effort while maintaining a seamless, context‑aware experience.
