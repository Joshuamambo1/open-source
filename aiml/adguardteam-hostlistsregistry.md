## 📝 Summary

The AdguardTeam/HostlistsRegistry is an open-source project providing known hosts blocklists to users of AdGuard products. These blocklists help protect users from malware, phishing, and other online threats by blocking malicious domains and IP addresses. By making this registry open-source, AdGuard promotes transparency and community-driven contributions to improve online safety and security.
