## 📝 Summary

WhatCable is a small, open-source menu bar application designed to help users identify and inspect their USB-C cables. This lightweight utility provides a convenient way to determine the capabilities and features of various USB-C cables, giving users valuable insights into their connectivity options. By running WhatCable, users can easily verify the authenticity and functionality of their cables.
