## 📝 Summary

Spacelift Spacectl is an open-source, command-line interface (CLI) for the Spacelift platform. It enables users to manage and interact with their cloud infrastructure and configurations from the terminal, streamlining DevOps and infrastructure operations. With Spacectl, users can perform a variety of tasks, including deployment, management, and monitoring of their cloud resources.
