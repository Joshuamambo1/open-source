MFlowCode (MFC) is an open‑source, exascale‑ready multiphase flow solver that earned finalist status for the 2025 Gordon Bell Prize by scaling to over 200 trillion grid points on more than 43 000 GPUs. Designed for high‑performance scientific computing, MFC provides a flexible frontend for setting up complex fluid‑dynamics simulations while leveraging advanced parallelism and GPU acceleration to deliver unprecedented resolution and speed.
