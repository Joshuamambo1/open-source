## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the open-source project "prism-coder":

This project, "prism-coder", is an open-source, HIPAA-hardened cognitive architecture that empowers AI agents with on-device Large Language Models (LLMs). The platform features advanced capabilities such as Hebbian learning, ACT-R spreading activation, and adversarial evaluation, enabling AI agents to reason and interact with their environment in a sophisticated manner. With its built-in visual dashboard and Hivemind multi-agent system, "prism-coder" facilitates seamless collaboration and knowledge sharing among AI agents.
