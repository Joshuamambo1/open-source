## 📝 Summary

The agent-os project is an open-source, portable operating system designed for agents that boasts incredibly fast coldstarts of around 6 milliseconds. Built on WebAssembly and V8 isolates, this operating system aims to provide a cost-effective solution, being 32 times cheaper than traditional sandbox environments. Its cutting-edge technology makes it an attractive choice for AI/ML applications.
