## 📝 Summary

Here's a 2-3 sentence summary of the adysec/tracker project:

The adysec/tracker project is an open-source initiative that aggregates and maintains a comprehensive list of BitTorrent trackers, offering a reliable and daily-updated catalog of available trackers. The project provides two primary lists: "trackers_all.txt" containing a full list of trackers (currently 2902 entries) and "trackers_best.txt" featuring a subset of live and functional trackers (currently 1137 entries). This resource is valuable for the BitTorrent community, facilitating tracker discovery, maintenance, and optimization.
