## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the open-source project akiomik/mado:

Mado is a fast and efficient Markdown linter written in Rust, designed to help developers catch and fix Markdown syntax errors and inconsistencies in their code. With its speed and accuracy, Mado is an ideal tool for developers who want to maintain clean and well-formatted Markdown documents. By integrating Mado into their development workflow, users can ensure their Markdown content is error-free and easy to read.
