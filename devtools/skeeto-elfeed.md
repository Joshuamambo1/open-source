## 📝 Summary

ElfFeed is an open-source, Emacs-based client that provides a convenient interface to manage and read web feeds. With ElfFeed, users can easily subscribe to and keep track of their favorite websites, blogs, and news outlets, all within the familiar Emacs environment. It offers a seamless reading experience, allowing users to efficiently stay up-to-date with the latest content from across the web.
