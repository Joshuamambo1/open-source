## 📝 Summary

The NVIDIA/makani project is an open-source initiative that enables the massively parallel training of machine-learning based weather and climate models. This project empowers researchers and developers to harness the power of parallel computing and accelerate the development of more accurate and efficient weather forecasts. By leveraging AI and ML techniques, makani aims to improve our understanding of complex weather and climate phenomena.
