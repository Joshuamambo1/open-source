## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the siderolabs/omni project:

Omni is an open-source platform that simplifies the deployment of Kubernetes on your own hardware, offering a Software-as-a-Service (SaaS)-like experience. This solution enables users to easily set up and manage their Kubernetes clusters without the need for complex infrastructure management. By doing so, Omni streamlines the process of adopting and running Kubernetes on premises.
