## 📝 Summary

The OpenTrons project is an open-source software platform designed to enable the creation, execution, and management of laboratory protocols on the Opentrons Flex and OT-2 robotic workstations. With OpenTrons, researchers can write and run custom protocols for automated pipetting, mixing, and other laboratory tasks with ease and precision. This flexible and user-friendly platform streamlines laboratory workflows, enhancing productivity and reproducibility in various scientific disciplines.
