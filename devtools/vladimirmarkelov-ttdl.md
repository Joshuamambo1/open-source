## 📝 Summary

The TTDL project is an open-source Terminal Todo List Manager that allows users to efficiently manage their tasks and to-do lists from the comfort of their terminal. This tool provides a simple and intuitive interface for adding, editing, and deleting tasks, making it an ideal solution for those who prefer to stay organized without cluttering their desktop. TTDL aims to enhance productivity by providing a distraction-free and easily accessible task management system.
