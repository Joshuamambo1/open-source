## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the open-source project:

Stripity-stripe is an open-source Elixir library for interacting with the Stripe payment gateway. It provides a simple and efficient way for Elixir developers to integrate Stripe's payment processing functionality into their applications. With stripity-stripe, developers can easily manage subscriptions, payments, and other Stripe-related tasks from within their Elixir projects.
