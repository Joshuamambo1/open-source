## 📝 Summary

This open-source project aims to identify and prevent potential issues in production by detecting hallucinated, typosquatted, and non-canonical dependencies. It helps developers detect and address these dependencies before they cause problems, ensuring a more stable and secure production environment. By catching these issues early, developers can reduce the risk of bugs, outages, and security vulnerabilities.
