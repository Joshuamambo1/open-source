## 📝 Summary

Variety is an open-source wallpaper downloader and manager for Linux systems, designed to provide a seamless experience in selecting and changing wallpapers. With its user-friendly interface, it allows users to download and manage a diverse collection of wallpapers, ensuring their desktop remains visually engaging and fresh. This project enables users to easily switch between different wallpapers, making it an ideal solution for those seeking to personalize their Linux desktop experience.
