## 📝 Summary

Here is a 2-3 sentence summary of the identity-wallet project:

The identity-wallet project is an open-source, Tauri-based wallet designed to manage Decentralized Identities and Verifiable Credentials securely. This wallet provides users with a decentralized platform to store, manage, and control their identity information, enabling them to take ownership of their digital identity. By leveraging verifiable credentials, users can securely share their identity information with trusted parties.
