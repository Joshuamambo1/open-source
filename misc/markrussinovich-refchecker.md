## 📝 Summary

Refchecker is an open-source tool developed by Mark Russinovich that validates academic paper references, ensuring their accuracy and consistency. By utilizing this tool, researchers and authors can streamline the process of verifying references, saving time and reducing errors in their work. Refchecker can help improve the credibility and reliability of academic papers.
