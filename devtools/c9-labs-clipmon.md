## 📝 Summary

Clipmon is an open-source, feature-rich clipboard manager designed specifically for macOS. This powerful tool allows users to store, organize, and recall multiple clipboard entries with ease, making it an indispensable productivity companion for developers and power users alike. By utilizing the macOS clipboard, Clipmon streamlines workflows and reduces the need for repetitive copying and pasting.
