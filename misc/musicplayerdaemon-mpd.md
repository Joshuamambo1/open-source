## 📝 Summary

MPD (Music Player Daemon) is a free and open-source music player server that allows users to control music playback remotely through a variety of clients. This lightweight daemon can be controlled via a command-line interface, web interface, or third-party clients to manage music libraries, playlists, and audio playback settings. With its flexible design, MPD enables users to create a customized music listening experience on a range of platforms.
