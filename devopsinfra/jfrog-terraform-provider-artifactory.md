## 📝 Summary

This open-source project provides a Terraform provider, allowing users to manage JFrog Artifactory infrastructure as code. With this provider, users can easily create, manage, and modify Artifactory resources such as repositories, repositories groups, and permissions, streamlining their DevOps and infrastructure management workflows. Integration with Terraform enables a unified and automated approach to managing Artifactory environments.
