## 📝 Summary

CipherTax is an open-source project that enables secure tax filing by utilizing AI while protecting sensitive personal information. It achieves this by locally redacting personally identifiable information (PII) on the user's device, ensuring that sensitive data remains confidential. This tool empowers users to harness the benefits of AI in tax filing without compromising their privacy.
