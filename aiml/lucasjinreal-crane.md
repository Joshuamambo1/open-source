## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the Crane project:

Crane is an open-source, Pure Rust-based inference engine for various AI and ML models, including LLM, VLM, VLA, TTS, and OCR. Built on top of Candle and Rust, Crane aims to provide a simpler and cleaner alternative to existing solutions, such as llama.cpp. This project enables developers to leverage Rust's safety and performance for AI and ML applications.
