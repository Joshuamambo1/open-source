Sec-deadlines is an open-source initiative providing a centralized platform for tracking deadlines of academic conferences in the fields of Security and Privacy. The project aims to help researchers stay organized and focused by keeping them informed about upcoming submissions, review periods, and other important timelines. By consolidating conference deadlines in one convenient location, sec-deadlines facilitates collaboration and knowledge sharing within the security and privacy research community.
