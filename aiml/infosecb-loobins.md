Here's a 2-3 sentence summary for the infosecB/LOOBins project:

infosecB/LOOBins is an open-source initiative that catalogues and documents macOS built-in binaries that can be exploited by threat actors for malicious purposes. This project, known as Living Off the Orchard: macOS Binaries (LOOBins), aims to educate security professionals and researchers about the potential risks associated with these "living off the land" (LOTL) techniques. By providing detailed information on these binaries and their uses, LOOBins seeks to enhance macOS security and threat detection capabilities.
