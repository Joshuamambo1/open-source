## 📝 Summary

### English

sdiehl/typechecker-zoo is an open‑source collection of compact, pedagogical implementations of a variety of modern type‑checking algorithms—from Hindley‑Milner inference to bidirectional and dependent type systems. Each example is deliberately small and well‑documented, making the repository a handy reference and learning tool for students and developers interested in the mechanics of type theory. The project is organized as a “zoo” of self‑contained programs, allowing readers to explore and compare different approaches side by side.
