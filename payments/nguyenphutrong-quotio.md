## 📝 Summary

Quotio is a free, open-source macOS menu bar app that simplifies AI account management by unifying popular AI tools such as Claude, Gemini, OpenAI, Qwen, and Antigravity. It features real-time quota tracking and smart auto-failover capabilities for seamless integration with AI coding tools like Claude Code and OpenCode. This solution streamlines your workflow and reduces the hassle of juggling multiple AI accounts.
