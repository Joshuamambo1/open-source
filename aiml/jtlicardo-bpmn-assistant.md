## 📝 Summary

The bpmn-assistant is an open-source project that leverages Large Language Models (LLMs) to aid in the creation, modification, and understanding of Business Process Model and Notation (BPMN) diagrams. This AI-driven tool aims to simplify the process of designing and interpreting complex business workflows, making it more accessible to users of all skill levels. By harnessing the power of LLMs, bpmn-assistant streamlines the BPMN diagramming process and enhances collaboration within organizations.
