## 📝 Summary

Qlever is an open-source graph database that supports the RDF and SPARQL standards, enabling efficient storage and querying of large-scale graph data. It is designed for high performance and scalability, capable of handling over a trillion triples on a single commodity machine. Qlever provides a robust and flexible solution for graph data management, catering to demanding applications in various fields.
