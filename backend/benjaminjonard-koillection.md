## 📝 Summary

Koillection is an open-source, self-hosted platform that enables users to efficiently manage various types of collections, providing a centralized hub for organization and tracking. This flexible solution caters to different collection formats, empowering users to tailor it to their specific needs. By hosting Koillection locally, users maintain full control over their data and customization.
