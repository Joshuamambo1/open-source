## 📝 Summary

Kotofetch is a lightweight, open-source command-line interface (CLI) that displays a selection of Japanese quotes in the terminal. Users can customize the application to display the quotes in their preferred language and style. This tool is ideal for those looking to add a touch of Japanese culture to their workflow or terminal experience.
