## 📝 Summary

Clawback is an open-source project designed to test and rehearse upgrades to OpenClaw, a software, before applying them to a live installation. This allows users to simulate potential issues and ensure a smoother transition to new versions. By doing so, Clawback helps minimize risks and prevent potential disruptions to critical systems.
