## 📝 Summary

The in-toto/attestation project is an open-source framework designed to provide robust and transparent supply chain assurance for AI/ML models and other software components. It achieves this through a combination of software provenance and attestation, enabling users to verify the integrity and authenticity of their software components. This framework is particularly useful for organizations seeking to ensure the trustworthiness of their software supply chain.
