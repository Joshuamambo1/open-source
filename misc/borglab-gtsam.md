## 📝 Summary

GTSAM is an open-source C++ library that provides a powerful framework for smoothing and mapping tasks in robotics and vision. It utilizes factor graphs and Bayes networks to efficiently solve complex problems, offering a more expressive and flexible alternative to traditional sparse matrix methods. This library is widely used in research and development for its ability to handle high-dimensional, non-linear, and uncertain data.
