## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the lordmathis/llamactl project:

Llamactl is an open-source project that provides unified management and routing for llama.cpp, MLX, and vLLM models, enabling seamless integration and deployment of these AI models. It comes equipped with a web dashboard, allowing users to easily monitor and control their models. By centralizing management and control, llamaactl streamlines the process of working with these AI models, making it easier to build and deploy AI-powered applications.
