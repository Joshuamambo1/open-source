## 📝 Summary

Here's a summary of the kubernetes-sigs/lws project:

The LeaderWorkerSet (LWS) API is an open-source project that simplifies the deployment of a group of pods as a single unit of replication in a Kubernetes cluster. This enables efficient management of leader-follower patterns, a common requirement in AI/ML and other distributed systems workloads. By providing a standardized API, LWS facilitates the creation and management of leader-worker sets, making it easier to build scalable and reliable distributed applications.
