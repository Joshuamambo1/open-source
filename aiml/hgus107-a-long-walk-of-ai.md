## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary of the open-source project:

This project provides a unique narrative walk through the history of artificial intelligence (AI), tracing the development of the field through a comprehensive collection of key papers from 1936 to 2025. By exploring these seminal publications, users can gain a deeper understanding of the technological advancements and scientific breakthroughs that have shaped the AI landscape. The project aims to serve as a valuable resource for researchers, students, and enthusiasts interested in the evolution of AI.
