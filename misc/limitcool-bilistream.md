## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary of the limitcool/bilistream project:

Bilistream is an open-source tool that enables automatic live streaming of Twitch and YouTube content (including preview-type live streams) to Bilibili without requiring manual supervision. This tool provides a convenient solution for content creators to reach a broader audience on multiple platforms. By automating the live streaming process, bilistream saves time and effort for users.
