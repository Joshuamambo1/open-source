## 📝 Summary

Here's a 2-3 sentence summary for the qiutongxue/oba-live-tool project:

The Obalivetool is an open-source live e-commerce tool that supports various platforms, including Douyin (TikTok) Shop, Juanguan Bao, Douyin Group Buy, Xiaohongshu, WeChat Video Number, Kuaishou Shop, and Taobao. This frontend-focused tool aims to simplify the process of live streaming and e-commerce for content creators and businesses. By leveraging the Obalivetool, users can efficiently manage their live sales across multiple platforms.
