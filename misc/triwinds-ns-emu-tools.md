## 📝 Summary

**triwinds/ns-emu-tools** is an open‑source utility designed to simplify the installation and updating of Nintendo Switch emulators. It automates downloading the latest emulator builds, handling dependencies, and applying patches, making it easy for users to keep their NS emulation environment current with minimal manual effort.
