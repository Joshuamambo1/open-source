## 📝 Summary

Linkerd2-proxy is an open-source, purpose-built proxy for the Linkerd service mesh, designed to provide high-performance and reliable communication between services. Written in Rust, this proxy is optimized for efficient traffic management and scalability. By utilizing Linkerd2-proxy, developers can build robust and secure service meshes for their applications.
