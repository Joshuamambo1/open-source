Pulpcore is an open-source package that serves as the core component of the Pulp project, providing a modular and scalable infrastructure for managing content repositories. It enables developers to build custom content management systems and integrate them with various storage solutions. Pulpcore is designed to be highly extensible and flexible, making it suitable for a wide range of use cases.
