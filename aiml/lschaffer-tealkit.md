## 📝 Summary

TealKit is an open-source, cross-platform user interface designed to interact with local AI agents and Model-Controller-Protocol (MCP) applications. It aims to provide a seamless and intuitive experience for users to leverage the capabilities of AI-powered systems. By utilizing TealKit, developers can create user-friendly interfaces that simplify the interaction between humans and AI agents.
