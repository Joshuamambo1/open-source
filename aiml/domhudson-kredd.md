## 📝 Summary

Kredd is an open-source SaaS application that utilizes AI/ML technology to rank cold inbound emails, providing businesses with a tool to streamline their email management processes. This innovative platform helps organizations prioritize and respond to emails more efficiently, allowing them to maximize their time and resources. By leveraging machine learning algorithms, Kredd aims to enhance the way businesses interact with cold inbound emails.
