## 📝 Summary

Cmochance/codex‑app‑transfer is a lightweight desktop gateway that intercepts responses from the OpenAI Codex CLI and reformats them as Chat Completion payloads, making them compatible with alternative large‑language‑model providers such as Kimi, DeepSeek, Zhipu GLM, Bailian, and any OpenAI‑compatible API. By handling the translation locally, it enables developers to seamlessly switch between multiple AI back‑ends without modifying their existing Codex‑based tooling.
