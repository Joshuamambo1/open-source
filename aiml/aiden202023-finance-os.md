## 📝 Summary

Finance OS is an open-source, self-hosted personal finance tracker that allows users to manage their financial data without sharing sensitive bank credentials. This project enables users to gain control over their financial information and track expenses, income, and budgets in a secure and private environment. By hosting the application themselves, users can access their financial data without relying on third-party services.
