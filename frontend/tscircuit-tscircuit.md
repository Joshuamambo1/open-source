## 📝 Summary

The tscircuit project is an open-source initiative that enables the creation of real-world electronics using TypeScript and React. This innovative platform allows developers to design, simulate, and fabricate electronic circuits in a user-friendly and accessible way. By bridging the gap between software development and electronics, tscircuit empowers developers to bring their ideas to life.
