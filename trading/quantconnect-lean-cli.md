## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary of the QuantConnect/lean-cli project:

QuantConnect's lean-cli is an open-source command-line interface (CLI) designed to facilitate the execution of the LEAN engine, a high-performance trading engine, on local machines as well as in cloud environments. This tool enables users to simplify the process of running their trading strategies and backtesting models. By providing a user-friendly CLI, lean-cli empowers traders and developers to optimize their LEAN engine workflow.
