## 📝 Summary

The rust-lang/thanks project is an open-source initiative that aims to showcase and celebrate the contributions of various individuals who have helped shape the Rust programming language. This project provides a platform to acknowledge and express gratitude towards the contributors, developers, and maintainers who have dedicated their time and effort to the Rust community. By highlighting their contributions, the project seeks to promote a sense of community and appreciation within the Rust ecosystem.
