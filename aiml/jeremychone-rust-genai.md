## 📝 Summary

Here's a summary for the open-source project:

The rust-genai project is an open-source, Rust-based client that provides a unified interface to various generative AI models from leading providers, including Ollama, OpenAI, Anthropic, and more. This multiprovider client enables seamless integration and experimentation with different AI models, fostering innovation and productivity in AI development. With rust-genai, developers can easily switch between AI models and explore their capabilities in a single, Rust-powered framework.
