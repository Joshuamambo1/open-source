## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the Decapod project:

Decapod is an open-source control plane that enables agents to converge on human intent, shape context, and enforce boundaries in concurrent multi-agent work. This daemonless, local-first solution delivers proof-backed completion and supports AI/ML applications. By providing a flexible and secure infrastructure, Decapod helps streamline complex workflows and improve collaboration among agents.
