## 📝 Summary

The mautrix/whatsapp project is an open-source bridge that enables seamless communication between the Matrix protocol and WhatsApp. This bridge allows users to send and receive messages, as well as engage in voice and video calls, across both platforms. By leveraging Matrix's decentralized architecture, mautrix/whatsapp facilitates cross-platform messaging and reduces dependency on proprietary services.
