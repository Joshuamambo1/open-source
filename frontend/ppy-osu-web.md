## 📝 Summary

The ppy/osu-web project is the open-source browser-facing portion of the popular rhythm game osu!, providing a rich online experience for players worldwide. This project combines frontend and backend components to offer a seamless and engaging gameplay experience, further enhancing the osu! community. Developed by the osu! team, it allows users to access their accounts, play songs, and interact with other players in a web-based environment.
