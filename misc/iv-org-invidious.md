## 📝 Summary

Invidious is an open-source project that offers an alternative front-end to YouTube, providing users with a more private and customizable viewing experience. By separating the front-end from YouTube's proprietary service, Invidious allows users to bypass YouTube's tracking and censorship while still accessing their favorite videos. This project promotes user freedom and flexibility on the internet.
