## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary of the stormchecker/storm project:

Storm is an open-source, modern probabilistic model checker designed to analyze and verify complex systems with uncertainty. Built on AI/ML foundations, it enables developers to model and reason about probabilistic systems, ensuring the reliability and accuracy of their models. By leveraging probabilistic model checking, Storm empowers users to identify potential issues and optimize system performance.
