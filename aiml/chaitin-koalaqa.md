## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary of the open-source project in English:

KoalaQA is an open-source, AI-driven customer support community that leverages large models to provide AI-powered answers, search, and operations. This platform enables rapid deployment of after-sales customer service, community Q&A, and self-service scenarios, thereby reducing operational costs and improving customer satisfaction and response efficiency. By utilizing KoalaQA, teams can strive towards achieving Zero Contact Resolution (ZCR) goals.
