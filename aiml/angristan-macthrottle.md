## 📝 Summary

MacThrottle is an open-source menu bar app that provides users with real-time notifications when their Mac is experiencing thermal throttling, helping to prevent overheating and associated system performance issues. This intuitive tool enables users to stay informed and take proactive measures to manage their Mac's thermal health. By monitoring thermal throttling, users can optimize their system for optimal performance.
