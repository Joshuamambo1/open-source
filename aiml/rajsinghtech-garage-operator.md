## 📝 Summary

The garage-operator is an open-source Kubernetes operator designed to manage Garage, a distributed S3-compatible object storage system ideal for self-hosting. This operator simplifies the deployment, scaling, and maintenance of Garage, allowing users to easily manage their object storage needs within their Kubernetes cluster. By leveraging Kubernetes automation, the garage-operator streamlines the process of running Garage, reducing the complexity of self-hosted storage management.
