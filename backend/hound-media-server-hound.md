## 📝 Summary

The Hound media server is an open-source, all-in-one platform for managing media content, allowing users to discover, watch, track, and organize their media collections. This comprehensive backend solution offers a robust framework for media enthusiasts to centralize and effortlessly access their movies, TV shows, and other digital content. With Hound, users can streamline their media experience and enjoy a seamless, integrated entertainment solution.
