## 📝 Summary

TheIdServer is an open-source identity server that supports multiple authentication protocols, including OpenID/Connect, OAuth2, WS-Federation, and SAML 2.0. Built on top of Duende IdentityServer and ITFoxtec Identity SAML 2.0, it provides a comprehensive solution for identity management and authentication. With its included admin UI, developers can easily manage and configure the server to meet their specific needs.
