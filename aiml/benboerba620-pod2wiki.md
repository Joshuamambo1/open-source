## 📝 Summary

This open-source project, "pod2wiki", automates the conversion of high-quality podcasts and long-form RSS feeds into source-summary pages compatible with the karpathy-claude-wiki framework. As part of a zero-code AI investment research toolkit, it facilitates the processing and organization of large amounts of data for efficient analysis. By leveraging this project, users can streamline their research workflow and tap into valuable insights from podcasts and long-form content.
