## 📝 Summary

The faramesh-core project is an open-source, runtime intervention layer designed to enhance the security of AI agents. It achieves this by enforcing approval, least privilege, credential isolation, and durable evidence collection before allowing AI agents to perform risky actions. This approach aims to contain and mitigate potential risks associated with AI decision-making.
