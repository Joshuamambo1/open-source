## 📝 Summary

A lightweight, fast, and dependency‑free library for parsing Cron expressions in Go, **gronx** can determine whether a schedule is due, compute the next or previous run times, and even act as a full‑featured task runner or daemon. It works with Go 1.13+ and can be used as a standalone scheduler, making it a bold alternative to the traditional crontab.
