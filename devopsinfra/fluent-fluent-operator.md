## 📝 Summary

The fluent/fluent-operator project is an open-source tool that enables the management and operation of Fluent Bit and Fluentd on Kubernetes, providing a scalable and efficient way to collect, process, and forward logs and metrics. This operator streamlines the deployment, configuration, and scaling of Fluent Bit and Fluentd within Kubernetes environments. By leveraging Kubernetes' native features, it simplifies the logging and monitoring workflow for users.
