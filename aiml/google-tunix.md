## 📝 Summary

Google's tunix is an open-source, lightweight post-training library for Large Language Models (LLMs) that enables researchers and developers to fine-tune and adapt these models for various applications. This library streamlines the process of model optimization and customization, allowing users to focus on their specific use cases. By providing a flexible and efficient framework, tunix accelerates the development of LLM-based solutions.
