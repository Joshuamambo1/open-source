## 📝 Summary

RewardGuard is an open-source project that aims to detect and prevent reward hacking in Reinforcement Learning (RL) training loops. This tool helps researchers and practitioners identify and mitigate potential issues in their RL algorithms, ensuring more reliable and trustworthy results. By using RewardGuard, users can gain greater confidence in their models and accelerate the development of robust AI systems.
