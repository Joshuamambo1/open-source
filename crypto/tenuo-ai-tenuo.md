## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary of the project:

Tenuo is a high-performance, open-source capability authorization engine designed for AI agents. It utilizes cryptographically attenuated warrants and task-scoped authority to ensure secure and verifiable access, even in offline scenarios. Built using Rust, Tenuo provides a robust foundation for AI agents to operate with confidence and integrity.
