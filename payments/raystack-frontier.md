## 📝 Summary

Frontier is an open-source, all-in-one user management platform that offers identity, access, and billing management capabilities to help organizations safeguard their systems and data. It serves as a free alternative to commercial platforms like Clerk and WorkOS, providing a comprehensive solution for securing digital assets. With Frontier, businesses can streamline user management, enhance security, and optimize their operations.
