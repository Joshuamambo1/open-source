## 📝 Summary

SSTap‑Rule is an open‑source rule set that extends the SSTap client with additional game‑specific network acceleration configurations, turning SSTap into a more comprehensive “online‑gaming accelerator.” By providing ready‑to‑use rules for a wider variety of popular games, the project simplifies the setup process and improves latency and stability for gamers. It is maintained under the Misc category and welcomes contributions from the community.
