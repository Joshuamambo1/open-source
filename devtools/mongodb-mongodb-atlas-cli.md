## 📝 Summary

The MongoDB Atlas CLI is an open-source project that allows users to manage their MongoDB instances in the cloud from the comfort of their local terminal. With this CLI, developers and administrators can easily create, configure, and monitor their MongoDB clusters, streamlining the process of deploying and managing MongoDB in the cloud. This tool simplifies the management of MongoDB Atlas resources, making it a valuable asset for developers and DevOps teams.
