## 📝 Summary

**overshard/timelite** is a lightweight, open‑source time‑tracking tool that lets developers quickly log and visualize how they spend their work hours. Featuring a clean frontend UI and a simple backend API, it integrates easily into existing workflows and provides real‑time analytics without the overhead of heavyweight project‑management suites. Ideal for anyone who wants a straightforward way to monitor productivity, especially during those early‑morning coding sessions.
