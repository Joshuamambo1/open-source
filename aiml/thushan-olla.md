Here's a brief summary of the thushan/olla project:

Olla is an open-source, high-performance proxy and load balancer designed specifically for Large Language Model (LLM) infrastructure. It enables intelligent routing, automatic failover, and unified model discovery across local and remote inference backends. By streamlining LLM deployment and management, Olla helps improve overall system efficiency and reliability.
