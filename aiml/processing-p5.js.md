## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary of the p5.js project:

p5.js is an open-source, client-side JavaScript platform that enables users to learn coding and express creative ideas on the web. Based on the core principles of Processing, this platform empowers artists, designers, students, and beginners to bring their ideas to life. With p5.js, users can create interactive and dynamic web experiences with ease.
