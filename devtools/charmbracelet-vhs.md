## 📝 Summary

Charmbracelet/vhs is an open-source CLI (Command-Line Interface) project that functions as a home video recorder. It enables users to record and manage video content from their terminal or command prompt, providing a unique and innovative way to interact with recorded media. This project is ideal for developers and hobbyists looking to experiment with video recording and playback in a flexible and customizable environment.
