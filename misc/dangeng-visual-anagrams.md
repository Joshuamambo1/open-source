## 📝 Summary

This project, "visual_anagrams," is an open-source implementation of the paper "Visual Anagrams: Generating Multi-View Optical Illusions with Diffusion Models." It utilizes diffusion models to generate multi-view optical illusions, creating an engaging visual experience. By leveraging this code, researchers and developers can explore the capabilities of diffusion models in generating visually striking and thought-provoking anagrams.
