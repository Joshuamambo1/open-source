## 📝 Summary

Leaker is an open-source, passive leak enumeration tool designed to aid in the detection and identification of potential security vulnerabilities. Utilizing AI/ML capabilities, this tool scans networks and systems to identify potential data leaks without actively interacting with them. By leveraging this passive approach, Leaker provides a non-intrusive method for organizations to identify and mitigate potential security risks.
