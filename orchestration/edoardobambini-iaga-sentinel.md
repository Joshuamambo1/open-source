## 📝 Summary

Here's a 2-3 sentence summary for the EdoardoBambini/IAGA-Sentinel project:

Agent Armor, an open-source framework, empowers organizations to govern and control the actions of AI agents by providing visibility and approval over every step of the process. By integrating with popular AI toolchains such as LangChain and CrewAI, Agent Armor ensures that AI agents utilize their granted access responsibly and securely. This orchestration framework promotes transparency and accountability in AI-powered workflows.
