## 📝 Summary

TbSync is an open-source Thunderbird add-on that provides a central user interface for managing cloud accounts and synchronizing contact, task, and calendar information between cloud services and Thunderbird. This add-on offers a seamless way to connect and synchronize your cloud data with Thunderbird, making it easier to manage your online presence. With TbSync, you can keep your Thunderbird and cloud accounts up-to-date and organized in one convenient interface.
