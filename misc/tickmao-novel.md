## 📝 Summary

Novel is an open-source project that aims to organize and provide book sources and software settings for eBook enthusiasts. It includes customizable configurations for font styles, text cleaning rules, and text-to-speech (TTS) settings, allowing users to personalize their reading experience. The project is designed for users who prefer to read novels in digital formats.
