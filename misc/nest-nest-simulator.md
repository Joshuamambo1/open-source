## 📝 Summary

The NEST simulator is an open-source software package for large-scale, parallel simulations of spiking neural networks. It allows researchers to model and analyze complex neural systems, enabling them to investigate the behavior of individual neurons and large populations of interconnected neurons. By leveraging parallel computing architectures, NEST facilitates efficient simulations of neural dynamics on a wide range of scales.
