## 📝 Summary

Celestia Mobile is an open-source universe simulator that brings the power of stargazing to your Android device. This intuitive app allows users to explore the night sky, visualize celestial bodies, and gain a deeper understanding of the solar system and beyond. With Celestia Mobile, you can take the wonder of the universe with you wherever you go.
