## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary of the project:

MentisDB is an open-source, append-only database designed to provide durable memory for AI agents, allowing them to improve over time through stored thought chains and a Git-like skills registry. This enables skills to evolve with experience, maintaining integrity and version control through fast retrieval and integrity-checked updates. By storing and versioning skills, MentisDB empowers AI agents to build upon their past experiences and knowledge.
