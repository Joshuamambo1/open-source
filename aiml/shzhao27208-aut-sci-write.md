## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary of the project:

The Aut_Sci_Write project is an open-source research skills suite designed for Claude Code, a powerful tool for academic tasks. It integrates literature search capabilities across arXiv, PubMed, and Web of Science, accompanied by features like PDF extraction, figure cropping, review writing, Zotero syncing, and automatic PPT generation. This comprehensive suite aims to streamline research workflows and enhance productivity for researchers and scholars.
