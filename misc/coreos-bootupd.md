## 📝 Summary

CoreOS Bootupd is an open-source project designed to automate the updating of bootloaders, ensuring that the system's boot process remains secure and up-to-date. This tool simplifies the process of updating bootloaders, eliminating the need for manual intervention. By leveraging Bootupd, users can maintain a secure and reliable boot environment.
