## 📝 Summary

The Zebra project is an open-source implementation of the Zcash cryptocurrency in Rust, offering financial privacy and secure transactions. By utilizing the Zcash protocol, Zebra enables users to enjoy a high level of anonymity and confidentiality in their financial dealings. This project is a key contribution to the Zcash ecosystem, providing a secure and private alternative to traditional digital currencies.
