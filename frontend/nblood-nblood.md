## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary of the NBlood/NBlood project:

NBlood is an open-source project that uses the EDuke32 engine to reverse-engineer and recreate classic Build games, including the popular NBlood, Rednukem, and PCExhumed. By leveraging EDuke32's technology and development principles, the project aims to preserve and expand the gameplay experience of these iconic titles. This initiative allows gamers to enjoy these classic games on modern platforms while contributing to their preservation and evolution.
