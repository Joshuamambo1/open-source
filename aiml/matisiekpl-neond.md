## 📝 Summary

NeonD is an open-source, self-hosted Postgres platform that offers advanced features including data branching, point-in-time recovery (PITR), and automated backups. This project aims to provide a robust and reliable solution for managing Postgres databases, allowing users to have full control over their data. By hosting NeonD on-premises, users can enjoy enhanced security, customization, and compliance.
