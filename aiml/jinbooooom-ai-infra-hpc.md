## 📝 Summary

This open-source project, "ai-infra-hpc", provides a comprehensive tutorial on High-Performance Computing (HPC) for AI and Machine Learning applications. It covers essential topics such as MPI and NCCL collective communications, CUDA programming, vectorized SIMD operations, and RDMA networking. This resource aims to equip developers with the knowledge and skills needed to optimize and scale AI and ML workloads on HPC infrastructure.
