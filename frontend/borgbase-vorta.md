## 📝 Summary

Vorta is an open-source, desktop backup client designed to work seamlessly with Borg Backup, a powerful and secure data archiving tool. This user-friendly client allows users to easily manage their backups, configure repositories, and monitor their storage usage. By integrating with Borg Backup, Vorta provides a convenient and intuitive interface for backing up and restoring data.
