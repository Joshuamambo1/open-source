## 📝 Summary

Here's a 2-3 sentence summary for the hugovk/norwegianblue project:

Norwegian Blue is an open-source command-line interface (CLI) tool designed to display end-of-life dates for various projects and software. It provides a simple and efficient way to check the status of different projects and plan maintenance or updates accordingly. By leveraging Norwegian Blue, developers can stay informed about the lifecycle of their dependencies and dependencies of their dependencies.
