## 📝 Summary

Koha is an open-source, integrated library system that provides free and flexible management of library collections, patrons, and circulation. Developed under the GNU GPL version 3 or later, Koha offers a comprehensive suite of tools for libraries of all sizes. Its community-driven project allows for ongoing development and support through collaborative bug tracking and patch submission.
