## 📝 Summary

### English

Agents.jl is an open‑source, Julia‑based framework for building and simulating agent‑based models. It provides a flexible, high‑performance infrastructure for defining agents, environments, and interaction rules, enabling researchers and developers to explore complex systems in fields such as ecology, economics, and social science. By leveraging Julia’s speed and expressive syntax, Agents.jl facilitates rapid prototyping, large‑scale simulations, and easy integration with other scientific‑computing tools.
