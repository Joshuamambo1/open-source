## 📝 Summary

This open-source project, korean-law-mcp, is a comprehensive platform that aggregates data from 41 Korean government APIs, providing 16 MCP (Machine-Generated Citation Parser) tools for searching, querying, and analyzing laws, court cases, ordinances, and treaties. The project utilizes AI-powered search and analysis capabilities, along with an LLM (Large Language Model)-based citation hallucination guard to ensure accuracy and authenticity of citations.
