## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the Harries/skills-desktop open-source project:

Skills Desktop is an open-source desktop client for skills.lc, a platform that helps users manage and track their skills. This client provides a user-friendly interface for users to access and utilize the skills.lc features directly from their desktop, enhancing their productivity and experience. By utilizing this desktop client, users can streamline their workflow and stay organized with their skills.
