## 📝 Summary

OmenAI is an open-source, autonomous polymarket scanner that leverages AI and machine learning to identify mispriced probabilities. This project, powered by Claude, enables users to capitalize on market edges by accurately sizing their bets with the Kelly criterion. By providing real-time market analysis, OmenAI aims to empower traders and investors in the crypto space with data-driven insights.
