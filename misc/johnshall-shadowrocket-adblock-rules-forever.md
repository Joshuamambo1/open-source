Here's a 2-3 sentence summary for the project:

Shadowrocket-ADBlock-Rules-Forever is an open-source project that provides a comprehensive set of rules for Shadowrocket, a popular ad blocker. These rules are designed to offer robust ad filtering capabilities, ensuring a seamless and distraction-free browsing experience. The rules are rebuilt daily at 8:00 AM, ensuring users have access to the latest and most effective ad-blocking functionality.
