## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the smartest-tv project:

The smartest-tv project is an open-source initiative that enables users to control their smart TVs using natural language commands. This project uses AI/ML capabilities to allow users to interact with their TVs from their terminal, making it possible to play content from popular streaming services like Netflix by simply mentioning its name. Currently, smartest-tv supports various TV brands, including LG, Samsung, and Roku.
