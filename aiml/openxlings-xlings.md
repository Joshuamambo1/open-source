## 📝 Summary

openxlings/xlings is a highly abstract package manager that enables seamless multi‑version handling and treats any resource—code, data, models, or other assets—as a package. By unifying version control, dependency resolution, and deployment under a single, extensible framework, it simplifies the management of AI/ML workflows and promotes reproducible research. The project is open‑source and designed for developers who need flexible, version‑agnostic packaging across diverse environments.
