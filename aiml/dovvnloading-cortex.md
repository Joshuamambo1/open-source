## 📝 Summary

Cortex is an open-source, private desktop AI assistant that leverages local Large Language Models (LLMs) through the Ollama framework. This secure and highly responsive tool keeps all models and data on the user's device, eliminating the need for cloud storage and third-party involvement. By utilizing local LLMs, Cortex provides a seamless and secure AI interaction experience.
