## 📝 Summary

OpenUPM is an open-source, community-driven package registry for Unity, providing a centralized and accessible platform for discovering, sharing, and managing Unity packages. This project aims to offer a free and open alternative to Unity's official package registry, allowing developers to easily share and use custom packages. By leveraging OpenUPM, developers can streamline their workflow and collaborate more efficiently.
