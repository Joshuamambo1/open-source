## 📝 Summary

The shadPS4 project is an open-source, cross-platform PlayStation 4 emulator written in C++ that supports Windows, Linux, macOS, and FreeBSD operating systems. This emulator aims to provide users with a way to play PS4 games on various platforms, although it may still be in development and not yet fully functional.
