## 📝 Summary

WorkProof is an open-source project that proposes a JSON schema for skill evidence graphs, aiming to provide a standardized framework for representing and verifying skills and knowledge. This initiative seeks to facilitate the creation of transparent and trustworthy skills-based systems, potentially applicable in various fields such as education, employment, and professional development. By standardizing skill evidence, WorkProof aims to enhance the accuracy and reliability of skills assessments.
