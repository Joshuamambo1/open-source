## 📝 Summary

Here's a 2-3 sentence summary for the phlx0/drift project:

Drift is an open-source terminal screensaver that brings ambient visuals to your idle screen, featuring mesmerizing effects such as constellations, rain, and particles. Activating when you're idle, it allows you to momentarily disconnect from your screen and resume use by simply pressing any key. This project combines frontend development with AI/ML elements to create a soothing and immersive experience.
