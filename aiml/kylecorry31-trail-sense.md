Trail-Sense is an open-source Android app that leverages your phone's sensors to provide critical assistance during wilderness treks or survival situations. By utilizing AI and machine learning algorithms, the app aims to enhance situational awareness, predict potential hazards, and offer valuable insights to help users stay safe and navigate challenging environments. This innovative app empowers adventurers and outdoor enthusiasts with a reliable and accessible tool for their next expedition.
