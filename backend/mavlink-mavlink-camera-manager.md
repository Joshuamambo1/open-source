## 📝 Summary

The MAVLink Camera Manager is an open-source backend service that provides a standardized interface for managing camera settings and configurations in MAVLink-enabled drone systems. This service enables seamless communication between cameras and flight controllers, ensuring efficient and reliable camera operations. By leveraging the MAVLink protocol, the Camera Manager streamlines camera management tasks and enhances overall drone system performance.
