## 📝 Summary

Here's a summary for the open-source project:

This open-source project, freellmapi, is a proxy that aggregates free-tier keys from 14 AI providers, making it easier for developers to experiment with OpenAI-compatible APIs. It features automatic failover, ensuring a seamless experience even when one provider is unavailable. Designed for personal experimentation, freellmapi streamlines access to a diverse range of AI capabilities.
