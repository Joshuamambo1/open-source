## 📝 Summary

The absorb project is an open-source, cross-platform Audiobookshelf client that provides seamless access to audiobooks on both Android and iOS devices. This mobile application allows users to manage their audiobook collections, stream content, and sync data across various platforms. With its open-source nature, the community can contribute to and benefit from its development.
