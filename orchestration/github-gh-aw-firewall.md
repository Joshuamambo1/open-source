## 📝 Summary

The **GitHub Agentic Workflows Firewall (gh‑aw‑firewall)** is an open‑source tool that secures and governs AI‑driven automation pipelines within GitHub. By acting as a policy‑enforcement layer, it monitors, validates, and restricts agentic workflow actions—preventing unauthorized or unsafe operations while preserving the flexibility of orchestrated AI/ML tasks. This makes it ideal for teams that need robust control over intelligent automation in their CI/CD and DevOps environments.
