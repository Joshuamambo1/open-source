## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary of the dbt-checkpoint project:

The dbt-checkpoint project provides a set of pre-commit hooks to enhance the quality of your dbt projects, ensuring that your database transformations are reliable and maintainable. These hooks offer a comprehensive way to catch errors, enforce best practices, and streamline your development workflow. By integrating these hooks into your pre-commit process, you can catch potential issues early and improve the overall quality of your dbt projects.
