## 📝 Summary

Soulsolid is an open-source music organizer designed for music enthusiasts, allowing them to manage and host their personal music collections locally. This self-hosted platform caters specifically to those who cherish their music libraries, providing a customized and private music management solution. By hosting their music collection locally, users can maintain control and accessibility to their music without relying on external services.
