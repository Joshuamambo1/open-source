## 📝 Summary

EmDash is an open-source, full-stack content management system built with TypeScript and Astro, designed to be a spiritual successor to WordPress. This CMS aims to provide a robust and scalable platform for managing digital content, offering a flexible and customizable solution for developers and content creators alike. By leveraging the strengths of Astro, EmDash seeks to revolutionize the way we build and manage websites.
