## 📝 Summary

The codemem project is a lightweight, open-source companion designed to work seamlessly with OpenCode and Claude, enhancing their capabilities by providing persistent memory functionality. This allows for efficient storage and recall of complex AI and ML models, facilitating smoother and more reliable operation. By integrating codemem, developers can unlock improved performance and productivity in their AI and ML applications.
