## 📝 Summary

Eclipse hawkBit is an open-source over-the-air (OTA) update solution designed to simplify the distribution of software updates to large fleets of devices. This cloud-based platform enables secure, efficient, and automated deployment of updates to a wide range of devices and systems. By leveraging hawkBit, developers can streamline the update process and ensure their devices remain up-to-date with the latest software and security patches.
