## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the Saber-Translator project:

Saber-Translator is an open-source, user-friendly comic translation tool designed to help comic enthusiasts overcome language barriers and enjoy authentic Japanese comics. Utilizing advanced AI technology, it accurately detects and translates Japanese text in comic dialogues, providing smooth and natural Chinese translations. Compatible with both image and PDF formats, Saber-Translator simplifies the reading experience for fans of Japanese comics.
