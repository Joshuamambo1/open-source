## 📝 Summary

Portabase is an open-source database backup and restore tool designed to simplify the management of data backups and recovery processes. This tool enables users to easily create, manage, and restore backups for various databases, providing a comprehensive solution for data integrity and security. By leveraging Portabase, users can ensure the safety and reliability of their databases with minimal effort.
